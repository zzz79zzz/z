Pharmacologic Category
  • Gastrointestinal Agent, Prokinetic
Reported Use

Relief of symptoms of functional (nonulcer) dyspepsia including heartburn, nausea, and vomiting.

Dosage Range

Adults: Oral: 5 mg 3 times daily.

Product Availability

Product available in various countries; not currently available in the United States.

Dosage Forms

Packet, Oral: 5 mg

Tablet, Oral: 2.5 mg, 5 mg
