Index Terms
  • Tropisetron Hydrochloride
Pharmacologic Category
  • Antiemetic; Selective 5-HT3 Receptor Antagonist
Reported Use

Prevention of nausea and vomiting induced by cytotoxic chemotherapy; treatment and prevention of postoperative nausea/vomiting

Dosage Range

Adults:

IV:

Chemotherapy-induced nausea and vomiting: 5 mg before chemotherapy on day 1.

Prevention of postoperative nausea/vomiting: 2 mg shortly before induction of anesthesia.

Treatment of postoperative nausea/vomiting: 2 mg as a single dose.

Oral: Chemotherapy-induced nausea and vomiting: 5 mg daily on days 2 through 6 following IV dose given on day 1.

Product Availability

Product available in various countries; not currently available in the US

Dosage Forms

Solution, Injection: 1 mg/mL

Solution, Injection/Oral: 1 mg/mL
