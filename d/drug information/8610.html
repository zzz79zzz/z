Contraindications

Hypersensitivity to aluminum chloride or any component of the formulation.

Warnings/Precautions

Other warnings/precautions:

• Application: Since large amounts of astringents may cause tissue irritation and possible damage, only small amounts should be applied.

Administration: Adult

Oral: Dispense a small amount of solution into a clean dappen dish or disposable cup. For hemorrhage control during restorative impression, soak a suitable length of retraction cord in solution and place cord into the gingival sulcus using a cord-packing instrument. For minor hemorrhage control in situations other than restorative impressions, soak a cotton pellet in solution and apply to affected area for several seconds, then rinse with water.

Use: Labeled Indications

Hemostatic: Control of minor gingival bleeding during dental restorative procedures.

Metabolism/Transport Effects

None known.

Drug Interactions

There are no known significant interactions.

Mechanism of Action

Precipitates tissue and blood proteins causing a mechanical obstruction to hemorrhage from injured blood vessels
