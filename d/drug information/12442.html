Warning
  • urn:lims:b498:s7290911 • No Severe infections have happened in patients who take this drug. Some of these have led to treatment in a hospital or death. Most people who had these infections were taking other drugs to lower the immune system like methotrexate or steroid drugs. If you have any infection, are taking antibiotics now or in the recent past, or have had many infections, talk with your doctor.
  • urn:lims:b498:s7290912 • No TB (tuberculosis) has been seen in patients started on this drug. These patients were exposed to TB in the past, but never got the infection. You will be tested to see if you have been exposed to TB before starting this drug. You may also be tested for TB while you take this drug.
  • urn:lims:b498:s4468885 • No Lymphoma and other cancers have happened in people who take this drug or drugs like it. This has been deadly in some cases. Talk with the doctor.
