Adverse Reactions

The following adverse drug reactions and incidences are derived from product labeling unless otherwise specified. Frequency not defined.

Ophthalmic: Corneal decompensation, corneal edema, corneal opacity, ophthalmic inflammation

Contraindications

There are no contraindications listed in the manufacturer labeling

Warnings/Precautions

Disease-related concerns:

• Diabetes: Use with caution in patients with diabetes mellitus; intraoperative lens changes have been observed when undergoing vitrectomy procedure.

Dosage form specific issues:

• BSS Plus: Do not use unless Part I of the product is fully reconstituted with Part II.

Other warnings/precautions:

• Appropriate use: Not for injection or IV infusion.

Administration: Pediatric

Ophthalmic: Not for injection or IV infusion. For administration by surgeon in surgical suite. Do not use if discolored or contains a precipitate.

BSS Plus: Use an administration set with an air-inlet in the plastic spike (bottle does not contain separate airway tube). Allow fluid to freely flow to remove air from the tubing prior to irrigation. If a second bottle of reconstituted BSS Plus is needed to complete a surgical procedure, vent vacuum in the second bottle prior to attachment to the administration set.

Administration: Adult

Ophthalmic: Not for injection or IV infusion. Do not use if discolored or contains a precipitate.

BSS: Attach surgeon’s sterile irrigating needle to adapter plug. Squeeze out several drops before inserting into anterior eye chamber. Remove needle from anterior eye chamber before releasing pressure to prevent suction. External irrigation may be done without irrigating needle. Discard any unused portion.

BSS Plus: Use an administration set with an air inlet in the plastic spike (bottle does not contain separate airway tube). Allow fluid to freely flow to remove air from the tubing prior to irrigation. If a second bottle of reconstituted BSS Plus is needed to complete a surgical procedure, vent vacuum in the second bottle prior to attachment to the administration set. Discard any unused portion within 6 hours of preparation.

Storage/Stability

Store at 2°C to 25°C (36°F to 77°F); do not freeze.

BSS Plus: Discard prepared solution after 6 hours.

Use

Irrigation during various intraocular surgical procedures involving perfusion of the eye (BSS: Procedure <1 hour; BSS Plus: procedures of any duration) (FDA approved in pediatric patients [age not specified] and adults).

Metabolism/Transport Effects

None known.

Drug Interactions

There are no known significant interactions.
