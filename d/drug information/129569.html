Administration: Pediatric

Topical: For external use only; avoid contact with eyes. Do not use on deep or puncture wounds, animal bites, or serious burns. Formulation-specific application instructions may vary; refer to manufacturer's labeling.

Administration: Adult

Topical: For external use only; avoid contact with eyes. Formulation-specific application instructions may vary; also refer to manufacturer's labeling.

Storage/Stability

Store at room temperature; do not freeze. Avoid excessive heat.

Use

Prevent and temporarily protect chafed, chapped, or cracked skin; prevent and protect skin from the drying effects of wind and cold weather (All indications: FDA approved in all ages).

Note: Approved uses and ages for pediatric patients may vary by product; consult product-specific labeling.

Medication Safety Issues
Look alike/sound alike issues:

Dimethicone may be confused with simethicone.

International issues:

Hedrin brand name for dimethicone (topical) [United Kingdom] may be confused with Hedrin brand name for simethicone [Israel].

Baritex brand name for dimethicone (topical) [Singapore] may be confused with Barex brand name for polyethylene glycol-electrolyte solution [Argentina].

Metabolism/Transport Effects

None known.

Drug Interactions

There are no known significant interactions.

Pregnancy Considerations

Dimethicone is not absorbed systemically following topical administration (Nair 2003). Use during pregnancy would not be expected to result in significant exposure to the fetus.

Mechanism of Action

Forms a protective layer on the skin that acts as a barrier to moisture loss and a protector against repeated hydration and chemical irritants.

Pharmacokinetics (Adult Data Unless Noted)

Absorption: Little to no absorption.
