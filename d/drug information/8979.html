Dosing: Adult
Eye irritation

Eye irritation: Ophthalmic: Instill 1 to 2 drops in affected eye(s) as needed.

Gonioscopic examination aid

Gonioscopic examination aid: Gonak, Goniosoft: Ophthalmic: Fill gonioscopic prism with solution as necessary.

Ophthalmic surgical aid

Ophthalmic surgical aid: OcuCoat Viscoelastic: Ophthalmic: Use during ophthalmic surgery as directed; consult specific product instructions.

Dosing: Kidney Impairment: Adult

There are no dosage adjustments provided in the manufacturer's labeling; however, dosage adjustment unlikely due to low systemic absorption.

Dosing: Hepatic Impairment: Adult

There are no dosage adjustments provided in the manufacturer's labeling; however, dosage adjustment unlikely due to low systemic absorption.

Dosing: Older Adult

Refer to adult dosing.

Adverse Reactions

There are no adverse reactions listed in the manufacturer's labeling.

Contraindications

Hypersensitivity to hydroxypropyl methylcellulose or any component of the formulation; in conjunction with hot laser treatment (Gonak, Goniosoft).

Warnings/Precautions

Other warnings/precautions:

• Appropriate use: Remove contact lenses prior to use. Do not use if solution changes color or becomes cloudy.

• Self-medication (OTC use): When used for self-medication, discontinue and contact a health care provider if eye pain, vision changes, continued redness, or eye irritation occur or if condition worsens or persists >72 hours.

Administration: Adult

Ophthalmic: For ophthalmic use only. Remove contact lenses before using. Wash hands thoroughly before administration; do not touch tip of container to eye or any surface; replace cap after use. Do not reuse single-use syringes.

Gonak, Goniosoft: Not for use in conjunction with hot laser treatment. If solution dries on optical surfaces, let stand in cool water before cleansing.

Use: Labeled Indications

Eye irritation: Temporary relief of discomfort due to minor irritations of the eye or from exposure to wind and sun; protection against further eye irritation; relief of dry eyes.

Gonioscopic examination aid (Gonak, Goniosoft): For professional use during gonioscopic examination.

Ophthalmic surgical aid (OcuCoat Viscoelastic): Ophthalmic surgical aid in anterior segment surgical procedures, including cataract extraction and intraocular lens implantation.

Medication Safety Issues
Sound-alike/look-alike issues:

Isopto Tears may be confused with Isoptin.

Metabolism/Transport Effects

None known.

Drug Interactions

There are no known significant interactions.
