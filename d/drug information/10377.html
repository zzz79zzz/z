Pharmacologic Category
  • Mucolytic Agent
Reported Use

Treatment of acute or chronic bronchopulmonary diseases associated with abnormal mucous secretion and clearance

Dosage Range

Children: Oral suspension:

15 to <20 kg: 175 mg twice daily

20 to 30 kg: 175 mg 3 times daily

>30 kg: 350 mg twice daily

Adults:

Oral capsule: 300 mg 2 to 3 times daily

Oral suspension: 350 mg twice daily

Product Availability

Product available in various countries; not currently available in the US

Dosage Forms

Capsule, Oral: 300 mg

Suspension Reconstituted, Oral: 35 mg/mL
