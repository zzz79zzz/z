Index Terms
  • Fenticonazole Nitrate
Pharmacologic Category
  • Antifungal Agent, Imidazole Derivative;
  • Antifungal Agent, Topical;
  • Antifungal Agent, Vaginal
Reported Use

Treatment of fungal infections including dermatomycoses, pityriasis versicolor, or vaginal candidiasis

Dosage Range

Adults:

Topical: Cream, powder, solution/spray: Apply to affected area once or twice daily

Vaginal:

Capsule: Insert 1 capsule (200 mg) at bedtime for 3 days or insert 1 capsule (600 mg) at bedtime as a single dose

Cream: Insert 1 applicator full (~5 g) into the vagina twice daily (morning and evening) for 3 days

Product Availability

Product available in various countries; not currently available in the US

Dosage Forms

Cream, External, as nitrate: 2%

Cream, Vaginal, as nitrate: 2%

Capsule, Vaginal, as nitrate: 200 mg, 600 mg

Powder, External, as nitrate: 2%

Solution, External, as nitrate: 2%
