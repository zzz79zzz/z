Warning

Call your doctor right away if you are not able to move or bear weight on a joint or if you hear or feel a snap or pop.
  • urn:lims:b498:s4642543 • No Call your doctor right away if you have signs of nerve problems. These may include not being able to handle heat or cold; change in sense of touch; or burning, numbness, tingling, pain, or weakness in the arms, hands, legs, or feet.

  • urn:lims:b498:s2700299 • No Do not take if you have myasthenia gravis. Very bad and sometimes deadly breathing problems have happened with this drug in people who have myasthenia gravis.
  • urn:lims:b498:s6206985 • No For some health problems, this drug is only for use when other drugs cannot be used or have not worked. Talk with the doctor to be sure that the benefits of this drug are more than the risks.
