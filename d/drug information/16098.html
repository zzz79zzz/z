Dosing: Adult

General dosing guidelines; consult specific product labeling.

Dietary fiber supplement

Dietary fiber supplement:

Chewable tablet (Benefiber Chewables): Oral: 3 tablets (3 g fiber/dose) up to 3 times daily; maximum daily dose: 9 tablets/day.

Gummies (Benefiber Prebiotic Gummies): Oral: 3 gummies (6 g fiber/dose) up to 2 times daily; maximum daily dose: 6 gummies/day.

Powder (bulk) (Benefiber Original Prebiotic Fiber Supplement): Oral: 2 teaspoons (4 g fiber/dose) added to 4 to 8 ounces of liquid or soft food 3 times daily.

Powder (bulk) (Benefiber Healthy Shape): Oral: 1 rounded tablespoon (5 g fiber/dose) added to 8 ounces of liquid or soft food 2 times daily.

Powder (individual packet) (Benefiber on the Go): Oral: 1 packet (3 g fiber/dose) added to 4 to 8 ounces of liquid or soft food 3 times daily.

Mechanism of Action

Wheat dextrin is a soluble fiber. It absorbs water in the intestine to form a viscous liquid which promotes peristalsis and reduces transit time.
