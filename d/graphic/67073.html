Treatment recommendation for individuals with IFG, IGT, or elevated A1C
Population Treatment
IFG, IGT, or A1C (5.7 to 6.4%) Lifestyle modification (ie, ≥7% weight loss and moderate-intensity physical activity, ≥150 minutes per week)
Individuals with IFG, IGT, or A1C ≥6%, especially for those:
  • <60 years of age
  • BMI ≥35 kg/m2
  • History of gestational diabetes
 Lifestyle modification (as above) and/or metformin*

A1C: glycated hemoglobin; BMI: body mass index; IFG: impaired fasting glucose; IGT: impaired glucose tolerance.

* Metformin 850 mg twice per day.
