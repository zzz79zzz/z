Histopathologic features of spiradenoma
Histology of spiradenoma at 4x (A) and 10x (B) magnification. Characteristic tubular structures comprised of PAS-positive hyaline basal membrane material as well as focal ductal differentiation are shown. The basaloid cells intermixed with paler cells and lymphocytes are clustered into dense nodules extending to subcutis.
PAS: periodic acid-Schiff.
