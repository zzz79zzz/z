Dermoscopic features of dyskeratosis congenita

Onychoscopy (DermLite DL4N, polarized mode ×10) demonstrating:

(A) Longitudinal ridging (arrow).

(B) Longitudinal splits forming deep furrows (arrow).

(C) Longitudinal splits, furrows, with splinter hemorrhage (circle), and distal lamellar splitting (arrow).
