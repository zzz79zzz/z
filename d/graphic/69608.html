T-cell receptor V gene use in Hashimoto's thyroiditis
Many different V genes coding for the TcR are present in T cells from the thyroid of patients with Hashimoto's thyroiditis, suggesting that many antigens are involved in the pathogenesis of the disease.
TcR: T-cell receptor.
