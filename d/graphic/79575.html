Malalignment ventricular septal defect in a patient with tetralogy of Fallot
This still frame from the accompanying echocardiographic video of the parasternal long-axis view demonstrates the malalignment of the ventricular CS and TS, which results in aortic override in a patient with tetralogy of Fallot. In the video clip, cephalad angulation of the imaging transducer reveals the hypoplastic pulmonary artery.
CS: conal septum; TS: trabecular septum; LV: left ventricle; RV: right ventricle; LA: left atrium; Ao: aorta.
