FEV1 after lung volume reduction surgery (LVRS) or lung transplantation in emphysema
Percent of predicted FEV1 before and after volume reduction (VR), single lung transplantation (SLT), and bilateral lung transplantation (BLT). The FEV1 with volume reduction at six months was significantly increased compared to the baseline level but less than with both bilateral and single lung transplantation.
