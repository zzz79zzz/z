Thin-cap fibroatheroma with recent intraplaque hemorrhage.
(1A) Low (x20) and (1B) higher-power (x200) views of a thin-cap fibroatheroma with recent hemorrhage. Note, in 1B there is spillage of erythrocytes from surrounding intraplaque vasa vasorum (arrow).
(2A, 2B) Serial section of the lesion in Panel 1A shows a large pool of extravasated erythrocytes with proximate micro-vessels (arrow). Panel 2B is a high-power view of boxed area in Panel 2A.
