Choledochal cyst type 1c
Previously healthy 20-month-old girl admitted with nausea, vomiting, and jaundice. MRCP image of the dilated CBD, which forms a type 1c choledochal cyst. The dilated CBD (cyst) is 5 cm wide and 7.2 cm long (panel A). The dilated CBD displaces the distended GB inferiorly (panel B).
CBD: common bile duct; GB: gallbladder; MRCP: magnetic resonance cholangiopancreatography.
