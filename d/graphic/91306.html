Branch calcification of popliteal artery on x-ray
A focused image from an A-P x-ray over the left tibia (A) shows calcification of the below knee popliteal artery just before it branches into the anterior tibial artery laterally and the tibioperoneal trunk medially. Image B is a magnification view and shows the branch point (arrow).
A-P: anteroposterior.
