Three-dimensional ultrasound picture of a clubfoot
Rendered three-dimensional image of a second-trimester fetus. The lower legs are demonstrated. Both feet are abnormally positioned with the plane of the lower legs parallel to that of the sole of the foot. A fetus with the feet persistently in this position has bilateral talipes equinovarus, or bilateral clubfoot.
