Location of fibrous plaques in carcinoid heart disease
In patients with carcinoid heart disease, distinctive lesions, termed carcinoid plaques, develop on the right side of the heart (tricuspid and/or pulmonic valves); such plaques are occasionally found on the left side of the heart.
