US hip gluteus minimus tendon insertion in transverse view
This ultrasound scan shows the gluteus minimus tendon insertion onto the greater trochanter in the transverse oblique plane (short-axis view).
Arrows: gluteus minimus tendon; arrowheads: iliotibial band; A: anterior facet of greater trochanter; B: lateral facet of greater trochanter; C: gluteus medius muscle.
Left side of image: posterior; right side of image: anterior.
