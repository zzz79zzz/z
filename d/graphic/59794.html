AV graft for hemodialysis
This drawing shows an AV graft in a person's arm. A graft is under the skin. A doctor makes a graft by doing surgery. They use a flexible rubber tube to connect an artery to a vein. During hemodialysis, 2 needles are put into the graft to remove and return blood to the body.
AV: arteriovenous (this means involving an artery and a vein).
