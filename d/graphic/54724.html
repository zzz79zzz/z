Gastric antral vascular ectasia (watermelon stomach)
Serial endoscopic images of gastric antral vascular ectasia ("watermelon stomach"), that has been treated with argon plasma coagulation. The top row shows the original appearance of the lesions, and their appearance following initial treatment. The middle row shows the appearance following the first treatment session and after the second treatment session. The third row demonstrates the marked improvement following the first two treatment sessions, and the appearance after a third treatment session.
