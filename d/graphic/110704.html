LMA Fastrach repositioning maneuvers for stopping a cuff leak
After the LMA Fastrach is inserted, cuff leaks may be present. Three basic maneuvers that can be used to improve the seal of the cuff and stop such leaks are shown in this video clip. The first maneuver shown involves rotating the handle side to side (handle moves in the coronal plane). The second involves rotating the handle up and down (handle moves in the sagittal plane). The third involves lifting the handle as if raising a skillet, with the handle remaining parallel to the floor.
LMA: laryngeal mask airway.
