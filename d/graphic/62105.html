Lentigo maligna melanoma
Lentigo maligna melanoma usually arises in areas of sun-damaged skin, particularly on the head and neck. It begins as a freckle-like, tan-brown macule and gradually enlarges and develops darker or lighter, asymmetric foci and raised areas, which signify dermal invasion.
