US transducer position start lateral knee examination
The photograph above shows the proper position of the ultrasound transducer to evaluate the distal iliotibial band in its long-axis and to begin the examination of the lateral knee. The transducer is located at Gerdy's tubercle.
Rectangle: US transducer position; arrow: direction transducer is translated, from imaging patellar tendon in long-axis to imaging iliotibial band.
