External rotation exercise for frozen shoulder
To stretch the anterior portion of the shoulder capsule, the patient stands in a door frame with their elbow bent at approximately 90 degrees and the inside of their distal forearm resting against the frame, and then externally rotates the shoulder by turning their body away from the forearm. The forearm is kept in place by the door frame.
