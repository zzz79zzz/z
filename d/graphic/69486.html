Neonatal resuscitation supplies and equipment
Warming and transport equipment
  • Preheated warmer
  • Warm blankets
  • Hat
  • Plastic bag or wrap (for preterm infants <32 weeks gestation)
  • Transport incubator
Suction equipment
  • Bulb suction
  • Mechanical suction with 10 or 12 French suction catheters and 80 to 100 mmHg wall suction
  • Manual suction with 8 French feeding tube and 20 cc syringe
  • Meconium aspirator
Monitoring equipment
  • Stethoscope for auscultation of the heart and lungs
  • Cardiac monitor
  • Pulse oximeter monitor with sensor
Oxygenation supplementation and ventilation
  • Source for free-flowing oxygen (tanks or wall supply of oxygen and air)
  • Oxygen blender (set to 21 to 30% oxygen concentration for neonates <35 weeks gestation; all others 21% oxygen concentration)
  • Face masks with appropriate sizing for term and preterm infant
  • Positive-pressure device (T-piece resuscitator, flow-inflating bag, or self-inflating bag)
  • Oropharyngeal airway
Intubation equipment
  • Laryngoscope blades (size 0 and 1), handle, and stylet
  • Endotracheal tubes (size 2.5, 3, and 3.5)
  • End-tidal carbon dioxide (CO2) detector
  • Endotracheal tube securing device or tape
  • Laryngeal mask airway
Vascular access and equipment to administer
  • Umbilical venous catheterization tray and supplies
  • Peripheral intravenous catheters with securing supplies
  • Needles
  • Syringes
Medications
  • Intravenous epinephrine 1 mg/10 mL vial (0.1 mg/mL)
  • Normal saline
  • 10% dextrose
  • Naloxone hydrochloride (0.4 mg/mL)
