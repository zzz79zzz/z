High resolution computed tomographic (HRCT) scanning of the chest in a 55 year old woman with idiopathic pulmonary hemosiderosis and daily hemoptysis for 6 months. There are several areas of ground- glass opacification (arrows) in both lungs, indicating alveolar filling.
