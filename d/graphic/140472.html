Fetal myelomeningocele with kyphosis
23-week female fetus. Longitudinal ultrasound (A) and sagittal SSFSE T2-weighted MRI (B) of the spine show a long myelomeningocele defect (arrows) from T9 to L5 with upper lumbar kyphosis (dashed arrow), which was quantified as 32 degrees.
SSFSE: single-shot fast spin echo; MR: magnetic resonance.
