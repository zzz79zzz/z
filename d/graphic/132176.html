Wandering atrial pacemaker
Wandering atrial pacemaker. Lead V1 and lead II rhythm strips show an irregularly irregular rhythm. On close examination there are more than 3 different P wave morphologies preceding each QRS complex. As the rate is <100 beats per minute, the rhythm is wandering atrial pacemaker (rather than multifocal atrial tachycardia).
