Fasted state: Empty gastric antrum
The antrum has a small hypoechoic cavity with very little fluid and no solid material or air. The walls of the antrum appear thick and non-distended. This is the appearance in the fasted state.
R: rectus abdominis muscle; L: liver; A: antrum; P: pancreas; SMA: superior mesenteric artery; D: diaphragm; AO: aorta.
