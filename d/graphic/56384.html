12-lead electrocardiogram (ECG) consistent with Mahaim fiber tachycardia
Electrocardiogram from a patient with an atrioventricular reentrant tachycardia due to a Mahaim accessory pathway. The QRS complexes are wide with a left bundle branch block morphology and left axis deviation; these changes are characteristic of a Mahaim fiber tachycardia.
