In this protocol, which is provided for guidance only, suggested doses of warfarin after day 2 are given as ranges. The clinician must judge the rapidity and magnitude of INR changes for the individual patient and make dosage adjustments accordingly. An algorithm for monitoring and adjustment of maintenance warfarin is presented separately. Refer to UpToDate topics on use of warfarin and table on suggested protocol for adjustment of maintenance warfarin.
