Earth-heart sign

(A) Posteroanterior chest radiograph showing earth-heart sign. The cardiac silhouette appears to be flattened. This sign indicates cardiac compression caused by tension pneumomediastinum.

(B) Normal cardiac silhouette on 12th postoperative day after air drainage.
Modified from: Obeso Carillo GA, Barge Caballero G, Canizares Carretero M, et al. The Earth-Heart sign: A new diagnostic finding in a patient with tension pneumomediastinum. Lancet 2014; 383:486.
