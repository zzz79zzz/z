CXR for redo sternotomy
CXR posterior-anterior and lateral in a patient who had a prior sternotomy for implantation of a VAD. Note the presence of sternal wires used to re-approximate the midline sternal incision in the posterior-anterior (panel A) and lateral (panel B) CXR. The outflow graft (labeled O) of the VAD is directed immediately beneath and behind the sternum. The inflow (labeled I) to the VAD is located at the left ventricular apex.
CXR: chest radiograph; VAD: ventricular assist device.
