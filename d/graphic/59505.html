Peroxide systems
AOSept (CIBA Vision) – 3-bottle system: MiraFlow (concentrated cleaner), SoftWear saline (to rinse off the MiraFlow), and AOSept (neutralized by a catalytic disc in the storage case).
Clear Care (CIBA Vision) – Similar to AOSept, but a 1-step system with cleaner incorporated into the Clear Care solution and neutralized with a catalytic disc in the storage case. 6-hour minimum.
Oxycept/UltraCare formula (Johnson & Johnson) – 4 parts: cleaner, saline rinse, peroxide solution, and a neutralizing tablet (solution changes color to indicate neutralization).
PeroxiClear (Bausch & Lomb) – 1-step system with a catalytic disc in the storage case. 4-hour minimum.
Sauflon One Step (Sauflon USA) – 1-step system with added wetting agent. Neutralization is with a catalytic disc. Available only through eye care professionals.
