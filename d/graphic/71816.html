Atrial bigeminy
Atrial bigeminy in which each sinus beat is followed by a premature atrial complex. The premature P waves (arrows) appear to be inverted in lead II, suggesting that they originate from a low atrial ectopic focus. The tall peaked P waves of the sinus beats are consistent with right atrial abnormality (overload).
