Collagenous colitis
Light micrograph of a colonic biopsy from a patient with collagenous colitis. The characteristic change is thickened subepithelial collagenous band in the colonic mucosa that stains in bright blue (arrows).
Courtesy of Prof Dr G Herrmann, Frankfurt.

Normal colon
Low (left) and high (right) power views of a biopsy of a normal colon. Low power reveals straight crypts and mild lamina propria mononuclear cell infiltration. High power shows the surface enterocytes with interspersed goblet cells (arrows).
