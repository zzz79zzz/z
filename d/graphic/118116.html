Growth in small intestine length
Small intestine length for (A) post-conception age, and (B) height, based on measured bowel length at laparotomy for 108 subjects between 24 weeks and 5 years post-conceptual age. The trendline is based on the optimal model from a curve fit process.
SBL: small bowel length.
Reproduced from: Struijs MC, Diamond IR, de Silva N, Wales PW. Establishing norms for intestinal length in children. J Pediatr Surg 2009; 44:933. Illustration used with the permission of Elsevier Inc. All rights reserved.
