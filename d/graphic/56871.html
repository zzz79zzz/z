Manual neck distraction test for cervical radiculopathy
The head and neck are placed in the neutral position. With the examiners hands firmly grasping the angle of the jaw and the base of the skull, traction is applied in the upward vertical direction. A positive result is a diminution of the patient's radicular symptoms.
