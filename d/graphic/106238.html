Change in intervertebral space with spine flexion
For lumbar neuraxial anesthesia, the patient is optimally positioned with the lumbar spine flexed. As shown in this graphic, flexion opens the space for needle insertion between adjacent spinous processes. For further details, refer to the UpToDate topic on spinal anesthesia technique.
