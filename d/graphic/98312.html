Chest radiograph of bronchiectasis in Williams-Campbell Syndrome
Chest computed tomogram of a patient with Williams-Campbell syndrome showing cystic bronchiectasis (arrow) and dilation of medium caliber airways. In Williams-Campbell syndrome, generalized tracheobronchomalacia is caused by deficient cartilage in the 4th to 6th order bronchi.
