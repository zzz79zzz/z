Translation from RNA to protein
Translation is the process in which the genetic information from mRNA is converted to proteins with biochemical or biologic functions. The process is highly regulated on multiple levels. Refer to UpToDate topics on gene expression for further details.
DNA: deoxyribonucleic acid; RNA: ribonucleic acid; mRNA: messenger RNA; tRNA: transfer RNA.
