Late evolution of anterior MI
Later stage in the evolution of an acute anterior ST-elevation/Q wave myocardial infarction. There is a QS pattern in leads V1 to V3 and T wave inversion in leads V2 to V4. The ST segment elevations in these leads have almost resolved.
