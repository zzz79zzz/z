US hip gluteus minimus tendon insertion in sagittal view
This ultrasound image shows the gluteus minimus tendon insertion onto the greater trochanter in the sagittal oblique plane (long-axis view).

Arrows: gluteus minimus tendon; *: gluteus minimus muscle; large arrowheads: iliotibial band; A: anterior facet of greater trochanter; B: gluteus medius muscle.

Left side of image: inferior; right side of image: superior.
