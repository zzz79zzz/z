Wallet card that can be carried by individuals with HIT
Individuals with a history of HIT can carry this wallet card and show it to clinicians who are not familiar with their diagnosis and the importance of avoiding all forms of heparin exposure. This may be especially important if they are injured and unable to convey this information themselves.
HIT: heparin-induced thrombocytopenia.
