MRI and sagittal CT of occult hamate fracture in a golfer with chronic wrist pain
The MRI images above show an occult hamate fracture in a golfer with chronic wrist pain. There is marrow edema (arrows) on both the axial T1W image (A) and the axial T2W fat saturation image (B) in the hook of hamate. The sagittal CT image (C) confirms the diagnosis of a fracture (dashed arrow).
H: hamate; MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
