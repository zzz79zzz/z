Survival after potentially curative resection in a multi-institutional series of 370 patients undergoing pancreaticoduodenectomy for a distal bile duct cancer
Survival according to the number of involved nodes in 370 patients. P<0.001 (pN0 versus pN1, 1 to 3 involved nodes; pN0 versus pN1, 4 or more involved nodes) (log rank test).
From: Kiriyama M, Ebata T, Aoba T, et al. Prognostic impact of lymph node metastasis in distal cholangiocarcinoma.
