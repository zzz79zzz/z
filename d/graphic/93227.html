Chronic hepatitis C virus infection
A portal tract with mild chronic inflammation. Focal spillage of inflammatory cells into the limiting plate (the first row of hepatocytes surrounding the portal tract) is termed interface hepatitis. The more severe the degree of interface hepatitis, the faster the progression of the liver disease and fibrous tissue deposition. An arrow points to interface hepatitis.
