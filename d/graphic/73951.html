Two dimensional echocardiogram of mitral stenosis
The precordial long axis during diastole from a two dimensional echocardiogram of a patient with mitral stenosis shows a very small, flow limiting orifice that is maximally opened (arrow). The flexible portion of the valve leaflet domes (assumes a knee bend appearance) under the continually elevated left atrial (LA) pressure and lowered left ventricular (LV) diastolic pressure. The LA and right ventricle (RV) are large and the LV small.
Ao: aorta.
