Gross pathology of an intralobar pulmonary sequestration

Gross pathology of an intralobar pulmonary sequestration
A large, distended systemic artery is present at one border (red arrow). A dilated, mucus-filled bronchus (blue arrow) is evident adjacent to the artery. The lung parenchyma has a microcystic appearance, distinguishing it from the adjacent normal parenchyma.
