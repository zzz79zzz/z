Acute MI with thrombus in LV aneurysm on CT scan
A CT scan through the LV shows acute thrombus in an aneurysm of the LV apex (arrow). The 46-year-old patient presented with an acute MI that was complicated by severe apical dyskinesis and apical thrombus formation (arrow).
LV: left ventricular; CT: computed tomography; MI: myocardial infarction.
