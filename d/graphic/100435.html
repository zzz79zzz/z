Chest radiograph showing features of mitral stenosis with left atrial enlargement and pulmonary edema
A posteroanterior chest radiograph of a patient with mitral stenosis with left atrial enlargement causing a straight heart border (arrowhead) and double density (arrow). There is also cephalization of the pulmonary vessels (dashed arrow) consistent with cardiogenic pulmonary edema.
