Parapneumonic effusion
Extrapulmonary sources of pleural space infections can occur hematogenously or through direct proximity to the pleural space. Common sources include esophageal perforation and intraabdominal infections.
* Biliopleural fistulae can produce sterile pleural space infections from the inflammatory effects of bile.
