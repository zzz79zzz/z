Hyperventilation and overlapping clinical pictures. In the assessment of the patient presenting with hyperventilation, one needs to consider the potential contributions of behavioral disorders and seemingly inappropriate dyspnea in the presence of known cardiopulmonary disease. The size of the circles is not meant to imply relative prevalence of the conditions.
