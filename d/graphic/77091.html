Carotid artery pulse tracing and phonocardiogram of aortic stenosis with bicuspid valve at the 2nd right intercostal space
Carotid artery pulse tracing and phonocardiogram of aortic stenosis as heard at the 2nd right intercostal space. S1 (mitral and tricuspid valve closure) is followed by an ejection click (opening of the stenotic aortic valve) and a midsystolic ejection murmur of aortic stenosis. A single S2 results from delayed closure of the aortic valve, which occurs simultaneously with pulmonic valve closure.
S1: 1st heart sound; S2: 2nd heart sound.
Provided by John M Criley, MD, The Physiological Origins of Heart Sounds and Murmurs, Little, Brown, Boston, 1996, 1-800-527-0145.
