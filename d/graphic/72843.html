Anterior wall myocardial infarction
Four-chamber view from a 2D echocardiogram shows a large dyskinetic region of the apex and anterior wall of a dilated left ventricle as a result of a large anterior wall infarction. The anterior wall and interventricular septum are thin and do not thicken during systole.
