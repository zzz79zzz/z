Although every other flutter wave can be seen at the end of the T wave in the first part of the tracing (arrows), a sinus mechanism cannot be excluded. The flutter waves become clearly apparent after carotid sinus massage is applied to slow conduction through the AV node, thereby increasing the degree of AV block.
