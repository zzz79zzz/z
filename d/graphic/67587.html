Postpneumonectomy shift of the liver
Chest CT demonstrates the postoperative change in location of the liver, showing the potential risk of hepatic injury with blind chest tube or needle placement into the postpneumonectomy space. The liver (closed arrow) abuts the chest wall; the open arrow denotes fluid in the postpneumonectomy space.
CT: computed tomography.
