Sharps disposal containers
These are examples of sharps disposal containers. They are hard plastic containers for collecting used needles and other sharps. Only use the container until it is three-quarters full. Then, seal it up and follow your local guidelines for disposing of it. You can go to www.safeneedledisposal.org and type in your zip code to see the options available near you.
