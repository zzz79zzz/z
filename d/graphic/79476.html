Continuous glucose monitoring
Glucose values in the interstitial fluid of the patient under consideration on three consecutive days (Thursday, Friday, and Saturday) and parts of two other days during continuous glucose monitoring. The device used in this patient measures glucose in the range of 40 to 400 mg/dL. Values below 40 mg/dL are depicted as a straight line at 40 mg/dL on the ordinate.
