Mammogram grouped calcifications A
Spot compression view of a mammogram showing a high-density spiculated mass (arrow) with heterogeneous linear clacifications in a ductal distribution (arrowheads). These "casting" calcifications are characteristic of high-grade ductal carcinoma in situ (DCIS). Pathology revealed infiltrating duct cell carcinoma with DCIS, comedo type.
