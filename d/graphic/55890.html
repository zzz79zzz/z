Molecular forms of cholecystokinin (CCK) and gastrin
Amino acid sequences of CCK-58, -33, and -8, and gastrin-34 and -17, all of which are biologically active. All forms of CCK and gastrin share an identical carboxyl terminal pentapeptide sequence (highlighted in the boxes).
