Specimen Method Volume Storage temperature* Comments
Genital
Fluids - endometrial, amniotic, pelvic (culdocentesis) Sterile anaerobic container 1 to 10 mL 4ºC Anaerobic culture is appropriate only for specimens obtained without traversing colonized areas (eg, vagina).
Chlamydia trachomatis/Neisseria gonorrhoeae (usually done together) Swab or chlamydia transport medium   4ºC Choice of swab depends upon test method (culture, EIA, DFA, molecular amplification). For culture specimens, avoid cotton swab on wooden stick.
Neisseria gonorrhoeae culture Inoculate selective medium or transport kit immediately   25ºC For culture specimens, use CO2 transport kits or devices and do not refrigerate. For molecular detection, use rayon or Dacron-tipped swab.
Herpes simplex virus Swab in viral transport media   4ºC Choice of swab depends upon test method (culture, DFA, molecular amplification). For culture specimens, avoid cotton swab on wooden stick and calcium alginate swab.
Vaginal Swab   4ºC For Trichomonas, Candida, etc.
Genitourinary
Prostate Prostatic massage specimens in sterile container 1 to 5 mL 4ºC  
EIA: enzyme immunoassay; DFA: direct fluorescent antibody.
* If immediate processing is not possible.
