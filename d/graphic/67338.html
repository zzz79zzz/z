Lymphoma arising in celiac disease
Small bowel follow through examination shows a large ulcerating extrinsic mass (white arrow) arising between and displacing loops of jejunum in a patient with celiac disease. Note the distended, featureless loops of jejunum, which have lost their normal fold pattern; these features are characteristic of celiac disease.
