Neonatal gastroschisis
Classic-appearing gastroschisis with the abdominal wall defect to the right of the cord insertion (the yellow clamp is on the umbilical cord). Loops of small bowel can be noted with one area of dilation at the base of the mass. The bowel dilation was a "new finding" during ultrasound examination and was the indication for delivery (37 weeks).
