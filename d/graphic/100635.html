PET CT scan of GCA patient with aortic involvement
PET CT scan of a patient with biopsy-proven GCA, showing increased FDG uptake in the walls of the ascending and descending aorta.
PET: positron emission tomography; CT: computed tomography; GCA: giant cell arteritis; FDG: fluorodeoxyglucose.
From: Docken WP. Two inflammatory conditions—polymyalgia rheumatica and giant cell arteritis-share clinical connection. The Rheumatologist 2013. http://www.the-rheumatologist.org/article/two-inflammatory-conditionspolymyalgia-rheumatica-and-giant-cell-arteritisshare-clinical-connection/.
