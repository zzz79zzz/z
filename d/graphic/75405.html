Muscular VSD multiple color still frame
Paired apical images with and without color flow mapping show a large posterior muscular VSD and two smaller apical muscular VSDs (arrows). Though the larger posterior defect near the crux is easily seen without color flow mapping, the apical defects are more easily missed without color flow Doppler interrogation of the septum.
LA: left atrium; RA: right atrium; LV: left ventricle; RV: right ventricle; VSD: ventricular septal defect; arrows: multiple muscular VSDs.
