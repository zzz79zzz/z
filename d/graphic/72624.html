Age Language milestones Literacy milestones
6 months
  • Turns to voice (4 months)
  • Orients to bell horizontally
  • Imitates speech sounds

  • Reaches for book
  • Brings book to mouth
  • Looks at pictures
  • Vocalizes or pats at pictures
  • Prefers pictures of faces
12 months
  • Orients to bell diagonally (10 months)
  • Understands "no"
  • Follows simple gestured commands
  • Uses "dada"/"mama" appropriately
  • Additional 1 or more words
  • Immature jargoning
  • Begins to point for desired objects
  • Holds book with help
  • Turns board book pages with assistance
  • Points to pictures with one finger, nonspecific
  • May begin to label pictures with "words"
  • Prefers pictures of familiar objects
18 months
  • Identifies familiar objects
  • Follows simple ungestured commands
  • Points to 2 to 4 body parts
  • Vocabulary of 10 to 25 words
  • Giant words ("Thank you")
  • Mature jargoning
  • Brings book to caregiver
  • Turns board book pages easily
  • Points to familiar pictures, specific
  • Scribbles spontaneously
24 months
  • Follows novel two-step commands
  • Vocabulary of >50 words
  • Creates 2- to 3-word sentences
  • Begins to use "I," "me," or "you"
  • Telegraphic speech, no jargon
  • 50% intelligible to strangers
  • Learning to handle paper pages
  • Looks "through" book for favorite pictures
  • May fill in words or complete rhymes from familiar books
  • Imitates vertical stroke
30 months
  • Follows 2 prepositional commands
  • Understands concept of "just one"
  • Names all body parts
  • Names 4 pictures, 1 color
  • Knows two actions
  • Echolalia ceases
  • "Reads" as part of creative play
  • May complete sentences from familiar books
  • Recites parts of well-known stories
  • Imitates circular motion with crayon
3 years
  • Follows 3 element commands
  • 3- to 4-word sentences, >500 words
  • 75% intelligible to strangers
  • Uses pronouns, plurals correctly
  • Counts to 3, names >1 color
  • Disfluency is common
  • Turns pages easily
  • Recites whole phrases, sometimes whole stories
  • Begins to coordinate text with pictures
  • Corrects mistakes made by the reader
  • Copies a circle
4 years
  • Understands 4 prepositions
  • Creates 4- to 5-word sentences
  • 100% intelligible to strangers
  • Uses past tense
  • Counts to 4, names 4 colors
  • Retells familiar stories
  • Developing awareness of print
  • Recognizes some letters of the alphabet
  • Beginning writing skills
  • Picks longer line
  • Copies a cross
5 years
  • Understands opposites
  • Creates complete (>5-word) sentences
  • Uses future tense, irregular verbs
  • Disfluencies uncommon
  • Counts to 10 or more
  • Understands print carries the message
  • May move finger along the text
  • Recognizes most letters of the alphabet, writes some
  • "Writes" name
  • Copies a square
