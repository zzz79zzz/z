Lunge
Stand with the feet together. Step the right foot approximately 36 inches in front of the body. The right knee should be over the right ankle. Hold for 5 seconds. Step the right foot back so that the feet are together. Rest as needed. Repeat with the left leg. Repeat 10 to 15 times (one set). Perform a total of 3 sets.
