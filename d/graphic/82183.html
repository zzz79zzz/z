Endometriosis of the ovary
Axial CT scan of the pelvis in a 29-year-old woman demonstrates a well-circumscribed, low attenuation mass (arrow) in the left pelvis immediately posterior to the uterus. This mass was found to be an endometrioma of the left ovary at surgery.
CT: computed tomography.
