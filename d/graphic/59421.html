Variety of transducer options
The center probe, a linear 12-5 mHz transducer, is most often used for peripheral vascular imaging. The left-hand probe, called a "hockey stick," can be useful in tight locations. The right-hand probe is a curved, lower-frequency transducer that allows for greater tissue penetration in obese patients.
