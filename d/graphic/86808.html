Simplified risk scores for POV/PONV
(A) When 0, 1, 2, 3, or 4 of the depicted independent predictors are present, the corresponding risk for PONV is approximately 10%, 20%, 40%, 60%, or 80%.
(B) When 0, 1, 2, 3, or 4 of the depicted independent predictors are present, the corresponding risk for PONV is approximately 10%, 10%, 30%, 50%, or 70% respectively.
POV: postoperative vomiting; PONV: postoperative nausea and vomiting.
(A) Recommendations for adults reproduced from: Gan T, Meyer T, Apfel C, et al. Society for Ambulatory Anesthesia guidelines for the management of postoperative nausea and vomiting.
