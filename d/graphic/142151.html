Diagnostic algorithm to differentiate lipedema from obesity
This algorithm is intended for use in conjunction with additional UpToDate content on lipedema.

OWL: obesity without lymphedema; OIL: obesity-induced lymphedema; BMI: body mass index.

* An abnormal test shows delayed inguinal uptake of tracer and/or dermal backflow.

¶ OIL can occur with a maximum BMI history >40.
