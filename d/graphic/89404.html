Combination of the 3 major dermoscopic patterns in melanocytic nevi of the palms and soles

In this nevus, the 3 major, benign dermoscopic patterns, the parallel furrow pattern (solid blue circle), fibrillar pattern (dashed black circle), and lattice-like pattern (dotted white circle), are detected. Note that they are arranged in an orderly fashion.
