Paget disease of the breast presentation
Various presentations of Paget disease of the breast are represented here. Paget disease is typified by erythematous, scaly, and weeping "eczema" that involves the nipple. Discoloration, depigmentation, and desquamation of the nipple and areola are sometimes seen.
