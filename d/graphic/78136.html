Some esterified estrogen-methyl testosterone (EEMT) combinations remain available in United States. These are considered unapproved products by US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) and are not recommended; refer to section on androgens in UpToDate topic review of treatment of menopausal symptoms with hormone therapy.

CEE: conjugated equine estrogens.

* For females with an intact uterus, a progestogen (ie, natural [micronized] progesterone or a synthetic progestin) must be added to prevent estrogen therapy-associated endometrial hyperplasia.
