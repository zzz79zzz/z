Ultrasonographic appearance of cartilage calcification (chondrocalcinosis) of the wrist
This long axis sonogram of the wrist of a 72-year-old female patient with cartilage calcification (chondrocalcinosis) shows both hypoechoic and hyperechoic areas in the triangular fibrocartilage complex. Areas of hyperechogenicity (arrows) are likely to represent calcium pyrophosphate dihydrate (CPP) crystal deposits.
