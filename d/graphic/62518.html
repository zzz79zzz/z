Nasolacrimal duct obstruction
Infants with nasolacrimal duct obstruction present with a history of chronic or intermittent tearing, and debris on the eyelashes. Conjunctival erythema is not typical, although chronic rubbing of the eyes may cause redness of the lower eyelid.
