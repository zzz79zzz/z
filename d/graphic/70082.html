Shift in mediastinal position following pneumonectomy
Chest CT demonstrates the postoperative change in location of mediastinal organs, with the heart abutting the chest wall. Blind chest tube or needle placement into the postpneumonectomy space may puncture the heart or great vessels.
LV: left ventricle, RV: right ventricle, LA: left artium, RA: right artium, a: descending aorta, e: esophagus; CT: computed tomography.
