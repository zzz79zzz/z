Pleomorphic undifferentiated sarcoma on MRI
A T1-weighted MRI in the coronal projection prior to contrast (A) shows a mass in the vastus lateralis muscle of the right thigh. There are surrounding soft tissue changes (arrow). The mass enhances following gadolinium administration (arrow B).
