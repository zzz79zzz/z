Activation of G proteins
Upon binding of hormone to the receptor, a change occurs in the structure of the hormone-receptor complex such that a G protein is recruited to the membrane and activated. G proteins are composed of three subunits (alpha, beta, and gamma). G protein stimulation then initiates a variety of cellular processes.
