P6 acupressure point
Pressure or massage at the P6 acupressure point is reported in some studies to relieve motion sickness. The point is found three of the patient's fingerbreadths proximal to the wrist fold, between the palmaris longus and flexor carpi radialis tendons, shown in this picture by the tip of the pen.
