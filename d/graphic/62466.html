Ragged red fibers
Skeletal muscle biopsy from another patient shows ragged red muscle fibers admixed with a population of relatively normal muscle fibers (Gomori trichrome stain). The ragged red fibers represent abnormal accumulations of mitochondria beneath the plasma membrane and between the myofibrils.
