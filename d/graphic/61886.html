Light chain deposition disease - electron micrograph
Electron micrograph showing granular deposits (arrow) along the tubular basement membrane in light chain deposition disease. The appearance of these deposits is different from the fibrils seen in amyloidosis even though both types of deposits are composed of light chain fragments.
