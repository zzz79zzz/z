Keeping track of your blood sugar
Each day, write down the date on a new row. Then, write down your blood sugar level each time that you check it. Use the "Notes" column to write down any other information that your doctor told you to track. This includes things like how much you exercised, any changes to your usual diet, and if you were feeling sick.
