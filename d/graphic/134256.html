Meningococcal disease by age, 2010-2019
The figure shows incidence rates (per 100,000 persons) of meningococcal disease by age group from 2010 to 2019. Infants, young adults, and adults >80 years of age have the highest rates of meningococcal disease in the United States.
Reproduced from: Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. Meningococcal Disease: Surveillance. Available at: https://www.cdc.gov/meningococcal/surveillance/index.html. (Accessed on November 3, 2021.)
