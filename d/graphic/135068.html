Full-length blood donor history questionnaire
Are you
  1. Feeling healthy and well today?
  2. Currently taking an antibiotic?
  3. Currently taking any other medication for an infection?
  4. Pregnant now?
Have you
  1. Taken any medications on the Medication Deferral List in the time frames indicated? (Review the Medication Deferral List.)
  2. Read the blood donor educational materials today?
In the past 48 hours, have you
  1. Taken aspirin or anything that has aspirin in it?
In the past 8 weeks, have you
  1. Donated blood, platelets, or plasma?
  2. Had any vaccinations or other shots?
  3. Had contact with someone who was vaccinated for smallpox in the past 8 weeks?
In the past 3 months, have you
  1. Taken any medication by mouth (oral) to prevent HIV infection? (ie, PrEP or PEP)
  2. Had sexual contact with a new partner? (Refer to the examples of "new partner" in the Blood Donor Educational Material)
  3. Had sexual contact with more than one partner?
  4. Had sexual contact with anyone who has ever had a positive test for HIV infection?
  5. Received money, drugs, or other payment for sex?
  6. Had sexual contact with anyone who has, in the past 3 months, received money, drugs, or other payment for sex?
  7. Used needles to inject drugs, steroids, or anything not prescribed by your doctor?
  8. Had sexual contact with anyone who has used needles in the past 3 months to inject drugs, steroids, or anything not prescribed by their doctor?
  9. Had syphilis or gonorrhea or been treated for syphilis or gonorrhea?
  10. Had sexual contact with a person who has hepatitis?
  11. Lived with a person who has hepatitis?
  12. Had an accidental needle-stick?
  13. Come into contact with someone else's blood?
  14. Had a tattoo?
  15. Had ear or body piercing?
  16. Had a blood transfusion?
  17. Had a transplant such as organ, tissue, or bone marrow?
  18. Had a graft such as bone or skin?
In the past 16 weeks, have you
  1. Donated a double unit of red blood cells using an apheresis machine?
In the past 12 months, have you
  1. Been in juvenile detention, lockup, jail, or prison for 72 hours or more consecutively?
In the past 2 years, have you
  1. Received any medication by injection to prevent HIV infection? (ie, long-acting antiviral PrEP or PEP)
In the past 3 years, have you
  1. Been outside the United States or Canada?
Have you EVER
  1. Had a positive test for HIV infection?
  2. Taken any medication to treat HIV infection?
  3. Been pregnant?
  4. Had malaria?
  5. Received a dura mater (or brain covering) graft or xenotransplantation product?
  6. Had any type of cancer, including leukemia?
  7. Had any problems with your heart or lungs?
  8. Had a bleeding condition or blood disease?
  9. Had a positive test result for Babesia?

Each question is intended to be answered yes or no. Additional questions can be added by the blood collection facility staff. Educational materials and a medication deferral list are also provided.
