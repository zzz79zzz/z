Epidural catheter tips
A variety of epidural catheters are available, including those with multiple holes and a closed end (A) and those with a single end hole (B). Wire reinforced catheters are available with a single end hole (C), and with multiple holes and a closed end. For further information, refer to UpToDate content on epidural anesthesia technique.
