Multiple, disseminated, hyperpigmented macules 2 to 5 mm in diameter in an adolescent girl with LEOPARD syndrome (lentigines, EKG [electrocardiogram] abnormalities, ocular hypertelorism, pulmonic stenosis, abnormal genitalia, retardation of growth, deafness).
