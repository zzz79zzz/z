Counting carbohydrates
To figure out the "carb count" in 1 serving, start with the number of grams of total carbohydrates (46 grams), then subtract the number of grams of dietary fiber (7 grams). It's also important to look at the serving size. In this example, the carb count is 39 grams. You can use this number when counting carbs for your insulin dose.
