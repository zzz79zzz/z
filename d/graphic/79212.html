Initiating vaginoscopy in a child
After carefully placing the irrigating endoscope approximately 3 to 4 cm into the vagina, the vulvar tissues are gently pressed against the endoscope, trapping the irrigating fluid in the vagina. At this point, the endoscope is withdrawn to approximately 1 cm from the introitus for full visualization of the vaginal canal.
