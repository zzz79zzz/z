Incidence of colorectal cancer according to age and family history
Graphic representation of the cumulative incidence of colorectal cancer demonstrates that in people with a family history, colorectal cancers occur earlier in life, not uncommonly in the 40s or even the 30s.
Data from Fuchs, CS, Giovannucci, EL, Colditz, GA, et al, N Engl J Med 1994; 331:1669.
