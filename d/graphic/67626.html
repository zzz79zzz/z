Mobile aortic dissection flap on transesophageal echocardiography
The transesophageal echocardiogram shows a large dissection of the aorta. The intimal flap (arrow) is flail, moving widely within the aorta and spontaneous echo contrast can be seen within the false lumen, suggesting turbulent blood flow.
