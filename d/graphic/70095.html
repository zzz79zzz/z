Bilateral fetal pleural effusions
Longitudinal ultrasound image of the fetal chest and abdomen showing bilateral pleural effusions and ascites (arrows). Note the diaphragm between the two arrows at the top of the image. The fetal lung (arrowheads) has a characteristic wedge shape within the thoracic cavity.
