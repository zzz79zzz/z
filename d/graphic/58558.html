Relationship of hypothalamic set-point to body temperature
Euthermia: Hypothalamic set-point is normal, and body temperature approximates set-point and is also normal.
Fever: Hypothalamic set-point is elevated, and body temperature follows set-point and is also elevated.
Heat illness: Hypothalamic set-point is normal, and body temperature is elevated despite normal set-point.
