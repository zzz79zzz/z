Lacunar brain infarcts
Axial FLAIR magnetic resonance imaging demonstrating lacunar infarcts (arrows). Lacunes are defined radiologically as round or ovoid, subcortical, fluid-filled cavities (signal similar to cerebrospinal fluid) of between 3 and approximately 15 mm in diameter.
FLAIR: fluid-attenuated inversion recovery.
