MRI of normal medial collateral ligament
The normal MCL appears as a linear low signal "strap" on all sequences, extending from the medial epicondyle of the femur to a point on the tibia approximately 7 cm below the tibial plateau.
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging; MCL: medial collateral ligament.
