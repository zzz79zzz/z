Mitral valve prolapse
Four chamber view from a 2-D echocardiogram shows mild prolapse of both anterior and posterior leaflets of the mitral valve. The leaflets are thickened and show redundancy. The left atrium and ventricle are slightly dilated, suggesting the presence of mitral regurgitation.
