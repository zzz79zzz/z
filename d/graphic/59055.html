12-lead electrocardiogram (ECG) of a right posteroseptal accessory AV pathway
The 12 lead ECG during rapid atrial pacing shows QRS complexes that are maximally preexcited; the complexes have a pattern suggesting an AV accessory pathway located in the right posteroseptal area. At the time of ablation, the pathway was localized to the midseptal region.
