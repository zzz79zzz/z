Comparison of survival on dialysis between countries and between patients with and without diabetes
5-year patient survival rates after the institution of maintenance hemodialysis in the United States (from the USRDS), Europe (from the EDTA), Japan, and Tassin, France. Survival was lowest in the United States and highest in patients without diabetes and in Tassin, where the patients were more intensively dialyzed.
USRDS: United States Renal Data System; EDTA: European Dialysis and Transplant Association.
Data from:
  1. Hull AR, Parker TF III. Proceedings from the Morbidity, Mortality and Prescription of Dialysis Symposium, Dallas, TX, September 15 to 17, 1989. Am J Kidney Dis 1990; 15:375.
  2. Charra B, Calemard E, Ruffet M, et al. Survival as an index of adequacy of dialysis. Kidney Int 1992; 41:1286.
