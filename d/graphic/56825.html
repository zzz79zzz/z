Rotational alignment of the lower extremity
Rotational alignment of the lower extremity is determined by the position of the foot, the rotation of the tibia in relation to the transcondylar axis of the femur (tibial torsion), and the rotation of the neck of the femur in relation to the transcondylar axis of the femur (femoral anteversion).
