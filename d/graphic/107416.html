Hysterosalpingogram of normal tubal micro-insert (Essure)
Hysterosalpingogram of a woman three months post-fallopian tube micro-insert (Essure) placement for contraception. The uterine cavity is filled adequately, indicated by corneal distention (asterisks). The Essure inserts are appropriately placed in the fallopian tubes with four radio-opaque markers (arrows) allowing for assessment of correct device location.
