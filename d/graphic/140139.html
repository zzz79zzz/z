Midshaft clavicle fracture triage and referral
* Referral to orthopedic surgery is also reasonable if the clinician is not experienced in basic fracture management or other factors are involved. Examples of such factors include involvement of the dominant arm in an overhead athlete, a heavy laborer who needs to return to work as soon as possible, or a patient who expresses concern about the appearance of the shoulder.
