Little brown mushrooms or backyard mushrooms: Lepiota naucina
One of the many types of "little brown mushrooms" found growing in backyards. Lepiota naucina are edible. These mushrooms are sometimes confused with Amanita virosa (and other Amanita species) resulting in severe toxicity.
