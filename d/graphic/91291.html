Echocardiography of normal arrangement (situs solitus) of abdominal organs and great vessels
Transthoracic echocardiogram showing abdominal situs solitus (normal arrangement of organs and great vessels) with a left-sided stomach and right-sided liver. The normal position of the inferior vena cava (IVC) and descending aorta (DAo) relative to the spine is also shown. The hepatic veins connect to the IVC, which then joins the right atrium. The IVC is located to the right of the spine, and the aorta travels in the paravertebral gutter, to the left of the spine.
