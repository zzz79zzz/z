Myocardial perfusion SPECT - 201Thallium
Myocardial perfusion scan with Thallium-201 from a 54-year-old male, asymptomatic patient with chronic Chagas' heart disease. A large reversible perfusion defect is present in the inferior, inferior-septal, and apical regions. The epicardial coronary arteries were normal.
SPECT: single-photon emission computed tomography.
