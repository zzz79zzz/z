Histologic features of AML
(A) Classic AML containing adipose tissue, abnormal blood vessels with thickened walls, and spindle smooth muscle-like cells.
(B) Epithelioid AML containing round-to-polygonal epithelioid cells with round nucleoli and abundant granular cytoplasm.
AML: angiomyolipoma.
