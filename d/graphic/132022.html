Age incidences of benign breast diseases in KARMA
Incidence rates (events per 100,000 person-years) were estimated for the benign breast diseases in the Karolinska Mammography Project for Risk Prediction of Breast Cancer (KARMA) cohort between the ages of 25 and 69 years.
BBD: benign breast disease.
From: Johansson A, Christakou AE, Iftimi A, et al. Characterization of benign breast diseases and association with age, hormonal factors, and family history of breast cancer among women in Sweden.
