Hemorrhage into collecting system post kidney biopsy on CT
A CT scan following a kidney biopsy (A) shows high density blood in the perinephric space (arrow) and in the collecting system (arrowhead). Image B is a coronal reconstruction and shows high-density blood in the renal pelvis (arrowhead) and perinephric space (arrow) with a large amount of layering in the bladder (asterisk).
CT: computed tomography.
