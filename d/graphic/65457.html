Nomenclature for acquired melanocytic nevi
The names applied to acquired nevi reflect the location of the nests of melanocytes (asterisks above). In junctional nevi, nests are at the dermal-epidermal junction (DEJ); in dermal nevi, nests are in the dermis, and in compound nevi, nests are at the dermal-epidermal junction and in the dermis.
