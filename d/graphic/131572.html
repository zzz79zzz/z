Refer to content on endoscopic ultrasound of the upper gastrointestinal tract.
During EUS scanning, the tip of the echoendoscope with ultrasound transducer is guided through the gastrointestinal lumen and is placed at the site directly adjacent to the area of interest. Anatomic structures are described based on location of echoendoscope tip.
