Leukocyte infiltration in COPD
Photomicrograph showing leukocyte infiltration in a small airway of a smoker with severe COPD (A); and that of smoker with a mild COPD (B). Immunostaining with monoclonal antibody anti-CD45. Leukocytes are stained in red. Original magnification: X400.
