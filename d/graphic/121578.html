2004 HLH criteria[1] 2004 MAS criteria[2] 2016 MAS classification criteria[3]
Diagnostic requirements
  • Requires >3 criteria
  • Requires 2 laboratory or >3 clinical and/or laboratory criteria in addition to fever in a patient with systemic JIA
  • Requires ferritin criterion and any other 2 criteria in addition to fever in a patient with systemic JIA

  • Platelet count <181,000/microL
Miscellaneous laboratory findings
  • Triglycerides >265 mg/dL
  • OR
  • Fibrinogen <1.5 g/L
  • AST >59 units/L
  • AST >48 units/L,
  • Fibrinogen <360 mg/dL, OR
  • Triglycerides >156 mg/dL
