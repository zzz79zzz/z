Level 1 and level 2 frosting during a chemical peel
Level 1 frost: A light, nonorganized, nonhomogeneous, white frost with associated erythema.
Level 2 frost: A solid, organized, more homogeneous, white frost with associated erythema.
The blue-green color is from a dye in the chemical peel solution.
