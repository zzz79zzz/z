Poor vitamin D absorption in Crohn disease
The absorption of both CC and 25-HCC are diminished in patients with Crohn disease who have had intestinal resection compared with normal (control) subjects.
25-HCC: 25-hydroxycholicalciferol; CC: cholecalciferol.
Data from: Leichtmann GA, Bengoa JM, Bolt MJG, Sitrin, MD. Intestinal absorption of cholecalciferol and 25-hydroxycholecalciferol in patients with both Crohn's disease and intestinal resection. Am J Clin Nutr 1991; 54:548.
