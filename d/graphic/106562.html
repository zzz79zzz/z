Slipped capital femoral epiphysis: Frog-leg lateral radiograph
This frog-leg lateral radiograph of right slipped capital femoral epiphysis demonstrates widening and irregularity of the capital femoral physis (arrow), osteopenia, and increased density of the metaphysis, as well as posterior displacement and step-off of the epiphysis on the femoral neck (arrowhead).
