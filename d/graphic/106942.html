US proximal hamstring and conjoint tendon in transverse view
This ultrasound scan shows the proximal hamstring tendon complex just distal to the ischial tuberosity origin in the transverse plane (short-axis view).
Arrows: semimembranosus tendon; arrowheads: conjoint tendon; dashed ellipse: sciatic nerve.
Left side of image: lateral; right side of image: medial.
GMax: gluteus maximus; ST: semitendinosus muscle.
