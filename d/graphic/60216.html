Serum markers of acute, self-limiting HBV/HDV coinfection
Schematic representation of the typical serologic response to acute HBV/HDV infection. Patients should be positive for HBsAg and have high titer IgM anti-HBc; serum HDAg and/or HDV RNA are usually positive at presentation.
