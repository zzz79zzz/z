Asthma Control Test®
For information on the interpretation and scoring of the Asthma Control Test® (ACT®)*, visit https://www.asthmacontroltest.com.
* The ACT is a validated instrument for assessment of asthma control. The scores range from 5 (poor control of asthma) to 25 (complete control of asthma), with higher scores reflecting greater asthma control. An ACT score >19 indicates well-controlled asthma.
