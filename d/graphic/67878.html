Epiglottitis: Lateral radiograph
Lateral neck radiograph demonstrating swollen epiglottis (arrow) and aryepiglottic folds (asterisks) in a child with epiglottitis due to Haemophilus influenzae type b. The swollen epiglottis is often called a "thumb sign."
