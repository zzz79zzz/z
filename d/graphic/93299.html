Nonalcoholic steatohepatitis
The photo shows mild steatosis and Mallory-Denk hyalines (arrows). Compared with alcoholic steatohepatitis, in patients with nonalcoholic steatohepatitis there are often fewer Mallory-Denk hyalines, and these are not as fully developed. In addition to steatosis and Mallory-Denk hyalines, some inflammatory cells may also be seen in the lobules (arrowheads).
