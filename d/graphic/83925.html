Mechanisms for urate secretion by the proximal tubule
Urate enters the cell at the basolateral membrane via exchange with alpha-ketoglutarate, mediated by OAT1 and OAT3. At the apical membrane, urate is secreted via MRP4, ABCG2, NPT1, and/or NPT4.
