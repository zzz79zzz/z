Possible locations of undescended and ectopic testes
Undescended testes may be located in the abdomen (1), inguinal canal (2), and suprascrotal (prepubic) regions (3). Ectopic testes may be located in the suprapubic (penile) region (4), femoral region (5), perineal region (6), and contralateral hemiscrotum (7).
