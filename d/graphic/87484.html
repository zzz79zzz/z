Uncombable hair syndrome
Spun, glass-like, silvery-blonde, unruly hair is characteristic of uncombable hair syndrome. Pili trianguli et canaliculi is visible on electron microscopy of the hair shaft. Uncombable hair syndrome does not cause fragile hair.
