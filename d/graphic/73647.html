Left atrial and pulmonary capillary wedge pressures in mitral regurgitation
Pressure tracings in mitral regurgitation. Although the mean pulmonary capillary wedge (PCW) and left atrial (LA) pressures are equivalent, a damped PCW pressure tracing obscures the waveforms, and the tall "v" wave of mitral regurgitation, seen on the LA tracing, is absent on the PCW tracing.
Redrawn from Kern MJ (Ed). Hemodynamic Rounds: Interpretation of Cardiac Pathophysiology from Pressure Waveform Analysis, Wiley-Liss, New York, 1993.
