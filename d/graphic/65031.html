Pendulum (Codman's) exercise
Bending over with the affected arm hanging down, the patient swings the arm from the shoulder in 6 to 12 inch circles. A three to five pound weight on the wrist or in the hand provides additional traction and momentum to the swing.
Reproduced with permission from Sheon, RP, Moskowitz, RW, Goldberg, VM. Soft Tissue Rheumatic Pain: Recognition, Management, Prevention, 3rd ed, Williams & Wilkins, Baltimore 1996.
