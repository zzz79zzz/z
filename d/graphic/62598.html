Localized granuloma annulare
A 65-year-old presented with a 14-month history of annular lesions of granuloma annulare on the backs of each hand and left dorsum foot. The periphery of the lesion on the right hand demonstrates papules in some foci and a rope-like rim in other areas. The central area of clearing is hypopigmented.
