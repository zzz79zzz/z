Blood pressure values for extremely preterm infants (gestational age ≤28 weeks) for the first 72 hours of life
Population estimate of blood pressure (BP) values for extremely preterm infants (gestational age ≤28 weeks) by postnatal age in hours. Dashed line represents the blood pressure estimate while solid line represents the boundaries of the 95% confidence interval. Orange: systolic BP; blue: mean arterial BP; green: diastolic BP.
