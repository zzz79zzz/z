Examples of lateral pulsed-tissue Doppler mitral annular velocities used to differentiate constriction from restriction. Patient A has constrictive pericarditis and elevated early diastolic velocities, and Patient B has restrictive cardiomyopathy from amyloidosis and depressed early diastolic velocities.
