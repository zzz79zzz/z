Bilateral vestibular schwannomas in a patient with NF2-related schwannomatosis
Axial postcontrast T1-weighted MRI showing bilateral vestibular schwannomas (arrows) in a male with NF2-related schwannomatosis at age 10 years (A) and age 17 years (B). There has been enlargement of both tumors over the 7-year interval.
NF2: NF2, moesin-ezrin-radixin like (MERLIN) tumor suppressor; MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
