Treatment of acute sarcoid arthropathy

NSAIDs: nonsteroidal antiinflammatory drugs.

* Refer to UpToDate topic on sarcoid arthropathy for details of drug choice, dosing, and duration of therapy.

¶ A response to treatment is characterized by control of symptoms and signs of joint inflammation, with resolution of joint tenderness, swelling, and pain.
