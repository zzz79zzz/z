Ultrasound measurement of transverse fetal cerebellar diameter
Gray-scale fetal ultrasound through the calvarium shows a transverse measurement of the cerebellum of 2.01 cm, which corresponds to an estimated gestational age of 19 weeks and 2 days, concordant with the gestational age based on the last menstrual period.
Cereb: cerebellum, GA: gestational age, w: weeks, d: days, CM: cisterna magna, NF: nuchal fold.
