Copenhagen hip adduction exercises
The Copenhagen hip adductor exercises shown above reduce the risk of adductor strain. As the adductors become stronger, athletes progress from the basic exercise (3A-B) to intermediate (2A-B) and more advanced (1A-B) versions. For each exercise shown, A is the starting/ending position and B is the mid-position.
Reproduced with permission from: Harøy J, Clarsen B, Guldahl Wiger E, et al. The Adductor Strengthening Programme prevents groin problems among male football players: a cluster-randomised controlled trial. Br J Sports Med 2019; 53(3):150-157. Copyright © 2019 BMJ Publishing Group Ltd.
