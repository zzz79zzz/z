Right ventricular enlargement increases mortality in dilated cardiomyopathy
Among patients with an idiopathic dilated cardiomyopathy and left ventricular dysfunction, survival was much lower in those who also had right ventricular enlargement (p = 0.001).
Data from Sun JP, James KB, Yang XS, et al. Am J Cardiol 1997; 80:1583.
