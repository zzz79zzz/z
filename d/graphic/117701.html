Minimum alveolar concentration (MAC) chart for sevoflurane
Iso-MAC chart for sevoflurane (age ≥1 year). The vertical shifts for the nitrous oxide 50 and 67% scales are 0.86 and 1.16, respectively.
MAC: minimum alveolar concentration; N2O: nitrous oxide.
Originally published in: Nickalls RW, Mapleson WW. Age-related iso-MAC charts for isoflurane, sevoflurane and desflurane in man. Br J Anaesth 2003; 91:170. Reproduced with permission from Dick Nickalls, MBBS, BSc, PhD, FRCA. Copyright © 2003 RWD Nickalls.
