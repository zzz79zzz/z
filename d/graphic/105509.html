Laser therapy for hypertrophic burn scars
Each laser treatment consists of two to four sessions each, spaced four to six weeks apart. Platforms are chosen based upon scar characteristics. The end points are determined by the patient and the provider. Contractures may require release and reconstruction. Hydroquinone and tretinoin can be used for improvement of color and texture. Consider fat grafting for refractory symptoms of pain and itching.
OT: occupational therapy; PT: physical therapy; fCO2: fractional carbon dioxide.
Yoshida & Hultman, University of North Carolina, 2015.
