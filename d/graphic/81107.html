Mechanism of pain referral from the cervical spine to the head
Nociceptive afferents of the trigeminal and upper three cervical spinal nerves converge onto second-order neurons in the trigeminocervical nucleus in the upper cervical spinal cord. This convergence mediates the referral of pain signals from the neck to regions of the head innervated by cervical nerves or the trigeminal nerve.
