Adenoid cystic carcinoma
Left panel shows a low power representation of a well-differentiated adenoid cystic carcinoma. The cribriform spaces are filled with blue mucinous or pink hyalin material (black arrow). The right panel is a higher power view demonstrating bone invasion. Perineural invasion is also typical.
