Lateral elbow tendinopathy injection
The photograph above shows an injection of the lateral elbow joint. For short-term relief of severe symptoms associated with elbow tendinopathy, glucocorticoid injection may be a reasonable treatment option when used as part of a comprehensive management program including physical therapy.
