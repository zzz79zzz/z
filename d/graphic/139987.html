Multiple spinal schwannomas in a patient with NF2-related schwannomatosis
Sagittal postcontrast T1-weighted MRI of the spine showing intra-axial, intradural enhancing masses (arrows) at multiple thoracic, lumbar, and sacral levels, consistent with spinal schwannomas in a patient with NF2-related schwannomatosis.
NF2: NF2, moesin-ezrin-radixin like (MERLIN) tumor suppressor; MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
