Doppler interrogation of LA appendage flow during atrial fibrillation and atrial flutter
The pulsed-Doppler tracing of LAA flow in atrial fibrillation is seen in panel A. Note the rapid fibrillatory flow waves, which are of higher velocity during ventricular diastole than systole (arrows). Pulsed-Doppler tracing of LAA flow in atrial flutter (with a 2:1 ventricular response) is seen in panel B. Flutter flow waves are, in general, slower and of higher velocity than fibrillatory flow waves.
