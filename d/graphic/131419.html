  • Sulfasalazine
 3 weeks[5]

Antiseizure medications
  • Phenytoin
 1 to 8 weeks[9-11]
  • Carbamazepine
 2 days to 3 weeks[9-11]

  • Quinidine
 2 weeks to 6 months[12-14]
  • Methyldopa
 11 days to 3 weeks[15]
Chemotherapeutic agents (eg, gemcitabine) 3 days[16]
Hydroxyurea 1 day to 6 weeks[17,18]
