Atrophy of supra- and infraspinatus muscles
The photograph above is of a 55-year-old woman with chronic suprascapular neuropathy. This image was taken 5 years after electromyography confirmed entrapment of the nerve at the transverse scapular ligament, which was treated nonoperatively. Note the marked atrophy of the right infraspinatus and supraspinatus muscles, accentuated by the prominence of the right scapula.
From: Bragg B, Cheung E, Safran MR. Diagnosis and management of neurovascular disorders about the shoulder in the athlete. In: Disorders of the Shoulder: Sports Injuries, 3rd ed, Miniaci A (Ed), Wolters Kluwer 2013. Copyright © 2013. Reproduced with permission from Wolters Kluwer Health.
