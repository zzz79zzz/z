Mean amniotic fluid volume across normal pregnancy
These values represent the 50th percentile. There is considerable variability around the mean. The 5th, 50th, and 95th percentiles at 30 weeks of gestation are approximately 260, 700, and 1900 mL, respectively.
Data from: Ounpraseuth ST, Magann EF, Spencer HJ, Rabie NZ, Sandlin AT Normal amniotic fluid volume across gestation: Comparison of statistical approaches in 1190 normal amniotic fluid volumes. J Obstet Gynaecol Res; 2017: 43:1122.
