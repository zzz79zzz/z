Pediatric cardiac arrest algorithm 2020 update

CPR: cardiopulmonary resuscitation; VF: ventricular fibrillation; pVT: pulseless ventricular tachycardia; PEA: pulseless electrical activity; IV: intravenous; IO: intraosseous; ROSC: return of spontaneous circulation; ET: endotracheal.

* If ROSC, go to post-cardiac arrest care checklist. The post-cardiac arrest care checklist can be found here.
