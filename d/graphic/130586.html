Two-dimensional TTE (A) and color-flow imaging (B) from a patient with a muscular VSD (arrow) demonstrated in the apex down format. Note the left-to-right shunt demonstrated on the systolic TTE image (B).
VSD: ventricular septal defect; TTE: transthoracic echocardiogram; RA: right atrium; RV: right ventricle; LV: left ventricle.
