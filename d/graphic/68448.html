Aneurysmal bone cyst periosteal reaction
Distal ulna – Note the large, expansile aneurysmal bone cyst affecting the distal portion of the ulna. Periosteal new bone formation near the peripheral margins of the lesion creates a buttressing effect, which is characteristic of aneurysmal bone cyst (arrows).
