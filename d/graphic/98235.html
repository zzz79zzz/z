Histologic features of spiradenoma
Spiradenomas lie on a spectrum with cylindromas but are composed of larger, nodular aggregations of basaloid keratinocytes instead of small ones. Prominent deposition of basement membrane material and duct formation is present in both spiradenoma and cylindroma (H&E 40x).
H&E: hematoxylin and eosin.
