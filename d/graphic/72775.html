Right ventricular pressure tracing
This schematic diagram shows the different components of the right ventricular pressure tracing. A simultaneous ECG is shown to demonstrate the timing of the different components. Right ventricular end diastolic pressure is measured after the a wave, just prior to the systolic upstroke.
Redrawn from Gore, JM, Alpert, JS, Benotti, JR, et al. Handbook of hemodynamic monitoring, 1st ed, Boston, Little Brown & Co, 1985.
