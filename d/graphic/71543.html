Crohn disease
Barium radiograph demonstrating Crohn disease. Note cobblestoning of the terminal ileum (arrow), narrow cecum, and separation of bowel loops. Cobblestoning should be distinguished from lymphonodularity of the terminal ileum, which is a normal finding in young children.
