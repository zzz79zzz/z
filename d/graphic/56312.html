Ultrasound of filarial dance
Transverse scan of left testis. In para-testicular position an enlarged lymphatic vessel can be seen (arrow). One or more adult worms are detectable by their typical movements (filaria dance sign). The following m-mode imaging presents the dilated lymphatic vessels, containing moving adult worms, as a section marked by the cursor in the b-mode image.
