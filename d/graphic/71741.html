Retained surgical sponge on magnetic resonance imaging
Paravertebral gossypiboma in patient with lumbar pain, fever, and palpable mass. Patient had undergone prior laminectomy. Coronal T1-weighted image after IV injection of contrast material shows strong enhancement of peripheral rim (arrows).
IV: intravenous.
