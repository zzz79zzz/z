Finger digital block
To perform a finger web space block, insert the needle into the subcutaneous tissue of the web space at the base of the finger, just distal to the MCP (metacarpal/phalangeal) joint. Slowly advance the needle straight through the web space toward the palmar surface, injecting as the needle is advanced. The black ink line illustrates the likely course of the dorsal digital nerve.
