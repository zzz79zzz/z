Estimate of survival according to absence or presence of significant sleep apnea among peritoneal dialysis patients
This figure shows a Kaplan-Meier estimate of survival among patients with and without significant sleep apnea (defined as AHI ≥15/hour or <15/hour, respectively). p = 0.015.
AHI: Apnea-Hypopnea Index.
