Contrast-enhanced thoracic MRI scans in a 75-year-old female with progressive paraparesis due to a midthoracic spinal meningioma.

(Left panel) The sagittal study reveals a dural-based lesion at T7-8.

(Right panel) The axial image shows the ventrally located lesion (arrow) causing spinal cord compression.
