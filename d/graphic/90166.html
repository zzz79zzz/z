Urticaria - Multiple images
Multiple images of urticaria are shown. Urticarial lesions may be rounded (F) or irregular (A) in shape and uniform or mixed (G) in morphology. Individual lesions may be small (H) or large (D). Urticaria are raised plaques, although they may appear flattened in patients taking antihistamines (C). Although lesions are consistently erythematous, this may be difficult to appreciate on darkly pigmented skin (I). Some urticaria show central clearing (E).
