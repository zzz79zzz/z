Parastomal hernia on CT
An axial CT scan through the lower abdomen (A) shows a loop of small bowel herniating through a colostomy defect (arrow). Contrast is seen in the colostomy bag (arrowhead). Image B is a coronal reconstruction and shows the herniated small bowel in the subcutaneous tissues (arrow).
CT: computed tomography.
