Schematic diagram showing no-heparin regimen in hemodialysis
Schematic representation of no-heparin regimen for anticoagulation during hemodialysis. Boluses of isotonic saline are given every hour to minimize the degree of hemoconcentration and to flush fibrin strands from the dialyzer into the bubble trap.
