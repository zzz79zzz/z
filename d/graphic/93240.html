Melanoma metastatic to liver by CT and angiography
An axial CT scan through the liver (A) shows diffuse homogeneous enhancement (arrow) and ring enhancement patterns of metastatic melanoma. Image B shows arterial enhancement of liver metastases (arrows). The lesions are better appreciated on delayed imaging (C arrows).
CT: computed tomography.
