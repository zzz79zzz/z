Overall survival stratified by pathologic stage group for jejunoileal neuroendocrine tumors
Kaplan-Meier survival curves for each 8th edition pathological stage for small bowel primary neuroendocrine tumors, with a follow-up time of three years (2010 to 2013) and adjusted for patient age. A total of 3366 patients were included in the pathological stage survival calculation. Data from the National Cancer Data Base.
