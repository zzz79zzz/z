Low-power photomicrograph of a hematoxylin and eosin-stained section of Paget disease of the nipple. The tumor cells, which are dispersed singly and in clusters within the epidermis, are characterized by abundant pale cytoplasm and large nuclei with variably prominent nucleoli.
