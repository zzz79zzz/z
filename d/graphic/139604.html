Adult difficult IV access (A-DIVA) score
Risk factor Points
Known history of difficult IV access 1
Do you expect a failed first attempt or difficult IV access? 1
Unable to identify a dilated vein by palpating the upper extremity* 1
Unable to identify a vein by visualizing the upper extremity* 1
Does the largest dilated vein have a diameter <3 mm?* 1
A-DIVA score (total points) Failed first attempt rate
0 to 1 4%
2 to 3 37%
4 to 5 94%
The A-DIVA score is the total number of 'yes' answers.

IV: intravenous.

* After tourniquet placement.
Adapted from: van Loon FHJ, van Hooff LWE, de Boer HD, et al. The modified A-DIVA scale as a predictive tool for prospective identification of adult patients at risk of a difficult intravenous access: A multicenter validation study. J Clin Med 2019; 8:144.
