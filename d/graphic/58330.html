High-grade prostatic intraepithelial neoplasia
Low-magnification (left panel) and high-magnification (right panel) photomicrographs of high-grade PIN. The low magnification view demonstrates the presence of pseudostratified neoplastic epithelial cells in benign-looking large glands. In the high-magnification view, the presence of prominent nuclear atypia (arrows) can be seen.
PIN: prostatic intraepithelial neoplasia.
