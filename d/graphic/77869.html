Knee chondrocalcinosis in hemochromatosis
Serial knee radiographs, separated by 11 years, from a patient with hereditary hemochromatosis. During this time there has been progressive chondrocalcinosis without the development of either joint space narrowing or secondary degenerative arthritis.
