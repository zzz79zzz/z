Sacral insufficiency fracture. Pelvic MR axial T1-weighted (A) and T2-weighted (B) images of the pelvis demonstrates the sacroiliac joints and an insufficiency fracture (arrow) through the right sacral ala seen as dark T1 and bright T2 linear signal.
