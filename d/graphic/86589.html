"I" exercise for scapular stabilizers
"I" exercises begin with the involved arm hanging straight down from the shoulder. Keeping the elbows straight, extend your shoulders until the arms are pointing straight down and are adjacent to your torso. Hold this position for one second, and then slowly return the arms to the starting position.
