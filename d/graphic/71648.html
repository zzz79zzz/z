Disease-related and non-Hodgkin mortality in Hodgkin lymphoma
Actuarial risk of death from Hodgkin lymphoma or other causes in 2498 patients treated for Hodgkin lymphoma at Stanford University from 1960 to 1995. Most of the early mortality is due to Hodgkin lymphoma, while almost all deaths after 10 to 15 years were due to other causes, mostly second malignancy and cardiovascular and pulmonary disease.
Modified from: Hoppe RT. Hodgkin's disease: complications of therapy and excess mortality. Ann Oncol 1997; 8(Suppl 1):S115.
