Diminished anti-GBM antibody binding in Alport syndrome
Indirect immunofluorescence microscopy with anti-glomerular basement membrane antibodies shows extensive binding to a normal glomerulus (A) but minimal binding to a glomerulus from a patient with Alport syndrome (B).
GBM: glomerular basement membrane.
