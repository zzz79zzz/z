Embryology of atrioventricular (AV) canal defects
Diagram of the embryologic development of the AV canal region and the spectrum of AVSD, including partial, transitional, complete, and intermediate forms. Types A, B, and C refer to the Rastelli classification based on the anatomy of the superior bridging leaflet (please refer to the UpToDate topic review on the clinical manifestations of AV canal defects).
AV: atrioventricular; AVSD: atrioventricular septal defect; A: anterior leaflet; P: posterior leaflet; S: septal leaflet; VSD: ventricular septal defect; LEC: lateral endocardial cushion; SEC: superior endocardial cushion; IEC: inferior endocardial cushion; DDCC: dextrodorsal conus cushion; L: lateral leaflet; AB: anterior bridging leaflet; PB: posterior bridging leaflet.
