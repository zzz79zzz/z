Pre-transplantation recipient evaluation
Referral to transplant center:
  • Analysis of medical and surgical history, current medications, current enteral and parenteral nutrition requirements
History and physical:
  • Comprehensive history and physical including review of systems
Laboratory studies:
  • Blood group
  • Tissue typing (HLA)
  • Panel of reactive antibody (PRA)
  • Serology (CMV IgG and IgM, EBV IgG and IgM, HIV, HCV, HBeAg, HBsAg, HBsAb)
  • CBC, comprehensive metabolic panel, PT/PTT/INR
Imaging studies:
  • Chest radiograph
  • Doppler US of liver
  • Magnetic resonance venography
  • Doppler US of upper and lower extremities veins
  • CT scan abdomen and pelvis
  • CT scan brain
  • Upper GI study, small bowel follow through
  • Barium enema
Endoscopic studies:
  • EGD
  • Colonoscopy
Motility studies:
  • Gastro-esophageal motility studies
  • Small intestine motility studies
  • Colon transit time studies
Liver:
  • Liver biopsy
  • Ultrasound-based elastography
Cardiology evaluation:
  • EKG
  • Baseline echocardiogram
  • Stress test and/or cardiac catheterization if >50 years or with cardiac history, risk factors (HTN, DM)
Nephrology evaluation:
  • US abdomen with size of kidneys
  • Triple renal scan
  • 24 hour creatinine clearance
Additional consults:
  • Neurology
  • Infectious disease
  • Critical care
  • Anesthesia
  • Pulmonary
Additional studies:
  • Dental evaluation
  • Mammogram
  • PAP smear test
  • Vaccinations:
    • Hepatitis A
    • Hepatitis B
    • Pneumovax
Multidisciplinary team evaluation:
  • Final decision by transplant physician, surgeon, gastroenterologist, nurse coordinator, nutritionist and social worker
CBC: complete blood count; CMV: cytomegalovirus; CT: computed tomography; DM: diabetes mellitus; EBV: Epstein-Barr virus; EGD: esophagogastroduodenoscopy; EKG: electrocardiogram; GI: gastrointestinal; HBeAg: hepatitis B e-antigen; HBsAb: hepatitis B surface antibody; HBsAG: hepatitis B surface antigen; HCV: hepatitis C virus; HIV: human immunodeficiency virus; HLA: human leukocyte antigen; HTN: hypertension; INR: international normalized ratio; PT: prothrombin time; PTT: partial thromboplastin time; US: ultrasound.
