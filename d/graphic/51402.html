Correlation of serum testosterone with severity of critical illness
The serum testosterone concentration on admission (left panel) and the nadir level in the hospital (right panel) in males with critical illness according to disease severity as measured by the APACHE score. Males with increasingly severe illnesses have lower serum testosterone concentrations. To convert serum testosterone values to ng/dL, divide by 0.0347.
APACHE: Acute Physiology and Chronic Health Evaluation.
Data from: Spratt DI, Cox P, Orav J, et al. Reproductive axis suppression in acute illness is related to disease severity. J Clin Endocrinol Metab 1993; 76:1548.
