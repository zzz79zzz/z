Two-dimensional subcostal transthoracic echocardiogram showing reduction in IVC size with inspiration
Subcostal view showing a decrease (normal) in IVC size with inspiration. This change occurs due to the temporary decrease in intrathoracic pressure associated with inspiration, resulting in augmented venous return to the right heart.
IVC: inferior vena cava; 2D TTE: two-dimensional transthoracic echocardiogram.
