Appendix A: Edinburgh Postnatal Depression Scale (EPDS) (continued)
We suggest a cutoff score of 11, which appears to maximize sensitivity plus specificity in screening for postpartum depression. Women who report depressive symptoms without suicidal ideation or major functional impairment (or score between 5 and 9 on the EPDS) should be re-evaluated within one month.
