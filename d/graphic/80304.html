Postinfectious glomerulonephritis
Low-power light micrograph showing diffuse, proliferative glomerulonephritis, as may be seen in postinfectious glomerulonephritis. The glomeruli are so hypercellular (arrows) that open capillary lumens cannot be seen, and the glomeruli may be hard to distinguish from the surrounding interstitium.
