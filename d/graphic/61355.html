Figure showing plasma C3a concentrations in patients on hemodialysis with different dialyzers
Venous plasma levels of C3a antigen during hemodialysis with four different types of dialyzers. Complement activation was greatest with cuprophan, intermediate with cellulose acetate, and minimal with cuprophan membranes reused for the third time or with polyacrylonitrile.
Data from: Chenoweth DE. Complement activation during hemodialysis: Clinical observations, proposed mechanisms, and theoretical implications. Artif Organs 1984; 8:281.
