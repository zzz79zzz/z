Abdominal computed tomography scan of gallstones
An abdominal computed tomography scan image with floating cholesterol stones layering in the center of the gallbladder. Note how pure cholesterol stones appear like black holes (arrows) within gallbladder bile due to the very low computed tomography density of cholesterol.
