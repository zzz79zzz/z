Excision of renal artery aneurysm and angioplasty repair
Following the longitudinal excision of a large saccular aneurysm (A) located at the primary branch point (B), primary angioplastic closure of the defect in a transverse (Heineke-Mikulicz) fashion (C) or patch angioplasty (D, E) may be considered.
