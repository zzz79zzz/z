Position of safety of the hand
The safe position shows the wrist extended 20 degrees, MCP joints flexed at 90 degrees, and the PIP and DIP joints fully extended.
MCP: metacarpophalangeal; PIP: proximal interphalangeal; DIP: distal interphalangeal.
