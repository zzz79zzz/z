Normal spirometry in a child with mild bronchiectasis
Spirometry in a 13-year-old child with mild bronchiectasis, depicting normal percent predicted values of FEV1 and FVC.
FEV1: forced expiratory volume in 1 second; FVC: forced vital capacity; FEV1% FVC: FEV1 as a percent of FVC; MFEF 75 to 25: maximal forced expiratory flow between 75 and 25% of FVC.
