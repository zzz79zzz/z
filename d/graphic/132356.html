Bedside ultrasound close-up view dilated IVC
This close-up ultrasound image shows a grossly dilated inferior vena cava (IVC). Possible causes include fluid overload, cardiac dysfunction (eg, acute decompensated heart failure), and obstruction (eg, pulmonary embolism, pericardial tamponade, pneumothorax).
