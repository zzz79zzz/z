Prevalence of benign prostatic hyperplasia pathology with age
Age-associated increase in pathologic evidence of benign prostatic hyperplasia in 1075 men at autopsy. The percentage with benign prostatic hyperplasia was determined during 10-year intervals from 5 different studies; the mean values are shown.
Data from Berry, SJ, Coffey, DS, Walsh, PC, et al. The development of human benign prostatic hyperplasia with age. J Urol 1984;132:474.
