Intra-abdominal hypertension and abdominal compartment syndrome
Intra-abdominal hypertension (IAH) is defined as a sustained intra-abdominal pressure >12 mmHg. Abdominal compartment syndrome (ACS) is defined as a sustained intra-abdominal pressure >20 mmHg that is associated with new organ dysfunction.
