Duodenal ulcer with visible vessel
Duodenal ulcer in a patient with recent upper gastrointestinal bleeding. The ulcer base (arrows) is visible as the whitish rim underlying the protruding vessel. The erythematous mound in the center of the ulcer represents an arteriole that has eroded into the lumen of the duodenum.
