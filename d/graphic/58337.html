Serum creatinine concentrations and heart transplantation
Graph showing the pooled weighted average serum creatinine concentrations (mg/dL) derived from published studies describing the kidney function in heart transplant recipients receiving cyclosporine based immunosuppressive therapy. In general, the serum creatinine level increases early after transplantation and then stabilizes over time.
