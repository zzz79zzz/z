Walking with crutches
To walk using crutches:
  1. Moving both crutches at the same time, place them both slightly in front of you.
  2. Use the crutches to support your weight and keep your balance. Move your "good" foot forward so it is even with the crutches. Do not swing your good leg past the crutches.
