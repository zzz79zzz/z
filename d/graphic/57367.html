Incidence of pulmonary embolism according to distance traveled by air
This figure indicates the incidence of pumonary embolism per million passenger arrivals, arranged according to flight distance in kilometers. Error bars indicate 95 percent confidence limits. To convert kilometers to miles, multiply by 0.62.
Data from Lapastolle, F, et al. N Engl J Med 2001; 345:779.
