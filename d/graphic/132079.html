Congenital onychodysplasia of the index finger (Iso-Kikuchi syndrome)
Micronychia in COIF syndrome, also known as Iso-Kikuchi syndrome. It is often associated with bifurcation of the underlying phalanx.
COIF: congenital onychodystrophy of the index finger.
