Technique for performing circumcision using a Plastibell
(A) An incision is made in the top of the foreskin.
(B) The Plastibell is placed over the head of the penis, and the foreskin is pulled over the Plastibell.
(C) A suture is tied around the foreskin over the tying groove in the Plastibell. Excess skin beyond the suture is trimmed away. The Plastibell falls off in three to seven days.
