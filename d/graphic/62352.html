Parasternal long axis echocardiogram showing aortic valve vegetations
The parasternal long axis view shows a bulky vegetation (veg) arising from the aortic (Ao) valve; the vegetation is large enough to prolapse into the left ventricular (LV) outflow tract. There is also a left atrial (LA) vegetation (arrows). Both the Ao and LA masses were shown to be bacterial vegetations.
RVOT: right ventricular outflow tract; MV: mitral valve.
