"Cutis marmorata" rash in a SCUBA diver
The rash pictured above, commonly referred to as "cutis marmorata," can develop in SCUBA divers. It is more correctly called livedo racemosa, an irregular, broken rash caused by thrombotic and embolic phenomena.
