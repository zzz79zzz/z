Involved-field radiation for a patient with stage II Hodgkin lymphoma
Patient with stage II Hodgkin lymphoma extensively involving the right neck and with a solitary node in the left neck. The gross tumor volume (PET + disease) is displayed in red; the clinical treated volume (involved lymph node regions) is displayed in green.
PET: positron emission tomography.
