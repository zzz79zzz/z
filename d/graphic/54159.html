Biliopancreatic diversion with duodenal switch
The biliopancreatic diversion with duodenal switch procedure involves a partial sleeve gastrectomy with preservation of the pylorus and creation of a Roux limb with a short common channel. This procedure has significant risks of long-term malabsorption and is used only for patients with very severe obesity (body mass index [BMI] >50 kg/m2).
