Hypovolemic stimulus to ADH release
Relationship of plasma ADH concentrations to isosmotic changes in blood volume in the rat. Much higher ADH levels can occur with hypovolemia than with hyperosmolality, although a relatively large fall in blood volume is required before this response is initiated.
ADH: antidiuretic hormone.
Data from: Dunn FL, Brennan TJ, Nelson AE, et al. J Clin Invest 1973; 52:3212.
