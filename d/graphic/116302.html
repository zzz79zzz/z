Rates of aneurysm and dissection
Out of 921 patients from the US Registry for Fibromuscular Dysplasia, 41.7% overall experienced an aneurysm and/or dissection, of whom 147 patients had an aneurysm, 184 patients had dissection, and 53 patients experienced both, as reported in the patient's medical history or as a presenting sign or symptom of fibromuscular dysplasia.
Reproduced from: Kadian-Dodov D, Gornik HL, Gu X, et al. Dissection and aneurysm in patients with fibromuscular dysplasia: Findings from the US Registry for FMD. J Am Coll Cardiol 2016; 68:176. Illustration used with the permission of Elsevier Inc. All rights reserved.
