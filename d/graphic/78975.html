Refractoriness to repeated exercise-induced bronchoconstriction
Exercise-induced bronchoconstriction (as measured by a fall in FEV1) is attenuated when a second exercise challenge is performed one hour after an initial challenge.
FEV1: forced expiratory volume in one second.
