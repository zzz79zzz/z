Sickled red blood cell by transmission electron micrography (TEM)
Transmission electron micrograph of a red blood cell from a patient with homozygous sickle cell disease. Strands of polymerized deoxygenated hemoglobin S running lengthwise through the cell are just discernible at this magnification.
