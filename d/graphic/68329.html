Interstitial brachytherapy
For interstitial brachytherapy several small hollow catheters are placed into the breast surrounding the partial mastectomy site. The number of catheters used is dependent on the size and shape of the target. Radioactive seeds are inserted into the catheters for a short time period of time each day and then removed. The catheters are removed after five days, when treatment is completed.
