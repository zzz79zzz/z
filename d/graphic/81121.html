Erythromelalgia involving the hands in essential thrombocythemia
This photograph shows the presence of erythromelalgia of the hands in a woman with essential thrombocythemia. This condition is associated with burning pain in the feet or hands accompanied by erythema, pallor, or cyanosis, in the presence of palpable pulses, and is common in both essential thrombocythemia and polycythemia vera.
