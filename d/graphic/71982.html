Plain chest radiograph of diaphragmatic injury with herniation
The plain chest radiograph shows a traumatic disruption of the left hemidiaphragm. The anteroposterior view (A) obtained on primary survey and lateral view (B) obtained in follow-up show gastrointestinal gas shadows occupying about two-thirds of the left hemithorax (arrowhead). Bowel gas can be seen adjacent the heart border (arrow).
