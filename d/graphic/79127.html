Variation in serum total testosterone concentrations
Serum total testosterone concentrations have a diurnal variation in young men (dashed line); concentrations are highest at 8 AM and lowest around 8 PM. In contrast, older men have little variation throughout the day (solid line). To convert serum testosterone values to nmol/L, multiply by 3.47.
Data from: Bremner WJ, Vitiello V, Prinz PN. Loss of circadian rhythmicity in blood testosterone levels with aging in normal men. J Clin Endocrinol Metab 1983; 56:1278.
