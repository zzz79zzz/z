Drawing by a patient with epileptic visual hallucinations
The patient had occipital seizures with this stereotyped hallucination for approximately 12 years without a diagnosis. He described a bright red circle that flickered with what looked like moving flames coming out of it. The hallucination typically lasted for seconds and was usually followed by subtle mental status changes, headache, and nausea.
