Shielding fetus from irradiation during surgery
During cholangiography, the fetus should be shielded by placing a lead apron on the lower abdomen of the patient. Because the biliary tree is in the upper abdomen, shielding the fetus in this way does not usually compromise the field of view necessary for proper imaging.
