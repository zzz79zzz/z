Weighing the benefits and harms when deciding about a preventive activity: Comparing estimated benefits and harms of screening mammography[1-3]
Chances among 1000 women who undergo annual screening mammography for 10 years: (A) of experiencing a false-positive mammogram, undergoing a breast biopsy, and developing breast cancer; and (B) of being cured of breast cancer regardless of screening, and averting death from breast cancer because of screening mammography.
