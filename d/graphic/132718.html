Hutchinson-Gilford progeria syndrome
(A) Ten-year-old male with Hutchinson-Gilford progeria syndrome exhibiting "double rows" of maxillary teeth.
(B, C) Hutchinson-Gilford progeria syndrome patients manifesting an ogival, palatal vault and a midline sagittal, palatal fissure.
