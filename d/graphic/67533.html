EORTC: European Organization for Research on Treatment of Cancer; AGES: patient age, histologic grade of the tumor, tumor extent (extrathyroidal invasion or distant metastases), and size of the primary tumor; AMES: patient age, presence of distant metastases, extent and size of the primary tumor; MACIS: metastasis, patient age, completeness of resection, local invasion, and tumor size; OSU: Ohio State University; MSKCC: Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center; NTCTCS: National Thyroid Cancer Treatment Cooperative Study; TNM: American Joint Committee on Cancer staging system of tumor size (T), nodal metastases (N), and distant metastases (M).
