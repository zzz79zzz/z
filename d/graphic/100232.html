Monteggia fracture on radiography
A lateral radiograph of the elbow (A) shows a comminuted fracture of the olecranon process of the ulna (arrow). Image B is an oblique view of the elbow and shows a dislocated (and fractured) radial head (arrowhead) and a proximal olecranon fracture (arrow).
