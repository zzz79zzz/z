Vulvar pseudoverrucous papules and nodules
Multiple shiny, white-gray, wart-like papules and nodules, sometimes partly confluent and crescent-shaped, involving the vulva and the surrounding erythematous skin.
From: Garrido-Ruiz MC, Rosales B, Luis Rodríguez-Peralto J. Vulvar pseudoverrucous papules and nodules secondary to a urethral--vaginal fistula. Am J Dermatopathol 2011; 33:410.
