Sensation over the anterolateral thigh
Sensory testing is performed in patients with suspected lateral femoral cutaneous nerve entrapment (meralgia paresthetica) to assess the anterolateral thigh. The skin is tested for light touch, pinprick, and deep pain sensation. Loss of sensation (hypesthesia) or a feeling of heightened sensation (dysesthesia) is characteristic of meralgia paresthetica.
