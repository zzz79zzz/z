Portal venogram
This portal venogram demonstrates the tip of the catheter in the superior mesenteric vein (arrow) and the distal end of the TIPS sheath in the intrahepatic segment of the right portal vein (arrowhead). The venogram shows patent portal circulation.
TIPS: transjugular intrahepatic portosystemic shunts.
