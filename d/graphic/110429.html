Schematic diagram of components of evidence-based medicine
EBM is the care of patients using the best available research evidence to guide clinical decision-making. The focus is upon applying the results of research involving patients and important clinical outcomes (eg, death, symptoms). EBM is meant to complement, not replace, clinical judgment tailored to individual patients.
