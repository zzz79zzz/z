Pediatric end tidal CO2 curve
This figures shows a capnogram during ventilation of a small child. With the rapid respiratory rate that is necessary in children, inspiration may occur before full expiration, and may give a falsely low end tidal carbon dioxide (ETCO2) value (solid line). A brief ventilator pause allows full expiration and display of the true ETCO2 (dashed line).
