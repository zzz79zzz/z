TEE view of cardiac tamponade
Transgastric mid-papillary short-axis image obtained in a patient with intraoperative hypotension caused by cardiac tamponade. A large pericardial effusion is identified by the echolucent spaces anterior and posterior to the LV.
TEE: transesophageal echocardiography; LV: left ventricular.
