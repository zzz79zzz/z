Pectoralis minor stretch
Stand with your elbow straight and your body at about a 45 degree angle from the wall. Raise your hand between waist and chest height and place your palm flush to the wall. Next, rotate your body away from the wall until you feel a stretch in the front of your biceps area. Hold for 30 seconds. Release the pressure for 5 to 10 seconds. Perform 3 repetitions.
