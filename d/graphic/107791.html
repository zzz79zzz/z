Crawford extent IV aortic repair
This figure illustrates repair of a Crawford extent IV thoracic aortic aneurysm showing replacement of the descending aorta below the diaphragm with the celiac, SMA and right renal artery anastomosed as a patch and the left renal artery replaced with a Dacron tube graft.
SMA: superior mesenteric artery.
