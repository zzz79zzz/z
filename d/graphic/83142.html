Multiple myeloma: Lytic bone disease and PET positive scan
This male patient is 81 years old and has multiple myeloma. The CT scan (A and C) in the region of the cervicothoracic junction reveals an expansile and lytic process in the region of T1 (arrow). The correlative PET scan (B and D) shows a moderately hot area (arrowhead).
PET: positron emission tomography; CT: computed tomography.
