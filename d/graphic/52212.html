Hydrosalpinx
Transvaginal ultrasound image of the adnexa showing a hydrosalpinx. There is a tubular fluid collection with low-level echoes; an incomplete septation is identified by the arrow. The walls are thin and there was no increase in flow (not shown) to suggest infection. The patient was nontender during the examination.
