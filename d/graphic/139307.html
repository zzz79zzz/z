Hip OA radiograph
Right hip radiograph showing superolateral joint space narrowing, marginal femoral and acetabular osteophyte, subchondral cysts (femoral head and superolateral acetabulum), periosteal osteophyte (inferior margin of femoral neck), and superolateral migration of the femoral head.
OA: osteoarthritis.
