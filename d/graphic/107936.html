Lumbar plexus transverse probe (lateral decubitus position)
For ultrasound-guided lumbar plexus block, the transducer is initially placed longitudinally to identify the spinous processes and is then turned 90 degrees, as shown in this photo, to obtain a transverse view of the psoas major muscle. For further details, refer to the UpToDate content on lumbar plexus block.
