Inhaled beta-agonists protect against exercise-induced bronchoconstriction
The degree of exercise-induced bronchoconstriction (as measured by a fall in peak expiratory flow) with placebo, inhaled salbutamol (albuterol), or the long-acting inhaled beta-2 agonist formoterol. Formoterol produced more prolonged protection than salbutamol.
Data from Henriksen JM, Agertoft L, Pedersen S, J Allergy Clin Immunol 1992; 89:1176.
