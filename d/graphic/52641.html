The angiogram of the LV in a patient with tetralogy of Fallot was obtained with the camera in a long-axial oblique projection. There is a large malaligned ventricular septal defect (*) with the AO overriding the IVS.
AO: aorta; IVS: intraventricular septum; LV: left ventricle.
