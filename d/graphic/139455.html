Seasonality of respiratory viruses in the United States
The incidence of acute bronchitis is highest when the transmission of respiratory viruses peaks. In the Northern hemisphere, the transmission of respiratory viruses peaks in the late fall and winter. In the Southern hemisphere, transmission peaks in late spring and summer.
