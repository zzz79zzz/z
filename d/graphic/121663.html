Curved multiplanar reconstruction of lower extremity angiogram
Curved multiplanar sagittal reconstruction CT angiogram of the aorta and bilateral lower extremities. The green line depicts the center line, a line that traverses through the center of the vessel of interest. The blue and red lines are used to measure specific distances, which are helpful in planning intervention for patients with particularly tortuous segments of the vasculature.
CT: computed tomographic.
