Frykman I radius fracture lateral view
On the lateral x-ray of the same fracture, the angulation and dorsal displacement of the distal fragment is apparent. Such fractures are often referred to by the eponym Colles. Note this is not a true lateral: the images of the radius and ulna are not superimposed, and the pisiform (arrow) is seen dorsal to the distal pole of the scaphoid.
