Sodium oxybate¶

Doses are administered twice nightly, at bedtime and 2.5 to 4 hours later

Recommended starting dose by weight:

  • 20 to <30 kg: ≤2 grams per night in 2 divided doses
  • 30 to <45 kg: ≤3 grams per night in 2 divided doses
  • ≥45 kg: ≤4.5 grams per night in 2 divided doses

2 to 9 grams in 2 divided doses at night

Maximum recommended total night dose by weight:

  • 20 to <30 kg: 6 grams
  • 30 to <45 kg: 7.5 grams
  • ≥45 kg: 9 grams
