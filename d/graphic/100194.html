Great toe taping
Patients with soft tissue strains related to the great toe may benefit from additional support during activity, such as that provided by the taping described here:
(A) Apply anchors.
(B) Apply strip to plantar surface.
(C) Apply additional support strips.
(D) Apply anchors to close.
