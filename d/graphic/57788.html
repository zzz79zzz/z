Principal and ancillary diagnostic studies in suspected idiopathic pulmonary hemosiderosis
Laboratory tests
  • Serologic studies
    • Antinuclear antibodies (ANA)
    • Antidouble stranded DNA antibodies (anti-dsDNA)
    • Antineutrophil cytoplasmic antibodies (ANCA)
    • Antiphospholipid antibodies (APLA: IgG and IgM antibodies to cardiolipin and beta2-glycoprotein I) and lupus anticoagulant test
    • Antiglomerular basement membrane antibodies
    • Rheumatoid factor
    • Plasma IgA anti-tissue transglutaminase; IgA and IgG antibody to synthetic deamidated gliadin peptides
    • Plasma cryoglobulins
    • Plasma immunoglobulins (IgG, IgA, IgM)
    • Plasma anti-cow's milk (IgG, IgE) antibodies (optional)*
  • Sputum examination for hemosiderin-laden macrophages
  • Serum creatinine, BUN, AST, ALT
  • Serum brain natriuretic peptide
  • Urinalysis for protein, hemoglobin, RBC casts

Ancillary testing in selected patients based on clinical presentation

  • Bone marrow biopsy for stainable iron
  • Examination for gastrointestinal, urinary, genital tract bleeding
  • Skin biopsy of cutaneous lesions (leukocytoclastic vasculitis)
  • Renal biopsy (glomerulonephritis)
  • Jejunal biopsy (celiac disease)
  • CTPA to exclude thromboembolic disease
  • Echocardiogram if heart failure suspected
 Evaluation for anemia and bleeding disorders
  • CBC and differential cell count
  • Serum iron and transferrin saturation
  • Serum ferritin, B12, folate
  • Reticulocyte count
  • Screening for coagulation defects (PT, INR, PTT)

Pulmonary function tests

  • Spirometry, lung volumes, DLCO, 6MWT

Imaging

  • Chest radiograph
  • HRCT of thorax

Flexible bronchoscopy

  • Sequential wedged bronchoalveolar lavage
  • Transbronchial lung biopsy

Lung biopsy

  • VATS or open

Histopathology

  • Perls Prussian blue stain
  • Immunofluorescence or immunohistochemistry
  • Electron microscopy (optional)

IgG: immunoglobulin G; IgM: immunoglobulin M; IgA: immunoglobulin A; IgE: immunoglobulin E; BUN: blood urea nitrogen; AST: aspartate aminotransferase; ALT: alanine aminotransferase; RBC: red blood cell; CTPA: computed tomography pulmonary angiography; CBC: complete blood count; PT: prothrombin time; INR: international normalized ratio (for prothrombin time); PTT: partial thromboplastin time; DLCO: diffusing capacity for carbon monoxide; 6MWT: six-minute walk test; HRCT: high resolution computed tomography; VATS: video-assisted thoracoscopic surgery; IPH: idiopathic pulmonary hemosiderosis.

* Role of cow's milk antibodies in the pathogenesis of IPH is unclear.
