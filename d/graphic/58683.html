Talocrural joint line
The talocrural joint line is indicated by the transverse lines on the skin at the point of the pen. Effusion and pain on palpation of the talocrural joint line suggest an osteochondral talar dome fracture resulting from direct trauma between the talus and the fibula or tibia.
