Using an epinephrine autoinjector

To use an epinephrine autoinjector, hold it in your fist, with your fingers away from either end. Press it firmly into your outer thigh, and hold it in place for at least a few seconds. (Check the directions that come with your autoinjector so you know how long to hold it.)

It is best to sit down. If you feel dizzy, lie down. If you pass out, another person should lower you to the ground and raise your legs up. This helps blood flow to your heart and brain.
