Histologic features of acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP)
Histologic sections of skin show epidermal acanthosis with spongiosis and microvesicle and pustule formation. The dermis shows a perivascular and interstitial infiltrate, extending to mid-reticular dermal levels.
