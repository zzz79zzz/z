PTH (1-84) followed by placebo or alendronate
Mean changes (percent) in volumetric trabecular spine BMD as measured by QCT over 24 months of treatment in PTH placebo group (blue line with squares) and PTH followed by alendronate group (dashed red line with circles).
PTH: parathyroid hormone; ALN: alendronate; PLB: placebo; BMD: bone mineral density; QCT: quantitative computed tomography.
Data from: Black DM, Bilezikian JP, Ensrud KE, et al. One year of alendronate after one year of parathyroid hormone (1-84) for osteoporosis. N Engl J Med 2005; 353:555.
