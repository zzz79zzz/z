Comparison of magnetic resonance imaging findings in a normal subject and in a spondyloarthritis patient
This is a STIR sequence. Bone marrow edema with an intermediate signal is seen in the patient with SpA but not in the normal subject. The bone marrow edema is indicated by the arrow.
STIR: short tau inversion recovery; SpA: spondyloarthritis.
