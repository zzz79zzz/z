Pure tone audiometry in a patient with congenital hearing loss
Results of pure tone audiometry testing performed in a child with bilateral congenital SNHL. The precise bilateral symmetry is consistent with genetic hearing loss. Other features of the child's clinical history that pointed to genetic hearing loss included congenital onset of hearing loss and an otherwise normal physical.
R: right; L: left; Hz: Hertz; SNHL: sensorineural hearing loss.
