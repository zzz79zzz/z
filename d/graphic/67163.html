Ear tube to drain fluid
This surgery might be done when fluid in the middle ear does not go away. It can also be used to prevent more ear infections in children who get them a lot. The figure on the left shows an eardrum before the tube is inserted. The figure on the right shows fluid draining from the middle ear in a child who got an ear infection after the tube was inserted.
