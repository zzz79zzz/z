Treponoma pallidum by darkfield microscopy
Darkfield examination of an exudate from a penile ulcer in a patient with syphilis. When viewed in a fresh specimen, Treponema pallidum moves with a characteristic forward and backward motion with rotation around the longitudinal axis.
