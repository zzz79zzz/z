Rachitic rosary
Chest radiographs of patients with rickets, demonstrating a "rachitic rosary."
(A) Anteroposterior view[1]. Observe the opaque, bulbous indentations of the lung adjacent to the enlarged costochondral junctions (thick arrows).
(B) Lateral view (in a different patient)[2] showing the prominent costochondral junctions (dashed arrows).
