US probe position for biceps brachii examination
Ultrasound (US) transducer position over the anterior proximal arm in preparation of evaluating the long head of the biceps brachii tendon in short-axis. The black rectangle depicts the US transducer head. The arrow demonstrates how the US transducer is glided proximally and curves medially during the long head of the biceps brachii tendon evaluation.
