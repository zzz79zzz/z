Approach to women considering vaginal delivery of the singleton pregnancy with persistent breech presentation
Algorithm applies to pregnancies in which external cephalic version failed or was declined or fetus reverted to breech.
* The optimum estimated fetal weight thresholds to minimize risk of morbidity with vaginal breech birth vary among guidelines. For example, some use a minimum threshold of 2500 grams or a maximum threshold of 3500 or 3800 grams.
