Change in bone mineral density in adults with asthma taking inhaled glucocorticoids
For both the total hip and the trochanter, the rate of decline in bone density was 0.00044 g/cm2 per year for each additional daily puff of inhaled glucocorticoid (p = 0.01 and p = 0.005, respectively). The solid line in each panel represents the mean yearly change in bone density. The dashed lines indicate the 95 percent confidence interval for the mean.
Data from Isreal E, Banerjee TR, Fitzmaurice GM, et al. N Engl J Med 2001; 345:941.
