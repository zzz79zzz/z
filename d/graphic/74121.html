Staphylococcus aureus adenitis
Acute unilateral cervical adenitis caused by S. aureus. Submandibular nodes are affected in more than 50 percent of cases. The lymph node is usually 3 to 6 cm in diameter, tender, warm, erythematous, nondiscrete, and poorly mobile.
