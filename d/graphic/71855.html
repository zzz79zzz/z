Microinvasive radial growth phase melanoma
Low-power light photomicrograph of a microinvasive radial growth phase superficial spreading melanoma. In addition to epidermal involvement, this superficial spreading melanoma is characterized by dermal extension of the tumor cells (arrow). However, the largest dermal nest is not larger than the largest epidermal nest, and there are no dermal mitoses.
