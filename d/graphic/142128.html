Ultrasound images showing common and split femoral artery
Femoral nerve block should be performed at a point where a single, common femoral artery is visualized. If the deep and superficial branches of the femoral artery are both visualized, the transducer should be moved cephalad until those branches coalesce into a single structure. For further information refer to UpToDate content on performing femoral nerve block.
