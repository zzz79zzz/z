Episodic cortisol secretion in Cushing's syndrome
Serum cortisol concentrations throughout the day in patients with different forms of Cushing's syndrome. There is episodic hormone release in both ACTH-dependent and ACTH-independent (adrenal adenoma) forms of the syndrome. To convert serum cortisol to nmol/L, multiply by 27.6.
ACTH: corticotropin.
Data from: Sederberg-Olsen P, Binder C, Kehlet H, et al. Episodic variation in plasma corticosteroids in subjects with Cushing's syndrome of differing etiology. J Clin Endocrinol Metab 1973; 36:906.
