MRI of a hip fracture
Traumatic hip fracture. Coronal T1-weighted (A) and coronal STIR images (B) of the left hip show the incomplete intertrochanteric fracture line (arrow) that was not seen on the plain radiograph in this patient with hip pain following a recent fall.
STIR: short tau inversion recovery.
