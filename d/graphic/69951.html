Structure of romiplostim (Nplate, Romiplate, AMG 531)
Romiplostim is a "peptibody" composed of two disulphide-bonded human IgG1 kappa heavy chain constant regions (Fc fragment), each of which is covalently linked at residue 228 via polyglycine with two identical 14-amino acid TPO agonist peptide sequences.
