Treatment of uncomplicated groin hernia

TAPP: transabdominal preperitoneal patch.

* Uncomplicated groin hernias are inguinal or femoral hernias that are not incarcerated or strangulated.

¶ Although we generally favor open repair for such patients, data are limited and some surgeons may perform TAPP repair.
