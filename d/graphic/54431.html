Echocardiogram four-chamber post MI mitral regurgitation
Four chamber view from a 2-D echocardiogram with color flow Doppler shows mitral regurgitation with a jet directed toward the posterior left atrial wall as a result of a previous anterior septal myocardial infarction and papillary muscle dysfunction involving primarily the anterior mitral valve leaflet.
