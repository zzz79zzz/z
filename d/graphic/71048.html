Pleural fluid accumulation in interstitial pulmonary edema
Schematic representation of the mechanism of pleural fluid accumulation in interstitial pulmonary edema. Lung interstitial liquid can move into the pleural space along a pressure gradient and across leaky pleural membranes (heavy solid arrow). Lymphatic fluid removal is able to increase, but not sufficiently to prevent fluid accumulation in the pleura.
