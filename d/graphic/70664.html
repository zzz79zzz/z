Pediatric coronoid fracture classification
(A) Type I: Avulsion of the tip of the coronoid.
(B) Type II: A single or somminuted fragment involving ≤50 percent of the coronoid process.
(C) Type III: A single or comminuted fragment involving >50 percent of the coronoid process.
