Effects of testosterone or placebo treatment for 12 months on estimated bone strength
Bars indicate means, and error bars indicate standard deviations. The p values indicate the significance of the difference in change in estimated bone strength from baseline to 12 months for men in the testosterone arm compared with the placebo arm.
