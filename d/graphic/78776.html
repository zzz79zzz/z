Normal blood pressure responses to the Valsalva maneuver
Normal Valsalva response:
  • Normal changes in systolic blood pressure during 10 seconds of straining (shaded).
  • Phase 1 - systolic blood pressure rises with initiation of the maneuver.
  • Phase 2 - systolic blood pressure returns to baseline after several seconds.
  • Phase 3 - systolic blood pressure drops below normal after strain is released.
  • Phase 4 - systolic blood pressure rises transiently before returning to baseline.
