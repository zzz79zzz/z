Continuous ECG strip recording at 25 mm/second, showing severe bradycardia (heart rate 33) and 3rd degree (complete) heart block. The first four QRS complexes have a right bundle branch (RBBB) morphology, the fifth shows a left bundle branch (LBBB) morphology, and the sixth again shows RBBB.
