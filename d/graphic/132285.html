Enhancing intracranial vessels associated with ICH due to AVM
Noncontrast head CT showing right posterior frontal ICH (A, B). T2-weighted MRI images (C, D) and post-contrast T1-weighted MRI image (E) show flow voids and focal enhancement (arrows), both suspicious for AVM nidus. Subsequent digital subtraction angiograms images (F, G) show both pial AVM nidus (thick arrows).
CT: computed tomography; ICH: intracerebral hemorrhage; MRI: magnetic resonance imaging; AVM: arteriovenous malformation.
