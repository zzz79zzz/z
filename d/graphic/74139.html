The association of cadmium exposure with kidney stone risk
Cumulative incidence of kidney stones in relation to the estimated cumulative inhaled exposure to cadmium among cadmium battery workers in Sweden. The risk for experiencing kidney stones increased with the cumulative inhaled dose of cadmium.
Data from Jarup L, Elinder CG. Incidence of renal stones among cadmium exposed battery workers. Br J Ind Med 1993; 50:598.
