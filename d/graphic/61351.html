Doppler-derived mitral flow and left ventricular pressure
Simultaneously recorded left ventricular pressure, with the change in pressure over time (dp/dt) and mitral valve inflow velocity derived from Doppler echocardiography. The peak early filling (PE) corresponds to the early part of diastole while peak atrial filling (PA) is the flow velocity increase due to atrial contraction.
Redrawn from Kern MJ (Ed). Cardiac Catheterization Handbook, 2nd ed, Mosby-Year Book, St. Louis, 1995.
