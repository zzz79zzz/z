Anterior subluxation of C1 on C2 in rheumatoid arthritis on radiography
A lateral examination of the cervical spine (A) shows anterior subluxation of C1 on C2. The magnified view (B) shows a 12 mm separation between the posterior border of the arch of C1 (arrowhead) and the anterior border of the odontoid process of C2 (arrow).
