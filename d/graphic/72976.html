Retinal vein occlusion
Thrombosis of the central retinal vein results in venous stasis, leading to disc swelling, diffuse nerve fiber layer and preretinal hemorrhage, and cotton wool spots that create a dramatic appearance, often called "the blood and thunder" fundus.
