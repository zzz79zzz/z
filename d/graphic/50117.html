Color flow doppler of aortic dissection
The transesophageal echocardiogram with color flow Doppler shows the intimal flap separating the true and false lumens. A jet of blood can be seen going from the true to the false lumen and spontaneous echo contrast in the false lumen is appreciated, suggesting turbulent blood flow.
