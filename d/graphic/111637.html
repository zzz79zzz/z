Computed tomography of pneumothorax in lymphangioleiomyomatosis
High resolution computed tomographic scan showing a right-sided pneumothorax in a patient with lymphangioleiomyomatosis. Note the pneumothorax is loculated and has collapsed anterolaterally, a feature that may be missed on plain radiograph.
