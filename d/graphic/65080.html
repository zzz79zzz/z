All values are given as a mean (M), followed by lower and upper boundary encompassing 95% of the population (B). Between 40 and 96 samples were assayed for each value for newborns.
PT: prothrombin time; aPTT: activated partial thromboplastin time; TCT: thrombin clotting time; VWF: von Willebrand factor; PK: prekallikrein; HMWK: high molecular weight kininogen; CTA: Committee on Thrombolytic Agents.
