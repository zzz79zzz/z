Regulation of excitation-contraction coupling
Schematic representation of the factors responsible for regulation of the calcium transient at the sarcoplasmic reticulum (top) and of the regulation of excitation-contraction coupling by the binding of calcium to troponin C.
SERCA: sarco/endoplasmic reticulum calcium transporting ATPase; RyR2: Ryanodine receptor 2; DAG: diacylglycerol; IP3: inositol triphosphate.
