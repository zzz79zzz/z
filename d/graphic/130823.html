Association between the CARG-BC score and secondary outcomes
Association of CARG-BC score (A) with grade 3 to 5 chemotherapy toxicity, compared with general CARG toxicity tool (B) and clinician-rated KPS (C) in the validation cohort.
CARG-BC: Cancer and Aging Research-Breast Cancer score; CARG: Cancer and Aging Research Group; KPS: Karnofsky performance status as rated by the clinician; AUC: area under the curve.
From: Magnuson A, Sedrak MS, Gross CP, et al. Development and validation of a risk Tool for predicting severe toxicity in older adults receiving chemotherapy for early-stage breast cancer.
