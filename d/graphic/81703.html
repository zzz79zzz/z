Atypical carcinoid tumor of the lung
Low power photomicrograph showing an atypical carcinoid tumor of the lung. The tumor shows a growth pattern reminiscent of that seen in carcinoid tumors of the more usual type, but differs in that there is associated tumor necrosis as seen in the upper portion of the photomicrograph (arrow).
