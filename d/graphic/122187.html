Histology of intrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma, large duct type
The large duct type of intrahepatic cholangiocarcinoma is characterized by large, neoplastic, ductular structures with a columnar cell lining and apical mucin in the background of densely hyalinized desmoplastic stroma.
