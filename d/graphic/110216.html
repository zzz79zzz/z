Phylloid hypomelanosis
Phylloid hypomelanosis due to 13q mosaicism. Note the leaf-like as well as oval, thumbprint-like, hypopigmented macules on the front of chest and abdomen and on the back.
From: Dhar SU, Robbins-Furman P, Levy ML, et al. Tetrasomy 13q mosaicism associated with phylloid hypomelanosis and precocious puberty.
