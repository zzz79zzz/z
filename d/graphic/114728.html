Management of solitary lymph node <4 cm in penile squamous cell carcinoma
All patients should undergo a staging procedure on the contralateral clinically negative inguinal node. Refer to UpToDate content on management of penile carcinoma.
LND: lymph node dissection; DSNB: dynamic sentinel node biopsy; LAP: laparoscopic; RAIL: robotic-assisted inguinal lymphadenectomy.
* Minimally invasive (eg, RAIL or LAP) surgical techniques are limited to centers with appropriate expertise.
