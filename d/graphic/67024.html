Anterior biopsy approach for a bone tumor
CT-guided needle biopsy of this posterior soft tissue mass (arrows) was accomplished using an anterior approach, which would not have compromised a planned limb-sparing procedure because the needle track could be entirely excised with the resection specimen.
CT: computed tomography.
