Primary lymphoma of bone
Histologic evaluation of a femoral resection in a case of diffuse large B cell lymphoma involving the bone. The photomicrograph demonstrates an infiltration of large pleomorphic cells, many of which contain highly convoluted, polylobated nuclear membranes with smudged chromatin and one to two inconspicuous nucleoli ("floret cells," black arrows).
