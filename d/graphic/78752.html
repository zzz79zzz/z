Heel-pocket-out method of putting on compression stockings
Stockings can be applied by turning the leg portion of the stocking inside-out down to the heel (A). The foot is slipped into the stretched stocking and pulled onto the foot by its folded edge (B). Once the stocking is over the heel, the remainder of the stocking can be gathered and progressively turned right-side out and gently worked up the leg (C).
