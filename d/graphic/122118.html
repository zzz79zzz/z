In the United States, the glycopyrrolate (glycopyrronium) dose is expressed as the amount of bromide salt delivered from the mouthpiece. In Canada, the dose is expressed as the amount of glycopyrronium base per capsule.

◊ Tiotropium SMI is available as multiple strengths (ie, 1.25 mcg/actuation and 2.5 mcg/actuation). For COPD, the 2.5 mcg/actuation preparation should be used.

§ Revefenacin use is not recommended in patients with hepatic impairment.
