Bottle feeding a baby

To give a baby a bottle, hold them close to your body in a cradle hold. This is when the baby's head is resting on the inside of your elbow, and they are facing up toward you. Their head should be slightly higher than the rest of their body. Support their head and neck.

Hold the bottle at a slight angle, not straight up and down. Hold the bottle the entire time you are feeding the baby. Do not prop it up.
