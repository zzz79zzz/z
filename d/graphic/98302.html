Histologic features of sebaceous adenoma​
Sebaceous adenomas can have overlapping histopathologic features with sebaceous hyperplasia, but typically lack a central follicle and often connect directly to the epidermis. Architecturally, sebaceous adenomas have a more disorganized appearance than sebaceous hyperplasia, with more basaloid germinative sebocytes (H&E 40x).
