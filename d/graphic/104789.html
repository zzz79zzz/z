Phonocardiogram recorded at the apex in a 20-year-old patient with rheumatic mitral stenosis. An echocardiogram documented a 13 mmHg peak and 6 mmHg mean gradient across the mitral valve. In diastole, there is an OS and a DM that has presystolic accentuation.
S1: first heart sound; S2: second heart sound; OS: opening snap; DM: diastolic murmur.
