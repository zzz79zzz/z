Endoscopic view of biliary sphincterotomy site following injection therapy
This endoscopic picture shows the biliary sphincterotomy site after injection of diluted epinephrine solution to control bleeding. Following injection of diluted epinephrine, the mucosa becomes a whitish color as a result of local ischemia.
