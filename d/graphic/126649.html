Endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography (ERCP) of pancreas divisum with a normal dorsal duct
A child with recurrent acute pancreatitis and MRCP that revealed pancreas divisum. In this image, we can see the classic finding of the dorsal pancreatic duct crossing the distal common bile duct (arrow) and ending into the minor papilla (thick arrow). This is diagnostic of pancreas divisum.
ERCP: endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography; MRCP: magnetic resonance cholangiopancreatogram.
