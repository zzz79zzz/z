Dobutamine echocardiography predicts cardiac mortality
In a study of 3156 patients, those with an abnormal dobutamine echocardiogram (ischemic, scar, or mixed) have a higher cardiac mortality compared to those with a normal echocardiogram. The highest mortality is in those with both scar and ischemia (mixed). Kaplan-Meier survival curves for the prediction of cardiac mortality based on results of dobutamine echocardiography.
Data from Marwick, TH, Case, C, Sawada, S, J Am Coll Cardiol 2001; 37:754.
