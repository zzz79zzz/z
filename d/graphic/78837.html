Adrenal CT scan of a 20-year-old female with PPNAD, Cushing's syndrome, and family history of Carney's complex. Note the right adrenal (A) of normal size but with discrete bead-like nodules (arrow). In the left adrenal (B), a larger 1.5 cm nodule was present (arrow).
CT: computed tomography; PPNAD: primary pigmented nodular adrenocortical disease.
