Pericardial effusion
Parasternal short-axis view from a 2-dimensional echocardiogram shows a very large pericardial effusion (PE) surrounding the LV and RV, which is collapsed. The patient had severe hemodynamic compromise and tamponade physiology.
LV: left ventricle; PE: pericardial effusion; RV: right ventricle.
