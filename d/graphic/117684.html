Aortic root replacement with a composite valved graft
A composite valved graft (Bentall) procedure involves replacement of the aortic root with a graft and aortic valve replacement with a prosthetic valve (mechanical as shown here, or bioprosthetic), with reimplantation of the coronary arteries.
