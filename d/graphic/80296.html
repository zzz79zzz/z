Red blood cell morphology in various forms of Plasmodium infections
* Identification of a schizont with >12 merozoites in the peripheral circulation is an important diagnostic clue for P. vivax. In general, schizonts of P. falciparum are very rarely seen in blood films; they are generally absent from the peripheral circulation except in cases of severe infection with overwhelming parasitemia.
