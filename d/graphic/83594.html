Identification of mitral valve prolapse
The currently accepted echocardiographic definition of mitral valve prolapse is billowing of any portion of the mitral leaflets at least 2 mm above the annular plane in the long-axis views (parasternal or apical 3-chamber). On this parasternal long axis there is pronounced prolapse of the posterior leaflet (arrows) above the annular plane (line).
LV: left ventricle; LA: left atrium; Ao: aorta.
