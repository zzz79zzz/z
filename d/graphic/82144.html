Calf stretch exercise
Sit with your legs straight and loop a towel around your foot. Then pull the top part of your foot towards you. Hold it like that for 10 to 30 seconds. Repeat this 5 times each session and do 2 sessions a day. You can also push the ball of your foot against the towel. This exercises and strengthens the muscles in your foot.
