Unusual presentation of purulent pericarditis
Chest CT scan shows an anterior mediastinal mass compressing and displacing the heart. This was a case of Staphylococcus aureus purulent pericarditis in a patient on hemodialysis who had been treated twice over a six-month period for S. aureus bacteremia.
CT: computed tomography.
