Verrucous sarcoidosis
Verrucous sarcoidosis consisting of discrete hyperkeratotic, thick, hyperpigmented plaques. These types of lesions may be misdiagnosed as lichenified psoriasis, prurigo nodularis, or hypertrophic lichen planus.
