Noncontact endocardial map of fascicular tachycardia
During tachycardia, the fascicular activation sequence is altered; panel A shows that activation begins near the left ventricular apex (red arrow) and panel B shows that activation (red arrow) propagates toward His bundle (white arrow). This rapid retrograde fascicular activation is closely followed by ventricular activation, seen as a white area in panel C. The relative delay in right ventricular activation produces a QRS complex with right bundle branch block pattern in ECG lead I.
