Circle size roughly reflects the relative prevalence of the subtypes of cutaneous lupus erythematosus.
SCLE: subacute cutaneous lupus erythematosus; ACLE: acute cutaneous lupus erythematosus; LE: lupus erythematosus; DLE: discoid lupus erythematosus; SLE: systemic lupus erythematosus.
