Scalpel-finger-bougie cricothyrotomy: Vertical skin incision
The initial incision for the scalpel-finger-bougie cricothyrotomy technique is a 5- to 10-cm vertical incision crossing the middle of the CTM.
CTM: cricothyroid membrane.
