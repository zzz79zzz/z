Corner stretch
Stand in the corner with your arms in a 90-90 position and each forearm braced against adjacent walls. Move one foot forward and gently lean forward until you feel a stretch in your chest and shoulders. Hold for 30 seconds. Release the pressure for 5 to 10 seconds and repeat. Perform 3 repetitions.
