CT scan showing renal angiomyolipoma with hemorrhage
Axial CT scan demonstrates a large fat-containing mass arising from the posterior aspect of the left kidney (arrow) which is displaced anteriorly by the mass. The mass has ruptured producing a hematoma in the perirenal space (arrowhead).
CT: computed tomography.
