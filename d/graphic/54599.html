Ten-year fracture probabilities according to BMD T-score and age
The relationship between BMD femoral neck T-score, age and hip fracture probability in females. The probability of hip fracture for any given T-score is higher with increasing age.
BMD: bone mineral density; SD: standard deviation.
Data from: Kanis JA, Johnell O, Oden A, et al. Ten year probabilities of osteoporotic fractures according to BMD and diagnostic thresholds. Osteoporos Int 2001; 12:989.
