Left upper lobe bronchial atresia seen on a coronal CT image
Coronal multiplanar reformat with mediastinal window settings shows the tubular hilar mass consistent with a mucoid impaction distal to the atretic bronchus. No evidence for an endobronchial tumor is seen, but tubular calcifications are seen in the mucoid impaction.
