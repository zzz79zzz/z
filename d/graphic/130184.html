Values in the table above are calculated from fortified human milk prepared according to the manufacturer's directions. For the liquid Similac and Enfamil products, 20 mL of fortifier is added to 100 mL of human milk. For the Prolacta +4 product, 20 mL of fortifier is added to 80 mL of human milk. For the Prolacta +6 product, 30 mL of fortifier is added to 70 mL of human milk.

HMF: human milk fortifier.

* Values for macronutrient and mineral content of human milk are based on average content of mature preterm milk (ie, milk produced by a mother 5 to 12 weeks after preterm birth)[1]. The actual macronutrient content of human milk varies substantially between individuals and over the course of lactation.
