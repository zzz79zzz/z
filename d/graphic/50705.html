Popliteal aneurysm on CT angiography
The CT angiogram demonstrates a large right popliteal artery aneurysm and tortuous distal superficial femoral artery. The true diameter of the aneurysm must be measured from cross-sectional two-dimensional images; this three-dimensional CT reconstruction shows only regions where intravenous contrast is present.
CT: computed tomography.
