Transgluteal sciatic ultrasound and schematic
This graphic shows the ultrasound image and corresponding schematic for performing ultrasound-guided transgluteal sciatic nerve block. The block needle is inserted through the gluteus maximus muscle to place the tip in close proximity to the sciatic nerve. For further detail refer to UpToDate content on the sciatic nerve block technique.
GT: greater trochanter; IT: ischial tuberosity.
