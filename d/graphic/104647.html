US popliteal fossa transverse view with doppler
The ultrasound image above shows a transverse view of the popliteal fossa. Image A is a grey scale image, while image B is a color Doppler image that highlights blood flow in the tibial vessels.
Arrow: tibial nerve (short-axis); V and blue color: tibial vein (short-axis); A and orange color: tibial artery (short-axis).
Left side of each image: lateral; right side of each image: medial.
