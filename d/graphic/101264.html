Helmet for noninvasive ventilation in children
(A) The helmet is secured by a "baby-body", which is worn under the pubic region as a pair of pants.
(B) The  bidirectional safety valve is positioned on the cover of an opening to prevent asphyxia and to permit provider access to the patient's head.
From: Codazzi D, Nacoti M, Passoni M, et al. Continuous positive airway pressure with modified helmet for treatment of hypoxemic acute respiratory failure in infants and a preschool population: a feasibility study.
