Battery versus coin ingestion
Enlarged radiographic comparison between the camera battery ingestion (left) and the coin ingestion (right) will show the subtle but characteristic differences between the two foreign bodies. The bilaminar structure of the button battery gives it a double-ring or halo appearance on plain radiograph, whereas the appearance of the coin is homogeneous.
