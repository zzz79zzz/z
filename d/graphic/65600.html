Cervical cerclage procedure with dilated cervix
(A) Membranes herniating through effaced and widely dilated cervix.
(B) Replacement of membranes into the uterus by gentle traction on stay sutures.
(C) Placement of 2 rows of encircling sutures.
(D) Final appearance of the cervix.
