US supraspinatus partial tear bursal surface long axis view
Long axis ultrasound (US) image of a partial thickness bursal surface supraspinatus (SS) tear (arrows). Hypertrophic bursal tissue (star) is lying in the region of the tear. Note the loss of the normal convexity of the superficial surface of the supraspinatus.
Deltoid: deltoid muscle; GT: greater tuberosity; Humerus: humerus bone.
Left: medial; Right: lateral; Bottom of screen: deep; Top of screen: superficial.
