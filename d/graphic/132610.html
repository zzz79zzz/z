Star excursion test
The star excursion test is used to assess strength, balance, and motor control in patients recovering from lower extremity injuries. It is performed with the patient standing on the rehabilitated leg and extending the non-injured lower limb as far as possible to 8 different points along an octagonal grid.
From: Lorch FE, Stoner L, Lieberman JA, et al. Exercise.
