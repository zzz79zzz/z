Calcitonin prevents osteoporosis
Mean change in lumbar bone mineral content in 39 postmenopausal patients treated for 2 years with intranasal salmon calcitonin or placebo. Bone mineral content was stable with calcitonin but fell in the placebo group (p<0.001 at 2 years).
Data from: Overgaard K, Riis BJ, Christiansen C, et al. Effect of salcatonin given intranasally on early postmenopausal bone loss. BMJ 1989; 299:477.
