Diagnosis of tardive dyskinesia (TD)

* A confident diagnosis of TD requires >4 weeks of current or past exposure to a dopamine receptor-blocking agent. Shorter durations of exposure require clinical judgment and may be consistent with TD.

¶ If the offending drug cannot be safely discontinued, movements that persist for >4 weeks are consistent with TD.
