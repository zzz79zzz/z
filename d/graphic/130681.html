Narrow band imaging for delineating a lesion
Optical chromoscopy provides enhanced endoscopic visualization of subtle mucosal and vascular changes and may be used for delineating a mucosal lesion. In this endoscopic image, the blue line represents the border of the lesion's vascular and mucosal abnormality based on the narrow band imaging features.
