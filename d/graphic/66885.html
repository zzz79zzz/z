United States TB case rates by reporting area, 2018
When considering incidence rates by reporting area, Alaska (8.5 cases per 100,000 persons) has the highest TB rate, followed by Hawaii (8.4), New York City (6.7), California (5.3), and the District of Columbia (5.1), and Texas (3.9).
TB: tuberculosis.
