US tibionavicular and tibiotalar portions of deltoid ligament long axis view
This ultrasound image shows the tibionavicular and anterior tibiotalar components of the deltoid ligament in their long axis. Note the presence of a tibiotalar joint effusion (*) and attenuation of the deep (tibiotalar) component of the deltoid ligament from prior injury.
Arrows: tibionavicular component of deltoid ligament; arrowheads: anterior tibiotalar component of deltoid ligament; TIB: tibia; TAL: talus; NAV: navicular bone.
Left of screen: proximal; right of screen: distal.
