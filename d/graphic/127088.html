Boussignac CPAP system
According to the manufacturer, an appropriately installed Boussignac CPAP System provides a CPAP level of 5, 7.5, or 10 cm H2O at oxygen flow rates of 15, 20, or 25 liters, respectively.
CPAP: continuous positive airway pressure.
