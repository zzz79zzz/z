Push-through method of fish-hook removal
A. Anesthetize the skin adjacent to the hook. B. Push the hook forward until the barb comes back through the skin. C. With eye protection in place, clip the barb of the hook with wirecutters. D. Back the remaining portion of the hook out of the skin.
