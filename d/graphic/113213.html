Leg or hamstring curl exercise
The photographs above show the starting position (A) and end position (B) for the hamstring curl exercise. This exercise is used in some rehabilitation programs when more functional exercises involving the hamstring, such as deadlifts, cannot be used due to injury or other restrictions.
