Pericardial lipoma on chest x-ray
A P-A chest x-ray (A) shows the border of the left ventricle (arrowhead) surrounded by a lower density border of a pericardial lipoma (arrow). A lateral view (B) shows the posteriorly positioned low-density lipoma of the pericardium (arrow).
P-A: posteroanterior.
