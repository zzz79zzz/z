Olsen growth chart for preterm and term infants - Boys
Intrauterine growth curves for boys.
(A) Weight-for-age in grams (gm).
(B) Length- and head circumference (HC)-for-age.
Of note, 3rd and 97th percentiles on all curves for 23 weeks should be interpreted cautiously, given the small sample size; for boys' HC curve at 24 weeks, all percentiles should be interpreted cautiously because the distribution of data is skewed left.
