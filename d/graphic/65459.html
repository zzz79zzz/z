White cell count in measles
Mean absolute neutrophil and lymphocyte counts during measles in 16 uncomplicated cases. Normal levels indicated by the broken line. There is a mild leukocytosis for a few days before the onset of fever. This is followed by leukopenia during the week of cough, coryza, conjunctivitis, Koplik spots and rash.
