Magnetic resonance image of fetal esophageal atresia
Sagittal T2w image at 28 weeks of gestation demonstrates a dilated fluid-filled proximal esophageal pouch (arrow). Polyhydramnios and a small stomach were also noted. Esophageal atresia was confirmed postnatally.
