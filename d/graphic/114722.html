Digit fasciotomy
The upper figure (A) shows the skin incision used to release the digit compartment. In the lower cross section of the finger (B), access to the Cleland ligament is achieved from a midaxial incision (dashed arrow I). The neurovascular bundle can be also accessed from a volar side by dissecting the Grayson ligament (dashed arrow II).
Modified from:
(A) Giladi AM, Chung KC. Fasciotomy for compartment syndrome of the hand and forearm. In: Operative Techniques: Hand and Wrist Surgery, 3rd ed, Chung KC (Ed), Elsevier, Philadelphia 2017.
(B) Jobe MT. Compartment syndromes and Volkmann contracture. In: Campbell's Operative Orthopaedics, 12th ed, Canale ST, Beaty JH (Eds), Elsevier, Philadelphia 2013.
