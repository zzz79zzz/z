Evaluation of acute pharyngitis in adults
All adults presenting with acute pharyngitis should have a thorough history and physical, including assessment for risk factors for acute HIV infection and sexually transmitted infections.

ARF: acute rheumatic fever; COVID-19: coronavirus disease 2019; GAS: group A Streptococcus; HIV: human immunodeficiency virus; NAAT: nucleic acid amplification test; RADT: rapid antigen detection test.
