Parasternal short axis echocardiogram in a patient with hypertrophic cardiomyopathy (HCM)
Short axis view from a two-dimensional echocardiogram shows marked asymmetric hypertrophy of the posterior left ventricular wall and the papillary muscles. During systole, there is near obliteration of the left ventricular cavity.
