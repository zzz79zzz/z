This ultrasound scan shows the sartorius muscle and tendon in the sagittal plane (long-axis view) as it inserts onto the ASIS.

SA: sartorius muscle; arrows: sartorius tendon; ASIS: anterior superior iliac spine; I: iliopsoas muscle.
