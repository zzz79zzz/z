Survival by diagnosis for lung transplants performed in adults from January 1992 to June 2017
AAT: alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency; COPD: chronic obstructive pulmonary disease; ILD: interstitial lung disease; IIP: idiopathic interstitial pneumonia (idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis).
* The median survival (half-life) is the estimated timepoint at which 50% of recipients have died. Survival was calculated using the Kaplan Meier method, based on data from transplants performed between January 1992 and June 2017, as compiled in the International Society of Heart and Lung Transplantation registry.
