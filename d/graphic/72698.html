Pulmonary Langerhans cell histiocytosis
High-resolution CT with thin section shows the cysts in pulmonary Langerhans cell histiocytosis (PLCH), which vary markedly in size and may be larger than 10 mm. The cysts are bizarre in shape, often closely related to pulmonary arteries, and mimic bronchiectasis. Few nodules are present in this case.
CT: computed tomography.
