Scalpel-finger-bougie cricothyrotomy: Stenting the opening with a scalpel to facilitate bougie passage
After the incision of the cricothyroid membrane is made, the scalpel blade can be used to stent open the stoma and facilitate passage of the bougie. This is done by gently anchoring the 10-blade against the side of the stoma opposite the operator.
