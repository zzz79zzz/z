Values are the number of cells per microliter × 10–3 expressing the indicated markers for the lymphocyte population presented as median (10th and 90th percentiles). For example, in the 3/4/45RO subset, the value reflects the number of CD3+ T cells that are also positive for CD4 and CD45RO. The presence of a surface marker on a cell is assumed unless the marker is followed by a negative sign. When both positive and negative signs are used, this indicates a total cell population.
