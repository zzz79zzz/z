Hemodynamic response to aortic crossclamping
Systemic hemodynamic response to aortic crossclamping. Preload does not necessarily increase. If during infrarenal aortic crossclamping blood volume shifts into the splanchnic vasculature, preload does not increase.
AoX: aortic crossclamping; Ao: aortic; R art: arterial resistance; CO: cardiac output; ↑: increase; ↓: decrease.
* Different patterns are possible.
