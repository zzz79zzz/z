S4 gallop in a neonate following a prolonged period of supraventricular tachycardia
Phonocardiogram recorded at the apex in 17-day-old neonate with severe left ventricular dysfunction secondary to a prolonged episode of supraventricular tachycardia. The electrocardiogram demonstrates Wolff-Parkinson-White syndrome with a prominent delta wave. In the recording, a S4 is heard coincident with the P wave on the electrocardiogram during the period of atrial contraction.
S1: first heart sound; S2: second heart sound; S4: fourth heart sound.
