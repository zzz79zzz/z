Capsules and ligaments of glenohumeral and acromioclavicular joints: Anterior view
The acromioclavicular, corocohumeral, and glenohumeral ligaments. Although shown on the external aspect of the joint capsule, the glenohumeral ligaments are actually a feature observed from the internal aspect of the joint. These ligaments strengthen the anterior aspect of the capsule of the glenohumeral joint, and the coracohumeral ligament strengthens the capsule superiorly.
* Coracoid process + coraco-acromial ligament acromion = coraco-acromial arch.
