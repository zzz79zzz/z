Polycystic ovary on ultrasound
Three-dimensional transvaginal ultrasound image of a polycystic ovary. There are typical features present, including multiple (>10) small peripheral follicles <1 cm in diameter ("string of pearls" sign). There is also typically increased central stroma, and overall volume of the ovary is increased to greater than 10 cc.
