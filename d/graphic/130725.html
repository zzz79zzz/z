Continuum of pulmonary developmental anomalies
The six pulmonary developmental anomalies can be considered to span a continuum, ranging from an abnormal lung containing normal vessels to a normal lung containing abnormal vessels. Although many cases of pulmonary developmental anomalies have the classic features of a single anomaly, other cases have features common to two or more anomalies.
AV: arteriovenous.
