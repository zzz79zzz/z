V-to-Y advancement flap for sacral pressure-induced injury
This patient with paraplegia presented with a stage IV sacral pressure-induced injury (A). In a one-stage procedure following debridement, a unilateral V-to-Y advancement fasciocutaneous flap was used for closure (B).
