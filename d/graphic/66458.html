Brain natriuretic peptide level predicts outcome in heart failure
Kaplan-Meier survival curves in patients with heart failure show that survival is significantly lower among those with a brain natriuretic peptide (BNP) level >73 pg/ml compared with patients with a level <73 pg/ml.
Data from: Tsutamoto T, Wada A, Maeda K, et al. Circulation 1997; 96:509.
