Lymphocutaneous sporotrichosis
Lymphocutaneous sporotrichosis on the right lower extremity of a young man who overturned his dirt bike and inoculated soil in multiple places in his leg. Following a plastic surgery procedure before the diagnosis of sporotrichosis, new lesions developed at the edge of the skin graft and extended up into the thigh.
