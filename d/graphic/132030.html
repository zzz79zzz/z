MRI contrast-enhancing sellar mass
This contrast-enhancing sellar mass was read as a pituitary adenoma. Biopsy showed no adenoma, only normal pituitary architecture infiltrated by lymphocytes, suggesting lymphocytic hypophysitis. After treatment with prednisone for 1 month, the pituitary size decreased to normal.
