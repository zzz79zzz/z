Ultrasound images of liver position in two fetuses with CDH
(Left) High resolution fetal ultrasound demonstrates fetal stomach (ST) herniated into the left chest and fetal liver (FL) below the level of the diaphragm.
(Right) Ultrasound demonstrates poor prognosis herniation of fetal liver (FL) into the left chest.
CDH: congenital diaphragmatic hernia.
