Resources for the promotion of safety of children with disabilities
Transportation safety

The Indiana University School of Medicine Automotive Safety Program provides a state-by-state listing of child safety passenger technicians

https://preventinjury.medicine.iu.edu/adaptive-needs/safe-travel-for-all-children-instructors/database

The American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP) policy statement on school bus transportation of children with special needs

pediatrics.aappublications.org/content/108/2/516.full

The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration provides information on child safety seats and federal motor vehicle safety standards and regulations

www.nhtsa.dot.gov
Wheelchair transportation

The University of Pittsburgh's School of Health and Rehabilitation Science, the Rehabilitation Engineering Research Center on Wheeled Mobility, and the National Institute on Disability and Rehabilitation Research provides information about wheelchair transportation

www.wheelchairnetwork.org
Home safety

Easter Seals provides information about home safety for individuals with disabilities

www.easterseals.com/shared-components/document-library/a_safe_home_is_no_accident.pdf
Fire safety

Centers for Disease Control and Prevention

https://www.redcross.org/get-help/how-to-prepare-for-emergencies/types-of-emergencies/fire.html

The US Fire Administration provides information on fire safety for people with disabilities

https://www.usfa.fema.gov/prevention/home-fires/at-risk-audiences/people-with-disabilities/
Respite

The National Respite Network helps caregivers find respite services in their local area

https://archrespite.org/respitelocator
Prevention of maltreatment

The AAP clinical report on maltreatment of children with disabilities

pediatrics.aappublications.org/content/119/5/1018.full

The Child Welfare Information Gateway provides information about reporting of child abuse

https://www.childwelfare.gov/organizations/

The state of Florida Agency for Persons with Disabilities provides information on prevention of abuse, neglect, and exploitation of individuals with disabilities

apd.myflorida.com/zero-tolerance/
