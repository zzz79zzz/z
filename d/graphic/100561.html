Scaphoid fracture on radiography and CT scan
An A-P view of the wrist (A) shows a transverse fracture through the distal third of the right scaphoid (arrow). Image B is a magnified view and shows a simple appearing transverse fracture (arrow). Image C is a coronal reconstruction of the wrist and shows a comminuted fracture (arrow). Image D is a magnified view and confirms the comminuted fracture (arrow), with anatomically aligned components.
A-P: anteroposterior; CT: computed tomography.
