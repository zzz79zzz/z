Butyl cyanoacrylate versus octyl cyanoacrylate application

Butyl cyanoacrylate versus octyl cyanoacrylate application
With the butyl cyanoacrylates, the adhesive is applied as discrete beads (left). With octyl cyanoacrylates, the adhesive is applied as one continuous film across the entire wound (right).
