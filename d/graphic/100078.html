Thromboelastography (TEG) tracing parameters
"R" is the reaction time (the time it takes the coagulation cascade to generate thrombin and fibrin). "K" is the clot firmness. "α" (alpha) is the angle (describes the kinetics of clot formation). MA is the maximum amplitude (describes the maximum clot strength). Ly30 is the percent clot lysis 30 minutes after the MA is reached. Refer to UpToDate topics on platelet function testing and trauma management for details of the use and interpretation of thromboelastography.
