Combination CO2 laser resurfacing for facial acne scars, postoperative
Combination CO2 fractional and non-fractional laser resurfacing. Three-months postoperative photo demonstrating reduced prominence of acne scarring. Notice the typical result of using two-pass fractional CO2 laser resurfacing followed by single-pass traditional CO2 laser resurfacing (no wiping). This procedure is predictably effective, but is associated with more intraoperative pain and postoperative downtime when compared with nonablative laser options.
