Parasternal long axis view with color flow Doppler mapping
Seen is the long axis parasternal view of the left ventricular outflow tract during systole; a normal color flow signal (red-orange) is seen in the left ventricular outflow tract. The occasional blue patches in the signal represent aliasing and suggest that the signal is at or exceeds the Nyquist limit.
Ao: aorta; IVS: interventricular septum; LA: left atrium; LV: left ventricle; MV: mitral valve.
