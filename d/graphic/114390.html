Ultrasound image of midurethral incontinence sling in C shape
3D EVUS image of the right mid-sagittal view of the anterior compartment showing an anterior C-shaped midurethral incontinence sling (asterisk). The sling does not lie parallel to the urethral lumen at rest but remains C-shaped along its width at both rest and during maximal Valsalva, consistent with increased sling tension.
3D: three-dimensional; EVUS: endovaginal ultrasound; A: anterior; B: bladder; C: cephalad; PS: pubic symphysis; U: urethra; T: transducer.
