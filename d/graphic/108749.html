Radiograph of thorax and pelvis in an infant with achondroplasia
AP view of 3-month-old female with achondroplasia. The iliac wings are round, there is lack of caudal widening of the interpedicular distances, the femurs are short, and the proximal ends (metaphyses) have a scooped appearance.
AP: anteroposterior.
