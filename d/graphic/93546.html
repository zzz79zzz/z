Auto-PEEP: Time-flow graphic
Shown on the upper graphic is time versus flow for an individual mechanically ventilated patient. Expiratory flow should normally reach zero before the onset of inspiration. However, when auto-PEEP is present, expiratory flow does not reach zero before inspiration. The arrow indicates that inspiration for each breath begins before expiratory flow has reached zero.
Auto-PEEP: Auto positive end-expiratory pressure; lpm: liters per minute.
