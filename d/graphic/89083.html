Cystic carcinoma of the pancreas with associated ascites on CT scan
A CT scan of the upper abdomen in a patient with pancreatic carcinoma. There is a cystic mass in the tail of the pancreas (arrow) with a moderate amount of ascites (arrowhead). There is also a nodule on the surface of the liver representing metastasis (dashed arrow).
CT: computed tomography.
