V/Q scan and CT of small pulmonary emboli
Small pulmonary emboli. VQ scan left anterior oblique view perfusion image (A) shows a subsegmental defect (arrowhead) reported as intermediate probability of pulmonary embolism. Chest CT pulmonary angiogram (B) shows a thrombus in one of the left lower lobe pulmonary artery branches (arrow).
V/Q: ventilation/perfusion; CT: computed tomography.
