Incidence of non-Hodgkin lymphoma in transplant recipients
Yearly incidence of non-Hodgkin lymphoma after transplantation in heart and kidney transplant recipients over time. The risk is greatest with higher degrees of immunosuppression in the first year and in heart transplant recipients.
NHL: non-Hodgkin lymphoma.
