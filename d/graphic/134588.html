Cystic fibrosis transmembrane conductance regulator (CFTR) gene mutation classes
CFTR: cystic fibrosis transmembrane conductance regulator; CF: cystic fibrosis; DNA: deoxyribonucleic acid; RNA: ribonucleic acid.
* Elexacaftor appears to function as both a corrector (which works synergistically with tezacaftor) and as a potentiator. The mechanism for the latter is not clear.
