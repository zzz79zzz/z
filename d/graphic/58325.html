Hepatorenal recess on thoracic ultrasound
The hepatorenal recess is seen as a curvilinear structure between the liver and kidney. Although of similar shape, echogenicity and orientation as the diaphragm, the hepatorenal recess can be easily identified by identification of the adjacent organs. A similar structure may be seen on the left side and represents the splenorenal recess.
