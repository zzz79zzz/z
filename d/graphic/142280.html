Posterolateral drawer test of knee
The posterolateral drawer test can help to identify soft tissue injuries of posterolateral corner (PLC). With the patient positioned as shown above, the examiner introduces a combined posterior and external rotation force through the proximal tibia, while observing the rotation of the tibial tubercle in reference to the femur. Excessive rotation compared with the asymptomatic side suggests a high-grade PLC injury.
