Management of histologic LSIL (CIN 1) preceded by HSIL cytology
This algorithm describes management of histologic LSIL (CIN 1) preceded by HSIL cytology.
LSIL: low-grade squamous intraepithelial lesion; CIN: cervical intraepithelial neoplasia; HSIL: high-grade squamous intraepithelial lesion; HPV: human papillomavirus; ASCCP: American Society for Colposcopy and Cervical Pathology; ASC-H: atypical squamous cells cannot exclude HSIL.
* Only if colposcopy visualizes the entire squamocolumnar junction and upper limit of lesion(s), and endocervical sampling is <CIN 2.
¶ Except in special populations (pregnant women and those <25 years).
Δ Cytology + colposcopy <25 years; HPV-based testing ≥25 years.
