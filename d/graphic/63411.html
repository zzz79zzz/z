Sigmoid cancer developing in ulcerative colitis, as seen on barium enema
Barium enema study demonstrates a focal stricture in the sigmoid colon caused by an infiltrating cancer. The adjacent bowel is featureless and folds are absent, findings characteristic of chronic ulcerative colitis.
