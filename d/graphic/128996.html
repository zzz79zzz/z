Risk of obstetric outcomes related to maternal age
Predictive probability of miscarriage, PE, SGA fetus, GDM, and LGA fetus plotted against maternal age.
PE: preeclampsia; SGA: small for gestational age; LGA: large for gestational age; GDM: gestational diabetes mellitus.
From: Khalil A, Syngelaki A, Maiz N, et al. Maternal age and adverse pregnancy outcome: a cohort study.
