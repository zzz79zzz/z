Sun-Med tracheal tube introducer (bougie)
The flexible endotracheal tube introducer (often referred to as a "bougie") is an invaluable tool for difficult airway management. Once inserted into the trachea, the introducer serves as a conduit for an endotracheal tube, as shown in the picture.
