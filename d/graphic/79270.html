Chest radiograph with rupture of the left hemidiaphragm
Plain antero-posterior radiograph of the chest in a patient with rupture of the left hemidiaphragm following a motor vehicle accident. The stomach is located in the chest (arrow A) and can be identified from the abnormal location of the nasogastric tube, which is located above the left hemidiaphragm (arrow B).
