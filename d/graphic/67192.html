Eosinophilic folliculitis histology
This high-power photomicrograph demonstrates the preponderance of eosinophils within the follicular, inflammatory infiltrate in the infundibulum and sebaceous gland. Eventually, the number of eosinophils becomes so numerous that an infundibular, eosinophilic pustule may develop.
