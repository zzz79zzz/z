Normal conduction system
Schematic representation of the normal intraventricular conduction system (His-Purkinje system). The Bundle of His divides into the left bundle branch and right bundle branch. The left bundle branch divides into anterior, posterior, and, in some cases, median fascicles.
AV: atrioventricular; RA: right atrium; LA: left atrium; RV: right ventricle; LV: left ventricle.
