Diagnostic criteria for pediatric joint hypermobility
A checklist for children and adolescents that clinicians can use to highlight presence of skin and hernia pathology, musculoskeletal complications, and comorbidities in their determination of the diagnosis pediatric hypermobility spectrum disorder.
EDS: Ehlers-Danlos syndrome; GJH: generalized joint hypermobility; hEDs: hypermobile subtype of EDS.
From: Tofts LJ, Simmonds J, Schwartz SB. Pediatric joint hypermobility: a diagnostic framework and narrative review. Orphanet J Rare Dis 2023; 18:104.
