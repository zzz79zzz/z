Criteria for defining MDR, XDR, CR, and PDR in Acinetobacter spp:
  • MDR: Non-susceptible to ≥1 agent in ≥3 antimicrobial categories*
  • XDR: Non-susceptible to ≥1 agent in all but ≤2 categories
  • CR: Non-susceptible to ≥1 antipseudomonal carbapenem
  • PDR: Non-susceptible to all antimicrobial agents listed
This terminology and these criteria were created by a group of international experts as part of a joint initiative by the European Centre for Disease Prevention and Control (ECDC) and the United States Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC). The criteria for MDR were updated in 2022 by the CDC.

MDR: multidrug resistant; XDR: extensively drug resistant; CR: carbapenem resistant; PDR: pandrug resistant.

* These agents are not included in the definition of MDR.
