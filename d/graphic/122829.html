Lymphedema grading by clinical examination[1,2]
The illustrations depict worsening lymphedema in accordance with Cheng's grading system.
  • Grade 0, reversible lymphedema, exhibits <9% circumference difference.
  • Grade 1, mild lymphedema, exhibits 10 to 19% limb circumference difference. Grade 2, moderate lymphedema, exhibits 20 to 29% limb circumference difference.
  • Grade 3, severe lymphedema, exhibits 30 to 39% limb circumference difference.
  • Grade 4, very severe lymphedema, exhibits >40% limb circumference difference.
References:
