Calcitonin chronically preserves bone density
Mean percent change in lumbar BMD in 87 postmenopausal patients treated with 500 mg of calcium/day alone or with intranasal sCT (50 international units/day). Both treatment regimens were given for 5 days each week. sCT preserved BMD, whereas there was a gradual decline with calcium alone.
BMD: bone mineral density; sCT: salmon calcitonin.
Data from: Reginster JY, Meurmans L, Deroisy R, et al. A 5-year controlled randomized study of prevention of postmenopausal trabecular bone loss with nasal salmon calcitonin and calcium. Eur J Clin Invest 1994; 24:565.
