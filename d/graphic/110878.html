Segmental distribution of hemangiomas in PHACE syndrome
Four segmental patterns were extracted from image analysis of large facial hemangiomas: segment 1 (frontotemporal), segment 2 (maxillary), segment 3 (mandibular), and segment 4 (frontonasal).
PHACE: posterior fossa anomalies, hemangiomas, arterial anomalies, cardiac anomalies, and eye anomalies.
