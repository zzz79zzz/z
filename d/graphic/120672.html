Ultrasound of metatarsal fracture showing edema and increased blood flow
The ultrasound images above show a fracture of the third metatarsal.
(A) The sagittal image shows cortical disruption and hypoechoic edema (arrow) superficial to the fracture site.
(B) The Doppler image shows increased blood flow at the fracture site.
