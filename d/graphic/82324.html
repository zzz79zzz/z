Onset of the menopausal transition
Concentrations of FSH, LH, E2, and inhibin in a volunteer studied at the onset of menopausal transition. The vertical dashed lines represent the times of menses. The horizontal shaded bar represents the young-normal range for FSH. Note the marked fluctuations in hormone levels.
E2: estradiol; FSH: follicle-stimulating hormone; LH: luteinizing hormone.
