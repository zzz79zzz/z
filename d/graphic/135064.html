Color fundus photograph and fundus fluorescein angiogram of a patient with RCVL-S
Color fundus photograph (A, C) and fundus fluorescein angiogram (B, D) of a middle-aged patient with RVCL-S. The color fundus photographs show a cotton wool spot, microaneurysms, telangiectasia, an intraretinal microvascular abnormality (A), neovascularization, and laser scars due to laser photocoagulation (C). The fluorescein angiograms show telangiectasia (B), and laser scars, neovascularization, and ischemic areas (D).
RVCL-S: retinal vasculopathy with cerebral leukoencephalopathy and systemic manifestations.
