Polymorphic eruption of pregnancy (pruritic urticarial papules and plaques of pregnancy)
In fair-skinned patients with polymorphic eruption of pregnancy (PEP), formerly called pruritic urticarial papules and plaques of pregnancy (PUPPP), a white halo may be present around the erythematous papules and plaques.
