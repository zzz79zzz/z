Histologic features of idiopathic T cell pseudolymphoma
Idiopathic (solitary) T cell pseudolymphoma, superficial type. Dense infiltrate in the superficial dermis with sharply defined lower border (A and B). High magnification shows scattered medium-sized to large atypical lymphocytes (C). An identical histologic pattern may be observed in lymphomatoid drug eruptions without systemic symptoms.
