Ultrasound image of embryonic pole with expanded amnion
A 3 mm embryonic pole is visible that lacks cardiac activity. While the small embryo without cardiac activity is not diagnostic for pregnancy loss, the diagnosis of pregnancy loss is confirmed by the presence of the expanded amnion seen in this image. The solid arrow is the embryonic pole. The dotted arrow is the expanded amnion.
