A schwannoma of the brachial plexus by MRI
(A) T1 showing a rounded mass with homogeneous signal intensity similar to muscle.
(B) T2 showing the same mass with high signal intensity, especially on the periphery.
(C) T1 with gadolinium showing homogeneous enhancement except for a small rim, suggesting encapsulation.
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
