Hepatic hemangioma
Dynamic CT scans showing a well-demarcated hypodense lesion on non-contrast scan (panel A) in the posterior right lobe of the liver. Panel B shows early phase of contrast enhancement demonstrating peripheral nodular enhancement with gradual "filling in" of the lesion. The center of the lesion remains hypodense. Panel C shows the post contrast delayed image, which demonstrates an isodense lesion characteristic of hepatic hemangioma.
CT: computed tomography.
