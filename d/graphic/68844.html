Salter II fracture
Oblique and lateral views of the right ankle. Salter Harris type II fracture through the posterior aspect of the distal tibial metaphysis with extension of the fracture line through the physis (arrowhead). The resultant metaphyseal wedge in this fracture is called the Thurston Holland fragment (arrow).
