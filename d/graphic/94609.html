Ear anomalies in fetal alcohol spectrum disorders
"Railroad track" ear is a minor anomaly common among children with fetal alcohol spectrum disorders. Note the upper curve of the outer ear is underdeveloped, folded over, and parallel to the curve beneath it, giving the appearance of a railroad track.
