Three-dimensional transesophageal echocardiogram of complex apical muscular ventricular septal defect
Paired still frame and three-dimensional echocardiographic clips of TEE image of complex apical muscular VSD viewed from the left ventricular aspect of the septum. All three orifices of the complex defect are visible in the moving clip. Arrows denote two of the orifices of the defect in the still frame.
LA: left atrium; LV: left ventricle; VSD: ventricular septal defect; TEE: transesophageal echocardiogram.
