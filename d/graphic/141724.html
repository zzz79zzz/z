Treatment of acquired hemophilia A in adults
Bleeding from acquired hemophilia A can be life-threatening, and treatment should not be withheld while awaiting results of confirmatory or post-diagnostic testing. Refer to UpToDate for the diagnostic evaluation of AHA and a discussion of the risks and benefits and dosing of different therapies for bleeding and different immunosuppressive therapies.
AHA: acquired hemophilia A; BU: Bethesda units.
