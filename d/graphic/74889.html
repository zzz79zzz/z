Computed tomographic scans of the chest
Upper image shows an endotracheal tube (ETT) at the level of the mid-trachea; the wide ring of air surrounding the tube suggests ETT cuff over-inflation, a risk factor for iatrogenic tracheal injury. The lower image demonstrates a focal injury to the posterior tracheal wall and herniation of the cuff into the posterior mediastinum.
