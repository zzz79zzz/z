External otitis: Patient education
Acute external otitis
Use sufficient medication to fill the entire ear canal, typically 4 to 6 drops for an adult ear.
Following instillation of ear drops, patients should lie on their (opposite) side for 3 to 5 minutes or place a cotton ball in the ear canal for 20 minutes.
Protect the ear from water.
In-ear devices (eg, hearing aids, headphone "ear buds") should not be worn until clinical resolution and should be disinfected prior to reuse.
Prevention of external otitis recurrence
Fingers, towels, cotton swabs, or other foreign objects should not be inserted into the canal.
For those who engage in water sports, the use of ear plugs and thorough ear drying is encouraged.
Hearing aids should be removed nightly and cleaned regularly.
