Physical examination maneuver for osteitis pubis
To assess the adductor and lower abdominal muscles, the patient should be placed supine on the examining table with hips and knees flexed 90 degrees. The athlete then performs an isometric adductor muscle contraction against the examiner's fist. A painful isometric muscle contraction is considered a positive test
