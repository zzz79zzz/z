Major midbody pancreatic injury due to blunt trauma in a child
This computed tomography (CT) shows a pancreatic transection of the body (arrow) with associated hematoma/pancreatic fluid within the lesser sac (asterisk) following a handlebar injury. This can be managed with either observation or distal pancreatectomy.
