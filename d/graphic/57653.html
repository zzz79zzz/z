Color Doppler sonogram of an endometrial polyp
Saline infusion reveals a polypoid lesion emanating from the fundus. Color flow Doppler clearly identifies a central feeder vessel. Presence of such a feeder vessel can be used to make the diagnosis of polyp even in the absence of saline infusion.
