Hydrodissection during ESP block
This video shows hydrodissection with a small volume of saline prior to injecting local anesthetic during ESP block. The saline lifts the erector spinae muscle off the spinous process, and appears as a black (anechoic) pocket below the muscle at the end of the video.
ESP: erector spinae plane.
