US pes anserine tendons coronal view
The ultrasound image above shows the pes anserine tendons in a coronal oblique plane. In this view, the tendons are adjacent to the tibial collateral ligament.
Arrows: pes anserine tendons; arrowheads: tibial collateral ligament; dotted ellipses: genicular artery.
Left side of image: caudal; right side of image: cranial.
