Triquetrum fracture CT
Above are radiographic images of a triquetrum fracture. A lateral radiograph of the wrist (A) initially showed a displaced osseous fragment at the level of triquetral bone (arrow). Subsequently, a CT was performed and axial (B) and sagittal reformatted (C) images confirmed the diagnosis of a triquetrum fracture.
CT: computed tomography.
