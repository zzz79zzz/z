Anatomy of an atriofascicular pathway
Schematic showing the normal conduction system (yellow) as well as an atriofascicular pathway (green) connecting the atria directly to the conduction system fascicles, bypassing the AV node.
AV: atrioventricular; LA: left atrium; LV: left ventricle; RA: right atrium; RV: right ventricle.
