Influenza virus pneumonia. High-resolution computed tomography scan in a 34-year-old man who developed influenza virus pneumonia following hematopoietic stem cell transplantation shows small foci of consolidation, patchy ground-glass opacities, and a few centrilobular nodules in the right lower lobe. Also noted are postoperative changes related to previous right upper lobectomy.
