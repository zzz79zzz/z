In-line commercially available percutaneous transtracheal ventilation control valve
A) Positive pressure ventilation is administered by pressing and releasing the valve (or opening and occluding the Y-connector, three-way stopcock, etc.). If necessary, a standard self-inflating resuscitation bag can be used as a temporizing measure. B) Permanent in-line valve PTV system.
