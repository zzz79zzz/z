Two-dimensional echocardiogram right ventricular inflow view
The parasternal RV inflow view from the 2-dimensional echocardiogram shows the RV and RA and the P and A leaflets of the tricuspid valve. The entrance of the coronary sinus into the RA is separated from the entrance of the inferior vena cava by the EV.
RV: right ventricular; RA: right atrium; P: posterior; A: anterior; EV: eustachian valve.
