Chest radiograph showing pneumomediastinum
These AP and lateral views of the chest demonstrate pneumomediastinum (inferior arrows) with air tracking up into the soft tissues of the neck (superior arrows) in a patient with a tracheobronchial injury.
AP: anterior-posterior.
