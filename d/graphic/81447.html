Centerline measurements of abdominal aorta
Centerline measurement of AAA allows the accurate measurement of the treatment length and the landing zone diameters, accounting for tortuosity. It also allows an accurate estimation of the aortic neck angulation between the renal arteries and the aneurysm sac.
AAA: abdominal aortic aneurysm; AP: antero-posterior.
