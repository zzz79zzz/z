Cloacal malformation
Transverse view of fetal abdomen at gestational age of 21 weeks shows a distended loop of bowel (arrows) with echogenic debris, some of which is calcified. A loop of bowel containing calcifications and located in the midabdomen/pelvis is a marker for cloacal malformation.
