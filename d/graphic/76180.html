Needle-knife papillotomy
In this patient with biliary obstruction, attempts to cannulate the bile and pancreatic ducts were unsuccessful using standard guidewire-assisted cannulation. Thus, needle-knife papillotomy was performed by starting the incision from the ampullary orifice (A) and extending it upwards until the bile duct was visualized (B) and cannulated (C).
