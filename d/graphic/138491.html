How to wrap your ankle with an elastic bandage
To wrap your ankle with an elastic bandage:
  • Start with the rolled bandage at the top of your foot below your toes. Wrap toward the inside of your ankle.
  • Loop bandage around your foot and bring it up toward your ankle.
  • Loop bandage around the back of your ankle and bring it down to the opposite side of your foot.
  • Loop bandage under your foot again and repeat the process until the roll is done. Secure.
