The combination of a statin and a bile acid binding resin is more effective than either alone
The reduction in LDL cholesterol concentration and the increase in HDL concentration is greater with the combination of a statin and a bile acid binding resin, given as cholestyramine, when compared with therapy with either drug alone. The effects of both drugs decline exponentially with increasing doses.
HDL: high-density lipoprotein; LDL: low-density lipoprotein.
Data from The Prevastatin Multicenter Study Group II, Arch Intern Med 1993; 153:1321.
