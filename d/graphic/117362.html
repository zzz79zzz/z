Fetal demise with hypercoiled umbilical cord
This umbilical cord is hypercoiled. The normal umbilical cord twists/coils a full 360 degrees over approximately every 5 cm of length, resulting in a cord coiling index (coils per centimeter) of approximately 0.2 or 2 coils per 10 cm.
