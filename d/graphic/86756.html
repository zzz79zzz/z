Piano key test for distal radioulnar joint stability
The piano key sign tests the stability of the distal radioulnar joint. With the patient’s elbow flexed and their forearm pronated, the examiner grasps the ulnar head between their index finger and thumb, and the distal radius with their opposite index finger and thumb, and then pushes the distal ulna in a dorsal and ventral direction
