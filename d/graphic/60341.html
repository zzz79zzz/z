Riedel's thyroiditis: Surgical specimen
Gross thyroid specimen from a patient with Riedel's thyroiditis is characterized by a pale color due to replacement of the thyroid parenchyma with dense, avascular fibrous tissue. The surface of the specimen is covered with ink to indicate the surgical margin.
