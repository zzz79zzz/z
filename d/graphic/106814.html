This ultrasound image shows the iliopsoas tendon in a transverse oblique plane (short-axis view).
AIIS: anterior inferior iliac spine; IP: iliopectineal eminence; arrows: iliopsoas muscle; arrowheads: iliopsoas tendon; dotted circle: femoral nerve; A: femoral artery.
