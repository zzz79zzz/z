Lipodermatosclerosis
This patient exhibits features of severe venous insufficiency combined with lymphedema. Chronic venous insufficiency may result in lipodermatosclerosis, a fibrosing panniculitis characterized by advanced hyperpigmentation and induration, in this case involving most of the leg circumferentially.
