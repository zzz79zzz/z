CT posterior shoulder dislocation reverse Hill Sachs
The CT image above shows a posterior glenohumeral dislocation complicated by a reverse Hill-Sachs impaction fracture of the anteriormedial aspect of the humeral head (arrow). The articular surface of the humeral head (arrowheads) has been forced posterior to the glenoid.
CT: computed tomography; G: glenoid; H: humeral head.
