Adrenal mass in disseminated histoplasmosis
Adrenal computed tomography (CT) shows a markedly enlarged left adrenal gland. This patient had disseminated histoplasmosis with involvement of the lungs, liver, and bone marrow, and had a painful tongue ulcer that on biopsy showed H. capsulatum on methenamine silver stain.
