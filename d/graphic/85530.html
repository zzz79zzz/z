MRI cluster of breast cysts
The T2-weighted MRI image of the breast (A) reveals hyperintense, small, clustered cysts (arrow) and larger isolated cysts (double arrow). The cysts do not show enhancement on the T1-weighted fat-saturated post-contrast sequence (B).
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
