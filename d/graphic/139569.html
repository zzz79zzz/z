Examples of foods to eat and foods to avoid for people on dialysis

This picture shows some examples of foods to eat and avoid (or limit) if you are on dialysis. A dietitian can help you figure out a meal plan that is right for you.

Examples of foods you can eat:

  • Fruits and vegetables like kale, green beans, and apples
  • Proteins like eggs, poultry, and tuna
  • Dairy products like nonfat Greek yogurt and a small glass of milk
  • Grains like white bread, bagels, and pasta

Examples of foods you should avoid or limit:

  • Fruits and vegetables like broccoli and dried fruit (raisins)
  • Instant mixes like biscuits, and salt and salt substitutes
  • Proteins like beans and fast food burgers
  • Dairy products like ice cream and cheese
  • Beverages like cola, and chocolate
  • Grains like whole-wheat bread and bran cereal
