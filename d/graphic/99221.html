Probe start position posterior shoulder examination
The position of the ultrasound (US) transducer to begin the evaluation of the posterior shoulder is shown above. The transducer starts over the scapular spine, and is glided caudally to image the infraspinatus muscle in a short-axis view.
Black rectangle: depiction of the US transducer head; arrow: direction US transducer is glided to identify the infraspinatus muscle.
