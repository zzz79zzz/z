Evaluation of a child for permanent versus transient primary congenital hypothyroidism
This algorithm describes the reevaluation during early childhood for a child with primary CH to determine if the condition is permanent. Reevaluation is rarely needed for children with central hypothyroidism because it is almost always permanent.

CH: congenital hypothyroidism; TH: thyroid hormone; TSH: thyroid-stimulating hormone; fT4: free thyroxine.

* For example, if serum TSH is >10 mU/L after 1 year of age, CH is most likely permanent.
