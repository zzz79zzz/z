Hormone therapy does not affect cognitive function
Data from the Nurses' Health Study shows a lack of relationship between use of hormone replacement therapy in older women without dementia and cognitive function as determined by the telephone version of the MMSE.
MMSE: Mini-Mental State Examination.
Data from: Grodstein F, Chen J, Pollen DA, et al. Postmenopausal hormone therapy and cognitive function in healthy older women. J Am Geriatr Soc 2000; 48:746.
