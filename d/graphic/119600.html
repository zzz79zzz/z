Ultrasound sagittal view of IVC distal to atrium in adult trauma patient
In the appropriate clinical setting, the diameter of the IVC can be measured at end expiration to help gauge fluid status. Image A shows a sagittal view of the IVC, while image B shows the IVC measurement being performed using M mode. The diameter in image B is 1.76 cm.
IVC: inferior vena cava.
