Ultrasound probe position distal biceps tendon long axis
This photograph shows proper position of the ultrasound transducer for imaging the distal biceps tendon in its long axis. The probe lies over the anterior elbow distal to the antecubital fossa, while the patient's elbow is extended and forearm is supinated.
