Autosomal dominant polycystic kidney disease on computed tomography
Axial (A) and coronal (B) noncontrast CT through the kidneys demonstrate replacement of the kidney parenchyma predominantly by simple cysts of varying sizes. Several lesions are of higher attenuation (arrows), usually indicating hemorrhagic content; without contrast material, solid lesions are not adequately assessed.
CT: computed tomography.
