Toe crest pad for flexor digitorum longus tendinitis
Toe crest pads are placed in the sulcus of the second through fifth toes distal to the metatarsal heads. They decrease use of the flexor digitorum longus tendon by creating an upward force on the toes and reducing the degree of toe flexion.
