Triage amalgamated dermoscopic algorithm
Triage amalgamated dermoscopic algorithm (TADA). This algorithm is for lesions on nonglabrous skin. It does not apply to lesions on palms, soles, nails, and mucosae.

* Diagnosis of dermatofibroma and seborrheic keratosis may require toggling between polarized dermoscopy and nonpolarized dermoscopy.

¶ Algorithm is based on use of polarized dermoscopy.

Δ Patients should continue self-monitoring, and changes in morphology or symptoms should raise concern.

◊ Ulceration without history of trauma.

§ White color including shiny structures, circles, and scar-like areas.

¥ Monitoring can include short-term, long-term, or self-monitoring for change.
