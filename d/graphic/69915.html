Intracranial epidural abscess by CT
Axial contrast-enhanced CT scan in a 36-year-old woman with frontal sinusitis who has developed headache and fever. There is a large peripherally enhancing extraaxial fluid collection in the anterior cranial fossa. The biconvex shape is a classic finding with an epidural empyema.
CT: computed tomography.
