Coxa valga radiograph
Plain radiographic findings of bilateral coxa valga that are depicted above include an increased angle between the femoral neck and shaft, which is normally between 120 and 135°, and deformities of the femoral head and acetabulum, including disruption of Shenton line (solid lines), incomplete femoral head coverage, and acetabular dysplasia.
