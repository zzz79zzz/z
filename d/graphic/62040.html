Electrocardiogram (ECG) showing concurrent Mobitz type I (Wenckebach) atrioventricular (AV) block and inferior myocardial infarction (MI)
This rhythm strip shows a Mobitz type I (Wenckebach) atrioventricular block with 4:3 and 3:2 conduction and progressive prolongation of the PR intervals of conducted beats. The marked ST segment elevation suggests acute inferior wall ischemia or infarction that may be responsible for the arrhythmia.
