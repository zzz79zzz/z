Hip arthritis in hemochromatosis
Hip radiograph from a patient with hereditary haemochromatosis. The most prominent abnormality is a zone of subchondral radiolucency in the superior pole with some adjacent sclerosis. There is also modest overall hyaline cartilage thinning and minor osteophytosis.
