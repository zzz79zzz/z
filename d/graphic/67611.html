Classification of leprosy
Diagram representing the different clinical classifications of leprosy using both the World Health Organization and the Ridley-Jopling system. The increase in number of acid-fast bacilli and defects in cell-mediated immunity are represented in the continuum from paucibacillary to multibacillary disease.
TT: tuberculoid; BT: borderline tuberculoid; BB: mid-borderline; BL: borderline lepromatous; LL: lepromatous; CMI: cell-mediated immunity; AFB: acid-fast bacilli; PB: paucibacillary; MB: multibacillary.
Reproduced with permission from Jacobson R, and Krahenbuhl JL. Leprosy. Lancet 1999; 353:655.
