Wobble board stabilization exercise
A wobble board has an unstable surface that rocks in all planes. The wobble board is typically used for rehabilitation and helps to develop balance and proprioception. A double-leg stance on a wobble board is shown in photograph A, and a single-leg stance in photograph B.
