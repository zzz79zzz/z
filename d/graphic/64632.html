Unilateral diabetic glomerulosclerosis
Light micrographs from a postmortem examination of a diabetic patient with unilateral renal artery stenosis on the right side. Classic Kimmelstiel-Wilson nodules are seen in the glomeruli in the left kidney (left panel); in contrast, the glomeruli are normal in the "protected" right kidney (right panel).
