* H. longicornis identification:
  • NVSL: USDA, National Veterinary Services Laboratories. NVSL performs taxonomic identfications and is the prefered lab for confirmation of new states, counties, and hosts.
  • Molecular: confirmation of tick species by PCR or other DNA-based technology.
  • Taxonomic: identfication by key tick features at a lab other than NVSL.
