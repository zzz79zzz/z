CT and radiograph of Type III odontoid fracture
Coronal reconstruction image from a cervical spine CT (A) and a lateral radiograph (B) show a fracture through the C2 vertebral body (arrows). Type III odontoid fractures are mechanically unstable, since they allow the dens and the occiput to move as a unit.
