Sacroiliitis grade 1 and 2
Right sacroiliitis is grade 1 (suspicious of sacroiliitis with no clearly specific abnormality). Left sacroiliitis is grade 2 (minimal changes of sacroiliitis with some loss of definition at the edge, some sclerosis, and perhaps minimal erosions and some joint space narrowing).
