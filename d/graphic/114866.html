The semimembranosus-tibial collateral ligament bursa
This drawing represents the medial view of the knee and demonstrates the relationships of the oval (superficial) and triangular (deep) pockets of the semimembranosus-tibial collateral ligament bursa, which is saddled on semimembranosus.
From: Hennigan SP, Schneck CD, Mesgarzadeh M, Clancy M. The semimembranosus-tibial collateral ligament bursa. Anatomical study and magnetic resonance imaging. J Bone Joint Surg Am 1994; 76:1322.
