Staging breast magnetic resonance image (MRI) for invasive carcinoma poorly assessed on mammography. (A) Preconstrast 3-D FSPGR MRI image with fat suppression. (B) Postcontrast MRI delineates the extent of the invasive carcinoma as well as several clinically and mammographically occult satelite lesions (arrow).
