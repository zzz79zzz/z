Contemporary labor curves by parity
Average labor curves by parity in singleton term pregnancies with spontaneous onset of labor, vaginal delivery, and normal neonatal outcomes. Note that for parous women, the inflection point for acceleration of cervical dilation is at approximately 6 cm and that there is no clear inflection point for nulliparous women.
Data from: Zhang J, Landy HJ, Branch DW, et al. Contemporary patterns of spontaneous labor with normal neonatal outcomes. Obstet Gynecol 2010; 116:1281.
