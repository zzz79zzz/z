Chest computed tomography of a patient with thoracic endometriosis
Computed tomogram (CT) of the chest showing opacities in the right lower lobe (arrow) in a young woman with chest pain during menstruation (panel A). The opacities have completely resolved two weeks later after menstruation (panel B), illustrating the importance of obtaining imaging when patients with suspected thoracic endometriosis are symptomatic peri-menstruation.
From: Chung SY, Kim SJ, Kim TH, et al. Computed tomography findings of pathologically confirmed pulmonary parenchymal endometriosis. J Comput Assist Tomogr 2005; 29:815. Copyright © 2005.
