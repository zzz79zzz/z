Goals/tips:
  • Hand express colostrum and save every drop for your baby
  • Continue to pump often, every 2 to 3 hours, even during the night
  • Ask your nurse or lactation staff for help with hand expression and pumping
  • Ask the lactation team about where you can get a hospital-grade pump for home use
Did you know?
The nurse can use your colostrum to give your baby mouth care.
