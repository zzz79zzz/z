US hip rectus femoris direct tendon in sagittal view
This ultrasound scan shows the direct head of the rectus femoris tendon in the sagittal plane (long-axis view) at its origin on the anterior inferior iliac spine.

Arrows: direct head of rectus femoris tendon; IC: iliocapsularis muscle; SA: sartorius muscle; AIIS: anterior inferior iliac spine.

Left side of image: superior; right side of image: inferior.
