Askling hamstring exercises: The Glider
The Glider is a challenging exercise used to develop hamstring strength, usually during the latter stages of rehabilitation from a muscle strain. It must be performed in a controlled manner.
From: Askling CM, Tengvar M, Thorstensson A. Acute hamstring injuries in Swedish elite football: a prospective randomised controlled clinical trial comparing two rehabilitation protocols. Br J Sports Med 2013; 47:953. Reproduced with permission from BMJ Publishing Group Ltd. Copyright © 2013.
