CT scan of acute necrotizing pancreatitis complicated by infected pancreatic necrosis
Computed tomography (CT) scan reveals gas bubbles (arrow) within an area of pancreatic necrosis. The presence of gas bubbles is a pathognomonic sign of infection of the necrosis.
