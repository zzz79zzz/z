Seasonal variation in venous thromboembolism
Depicted are the monthly percentage variations in French hospital admissions for deep vein thrombosis and pulmonary embolism. Hospital admissions for venous thromboembolism were most frequent in the winter and least frequent in the summer.
Reproduced with permission from: Boulay, F, Berthier, F, Schoukroun, G, et al. Seasonal variations in hospital admission for deep venous thrombosis and pulmonary embolism: analysis of discharge data. BMJ 2001; 323:601. Copyright © 2001, BMJ.
