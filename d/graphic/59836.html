Fracture of the hook of the hamate
The carpal tunnel or 45 degree supination oblique view was used in this radiograph of the wrist in order to isolate the image of the hook of the hamate from the shadows of other carpal bones. In this view, a fracture of the hook of the hamate is easily seen (arrow).
