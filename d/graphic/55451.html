(A) The domain structure of these proteins is shown, with positions of known mutations causing HS and HE/HPP indicated beneath each protein.
(B) The structure of the spectrin tetramer is shown, illustrating the triple helical coiled-coil repeats of the spectrin peptides, the head-to-head spectrin self-association sites, and the nucleation sites that initiate the side-to-side interaction between alpha- and beta-spectrin chains.
RBC: red blood cell; HS: hereditary spherocytosis; HE: hereditary elliptocytosis; HPP: hereditary pyropoikilocytosis.
