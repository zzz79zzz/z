Skin biopsy incision parallel to wrinkle lines
Skin tension lines (Langer's lines) can be revealed by compressing the skin. A diamond-shaped or elliptical excision is outlined with its long axis parallel to the wrinkle lines. Incisions performed parallel to tension lines will close more easily and with a better cosmetic result than those performed at right angles to the tension lines.
