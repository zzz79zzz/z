Clear fluid in the gastric antrum
The antrum is round and distended with thin walls. The content appears anechoic or hypoechoic. The size of the antrum is proportional to the gastric volume. The antrum will appear larger in the right lateral decubitus position compared to the supine position.
R: rectus abdominis muscle; L: liver; A: antrum; D: diaphragm; P: pancreas; AO: aorta.
