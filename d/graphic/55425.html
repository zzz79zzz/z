Estrogen-progestin therapy reduces hip fracture
In the Women's Health Initiative, combined estrogen-progestin replacement therapy was associated with significant reduction in hip fracture (five fewer hip fractures per 10,000 person-years; HR 0.7, unadjusted 95% CI 0.4-1.0).
HR: hazard ratio.
