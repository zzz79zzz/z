Endosalpingiosis in pregnancy
Macroscopic view of the cystic endosalpingiosis lesions after the cesarean delivery. The uterine fundus and part of the body are completely seeded with multitude of cyst-like structures resembling hydatids of Morgagni but with a harder structure due to the muscular component.
