Spinal hemangioblastoma imaging
Multiple spinal intramedullary hemangioblastomas in a patient with von Hippel-Lindau syndrome. Sagittal short-tau inversion recovery (STIR) image (A) shows extensive hyperintense signal abnormality in the cervical and upper thoracic spinal cord. Postcontrast T1-weighted MRI in sagittal (B1) and transverse axial planes (B2, B3) at levels indicated by solid lines demonstrates intensely-enhancing intramedullary tumors (asterisks).
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
