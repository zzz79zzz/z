Cutaneous cribriform carcinoma histopathology
Cutaneous cribriform carcinoma shows a nodular configuration in the dermis with a fibromyxoid stroma and peripheral lymphoid aggregates. Irregular ductal spaces create focal cribriform architecture at the periphery of the tumor in this case (H&E 40x).
H&E: hematoxylin and eosin.
