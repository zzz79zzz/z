Nondisplaced distal phalanx fracture of great toe
On the middle view, note the apparent lucency running obliquely across the medial sesamoid of the great toe (black arrow). This is an illusion created by the overlying cortex of the first metatarsal, and does not represent a fracture. This is supported by the normal appearance of the sesamoid on the other views.
