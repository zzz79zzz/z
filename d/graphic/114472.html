Endovaginal ultrasound images comparing women with intact pelvic floor and disrupted levator ani bilaterally
(A) 3D endovaginal ultrasound image of a woman with an intact pelvic floor.
(B) 3D endovaginal ultrasound image of axial view of bilateral levator ani detachment (arrows).
Note the elongated antero-posterior dimension, marked by the dashed arrows, in (B) compared with (A).
3D: three-dimensional; A: anterior; P: posterior; R: right; L: left; PS: pubic symphysis; U: urethra; T: transducer; LAM: levator ani muscle; AR: anorectum.
