The platelet and its interactions
Schematic drawing of the platelet (top figure), showing its alpha and dense granules and canalicular system. The bottom figure illustrates the platelet's major functions, including secretion of stored products, as well as its attachment, via specific surface glycoproteins (GP), including von Willebrand factor (VWF), to denuded epithelium (bottom) and other platelets (left).
VWF: von Willebrand factor; TSP: thrombospondin; PF4: platelet factor 4; PDGF: platelet derived growth factor; β-TG: beta thromboglobulin; ADP: adenosine diphosphate; ATP: adenosine triphosphate.
