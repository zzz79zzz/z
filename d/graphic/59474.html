Depressed skull fracture with intracerebral hemorrhage.
(A) Lateral radiograph shows a comminuted depressed fracture in the parietal region (arrows).
(B) CT image using bone windows shows the depressed fragment (arrow).
(C) Same image using brain windows shows multiple areas of intracranial hemorrhage (arrows) adjacent to the fracture.
