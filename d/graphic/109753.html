Initial management of erythema induratum
EI: erythema induratum; TB: tuberculosis.
* Symptom-directed therapy includes nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs, rest, elevation, and compression.
¶ Evaluation for TB includes:
  • Tuberculin skin test or interferon-gamma release assay
  • Review of systems
  • Chest radiograph
Δ Basic evaluation includes:
  • Complete blood count
  • Erythrocyte sedimentation rate
  • Liver function tests
  • Hepatitis C virus serology
  • Additional testing as indicated based upon review of systems
