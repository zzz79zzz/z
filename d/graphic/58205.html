Benefit of treating isolated systolic hypertension
Incidence of stroke in older adult patients with isolated systolic hypertension treated with antihypertensive drugs or placebo. At 6 years, active therapy prevented strokes in 4% of patients compared with the placebo group.
Data from SHEP Cooperative Research Group, JAMA 1991; 265:3255.
