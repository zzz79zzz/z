Thoracic aortic aneurysm expansion by location
Linear expansion rate is shown according to segment level; aneurysms located in the mid portion of the thoracic aorta (segments D and E) have the greatest rate of expansion, while those in the ascending aorta (segments A to C) had the slowest rate of growth.
Data from Bonser, RS, Pagano, D, Lewis, ME, et al. Heart 2000; 84:277.
