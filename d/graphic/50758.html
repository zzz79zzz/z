Charged particle radiation of uveal melanoma
Three dimensional model of eye with a 13 mm (diameter) tumor. Magenta outline corresponds to the shape of the treatment aperture. The lens (area outlined in blue), optic disc and nerve (areas outlined in yellow) are excluded from the beam field.
