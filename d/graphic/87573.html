Supraperiosteal infiltration
(A) Nerve supply of the maxillary teeth.
(B) Needle insertion at the mucobuccal fold allows deposition of anesthesia close to the tooth apex.
(C) Minimal depth of needle insertion (about 2 mm) is required for the anterior primary maxillary teeth.
