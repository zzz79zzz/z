Schematic showing anatomic locations of accessory pathways in the left ventricular (LV) free wall
Longitudinal cross section of the free wall of the left ventricle at the level of the atrioventricular (AV) groove. The accessory pathways are present in the AV groove fat pad at variable depths in relation to the mitral annulus and the epicardium overlying the AV groove.
