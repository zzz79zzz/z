Peritoneal carcinomatosis inguinal and umbilical adenopathy on CT
(A) The image is a CT scan through the lower pelvis and shows a low-density right-sided inguinal lymph node in a patient with ovarian cancer. 
(B) The image is a CT scan through the mid-abdomen of a second patient with ovarian carcinoma and shows a tumor nodule in the umbilicus (arrowhead) known as a Sister Mary Joseph node. The image also shows omental metastases (arrow).
(C) The image is a CT scan through the lower pelvis of a patient with fallopian tube carcinoma and shows inguinal adenopathy (double arrow).
CT: computed tomography.
