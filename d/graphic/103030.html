Lisfranc injury on lateral plain radiograph
The lateral x-ray shows a significant subluxation of the first tarsal–metatarsal joint and disruption of the normal dorsal cortical line from the first metatarsal to the medial cuneiform, suggesting a Lisfranc injury.
