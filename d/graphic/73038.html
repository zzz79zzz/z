Chest compression for infant resuscitation: Two fingers technique (Single rescuer)
Chest compressions for infants (under one year) may be performed with two fingers placed on the sternum just below the nipples. This picture shows the site of compressions. When compressions are performed the two fingers used should be perpendicular to the chest and straight.
