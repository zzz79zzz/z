Urinary catheter
A urinary catheter is a thin, flexible tube that drains urine from your bladder. The catheter connects your bladder to a special bag outside of your body. Panel A shows a catheter in a female, and panel B shows a catheter in a male. The bag can attach to your body or be hung from a bed frame (see inset in panel B).
