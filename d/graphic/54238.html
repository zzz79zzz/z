Normal and abnormal urinary flow patterns in the evaluation of bladder dysfunction
Abnormal urine flow patterns can be helpful in identifying underlying causes of voiding dysfunction. A tower curve pattern may be seen in patients with an overactive bladder, a plateau curve in patients with bladder obstruction, a plateau or staccato curve in patients with dysfunctional voiding, and an interrupted curve in patients with an underactive bladder.
