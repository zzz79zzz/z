Subacute cutaneous lupus erythematosus
Light micrograph of skin biopsy demonstrating prominent suprabasilar lymphocytosis accompanied by suprabasilar dyskeratosis in a patient with subacute lupus erythematosus. These findings reflect antibody-dependent cell-mediated injury of suprabasilar keratinocytes which manifest enhanced Ro antigen expression due to UV exposure.
UV: ultraviolet.
