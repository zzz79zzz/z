Compensation to chronic respiratory acidosis
95% significance bands for plasma pH and H+ and HCO3– concentrations in chronic hypercapnia. Because of the compensatory rise in the plasma HCO3– concentration, there is much less change in H+ concentration and pH than in acute hypercapnia.
