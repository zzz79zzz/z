Clinical photographs of a 54-year-old female patient with lower facial skin laxity prior to (A) and 6 months following (B) 3 monthly treatments with broadband infrared light. These photographs demonstrate an average expected outcome from nonablative skin tightening treatment, with a modest reduction in melolabial folds and jowls observed.
