Yale Department of Urology recommended algorithm for management of neonatal testicular torsion
The recommendations are based upon the availability of a team that includes a pediatric urologist/surgeon and pediatric anesthesiologist. If pediatric specialists are unavailable, surgical intervention is delayed until tertiary care access is available. Birth examination occurs either at delivery or the initial newborn examination within 24 hours of delivery.
