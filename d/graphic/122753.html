Completion of EVAC
EVAC is completed when the leak site is completely closed and there is no further contamination. This may appear as a shallow wound bed and possible small amount of outpouching, but the continuity of the gastrointestinal tract is reestablished.
EVAC: endoluminal vacuum therapy.
