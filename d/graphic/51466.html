Anteroposterior and lateral views of the chest demonstrating an elevated right hemidiaphragm in a neonate. On the lateral view, the anterior and posterior diaphragmatic attachments are in the correct positions. With diaphragmatic paralysis, the elevated hemidiaphragm would be uniformly elevated.
