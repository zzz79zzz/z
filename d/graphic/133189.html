Desirable components of pulmonary rehabilitation
Desirable components of the pulmonary rehabilitation model were identified through a Delphi process. A desirable component was defined as having a median score ≤2 (strongly agree or agree) but as having some variation in scores (interquartile range, 1).
ACT: airway clearance techniques.
