Restenosis within a saphenous vein graft
A saphenous vein bypass graft to the obtuse marginal branch of the circumflex artery is shown here. In the mid portion of the graft a long area of irregular narrowing is seen. This represents a restenosis from a previous intervention (stent placement) two months ago.
