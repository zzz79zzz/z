Radiographic appearance of a thymic carcinoid tumor on computed tomography (CT) scanning
Axial contrast-enhanced CT image of the chest showing a homogeneously enhancing anterior mediastinal mass encasing the great vessels and extending to the anterior chest wall, consistent with a pathologically proven thymic carcinoid tumor.
