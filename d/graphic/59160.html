Medial mitral annular time-velocity plots
The medial mitral annular time-velocity plots are derived from color-coded tissue Doppler M-mode echocardiogram. Shown are the plots from a patient with constrictive pericarditis (panel A) and a restrictive cardiomyopathy (panel B).
E: peak early diastolic velocity; A: peak atrial velocity.
