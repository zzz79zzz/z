Brain MRI and CT of Huntington disease
On brain MRI, T1-weighted (A) and T2-weighted (B) axial images show caudate atrophy and enlargement of the frontal horns. On CT, an axial image (C) from another patient shows similar findings.
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging; CT: computed tomography.
