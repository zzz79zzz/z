Pathways in neurally mediated syncope
Schematic representation of the specific syndromes, the suspected peripheral receptors (triggers), the afferent neural pathways, and the efferent pathways responsible for the clinical manifestations of bradycardia and hypotension in specific syncopal syndromes.
GI: gastrointestinal; GU: genitourinary; NTS: nucleus tractus solitarius; V, VII, VIII, IX, and X: cranial nerves.
