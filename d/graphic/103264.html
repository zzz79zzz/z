Metacarpophalangeal extension block splint
Dynamic splints, such as the metacarpophalangeal (MCP) block splint pictured above, are an effective tool for treating stable, extra-articular proximal phalanx fractures, and reduce the number of patients with proximal interphalangeal (PIP) and distal interphalangeal (DIP) joint stiffness following treatment.
