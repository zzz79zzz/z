Chagas' heart disease
Electrocardiogram from a patient with chronic Chagas' disease without evidence of heart failure. The 12-lead ECG shows sinus bradycardia, first degree heart block, and the typical combination of right bundle branch block and left anterior hemiblock. Poor R wave progression and abnormal Q wave in V4 suggest the presence of anterior-apical necrosis.
