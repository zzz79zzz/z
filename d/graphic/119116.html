Magnetic resonance image of closed- and open-lip schizencephaly
Closed- and open-lip unilateral schizencephaly.
(A) Coronal T1-weighted image shows irregular gray matter extending from the cortex to the ventricular surface. The lips of the cleft come in contact (arrows) in only part of the cleft.
(B) Coronal T2 image shows a moderate-sized, gray matter–lined open-lip cleft (arrows) into the right sylvian fissure.
