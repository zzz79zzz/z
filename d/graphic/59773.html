Dilated cardiomyopathy chest radiograph
This plain frontal radiograph of the chest in a 51-year-old male demonstrates marked enlargement of the cardiac silhouette compatible with a dilated cardiomyopathy. Cardiomegaly is nonspecific and can be seen with any etiology of cardiomyopathy.
