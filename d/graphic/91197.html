Invasion of lung cancer into the mediastinum on CT scan
A non-contrast CT scan in axial projection (A) and with coronal reconstruction (B) shows a paramediastinal lung carcinoma invading the mediastinum (arrows). Contrast-enhanced CT scans are generally preferred for the evaluation of lung care; contrast is particularly helpful in assessing invasion of the mediastinum.
CT: computed tomography.
