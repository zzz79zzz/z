Nonhealing toe amputation wound
This picture depicts an ischemic wound following amputation of the 2nd, 3rd, and 4th digits at the metatarsal-phalangeal joint level. A fibrotic wound base remains with little to no evidence of healing. There is no granulation tissue or epithelial ingrowth.
