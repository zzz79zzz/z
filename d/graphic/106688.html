Computed tomography (CT) angiography with 3-dimensional reconstruction showing an extralobar bronchopulmonary sequestration in a two-month old infant. The study demonstrates the large thoracic feeding artery (arrow) and communication with the dilated right inferior pulmonary vein (arrowhead).
