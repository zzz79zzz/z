Mastectomy incisions for tumors of the inner quadrants of the breast
Design of the obliquely placed modiﬁed Stewart incision for cancer of the inner quadrant of the breast. The medial extent of the incision often must incorporate skin to the midsternum to allow a 1 to 2 cm margin in all directions from the edge of the tumor. Lateral extent of the incision ends at the anterior margin of the latissimus.
