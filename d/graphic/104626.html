US medial collateral ligament coronal view
The ultrasound image above shows the medial collateral ligament (outlined by the arrows) in a coronal plane (long-axis).
Arrows: superficial layer of medial collateral ligament (tibial collateral ligament); arrowheads: deep layer of medial collateral ligament (meniscotibial ligament); *: deep layer of medial collateral ligament (meniscofemoral ligament); MM: medial meniscus.
Left side of image: caudal; right side of image: cranial.
