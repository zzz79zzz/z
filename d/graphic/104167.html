Sleep spindles of NREM 2 sleep
30-second epoch of PSG showing sleep spindles of NREM 2 sleep (which may linger into early NREM 3) may be more prominent over the frontal, central, or occur independently over the frontal and central regions in children younger than 13 years.
PSG: polysomnography; NREM: non-rapid eye movement.
