Magnetic resonance image of cleft soft palate
(A) T2 weighted magnetic resonance imaging of fetus at 20 weeks gestational age with micrognathia and cleft soft palate (without cleft lip). Note how the tongue is high in the oropharynx and there is absence of the soft palate.
(B) Magnetic resonance imaging of normal fetal soft palate (arrow).
T: tongue.
