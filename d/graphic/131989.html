Tubercular posterior uveitis: Funduscopic findings
(A) Multifocal retinitis, retinal vasculitis and focal vascular tortuosities. The optic disc is edematous and neovascularization is seen over the disc.
(B) Following antituberculous treatment, resolved vasculitis and optic disc edema. Peripheral photocoagulation spots are seen; this intervention was performed to prevent further neovascularization.
