United States data on fetal, perinatal, and neonatal mortality
Fetal mortality, perinatal mortality, late-fetal mortality, and early NMRs: 2000, 2006, 2011, and 2012. Note: FMR indicates fetal deaths per 1000 live births plus fetal deaths; early FMR, early-fetal deaths (20 to 27 weeks) per 1000 live births plus fetal deaths; late FMR, late fetal deaths (≥28 weeks) per 1000 live births plus fetal deaths; PMR, late-fetal deaths and early neonatal (<7 days of age) deaths per 1000 live births plus fetal deaths; early NMR, early-neonatal (<7 days of age) deaths per 1000 live births.
FMR: fetal mortality rate; PMR: perinatal mortality rate; NMR: neonatal mortality rate; NCHS: National Center for Health Statistics.
