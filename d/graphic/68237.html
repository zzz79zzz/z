Nasal calcitonin improves bone pain
Effect of intranasal salmon calcitonin or placebo on the intensity of spontaneous bone pain (score from 0 to 20) in females with recent osteoporotic spinal fractures. Salmon calcitonin led to a more rapid reduction in pain.
sCT: salmon calcitonin.
Data from: Gennari C, Agnusdei D, Camporeale A. Use of calcitonin in the treatment of bone pain associated with osteoporosis. Calcif Tissue Int 1991; 49 Suppl 2:S9.
