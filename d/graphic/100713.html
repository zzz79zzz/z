Optic pathway glioma on MRI
An axial T2-weighted MRI (A) and coronal T2-weighted image (B) show diffuse, high signal intensity of a large, left intraorbital optic nerve glioma (arrow). An axial T1-weighted image following gadolinium (C) shows diffuse, intense enhancement of the tumor (arrow).
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
