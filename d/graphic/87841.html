Anterior uveitis due to ocular tuberculosis
(A) Granulomatous keratic precipitates and posterior synechiae in 12-year-old girl.
(B) Fundus examination shows neuroretinitis in the right eye.
(C) Fundus examination shows optic neuritis in the left eye.
(D) Patient presented again after 10 years, with no recurrence of uveitis. The right eye disc is pale due to partial optic atrophy.
(E) Normal disc of left eye, no active uveitis after 10 years.
