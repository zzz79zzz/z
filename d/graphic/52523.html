Abnormal ultrasound in a child with Clostridioides difficile colitis
Right lower quadrant intestinal ultrasound, 13-year-old with Down syndrome and acute lymphoblastic leukemia. This was the third episode of frequent voluminous watery diarrhea without blood or mucus following initiation of intravenous antimicrobial treatment for fever and neutropenia. C. difficile toxin A/B enzyme immunoassay was positive. Plain films showed marked paucity of intestinal gas; ultrasonography showed up to 4 mm of bowel wall thickening in both right (above) and left lower quadrants.
