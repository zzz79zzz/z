Increased stroke risk with low-dose warfarin plus aspirin in AF
Results from the SPAF III trial of high-risk patients showing significantly higher event rates for intracranial hemorrhage and ischemic stroke in patients treated with fixed low-dose warfarin plus aspirin compared with standard adjusted-dose warfarin. The risk was greater in those with a previous thromboembolic event.
AF: atrial fibrillation.
Data from Stroke Prevention in Atrial Fibrillation Investigators. Lancet 1996; 348:633.
