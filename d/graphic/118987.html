Spontaneous pneumomediastinum complicated by pneumorrhachis (air in the spinal canal)
In the 14-year-old girl with sudden onset of chest pain, the CT scan of thorax and neck demonstrates a normal lung parenchyma, subcutaneous emphysema, and air in the spinal space (A), and some mediastinal air (B).
CT: computed tomography.
