Port wine stain affecting an adult with Sturge-Weber syndrome
The appearance of the facial port wine stain changes with age. In the newborn, the lesion is flat and usually light pink in color. It typically darkens with age to a deep red, port wine appearance, and vascular ectasias develop as depicted above.
