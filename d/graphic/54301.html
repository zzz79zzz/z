Metatarsal stress fracture
In panel A, there is mild narrowing of the medullary canal of the second metatarsal, one of the earlier signs of stress fracture. Callus formation can be seen as early as four to five weeks after symptom onset (panel B). After three months, there is well organized callus (panel C).
