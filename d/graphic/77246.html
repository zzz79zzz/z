Gestational sac without yolk sac or embryo
Transverse view of the uterus shows an abnormally shaped intrauterine gestational sac with mean sac diameter of 31 mm. At this sac size, a yolk sac and embryo should be seen but were not. Arrows indicate the empty amnion, which is consistent with an early pregnancy loss.
