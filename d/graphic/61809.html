Survival in acute coronary syndromes
Among 12,142 patients entered into GUSTO IIb, the one-year survival was the same for those with an ST elevation (Q wave) or non-ST elevation (non Q-wave) myocardial infarction (NSTEMI) who did not have recurrent ischemia (90.4 versus 91.2 percent); a higher one-year survival was seen in patients presenting with unstable angina (93 percent, p <0.001).
Redrawn from Armstrong PW, Fu Y, Chang WC, et al, for the GUSTO-IIb Investigators. Circulation 1998; 98:1860.
