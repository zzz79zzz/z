Adenoviral inclusions
Light micrograph of adenovirus type 5 hepatitis in a bone marrow transplant recipient who died from fulminant liver failure. Intranuclear viral inclusions are present within multiple hepatocytes, representing cells at different stages of infection with adenovirus (arrows). A "smudge" cell with a large inclusion that obscures the nucleus is present (center arrow).
