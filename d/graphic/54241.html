Grafting technique for Peyronies disease
Grafting procedures can be performed in men with Peyronie's disease who have a short penis, extensive plaque, or complex deformities. Grafting lengthens and straightens the concave side of the penis. Dissection of the neurovascular bundle and/or corpus spongiosum may be required depending upon the location of the plaque.
