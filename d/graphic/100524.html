Flow cytometry for CD11b in leukocyte adhesion deficiency I (LAD-I).
Flow cytometry on stimulated neutrophils from an individual with normal neutrophil function, an LAD-I carrier (heterozygote), and moderate and severe LAD-I, showing CD11b fluorescence (solid line) versus a nonspecific control (dashed line).  Percentages refer to the amount of fluorescence intensity relative to the normal control.
