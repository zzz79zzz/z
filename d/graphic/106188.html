Computed tomography of interstitial lung involvement from rheumatid arthritis
Axial (A and C) and coronal (B) computed tomographic images of rheumatoid arthritis-associated interstitial lung disease. Images show ground-glass and consolidative opacities involving the lower two-thirds of the lungs that spare the upper lobe. This pattern of lung involvement corresponds to cellular nonspecific interstitial pneumonia.
