Hand and wrist anatomy palmar view
Arterial anastomoses of the hand. The radial and ulnar arteries connect to each other through two loops: a superficial palmar arch and a deep palmar arch. This ensures adequate perfusion in the event of lacerations, which are common injuries to the fingers.
