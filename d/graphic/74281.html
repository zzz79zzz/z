Microfollicular adenoma: Findings on fine-needle aspirate
Fine-needle aspirate of a thyroid nodule showing microfollicles with little or no colloid in the background. There is no clumping or pleomorphism. Nevertheless, surgical excision was advised because of the microfollicular pattern. The nodule was benign.
