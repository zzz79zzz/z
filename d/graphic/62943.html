Pulmonary artery wedge pressure tracing
This schematic diagram shows the different components of the pulmonary artery wedge pressure tracing. A simultaneous ECG is shown to demonstrate the timing of the different components. The peak of the 'v' wave falls outside the peak of the electrocardiographic T wave.
Redrawn from Gore, JM, Alpert, JS, Benotti, JR, et al. Handbook of hemodynamic monitoring, 1st ed, Boston, Little Brown & Co, 1985.
