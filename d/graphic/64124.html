Sinus rhythm with complete heart block
Sinus rhythm with complete (third-degree) heart block. There is independent atrial (as shown by the P waves) and ventricular activity, with respective rates of 83 and 43 beats/min. The wide QRS complexes may represent a junctional escape rhythm with underlying bundle branch block or an idioventricular pacemaker.
