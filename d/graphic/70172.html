Pneumonia with air bronchogram
Lymphocytic interstitial pneumonia in a patient with HIV infection. The main changes are ground glass opacification in the right middle lobe and consolidation suggestive of air space disease in the right lower lobe with a clearly visible air bronchogram.
HIV: human immunodeficiency virus.
