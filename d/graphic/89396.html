Schematic representation of the dermoscopic patterns of melanocytic lesions located on the palms and soles
The parallel furrow, lattice-like, and fibrillar patterns are major dermoscopic patterns seen in acquired melanocytic nevus of the palms and soles, whereas the parallel ridge pattern is the most sensitive and specific dermoscopic pattern detected in acral melanoma.
