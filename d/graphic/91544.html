Computed tomogram of bronchiolitis obliterans
Computed tomography (CT) image of a patient with bronchiolitis obliterans. A mosaic pattern of attenuation is present in which higher attenuation indicates normal lung with relatively greater perfusion, whereas lower attenuation areas are caused by air trapping and hypoxic vasoconstriction in areas with narrowed small airways.
