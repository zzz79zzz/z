Moraxella catarrhalis in sputum
Gram stain of sputum (x1000) shows inflammatory cells and gram-negative diplococci. Moraxella (formerly Branhamella) catarrhalis grew from this patient's sputum. Although encountered less frequently, either Neisseria or Acinetobacter could have this appearance.
