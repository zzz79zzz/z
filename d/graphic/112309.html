Intermittent Explosive Disorder Screening Questionnaire (IED-SQ)
Instructions: Most everyone experiences aggressive thoughts or behaviors at different points in life. Please answer the following questions about your experiences regarding this as accurately as possible. Your responses will remain completely confidential.
Part 1:
How many times would you say you have done the following things over the course of your life, since you turned age 13?
0 = Never happened (eg, zero)
1 = Only happened "once" (eg, one)
2 = Happened "a couple" or "a few" times (eg, two to three)
3 = Happened "several" times (eg, four to nine)
4 = Happened "many" times (eg, 10 or more)
5 = Happened "so many" times that I couldn't give a number
  0 1 2 3 4 5
1. "Throw" a temper tantrum (eg, screaming, slamming doors, throwing things when frustrated to the "breaking point")            
2. Get into verbal fights or arguments with other people            
3. Deliberately struck or deliberately broke objects (eg, windows, dishes, etc) in anger            
4. Get into physical fights with other people            
5. Deliberately struck or hit another person in anger

Thank you for completing the Intermittent Explosive Disorder Screening Questionnaire.
IED-SQ scoring key:
Part 1
  • Lifetime aggression positive: ≥12 on Part 1 items
Part 2
  • A1 positive: ≥3 months on question 1a.
  • A2 positive: ≥3 times on question 2.
  • C positive: "Unplanned" on question 3.
  • D positive: "Yes" on question 4a or 4b.
  • E positive: ≥6 years of age on question 1b or 2a.
  • F positive: "Yes" on question 5a or 5b.
IED-5 positive if:
  • Lifetime aggression positive and
  • A1 or A2 positive and
  • C positive and
  • D positive and
  • E positive and
  • F positive
IED-5 current if:
  • "Yes" on question 1C and/or 2b
