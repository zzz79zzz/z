Skin changes of chronic venous insufficiency
Longstanding edema in this patient with chronic venous insufficiency led to moderately advanced pigment changes on the medial and lateral ankles, which extend onto the dorsum of the foot. The left medial ankle displays a healed venous ulcer below the malleolus; the right lateral ankle has a small active venous ulcer (arrow).
