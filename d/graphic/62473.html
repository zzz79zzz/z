Effect of octreotide on serum IGF-1
In this study of previously untreated acromegalic patients, 18 with macroadenomas and 6 with microadenomas, short-acting octreotide was administered for 24 weeks and then long-acting octreotide to 15 of the patients for another 24 weeks. Open circles represent the pre-treatment IGF-1 concentration, bull's eye marks the values at 24 weeks, and the closed circles the values at 48 weeks. N indicates normal age-related IGF-1 concentration at latest assessment.
IGF-1: insulin-like growth factor-1.
Data from: Bevan JS, Atkin SL, Atkinson AB, et al. Primary medical therapy for acromegaly: an open, prospective, multicenter study of the effects of subcutaneous and intramuscular slow-release octreotide on growth hormone, insulin-like growth factor-I, and tumor size. J Clin Endocrinol Metab 2002; 87:4554.
