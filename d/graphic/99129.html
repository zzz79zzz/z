Pre- and postoperative radiography for Grade III AC separation using Zanca view
Image A is a Zanca view of the left AC joint showing a grade III AC separation (arrow). Image B is a postoperative radiograph using the Zanca view to demonstrate the AC joint (arrow) following left coracoclavicular ligament reconstruction with semitendinosis allograft, fibertape repair, and focal resection of the osteoarthritic distal clavicle.
AC: acromioclavicular.
