Ocular findings of carotid cavernous fistula
(A) A six-year-old girl presented with injection of the right eye for four months since being hit with a fishing pole. The examination showed mild proptosis and engorgement of the episcleral vessels.
(B) A dilated fundus examination showed dilatation of the retinal vasculature in the right compared to the left eye. A computed tomography angiogram showed a right carotid cavernous fistula.
