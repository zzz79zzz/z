Sarcoidosis: Galaxy sign
Coronal high-resolution computed tomography (CT) demonstrates a large right upper lobe nodule surrounded by multiple small nodules (arrows), resembling a galaxy. Also noted are nodules along the right major fissure (dashed arrows) and mild involvement of the left lung.
