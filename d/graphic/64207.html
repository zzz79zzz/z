Glucose-induced hyperkalemia in diabetes
Effect of a glucose load on the plasma potassium concentration in diabetics (solid line) and nondiabetics (dashed line). Glucose led to a fall in the plasma potassium concentration in nondiabetics due to stimulation of insulin release but a rise in diabetics due to the elevation in plasma osmolality.
Data from: Nicolis GL, Kahn T, Sanchez A, et al. Arch Intern Med 1981; 141:49.
