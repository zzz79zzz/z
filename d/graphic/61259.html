Phalanx fracture reduction: Three-point technique
Reduction of a middle phalanx fracture is performed by applying longitudinal traction, and then using three-point pressure (shown above) to bring the distal fragment into alignment with the proximal fragment. Each thumb represents one point and adjacent index fingers a third point.
