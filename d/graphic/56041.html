Neonatal herpes simplex virus scalp vesicles
Scalp lesions of neonate with skin, eye, and mouth neonatal HSV infection associated with fetal scalp monitor. Gram-stained smear and bacterial cultures were negative, and the lesions did not respond to topical and systemic antibiotics. Viral cultures grew HSV type 2, and the lesions responded to intravenous acyclovir.
