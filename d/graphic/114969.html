Knee valgus during walking gait
These images above provide a contrast between normal knee motion (B) and slight valgus motion (A). In the image of the valgus knee, the patella lies medial to a line drawn between the hip and ankle joints. Note also that the knees are closer together as they pass one another in the patient with valgus knee.
