Chest CT of COVID-19 pneumonia, exudative phase
Chest CT, coronal reformation, displays extensive bilateral ground-glass opacities with a predilection for the subpleural lung parenchyma, which is a classic CT finding of the exudative phase of COVID-19 pneumonia.
