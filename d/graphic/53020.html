Diffuse alveolar damage with alveolar hemorrhage
Light micrograph of diffuse alveolar damage, as can occur in the acute respiratory distress syndrome. The alveolar septa are edematous but without acute inflammation. Hyaline membranes are lining the alveolar spaces (arrow), and alveolar hemorrhage is present.
