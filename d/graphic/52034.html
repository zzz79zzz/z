Causes of cryoglobulinemia

Causes of cryoglobulinemia
Idiopathic
Infections
Viral
  • Adenovirus
  • Hantavirus
  • Hepatitis viruses: A, B, C
  • Herpes viruses
    • Epstein-Barr
    • Varicella-Zoster
  • Human T cell leukemia virus (HTLV)-I
  • HIV
  • Influenza virus
  • Parvovirus B19
  • Rubella virus
Bacterial
  • Brucella
  • Infective endocarditis
  • Leprosy
  • Lyme disease
  • Rickettsia, including Coxiella burnetii (Q fever)
  • Poststreptococcal disease
  • Syphilis
Fungal
  • Coccidiomycosis
Parasitic
  • Echinococcosis
  • Leishmaniasis
  • Malaria
  • Schistosomiasis
  • Toxoplasmosis
  • Trypanosomiasis
Autoimmune
Systemic rheumatic diseases
  • Drug-induced lupus
  • Polymyositis
  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Sarcoidosis
  • Sjögren's syndrome
  • Systemic lupus erythematosus
  • Systemic sclerosis
  • Systemic vasculitis
    • Behçet syndrome
    • Immunoglobulin A (IgA) vasculitis (Henoch-Schönlein purpura)
    • Kawasaki disease
    • Polyarteritis nodosa (+/– hepatitis B)
Dermatologic disease
  • Cold-induced urticaria
  • Cutaneous small-vessel vasculitis
  • Epidermolysis bullosa acquisita
  • Erythema elevatum diutinum
  • Erythema multiforme
  • Pemphigus vulgaris
  • Pyoderma gangrenosum
Gastrointestinal disease 
  • Celiac disease
  • Crohn disease
  • Ulcerative colitis
Paraneoplastic
  • Atrial myxoma
  • Bone marrow transplantation
  • Lung cancer
  • Lymphoproliferative disease
    • Leukemia
      • Chronic lymphocytic leukemia
      • Chronic myeloid leukemia
      • Hairy cell leukemia
    • Lymphoma: Hodgkin and non-Hodgkin
    • Plasma cell disorders
      • Dysproteinemias
      • Macroglobulinemia, primary or secondary (eg, Waldenström)
      • Multiple myeloma
      • POEMS syndrome
    • Angioimmunoblastic lymphadenopathy
  • Kaposi sarcoma
  • Mycosis fungoides
  • Nasopharyngeal carcinoma
Iatrogenic
  • Bacille Calmette-Guerin administration
  • Influenza vaccine
  • Pneumococcal vaccine
Familial
  • C4 deficiency
  • C3 deficiency
  • Factor XIII deficiency
Miscellaneous
  • Cirrhosis
    • Biliary, Laënnec, postnecrotic
  • Endomyocardial fibrosis
POEMS: polyneuropathy, organomegaly, endocrinopathy, monoclonal protein, skin changes.
