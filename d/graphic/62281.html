Typical appearance of pancreatic adenocarcinoma
Multidetector enhanced CT of two different patients, in which ill-defined hypodense, solid tumors are present in the head and uncinate process of the pancreas (arrows). Typically, normal pancreatic tissue enhances to a greater extent than do pancreatic adenocarcinomas.
CT: computed tomography.
