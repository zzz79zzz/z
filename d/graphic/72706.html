Cystic bronchiectasis
Post-tuberculous bronchiectasis characterized by marked destruction of the right upper lobe with cylindrical and cystic bronchiectasis and cicatrization atelectasis. These findings explain the ipsilateral rotational shift of the mediastinum. Cylindrical and varicose bronchiectasis is present in the left upper lobe.
