Upper extremity compartments: Contents and clinical signs
Compartment Muscle Artery Nerve Clinical signs
Shoulder and brachium
Anterior Biceps, brachialis, coracobrachialis Brachial Musculocutaneous
  • Swelling and tenderness of the biceps
  • Rest pain in the anterior side of the brachium
  • Pain caused by passive extension of the elbow
Posterior Triceps Profunda brachii Radial
  • Swelling and tenderness of the triceps
  • Rest pain in the posterior side of the brachium
  • Pain caused by passive flexion of the elbow
  • Numbness and pain in a distribution innervated by the superficial branch of the radial nerve (radial dorsal side of the hand)
Deltoid Deltoid – Axillary
  • Swelling and tenderness of the deltoid
  • Rest pain of the lateral side of the shoulder
Forearm
Volar
Superficial Pronator teres, flexor carpi radialis, palmaris longus, flexor digitorum superficialis, flexor carpi ulnaris Radial, ulnar Median, ulnar, anterior interosseous
  • Numbness, tingling, and rest pain in a distribution of the median and ulnar nerve
  • Rest pain in the volar side of the forearm
  • Pain caused by passive extension of the wrist and fingers
Deep Flexor pollicis longus, flexor digitorum profundus, pronator quadratus Radial, ulnar Median, ulnar, anterior interosseous
  • Rest pain in the volar side of the forearm
  • Pain caused by passive extension of the wrist and fingers
Dorsal
Superficial Extensor digitorum communis, extensor digiti minimi, extensor carpi ulnaris Posterior interosseous Posterior interosseous
  • Rest pain in the dorsal side of the forearm
  • Pain caused by passive flexion of the wrist and fingers
Deep Abductor pollicis longus, extensor pollicis brevis, extensor pollicis longus, extensor indicis proprius, supinator Posterior interosseous Posterior interosseous
  • Rest pain in the dorsal side of the forearm
  • Pain caused by passive flexion of the wrist and fingers
Middle wad Brachioradialis, extensor carpi radialis longus/brevis – Radial
  • Numbness, tingling, and rest pain in a distribution of the radial nerve
  • Rest pain in the lateral side of the forearm
  • Pain caused by passive extension of the elbow and flexion of the wrist
Hand
Thenar Abductor pollicis brevis, opponens pollicis, flexor pollicis brevis Digital Recurrent motor of median nerve
  • Swelling and tenderness of the volar radial side of the hand
Hypothenar Abductor digiti minimi, opponens digiti minimi, flexor digiti minimi – Ulnar
  • Swelling and tenderness of the ulnar side of the hand
Adductor Adductor pollicis – Ulnar
  • Swelling and tenderness of the radial side of the hand
Interosseous Interosseous muscles (4 dorsal, 3 palmar) – Ulnar
  • Swelling and tenderness of the dorsal side of the hand
Carpal tunnel (Tendons) flexor digitorum profundus, flexor digitorum superficialis, flexor pollicis longus – Median
  • Numbness, tingling, and rest pain in thumb, index finger, and middle finger, and the radial half of the ring finger
  • A positive Phalen test*
  • A positive Tinel sign¶ in the carpal tunnel
Digit – Digital Digital
  • Numbness, tingling, and rest pain in the half of the affected finger
  • Pain caused by passive extension of the affected finger
  • Swelling of the finger
* Phalen test: Pain or paresthesia in the median-innervated fingers with more than one minute of wrist flexion. The wrists can be flexed with the arms extended, or the backs of the hands can be placed against each other to provide hyperflexion of the wrist.
¶ Tinel sign: Pain or paresthesia of the median-innervated fingers that occurs with percussion over the median nerve just proximal to the carpal tunnel.
