Prevalence of diabetes mellitus in youth by age group
Prevalence of type 1 and type 2 diabetes mellitus in youth in the United States, from the SEARCH for Diabetes in Youth study group, 2001 to 2017. For type 1 diabetes, there are 2 peaks in incidence: in mid-childhood and early puberty. This bimodal distribution is not evident from the age categories used for this figure. For type 2 diabetes, there were no data for children <10 years.
Data from: Lawrence JM, Divers J, Isom S, et al. Trends in prevalence of type 1 and type 2 diabetes in children and adolescents in the US, 2001-2017. JAMA 2021; 326:717.
