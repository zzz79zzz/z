Pentrating ulcer with hematoma on radiography and CT scan
A frontal chest radiograph (A) shows an enlarged aortic knob (arrow) and a prominent aorto-pulmonary window (arrowhead). A computed tomography scan (B) through the aortic arch shows a penetrating ulcer with a mural hematoma (arrowhead).
CT: computed tomography.
