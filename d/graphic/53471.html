CT scan of the chest in a patient with cystic fibrosis demonstrating mild cylindrical bronchiectasis, with thickened bronchial walls involving the upper lobes and the superior segments of the lower lobes. Note the subsegmental atelectasis in the posterior segment of the right upper lobe.
CT: computed tomography; CF: cystic fibrosis.
