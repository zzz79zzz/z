Fluoroscopic upper gastrointestinal series in an infant with hypertrophic pyloric stenosis

Images from a barium contrast upper gastrointestinal series demonstrate a dilated stomach (asterisk) with:

(A) The "string sign" showing an elongated and narrowed pyloric canal (arrow).

(B) The "beak" sign (arrowhead), with contrast pointing toward the narrowed canal with impressions from the enlarged pyloric muscle.
