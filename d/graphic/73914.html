Respiratory change from a patient with constrictive pericarditis
Left ventricular (LV) and right ventricular (RV) tracings from a patient with constrictive pericarditis. There is early rapid filling in diastole and equalization of the diastolic pressures. There also is discordance of the LV and RV systolic pressures with respiration due to enhancement of ventricular interdependence.
