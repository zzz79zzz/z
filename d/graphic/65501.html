Lateral soft tissue radiographic demonstration of retropharyngeal abscess
Lateral radiographs of the neck show: (A) normal lateral cervical view; (B) expansion of the prevertebral soft tissues by a retropharyngeal abscess. The arrows in both images indicate the anterior border of the retropharyngeal space.
