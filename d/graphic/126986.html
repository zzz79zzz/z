SVS/STS classification of aortic dissection by zone[1]
The figure illustrates the zones of the aorta, which can be used to classify aortic dissection. In this example the dissection originates in zone 3 (type B aortic dissection), and terminates in zone 9. An aortic dissection originating in zone 0 would be a type A aortic dissection.
SVS: Society for Vascular Surgery; STS: Society of Thoracic Surgeons.
Reference:
  1. Lombardi JV, Hughes GC, Appoo JJ, et al. Society for Vascular Surgery (SVS) and Society of Thoracic Surgeons (STS) reporting standards for type B aortic dissections. J Vasc Surg 2020; 71:723.
