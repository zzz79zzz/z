Normal CC (craniocaudal) views of both breasts
By convention, the mammograms of both breasts are viewed as mirror images. The upper half represents the lateral or outer aspect of the breast and the lower half represents the medial or inner aspect of the breast. The nipples are in profile and normal fat is visible between the edge of the film and the glandular tissue.
