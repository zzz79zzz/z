Tuberous sclerosis-lymphangioleiomyomatosis complex
Tuberous sclerosis-lymphangioleiomyomatosis complex. Noncontrast (A) and postcontrast CT (B) show innumerable bilateral fat-containing lesions (AMLs) consistent with TSC. Imaging through the lung bases (C) shows numerous cysts in the lungs compatible with lymphangioleiomyomatosis (TSC-LAM).
AML: angiomyolipoma; CT: computed tomography; TSC: tuberous sclerosis.
