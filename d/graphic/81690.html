This electrocardiogram (ECG) illustrates repetitive monomorphic ventricular tachycardia (RMVT) with a right bundle, inferior axis morphology signifying its left ventricular site of origin. This VT was localized to the area of the aorto-mitral continuity in the left ventricular outflow tract (LVOT).
