Wound repigmentation
Repigmentation of healed wounds occurs as melanocytes migrate from the epidermal appendages across the wound bed. Skin color in a healed wound is difficult to predict. Exposure to ultraviolet radiation can permanently increase the pigmentation. Bleaching agents are discouraged because of the risk of further tissue damage.
