Congenital herpes simplex viral disease with wedge shaped involvement of the adrenal cortex in a markedly autolyzed 28 week fetus. Calcified infarcts in this region are nearly pathognomonic for herpes infection, which mostly go clinically unsuspected and silent in the mother.
