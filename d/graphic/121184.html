Robotic OR and platform for groin hernia repair
In robotic groin hernia surgery, the robot is connected to the ports and robotic instruments and camera are mounted on the robotic arms into the ports. The surgeon controls the camera and instruments at a remote console that is typically within the same operating suite.
OR: operating room.
