Clinical features of Turner syndrome
Eleven-year-old with classical appearance of 45,X Turner syndrome, including short stature, lack of breast development, and shield chest with widely spaced nipples. Additional features may include webbed neck, cubitus valgus, and shortened fourth metatarsals.
