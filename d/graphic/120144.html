Calf muscle hematoma sagittal ultrasound
Hematoma (asterisk) in the fascial plane between the medial gastrocnemius and soleus is seen on this longitudinal ultrasound image. This is the most common location for pathological swelling after mild to moderate gastrocnemius injury. 
Originally published in: Fields KB, Rigby MD. Muscular calf injuries in runners. Curr Sports Med Rep 2016; 15:320. Reproduced with permission. Image may only be used with permission from Karl B. Fields, MD.
