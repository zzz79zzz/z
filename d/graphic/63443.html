Anatomy of the triangular fibrocartilage complex
The triangular fibrocartilage complex is comprised of the triangular fibrocartilage, ulnar collateral ligament, dorsal and palmar radioulnar ligaments, ulnolunate and ulnotriquetral ligaments, and the extensor carpi ulnaris tendon sheath. This figure shows the dorsal surface of the wrist.
