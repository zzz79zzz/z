Pleural calcifications due to chronic tuberculous pleurisy
Chronic tuberculous pleurisy with extensive pleural calcifications and restriction of the right lung, accounting for the small right hemithorax and the cardiomediastinal shift to the right. Right-sided parenchymal lung scars and left-sided nodular calcified granulomata are also evident.
