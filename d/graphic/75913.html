Left ventricular wall thickness predicts sudden death in HCM
In a study of 480 patients with an HCM, the incidence of sudden death during a 6.5-year follow-up was directly related to maximal wall thickness. The incidence of sudden death almost doubled for each 5 mm increase in wall thickness.
HCM: hypertrophic cardiomyopathy.
Data from Spirito P, Bellone P, Harris KM, et al. Magnitude of left ventricular hypertrophy and risk of sudden death in hypertrophic cardiomyopathy. N Engl J Med 2000; 342:1778.
