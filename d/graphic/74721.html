Cervical spine flexion teardrop fracture
Lateral radiograph (A) and a sagittal reconstruction image from a CT (B) of the cervical spine show a C5 vertebral body fracture (arrows) with anterior displacement of the fracture fragment, also termed the "teardrop." This fracture results when sudden, extreme flexion causes a vertebral body to collide with the one below.
CT: computed tomography.
