Recommended antibiotics for treatment of pneumonia caused by Chlamydia pneumoniae in adults
  Dose Duration*
Preferred
Azithromycin 500 mg orally on day 1 followed by 250 mg once daily on days 2 to 5 5 days
Alternatives
Clarithromycin 500 mg immediate-release orally twice daily or 1000 mg extended-release once daily 10 days
Doxycycline¶ 100 mg orally or IV twice daily 10 days
LevofloxacinΔ 500 or 750 mg orally or IV once daily 7 to 10 days
Moxifloxacin 400 mg orally or IV once daily 10 days
IV: intravenously.
* Suggested durations derived from studies demonstrating microbiologic eradication of C. pneumoniae in patient with community-acquired pneumonia (CAP); duration may differ from those recommended for CAP in general.
¶ Doxycycline should not be used in pregnant women. The IV preparation of doxycycline is a venous irritant; other IV antimicrobials are generally preferred when parenteral treatment is warranted.
Δ Dose adjustment is needed in setting of renal impairment.
