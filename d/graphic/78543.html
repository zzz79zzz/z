Asymmetric leg folds
Asymmetry of the thigh or popliteal creases is suggestive of developmental dysplasia of the hip (DDH), with the abnormality on the side where the crease is most proximal. Asymmetry of the thigh folds or popliteal creases is not helpful in cases of bilateral DDH.
