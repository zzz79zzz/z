Horner-Trantas dots
(A) Right eye - Broad, gelatinous, thickened, opacified, mucoid nodular infiltrates of eosinophils and epithelial debris. Horner-Trantas Dots (punctiform calcified concretions) are at the apices of the nodules.
(B) Left eye - Limbal conjunctivitis: broad, gelatinous, thickened, opacification of superior limbus.
