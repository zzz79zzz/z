Breast MRI of inconclusive mammographic findings - case 1

Breast MRI can be useful for problem solving in cases with inconclusive clinical or imaging findings.

(A) The mammogram shows distortion in the medial right breast, visible only on the CC projection.

(B) Breast MRI revealed a suspicious irregular enhancing mass in the medial superior breast (arrow). Biopsy confirmed invasive lobular carcinoma.
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging; CC: craniocaudal.
