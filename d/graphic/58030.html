Immersion burn
Note the sharp demarcation between the burn and normal skin and the absence of drip or splash marks, as well as the characteristic distribution on the buttocks and lower legs. Sparing of the thicker skin of palms or soles, and of skin folds where knees and hips are flexed, is also a common finding.
