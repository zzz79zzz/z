US transverse view of medial head of gastrocnemius
The photograph on the left shows the position of the ultrasound transducer for obtaining the image on the right, a transverse view of the medial head of the gastrocnemius muscle. The ultrasound image shows hypoechoic change confined within the fascia (rectangle), which lies superficial to the muscle belly.
