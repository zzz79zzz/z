Intraductal papillary mucinous neoplasm of the pancreas on CT scan
A CT scan of the abdomen and pelvis in a patient with IPMN shows a mass in the head of the pancreas (arrow in panel A) and a dilated pancreatic duct in the uncinate process (arrow in panel B). Histopathology demonstrated a neoplasm with both papillary and mucinous features.
CT: computed tomography; IPMN: intraductal papillary mucinous neoplasm.
