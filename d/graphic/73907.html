Calcitonin reduces vertebral fractures
Change in the SDI (with a higher value reflecting more deformity) in 72 postmenopausal patients with osteoporosis treated with calcium alone or with nasal salmon calcitonin. There was less deformity in the calcitonin group (p<0.001).
Ca: calcium; CT: calcitonin; SDI: spinal deformity index.
Data from: Rico H, Revilla M, Hernandez ER, et al. Total and regional bone mineral content and fracture rate in postmenopausal osteoporosis treated with salmon calcitonin: a prospective study. Calcif Tissue Int 1995; 56:181.
