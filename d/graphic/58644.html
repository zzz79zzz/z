Gastric varix
Upper endoscopy of a gastric varix in the fundus of the stomach. Gastric varices can arise in conjunction with esophageal varices. They can also be isolated when they result from segmental portal hypertension due to obstruction of the splenic vein by pancreatic carcinoma or chronic pancreatitis.
