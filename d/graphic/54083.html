Medium power view of a lung biopsy from a patient with measles pneumonia shows a nodular pattern with acute and chronic inflammation and areas of necrosis and fibrosis. Multinucleated giant cells with inclusions (arrows) are also shown (hematoxylin-eosin, 500X).
