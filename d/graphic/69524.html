The National Association for Public Health Statistics and Information system algorithm for reporting live births, infant deaths, fetal deaths, and induced terminations of pregnancy.
* Jurisdictional reporting requirements will vary. For most jurisdictions, a Report of Fetal Death is required when the birth weight is 350 grams or greater, or the gestational age is 20 weeks or greater.
