Distribution of race/ethnicity among adolescents (ages 13 to 19 years) diagnosed with HIV infection compared with the overall United States population, 2018
Data include persons with a diagnosis of HIV infection regardless of stage of disease at diagnosis. All displayed data have been statistically adjusted to account for reporting delays but not for incomplete reporting.

HIV: human immunodeficiency virus.

* Hispanics/Latinos can be of any race.

Data from: Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. HIV Surveillance - Adolescents and Young Adults 2018. Available at: https://www.cdc.gov/hiv/pdf/library/slidesets/cdc-hiv-surveillance-adolescents-young-adults-2018.pdf (Accessed on April 18, 2022).
