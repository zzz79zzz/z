Normal individuals show induction of TNF-alpha in monocytes upon LPS stimulation. Individuals with IRAK-4 deficiency do not show optimal TNF-alpha expression.
IRAK-4: interleukin 1 receptor-associated kinase 4; LPS: lipopolysaccharide; TNF: tumor necrosis factor.
