Hormone levels: Older and younger reproductive age
Mean daily levels of gonadotropins, sex steroids, and inhibins in older (ages 35 to 46 years; n = 21), shown in orange, and younger females (ages 20 to 34 years; n = 23), shown in blue.
FSH: follicle-stimulating hormone; LH: luteinizing hormone.
