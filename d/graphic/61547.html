Fibrillary glomerulonephritis
Immunofluorescence microscopy in fibrillary glomerulonephritis shows coarse linear pattern of IgG deposition (best seen at arrows). The coarseness and the lack of circulating anti-GBM antibodies distinguish this disorder from anti-GBM antibody disease.
IgG: immunoglobulin G; GBM: glomerular basement membrane.
