Mushroom-shaped RBCs
These images show a blood smear from a 55 year old admitted to the intensive care unit with severe COVID-19. The arrows point to examples of mushroom-shaped RBCs, also called pincer cells. In a series of 50 patients with COVID-19, 33 (66%) had mushroom-shaped RBCs on the blood smear, and all 50 had abnormal RBC morphologies.
RBCs: red blood cells.
From: Gerard D, Brahim SB, Lesesve JF, Perrin J. Are mushroom-shaped erythrocytes an indicator of COVID-19? Br J Haematol 2021; 192(2):230. https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/bjh.17127.
