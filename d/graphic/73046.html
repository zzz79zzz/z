Tubuloglomerular feedback
Relationship of single-nephron GFR to distal nephron (macula densa) perfusion rate in dogs. As the perfusion rate increases (via insertion of a micropipette into the late proximal tubule), there is a progressive reduction in GFR to a minimum of about one-half the basal level.
GFR: glomerular filtration rate.
