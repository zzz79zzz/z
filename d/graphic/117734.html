(A) The GORE CARDIOFORM Septal Occluder device with its double-disk configuration partially deployed from the delivery sheath.
(B) Fluoroscopic "en face" appearance of the CARDIOFORM device in vivo.
(C) The device, completely deployed and locked, flattens to the ventricular septum. The soft, double-disk design allows the proximal (right atrial) and distal (left atrial) disks to splay around the atrial septum.
