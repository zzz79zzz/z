Possible ventilator-associated pneumonia (VAP)

VAP: ventilator-associated pneumonia; VAC: ventilator-associated condition; IVAC: infection-related ventilator-associated complication; VAE: ventilator-associated event.

* Excludes the following:
  • Normal respiratory/oral flora, mixed respiratory/oral flora, or equivalent
  • Candida species or yeast not otherwise specified
  • Coagulase-negative Staphylococcus species
  • Enterococcus species
