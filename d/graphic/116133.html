Grade II germinal matrix hemorrhage-intraventricular hemorrhage
Cranial ultrasound images of a preterm infant born at 28 weeks gestation with a grade II germinal hemorrhage-intraventricular hemorrhage (GMH-IVH) on the right side (arrows). Coronal view (A) showing that the right ventricle is larger than the left one. Parasagittal view (B) showing the GMH and blood in the occipital horn.
