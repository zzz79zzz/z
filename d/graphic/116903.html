Cardiac monitoring for women 16 years or older with Turner syndrome
Suggested cardiac monitoring protocol for girls and women with Turner syndrome above 16 years of age.
TTE: transthoracic echocardiography; CMRI: cardiac magnetic resonance imaging; ECG: electrocardiogram; CoA: coarctation of aorta; BAV: bicuspid aortic valve; HTN; hypertension; ASI: aortic size index (absolute aortic diameter in cm divided by body surface area in meters2).
