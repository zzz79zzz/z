Afferent and efferent loops
The afferent and efferent limbs are two segments of jejunum formed by Billroth II reconstruction after partial gastrectomy. The afferent limb is the duodenojejunal segment proximal to the gastrojejunal anastomosis. The efferent limb is distal to the anastomosis.
