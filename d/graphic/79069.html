Color flow Doppler of severe aortic regurgitation
Color flow Doppler mid-esophageal five-chamber view from a transesophageal echocardiogram. The outflow tract is completely occupied by the aortic regurgitant color flow jet (AR jet); when the jet exceeds 65% of the left ventricular outflow tract (LVOT) width, the regurgitation is judged severe.
Ao: aorta; LV: left ventricle.
