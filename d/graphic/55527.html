Contarini's condition
Patient with a right sided transudate from left ventricular failure and with a left sided empyema that is loculated, adjacent to a focus of pneumonia with air bronchograms in the subjacent lung. This is also called Contarini's condition and refers to bilateral pleural effusions of differing etiologies.
