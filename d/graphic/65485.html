Forearm extensor stretch
Hold your injured arm straight out, and point your fingers down to the ground. Use your other hand (with the thumb pressing on the palm) to grab this hand. Then, press down on the back of the hand to bend the wrist more. Hold this position for 30 seconds. Repeat the stretch 3 times. Do this exercise 1 time a day.
