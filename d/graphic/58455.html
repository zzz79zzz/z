Cryptococcus neoformans in cerebrospinal fluid
Gram stain of cerebrospinal fluid (x1000) shows likely yeast forms, a few with cell walls and budding. These forms can be confused with host cells on Gram stain and are more easily identified by India ink. The most definitive procedures are testing for cryptococcal antigen and culture. Cryptococcus neoformans grew from this specimen.
