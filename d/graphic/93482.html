LV aneurysm with CHF arrhythmia, pacemaker on chest radiograph and CT scan
A chest radiograph in the PA projection shows a calcified left ventricular aneurysm (between arrows). A biventricular pacemaker is present. Image B is an axial image through the calcified aneurysm of the LV apex (arrowhead). A coronal reconstruction of the CT scan (C) shows a calcified ovoid aneurysm (dashed arrow).
LV: left ventricular; PA: posteroanterior; CT: computed tomography; CHF: congestive heart failure.
