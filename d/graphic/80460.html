Contact lens-associated fungal keratitis
Fungal keratitis in a 30-year-old female who wore daily disposable soft contact lenses.
(A) The central corneal infiltrate progressed dispite intensive antifungal therapy.
(B) A central 7 mm corneal transplant (penetrating keratoplasty) was performed 8 days after diagnosis to control the fungal infection.
