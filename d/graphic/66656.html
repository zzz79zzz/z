Acute lymphoblastic leukemia in bone marrow
Low power view of this bone marrow aspirate shows a monotonous collection of blast forms, in a patient with acute lymphoblastic leukemia (ALL). Even at this magnification, the cells can be seen to have a high nuclear:cytoplasmic ratio and prominent nucleoli (arrows).
