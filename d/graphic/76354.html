This table provides estimated values for blood pressures after 2 weeks of age in infants from 26 to 44 weeks postmenstrual age. The 95th and 99th percentile values are intended to serve as a reference to identify infants with persistent hypertension that may require treatment.
DBP: diastolic blood pressure; MAP: mean arterial pressure; SBP: systolic blood pressure.
