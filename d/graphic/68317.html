Axial MRI sections through the thoracic spine, demonstrating the recurrent chondrosarcoma (arrows) compressing the spinal cord (arrowheads) and the soft tissue extension adjacent to the allograft. The tumor extended to the dural margin, and intraoperative radiation was administered.
