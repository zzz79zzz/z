Restricted diffusion associated with acute ICH
Axial diffusion-weighted image (DWI) on brain MRI (A) and corresponding apparent diffusion coefficient (ADC) image (B) showing hyperintense DWI and hypointense ADC foci (arrows) suggestive of acute ischemia associated with acute ICH (arrowheads).
ICH: intracerebral hemorrhage; MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
