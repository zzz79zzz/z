Histopathologic features of angioinvasive lymphomatoid papulosis
Atypical medium-sized lymphocytes surrounding and infiltrating blood vessels (A). Rimming of atypical lymphoid cells around adipocytes (B). Staining for CD30 (C), CD4 (D), CD8 (E), and TIA-1 (F). Note the small number of CD8+ cells (E), whereas TIA-1 is expressed by most lymphocytes (F).
