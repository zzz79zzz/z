Primary palliative care educational offerings
Center for Advancement of Palliative Care (CAPC) CAPC is a national membership based organization offering tools, training, and technical assistance to front-line clinicians. www.capc.org
Vitaltalk Vitaltalk is an online resource to teach clinicians end-of-life communication skills. www.vitaltalk.org
End of Life Nursing Education Consortium (ELNEC) ELNEC is offered worldwide to nurses of all levels. Classroom courses and online modules are available on basic palliative care and specialty topics. www.aacnnursing.org/ELNEC/Courses
Palliative Care Education and Practice (PCEP) Training for nurse educators and physicians. www.cmecatalog.hms.harvard.edu/course/palliative-care-education-and-practice-pcep
MJHS Institute for Innovation in Palliative Care Online palliative care webinars for health care professionals. www.mjhspalliativeinstitute.org/e-learning
Professional Societies
  • The American Academy of Hospice and Palliative Care Medicine. www.aahpm.org
  • Hospice and Palliative Nurses Association. www.advancingexpertcare.org/
  • The National Hospice and Palliative Care Organization. www.nhpco.org
  • The European Association of Palliative Care (EAPC). Identifies 10 core competencies for health and social care professionals delivering basic palliative care. www.eapcnet.eu
