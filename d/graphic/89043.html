Erythrodontia in congenital erythropoietic porphyria (CEP)
Brownish discolored teeth fluoresce (erythrodontia) when exposed to ultraviolet light. The erythrodontia is the result of the accumulation of uroporphyrin I and coproporphyrin I in the patient's teeth.
