Solitary plasmacytoma of the acetabulum on radiography and CT
An A-P view of the pelvis (A) shows an expansile lytic lesion in the right acetabulum (arrows). Image B is an axial CT scan through the lower pelvis and shows an expansile, homogenous soft tissue mass in the right acetabulum (arrows).
A-P: anteroposterior; CT: computed tomography.
