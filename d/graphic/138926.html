Standing hip internal rotation stretch
The photographs above show the positioning and movement needed to perform a standing internal rotation stretch of the left hip. Gradually, the right leg pivots in an arc toward the target (or stretching) side while the left foot remains in position, thereby creating a closed-chain hip internal rotation stretch.
