Product-limit estimate of the cumulative probability of an ischemic stroke in the territory of the stenotic artery versus years after randomization, according to percent stenosis (≥70 percent stenosis shown as red solid line, <70 percent stenosis as blue dashed line); log-rank test P 0.0010.
