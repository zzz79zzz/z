Tricuspid regurgitation in carcinoid
Four chamber view from 2-D echocardiogram with color flow Doppler shows severe tricuspid regurgitation. The patient had a carcinoid syndrome with fibrotic tricuspid valve leaflets that were stenotic and immobile, remaining opened during systole.
