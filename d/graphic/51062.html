Continuous wave Doppler of the mitral regurgitation jet demonstrating the 'V-wave cutoff' configuration
This configuration is characterized by an abrupt deceleration from peak velocity and reflects the abrupt drop in gradient between ventricle and atrium resulting from regurgitant V-wave.
