Bilateral bundle branch block
Blocked or delayed conduction in both the left and right bundle branches leads to wide QRS complexes, which in this case have coexisting complexes with a left or right bundle branch block morphology. The first two beats have the appearance of a right bundle branch block, followed by four normal beats, and then beats with the appearance of a left bundle branch block.
