Blood supply to the urethra
The urethra is supplied proximally by the bulbar arteries, which are branches of the penile artery. The distal urethra is perfused by retrograde flow from the dorsal penile artery.
By convention, the surfaces of the penis are defined with the penis extended cranially; the urethra is ventrally located.
