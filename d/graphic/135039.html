Royal Free Hospital-Nutritional Prioritizing Tool
The Royal Free Hospital-Nutrition Prioritizing Tool (RFH-NPT) is a cirrhosis-specific nutrition risk screening tool that includes metabolic and nutritional parameters. Refer to UpToDate content on evaluating nutritional status in adults with cirrhosis for details.
BMI: body mass index.
