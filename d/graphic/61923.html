Cobb angle measurement of kyphosis
The Cobb angle of kyphosis, calculated from a lateral radiograph, is shown. T4 is used as the superior landmark and T12 as the inferior landmark. The greater the angle, the greater the thoracic curvature.
Reproduced with permission from: Kado DM, Christianson L, Palermo L, et al. Comparing a supine radiologic versus standing clinical measurement of kyphosis in older women: The Fracture Intervention Trial. Spine 2006; 31:463. Copyright © 2006 Lippincott Williams & Wilkins.
