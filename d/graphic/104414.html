Standard anesthesia machine breathing circuit with adaptor to fit nasal cannula tubing
For anesthesia machines without a common gas outlet, adjustment of oxygen concentration during open delivery of oxygen via a nasal cannula (or facemask) can be accomplished by using the universal airway connector from a #3 or #4 endotracheal tube with the standard breathing circuit.
