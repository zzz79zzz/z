Approach to selection of PFTs in a patient with dyspnea
An efficient stepwise method of determining the cause of chronic dyspnea using pulmonary function tests.
FEV1: forced expiratory volume in one second; FVC: forced vital capacity; VC: vital capacity; DLCO: diffusing capacity; WNL: within normal limits; COPD: chronic obstructive pulmonary disease; PImax: maximal inspiratory pressure; PEmax: maximal expiratory pressure.
