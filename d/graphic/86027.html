Row exercise for scapular stabilizers
Rowing exercises performed against resistance help to strengthen the muscles involved in stabilizing the scapula. In this example, the patient is holding in each hand one end of resistance tubing that is wrapped around the weight rack before him. Resistance increases as he pulls the tubing towards his armpits.
