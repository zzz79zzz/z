Cribriform ductal carcinoma in situ
Light micrograph of a lesion from the breast of a woman with cribriform ductal carcinoma in situ shows a back to back glandular growth pattern. The nuclei are well differentiated (low grade). A small calcification is noted near the center of the involved space (arrow).
