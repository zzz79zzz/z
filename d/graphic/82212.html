Segmental infantile hemangioma of the head and neck
Segmental hemangiomas are usually plaquelike and demonstrate a linear and/or geographic presence over a specific cutaneous territory. Infants with segmental hemangiomas should also be evaluated for PHACE syndrome.
PHACE: posterior fossa anomalies, hemangioma, arterial anomalies, cardiac anomalies, and eye anomalies.
