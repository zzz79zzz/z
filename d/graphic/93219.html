Severe acute hepatitis
High-power magnification of an inflammatory infiltrate. Arrows point to lymphocytes surrounding a single hepatocyte. There is also bridging necrosis, which is exemplified by the linear pink material representing loss and/or necrosis of a single row or several rows of hepatocytes. Bridging necrosis as shown here typically links one vascular structure to another.
