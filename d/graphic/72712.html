T3-resin test in TBG excess
The radiolabeled T3 (*T3) is able to bind to the excess binding sites on TBG; as a result, the amount left over to bind to the resin is lower than normal. The serum total T4 is high; the T3-resin uptake and THBR are low, and the serum free T4 index is normal.
T3: triiodothyronine; T4: thyroxine: TBG: thyroxine-binding globulin; THBR: thyroid hormone binding ratio.
