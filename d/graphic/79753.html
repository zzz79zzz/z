Infant head control
By 40 weeks conceptional age, the infant has sufficient neck and truncal strength to normally maintain the head in line with the trunk for one to two seconds while being pulled from a supine to sitting position. In infants with hypotonia (shown in the inset), the head lags behind as the infant is pulled from the supine to sitting position and continues to lag when the sitting position is reached.
