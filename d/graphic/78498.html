Abdominal perineal resection - Anatomic outline for resection
This figure depicts the segments of the colon and rectum resected for an abdominal perineal resection (APR). An APR is most commonly performed for low lying rectal cancer, anal cancer, Crohn's proctitis, or ulcerative colitis.
