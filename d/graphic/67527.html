Normal lungs
The lungs sit in the chest, inside the ribcage. They are covered with a thin membrane called the "pleura." The windpipe, or trachea, branches into 2 smaller airways called the left and right "bronchi." The space between the lungs is called the "mediastinum." Lymph nodes are located within and around the lungs and mediastinum.
