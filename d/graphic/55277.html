Atrial tachycardia with 2:1 block
Atrial tachycardia with 2:1 atrioventricular (AV) block. The atrial rate is about 160 beats/min while the ventricular rate is about 80 beats/min. The nonconducted P waves (arrows) are superimposed on the ST-T segments. The P waves have a similar morphology to normal P waves suggesting that the ectopic site is near to the sinoatrial node. This arrhythmia may be an important sign of digitalis toxicity, but can also occur in other settings.
