One dose of PPSV23 at age ≥2 years and ≥8 weeks after the last indicated dose of PCV13

A second dose (usually ≥5 years after the first) is recommended for children who are immunocompromised, including those who have sickle cell disease or functional or anatomic asplenia

A second dose is not recommended for immunocompetent children with cochlear implants or chronic illness, including chronic heart disease, chronic lung disease, diabetes mellitus, and cerebrospinal fluid leaks
