Digital angiogram in mixed connective tissue disease (MCTD) vasculopathy
Digital angiogram in a patient with mixed connective tissue disease shows an attenuated distal ulnar artery (arrow), absence of the deep palmar arch, and absence or compromise of several digital arteries including that to the fifth digit. Such medium-sized arterial disease is common in this disorder.
