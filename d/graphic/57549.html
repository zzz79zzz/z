Kidney stone detected by ultrasonography
The sagittal view of the right kidney reveals a 7 mm shadowing stone in mid-portion of the kidney, characteristic of a non-obstructing stone. The echogenic focus (arrow) represents the stone. The calcified stone inhibits transmission of sound waves, resulting in a shadow behind the stone (arrowheads).
