Pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma on contrast-enhanced ultrasound
Pancreatic ductal adenocarcinomas are characterized by a relatively low mean vascular density, resulting in hypoenhancement on contrast-enhanced ultrasound during all vascular phases.
DW: duct of Wirsung (main pancreatic duct); TU: tumor.
