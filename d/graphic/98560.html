Mechanical valves and their transesophageal echocardiographic characteristics.
Examples of bileaflet, single-leaflet, and caged-ball mechanical valves and their transesophageal echocardiographic characteristics taken in the mitral position in diastole (middle) and in systole (right). The arrows in diastole point to the occluder mechanism of the valve and in systole to the characteristic physiologic regurgitation observed with each valve.
