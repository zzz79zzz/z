Electronic pressure gauge for measuring PImax, PEmax, and maximal SNIP.
The device on the left has a blue mouthpiece with flange for measurement of the PImax and PEmax, while the device on the right has a pressure transducer attached for the measurement of SNIP.
PImax: maximal inspiratory pressure; PEmax: maximal expiratory pressure; SNIP: sniff nasal inspiratory pressure.
