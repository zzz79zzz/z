Cholangiocarcinoma demonstrated by percutaneous transhepatic cholangiography
This percutaneous transhepatic cholangiogram demonstrates dilated intrahepatic bile ducts (dashed arrows) due to a circumferential cholangiocarcinoma involving the common bile duct. The mass appears as a filling defect (arrow) below the dilated ducts.
