Ultrasound provides only limited views of the anterior cruciate ligament. The image above shows the posterior view of the ligament in short axis.
Arrowheads: anterior cruciate ligament; arrows: intercondylar notch; MFC: medial femoral condyle; LFC: lateral femoral condyle; A: popliteal artery; MHG: medial head of gastrocnemius muscle; *: articular cartilage.
Left of the screen: lateral; right of the screen: medial.
