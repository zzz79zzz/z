Treatment for Bennett fracture of the thumb
A Bennett fracture of the thumb is an oblique, intraarticular fracture of the thumb metacarpal base. The fracture is reduced by longitudinal traction, pronation of the thumb (rotate counter-clockwise), and pressure on the dorsal metacarpal base. This is an unstable fracture pattern that requires fixation after reduction of the fracture.
