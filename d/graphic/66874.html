Elbow joint articulations
The elbow is a complex hinge joint composed of three separate articulations: ulnohumeral (large purple circle), radiohumeral (small purple circle), and radioulnar (red circle). The radiohumeral and ulnohumeral articulations combine to provide hinge movement (purple arrows) and supination and pronation of the forearm is facilitated by pivoting at the radioulnar articulation (red arrows).
