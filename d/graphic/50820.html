Common bile duct obstruction following liver transplantation
Endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography in a patient with jaundice after liver transplantation demonstrates almost complete obstruction of the bile duct anastomosis (large arrow) with minimal passage of contrast throughout the obstruction (small arrows). This lesion was successfully treated with balloon dilation
