Pressure monitor for muscle compartments
Pressure monitors such as the one pictured above are used to detect elevations in muscle compartment pressures and diagnose compartment syndromes. While the photograph shows the needle being inserted at an angle, we prefer to position the device and needle horizontally for insertion.
