MRI radial slices around the femoral neck
Radial slices around the femoral neck are an important part of the MRI evaluation of anterosuperior femoral neck morphology in patients with femoroacetabular impingement. The anterior position is denoted 3 o'clock and superior position 12 o'clock for both the left and right hips.
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
