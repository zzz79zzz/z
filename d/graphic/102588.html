Relationship between diaphragm strength and supine position
Relationship between percentage fall in vital capacity on adopting the supine posture and diaphragm strength (assessed by measuring transdiaphragmatic pressure during maximum sniffs). Fall in vital capacity is seldom substantial until strength is reduced to 30% of normal.
