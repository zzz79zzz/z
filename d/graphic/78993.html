Myelokathexis
Bone marrow smear from a patient with neutropenia due to myelokathexis. There is an increased number of mature and hypersegmented neutrophils. The myelokathexis morphology is characterized by pyknotic nuclei with lengthening and thinning of intrasegmented filaments and vacuoles.
