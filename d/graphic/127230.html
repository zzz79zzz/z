Lateral plain radiograph of minimally displaced nasal fracture
Imaging is not necessary for the most children with isolated nasal trauma. This plain radiograph was taken during the follow-up specialty visit because a displaced fracture was suspected but not clearly evident on physical examination. It shows minimal displacement of the nasal bone (arrow).
