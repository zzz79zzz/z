Correlation between hepatic iron concentration and total body iron stores in thalassemia major patients without cirrhosis
The correlation is shown for 25 patients with liver biopsy samples that had a dry weight of 1.0 mg or more. The regression line (middle blue line) and 95 percent confidence limits (upper red and lower green lines) are shown.
Adapted from: Angelucci E, Brittenham GM, McLaren CE, et al. Hepatic iron concentration and total body iron stores in thalassemia major. N Engl J Med 2000; 343:327.
