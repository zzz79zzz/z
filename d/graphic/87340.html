Bilateral grade III germinal matrix hemorrhage-intraventricular hemorrhage in a preterm infant
Cranial ultrasound images of a preterm infant born at 29 weeks gestation with a bilateral grade III germinal matrix hemorrhage-intraventricular hemorrhage (arrows) with acute dilatation due to a large clot in both ventricles. Coronal view angling backwards (A) and parasagittal view (B).
