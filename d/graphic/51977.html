Stage I fracture of the lateral condyle
(A) Anteroposterior radiograph shows 1-mm fracture line.
(B) Lateral view shows 2 mm of displacement with posterolateral Thurston-Holland fragment.
(C and D) Anteroposterior and lateral radiographs four weeks later show healed fracture with callus formation.
