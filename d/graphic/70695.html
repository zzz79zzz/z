Normal cardiac hemodynamics during cardiac catheterization wth a hi-fidelity catheter. One can see the relationships between the surface ECG and simultaneous pressure waves from the aorta (Ao), left ventricle (LV), right atrium (RA), left atrium (LA), and rate of pressure change (dp/dt).
