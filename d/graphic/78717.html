Cervical spine MRI postenhancement in a 20-year-old male.
(A) The sagittal image demonstrates a contrast-enhancing, circumscribed lesion at the C6-7 level (arrow).
(B) Axial images confirm this finding (arrows). The lesion was completely excised without causing neurological deficit and no radiation was administered.
