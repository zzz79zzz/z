Hand radiograph of a newborn with achondroplasia
Hand AP film of newborn with achondroplasia. There is generalized shortening of metacarpals and all phalanges. The spaces between the second and fourth digits with the middle finger suggest the presence of the trident deformity.
AP: anteroposterior.
