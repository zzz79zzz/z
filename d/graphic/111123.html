There is no consensus regarding the appropriate threshold for TB screening. Guidelines are developed for public health goals and therefore may not represent the best clinical choice for individual patients. Decisions regarding TB screening should be based on risk, and local or regional TB incidence data should be used to help define such risk.

TB: tuberculosis; WHO: World Health Organization; TB/HIV: tuberculosis/human immunodeficiency virus; MDR-TB: multidrug-resistant tuberculosis.

* An incidence of TB of >100 per 100,000 population is associated with an annual risk of infection of approximately 1%.

¶ Designated as a high-burden country for TB/HIV in the WHO 2020 Global report.

Δ Designated as a high-burden country for MDR-TB in the WHO 2020 Global report.

◊ Designated as a high-burden country for TB in the WHO 2020 Global report.
