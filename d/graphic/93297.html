Alcohol-associated steatohepatitis with chicken-wire fibrosis
The central venule (arrow) is narrowed by fibrous tissue that surrounds it. This is known as central hyaline sclerosis. Arrowheads point to pericellular and perisinusoidal fibrosis that result in the appearance of the characteristic chicken-wire fibrosis.
