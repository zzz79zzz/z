Hymenolepis nana eggs
(A-D) Eggs of Hymenolepis nana. These eggs are oval and smaller than those of H. diminuta, with a size range of 30 to 50 micrometers. On the inner membrane are two poles, from which four to eight polar filaments spread out between the two membranes. The oncosphere has six hooks.
