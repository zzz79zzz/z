Prognosis of myocardial infarction in relation to ST changes at 90 minutes
All ST changes measured in lead with maximum change at baseline. Persistence of bundle-branch block is prognostic indication for worst outcome except when there is <2 mm change at 90 minutes with an inferior infarction, when it indicates medium prognosis.
* Baseline ST elevation >4.5 mm.
• Baseline ST elevation ≤4.5 mm.
