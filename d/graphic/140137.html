Glenoid labrum SLAP lesion on MRI
The coronal (A) and axial (B) MRI images above reveal a superior labral tear (arrows), which extends into the biceps anchor. This is a Type IV SLAP lesion.
SLAP: superior labrum anterior-posterior; MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
