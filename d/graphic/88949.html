Venous drainage of the small intestine
The mesenteric veins parallel their corresponding arteries. The SMV drains the small intestine, cecum, ascending, and transverse colon via the jejunal, ileal, ileocolic, right colic, and middle colic veins. The SMV joins the splenic vein to drain into the portal vein.
SMV: superior mesenteric vein.
