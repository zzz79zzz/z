Aortic dissection on magnetic resonance imaging
Transverse (axial) spin-echo CMR in a patient with an aortic dissection at the level of the aortic arch. The true lumen (TL), false lumen (FL), and intimal flap can be easily identified. The trachea and superior vena cava (SVC) are also seen.
CMR: cardiovascular magnetic resonance.
