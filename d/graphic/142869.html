Overview of treatment of acute neonatal seizures
This algorithm represents our suggested approach to the treatment of neonatal seizures. Refer to UpToDate topics on neonatal seizures for full details regarding the use of ASMs, including initiating therapy, selecting appropriate agents, dosing regimens, and duration of therapy.

ASM: antiseizure medication; CNS: central nervous system; EEG: electroencephalography; HIE: hypoxic-ischemic encephalopathy; NE: neonatal encephalopathy.

* For neonates with status epilepticus, refer to separate algorithm for the treatment of neonatal status epilepticus.
