Cauda equina involvement by non-Hodgkin lymphoma
Sagittal gadolinium-enhanced T1-magnetic resonance image showing abnormal enhancement, thickening, and clumping of the roots of the cauda equina (arrowheads). The conus medullaris (arrow) is encased by enhancing soft tissue. These findings are indicative of infiltration of the subarachnoid space by lymphoma.
