Left ventricular hypertrophy​
Intraoperative TEE transgastric LV short-axis image at end-diastole (thick arrow) for measuring inferolateral wall thickness in a patient with severe concentric left ventricular hypertrophy. According to guidelines for two dimensional imaging, measurement of the inferolateral wall should be taken between the epicardial/endocardial boundary and the endocardial/trabecular boundary for maximal accuracy of true myocardial thickness and estimation of left ventricular mass. In this patient, the diastolic inferolateral LV wall thickness was 2.1 cm, signifying severe hypertrophy.
TEE: transesophageal echocardiography; LV: left ventricle.
