Neer test for shoulder impingement
The "passive painful arc maneuver" shown above involves passively flexing the glenohumeral joint while simultaneously preventing shoulder shrugging. The test is often referred to as the Neer test and is used to assess shoulder impingement. Pain during the maneuver marks a positive test.
