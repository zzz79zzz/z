Severity of disease for hospitalized patients with RSV (304 patients), influenza (746 patients), and SARS-CoV-2 (4,734 patients) comparing four in-hospital outcomes. Adjusted odds ratio (aOR) compared to RSV.
RSV: respiratory syncytial virus; NIV: non-invasive ventilation.
Data from: Surie D, Yuengling KA, DeCuir J, et al. Disease severity of respiratory syncytial virus compared with COVID-19 and influenza among hospitalized adults aged ≥60 Years – IVY Network, 20 U.S. states, February 2022-May 2023. MMWR Morb Mortal Wkly Rep. 2023;72:1083.
