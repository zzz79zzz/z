Modified hair apposition of scalp wounds
(A) Grasp 5-15 hairs on either side of the wound with hemostats or forceps.
(B) Twist the hairs once until the wound edges appose.
(C) Place one drop of glue on the twist. Do not get glue in the wound.
(D) Repeat the procedure until the laceration is closed.
