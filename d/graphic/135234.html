Initial approach to treatment of patients with intermediate, high, or very high risk localized prostate cancer
Newly diagnosed prostate cancer is risk stratified based on digital rectal examination, serum PSA, prostate biopsy, and imaging studies. Intermediate-risk disease is further subdivided into favorable and unfavorable subsets, although definitions are variable. This is discussed separately in UpToDate.

PSA: prostate-specific antigen; ADT: androgen deprivation therapy.

* The choice between radiation therapy versus prostatectomy is individualized based on the advantages and disadvantages associated with each approach, contraindications, and personal preference.
