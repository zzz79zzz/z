IgM deposition in preeclampsia
Immunofluorescence microscopy in preeclampsia showing diffuse immunoglobulin M (IgM) deposition. This represents nonspecific entrapment of larger proteins in the more permeable glomerular capillary wall, rather than the formation of discrete immune complexes. There is, for example, generally no deposition of immunoglobulin G (IgG).
