Mammogram grouped calcifications B
Magnified craniocaudal mammogram showing linear branching calcifications in a segmental distribution (red arrow). Grouped microcalcifications such as these are highly suggestive of carcinoma, and the linear branching is suggestive of a ductal lesion. Biopsy confirmed a high-grade ductal carcinoma in situ (DCIS).
