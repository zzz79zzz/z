An MRCP (A) shows the origin of the bile leak near the cystic duct amputation site (arrow) following cholecystectomy. The bile duct is mildly dilated (arrowhead). Image B is a T2-weighted sequence through the kidneys and shows free fluid in Morison's pouch (dashed arrow).
