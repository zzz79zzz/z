US deep fibular nerve crossing superficial to dorsalis pedis artery
This series of ultrasound images shows the deep fibular nerve in short-axis as it travels distally, moving from the lateral side of the dorsal pedis artery to the medial side by coursing superficially over the artery. Image A is proximal, image B is intermediate, and image C is distal.
Dotted ellipse: deep fibular nerve; A: dorsalis pedis artery.
Left of the screen: lateral; right of the screen: medial.
