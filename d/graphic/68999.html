Alloprosthesis reconstruction after resection of an extremity bone fibrosarcoma
A 68-year-old woman with a fibrosarcoma (malignant fibrous histocytoma) arising in an bone infarct underwent resection of the proximal tibia and proximal fibula, followed by reconstruction using a combined bone allograft and metal prosthesis.
