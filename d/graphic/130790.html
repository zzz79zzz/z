Intraoperative photo of the left gastric artery
Retract the lesser curve (A) laterally and anteriorly toward the abdominal wall. This maneuver places the left gastric artery (B) on tension and allows its base to be easily visualized. Also shown are the right crus (C) and caudate lobe of liver (D).
