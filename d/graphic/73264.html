Membranous VSD with LV to RA shunt
This movie clip obtained from a subcostal coronal imaging plane shows the relationship between the jet of flow (orange) from the LVOT to the RA and the tricuspid valve. There is also a small jet of flow across the atrial septum from left to right, consistent with a coexistent patent foramen ovale.
VSD: ventricular septal defect; LA: left atrium; RA: right atrium; LV: left ventricle; RV: right ventricle; IVS: intraventricular septum; LVOT: left ventricular outflow tract; TV: tricuspid valve; arrow: VSD.
