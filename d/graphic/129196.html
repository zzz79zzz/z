Death-censored graft failure among adult deceased-donor kidney transplant recipients
Estimates are unadjusted, computed using Kaplan-Meier competing risk methods. Recipients are followed to the earliest of kidney graft failure; kidney retransplantation; return to dialysis; death; for 6 months, 1, 3, 5, or 10 years posttransplant. Death-censored graft failure is defined as a return to dialysis, reported graft failure, or kidney retransplantation.
