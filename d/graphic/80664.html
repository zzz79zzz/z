Neonatal testicular torsion
In a newborn male, a clinical diagnosis of prenatal testicular torsion was made based upon the physical finding of a firm, enlarged, nontender right testicle. Surgical exploration through an inguinal incision confirmed extravaginal testicular torsion and an ischemic testicle.
