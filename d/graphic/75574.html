Penile anatomy in bladder exstrophy
Penile anatomy in boys born with bladder exstrophy. The penis is shorter than normal because of a short anterior corporal segment (ACL).
ACL: anterior corporal length; PCL: posterior corporal length; TCL: total corporal length; ISD: intersymphyseal distance; aCC: corpora cavernosa subtended angle; Cdiam: corpus cavernosum diameter; ICD: intercorporal distance.
