Manifestations of vasculitis in deficiency of adenosine deaminase 2 (DADA2)
(A) Enhancement of basal ganglia and internal and external capsules on magnetic resonance imaging (MRI).
(B) Bilateral renal infarction (arrows) on computed tomography (CT) scan.
(C) Aneurysms of the left lower intercostal artery and hepatic, splenic, renal, and mesenteric arteries on CT angiography with 3D reconstruction.
(D) Marked attenuation of blood flow to the left ulnar artery on CT angiography (thick arrow).
From: Sharma A, Naidu G, Sharma V, et al. Deficiency of adenosine deaminase 2 in adults and children: Experience from India.
