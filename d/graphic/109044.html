CT 3D reconstruction of a cuboid fracture
The image above shows a three-dimensional (3D) reconstruction of a CT scan of the left foot. The image is in an anterolateral orientation and shows an isolated cuboid fracture that is minimally displaced (arrow).
CT: computed tomography.
