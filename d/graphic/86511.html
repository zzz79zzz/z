Chronology of drug eruptions
Approximate onset and evolution of uncomplicated, drug-induced exanthemas with probable T cell-mediated pathophysiology in previously sensitized individuals. The height of the lines reflects acuity, their ends the approximate duration. In newly sensitized individuals, typically exanthems develop between the 8th and 12th day (red dashed line).
SDRIFE: symmetrical drug-related intertriginous and flexural exanthem.
