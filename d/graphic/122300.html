Malignant melanoma with pleural metastases
(A) Computed tomography (CT) thorax with coronal reformation shows a mass above the left lateral costophrenic sulcus and an additional left lateral nodule (arrows) consistent with metastases in a patient with malignant melanoma.
(B) Corresponding 18F-fluorodeoxyglucose-positron emission tomography-CT fused image shows high glucose uptake with yellow color along the left lateral and left medial pleura.
