Biopsy showing cirrhosis due to hepatitis C and alcohol
Needle biopsy of the liver (100x) of a 57-year-old female with cirrhosis from both hepatitis C virus infection and chronic alcohol consumption. Hematoxylin and eosin stain demonstrates prominent steatosis as well as portal and peri-portal inflammation and fibrosis.
