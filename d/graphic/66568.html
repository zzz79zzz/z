MRI images of a chain of neurofibromas in a patient with neurofibromatosis type 1
(A) T2 image showing high signal intensity with central area of low-intensity "target sign" (arrows).
(B) T1 imaging showing fusiform tumors with signal intensity isodense to muscle (arrows).
(C) T1 postgadolinium showing slight, patchy enhancement of the tumor (arrow).
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
