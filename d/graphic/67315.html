Echocardiogram of a normal heart and a case of dilated cardiomyopathy
A four-chamber view from the two-dimensional echocardiogram of a normal heart is shown in the panel A. In panel B, the echocardiogram from a patient with dilated cardiomyopathy is shown and diagrammed. Note the dilated cardiomyopathy heart is more spherical than its normal counterpart.
LV: left ventricle; RV: right ventricle; RA: right atrium; LA: left atrium; aML: anterior mitral leaflet; pML: posterior mitral leaflet.
