Three-part proximal humerus fracture
This anteroposterior radiograph shows a fracture of the surgical neck of the humerus. A portion of the surgical neck and the greater tuberosity are displaced from head and shaft. In the Neer classification system this is categorized as a three-part proximal humerus fracture.
