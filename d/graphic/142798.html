Convergence VOMS examination

The vestibular and oculomotor examination is an important part of the assessment of the patient with a possible concussion. The portion of that examination demonstrated here must be used as one part of a complete assessment to determine the extent and severity of the symptoms and signs caused by a concussion.

Equivalent SI units for values used in the video are as follow:

  • 1.5 feet = 0.45 meters
  • 3 feet = 0.9 meters
  • 6 inches = 15 cm
VOMS: vestibular-ocular motor screening.
