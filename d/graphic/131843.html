Sacral fracture CT: Zone one injury
The CT images above show a Zone I sacral fracture (neural foramina not affected). The injury was sustained by a 62-year-old man involved in a motorcycle accident. The coronal reformatted CT image (A) and 3D CT reconstruction (B) show a fracture of the right sacrum that does not involve the neural foramina (arrow).
From: Lower limb I: Pelvic girdle, sacrum, and proximal femur. In: Orthopaedic Imaging: A Practical Approach, 7th ed, Greenspan A, Beltran J (Eds), Lippincott Williams & Wilkins, 2020.
