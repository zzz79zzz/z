Vessels supplying the palatine tonsil
The tonsillar vascular supply is from branches of the external carotid artery. Superior pole: descending palatine artery. Mid-fossa: ascending pharyngeal artery. Inferior pole: tonsillar branch of the lingual artery and ascending palatine branch of the facial artery.
