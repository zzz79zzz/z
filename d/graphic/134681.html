Wound dressing after placement of a peritoneal dialysis catheter
Bandaging techniques for new peritoneal dialysis catheters vary. Shown are nonocclusive dressings over the exit and incision sites, which help immobilize the catheter and allows for drainage of blood or dialysate without pooling.
