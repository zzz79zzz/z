Healed tympanic membrane perforation after tympanostomy tube extrusion
Otoscopic image of the TM in a child who previously had a tympanostomy tube in place. The tube has subsequently extruded. An area of a prior TM perforation (arrows) can be seen that has now healed.
TM: tympanic membrane.
