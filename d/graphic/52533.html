Prenatal ultrasound image of jejunal atresia
(A) T2w coronal magnetic resonance image at 33 weeks of gestation shows a single high-signal dilated loop of bowel in the midabdomen.
(B) T1w coronal magnetic resonance image shows the dilated loop is low signal. The distal bowel was collapsed with normal-appearing meconium in the colon and rectum.
