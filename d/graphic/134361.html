Needle placement for subcostal TAP block
Subcostal TAP block can be performed medially, with injection of local anesthetic between the transversus abdominis muscle and the rectus muscle (A), or more laterally, with injection between the transversus abdominis muscle and the external oblique muscle (B).
m.: muscle.
