The associations of different elevated biomarkers with acute kidney injury, proteinuria, and length of hospital stay in Puumala hantavirus infection[1-7]
Acute kidney injury IL-6, PTX3, IDO, suPAR, GATA-3, Gal-3BP
Proteinuria IL-6, CRP, suPAR
Length of hospitalization IL-6, PTX3, IDO, cf-DNA, suPAR, Gal-3BP
IL-6: interleukin 6; PTX3: pentraxin 3; IDO: indoleamine 2,3-dioxygenase; suPAR: soluble urokinase-type plasminogen activator receptor; GATA-3: GATA binding protein 3; Gal-3BP: Galectin-3-binding protein; CRP: C-reactive protein; cf-DNA: cell-free DNA.
