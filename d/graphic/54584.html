Chromoendoscopy with indigo carmine of a colon adenoma
High resolution endoscopic image of a small flat colon adenoma in a patient with familial adenomatous polyposis after spraying with indigo carmine. The indigo carmine is not absorbed and collects in the crevices between epithelial cells thereby enhancing the mucosal details.
