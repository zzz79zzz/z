Keratic precipitates in granulomatous uveitis

Keratic precipitates in granulomatous uveitis
Keratic precipitates are deposits of leukocytes on the corneal endothelium. The cellular concretions in this picture are large. This indicates a granulomatous type inflammation, although the cellular collections are not granulomas histologically.
