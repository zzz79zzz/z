Estrogen therapy is not effective for treatment of Alzheimer disease
Data from a randomized clinical trial show no difference between estrogen replacement therapy and placebo on the course of Alzheimer disease using a Clinical Global Impression of Change score (mean, 95% CI).
Data from: Mulnard RA, Cotman CW, Kawas C, et al. Estrogen replacement therapy for treatment of mild to moderate Alzheimer disease: A randomized controlled trial. Alzheimer's Disease Cooperative Society. JAMA 2000; 283:1007.
