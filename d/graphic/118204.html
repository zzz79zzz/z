Superior mesenteric artery exposure
The intraoperative photograph shows the exposure of the SMA (thick arrow), which has been dissected circumferentially. The renal vein (thin arrow), which typically passes beneath the SMA at its origin from the aorta, is retracted to aid SMA dissection.
SMA: superior mesenteric artery.
