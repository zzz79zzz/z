Clinical and dermoscopic image of an epidermolysis bullosa nevus
Dermoscopic image of an epidermolysis bullosa nevus on the thumb of a 5-year-old boy with intermediate EBS with muscular dystrophy showing an atypical pigment network; irregular dots and globules; and homogeneous, white and reddish areas (multicomponent pattern).
EBS: epidermolysis bullosa simplex.
