Treatment strategy for Lambert-Eaton myasthenic syndrome
This algorithm is intended as a general guide to the treatment of LEMS. Sufficient benefit with treatment is achieved when the patient is satisfied with improvement in strength and function.
LEMS: Lambert-Eaton myasthenic syndrome; 3,4-DAP: 3,4-diaminopyridine; IVIG: intravenous immune globulin.
* Refer to UpToDate topic for details on alternative symptomatic therapies, which have either significant toxicity concerns or limited efficacy.
¶ Some experts are reluctant to use oral immunosuppressive agents such as azathioprine in patients with cancer because of concerns that such treatment may exacerbate the malignancy.
