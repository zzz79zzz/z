US suprapatellar region sagittal plane view
This ultrasound image shows the suprapatellar recess and the quadriceps tendon in the sagittal plane.
Arrows: suprapatellar recess; white outline: quadriceps tendon; SL: superficial layer; ML: middle layer; DL: deep layer; QFP: quadriceps fat pad; PFFP: prefemoral fat pad.
Left side of image: caudal; right side of image: cranial.
