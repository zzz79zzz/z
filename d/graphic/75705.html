Ascending aortic dissection on echocardiogram
Modified long axis view shows a proximal dissection of the aortic root (Ao), with a flap extending to the aortic valve (V). This aortic pathology is seen by imaging the ascending aorta one interspace above the usual long axis precordial window.
LA: left atrium; LV: left ventricle.
