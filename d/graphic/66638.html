Determining grip size for tennis racquet
The Nirschl criteria suggest that appropriate grip size is determined by the distance from the middle of the proximal palmar crease of the hand to the distal tip of the ring finger in inches.
Adapted from: Nirschl RP. The etiology and treatment of tennis elbow. J Sports Med 1974; 2:308.
