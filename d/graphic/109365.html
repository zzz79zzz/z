Convection volume per session needed for an individual patient to have a BSA adjusted convection volume of 23 L or more
Convection volume per session needed for an individual patient to have a BSA-adjusted convection volume of 23 L or more. The formula used was convection volume = (23 × individual BSA) / 1.73. Here, BSA (m2) = 0.0235 × height (cm)0.42246 × weight (kg)0.51456.
BSA: body surface area.
