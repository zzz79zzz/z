Computed tomography scan of intracranial calcifications in congenital cytomegalovirus infection
Intracranial computed tomography scan of an infant born with congenital cytomegalovirus disease and central nervous system involvement. The scan shows classic linear periventricular calcifications and cortical atrophy. The infant had microcephaly at birth and developmental disabilities and major motor impairment at 8 years of age.
