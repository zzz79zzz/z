Major causes of dilated cardiomyopathy

Major causes of dilated cardiomyopathy
Cardiac causes
  • Ischemic heart disease
  • Valve disease
  • Congenital heart disease
  • Tachycardia

Infectious diseases

  • Viral
    • Adenovirus
    • Coxsackie virus
    • Cytomegalovirus
    • HIV
    • Influenza virus
    • Varicella
    • Hepatitis
    • Epstein-Barr
    • Echovirus
    • Parvovirus

  • Bacterial

    • Streptococci-rheumatic fever
    • Typhoid fever
    • Diphtheria
    • Brucellosis
    • Psitticosis
    • Mycobacteria
    • Rickettsial
    • Leptospirosis
    • Syphilis
    • Lyme disease
    • Listeria

  • Fungal

    • Histoplasmosis
    • Cryptococcosis

  • Parasitic

    • Toxoplasmosis
    • Trypanosomiasis (Chagas disease)
    • Shistosomiasis
    • Trichinosis
 Deposition diseases
  • Hemochromatosis
  • Amyloidosis

Medications

  • Chemotherapeutic agents
    • Anthracyclines
    • Cyclophosphamide
    • Trastuzumab

  • Antiretroviral drugs

    • Zidovudine
    • Didanosine
    • Zalcitabine

  • Phenothiazines

  • Chloroquine
  • Hydroxychloroquine
  • Immune checkpoint inhibitors

Toxins

  • Ethanol
  • Cocaine
  • Amphetamines
  • Cobalt
  • Lead
  • Lithium
  • Mercury
  • Carbon monoxide
  • Beryllium

Electrolyte and renal abnormalities

  • Hypocalcemia
  • Hypophosphatemia
  • Uremia

Nutritional deficiencies

  • Thiamine
  • Selenium
  • Carnitine
  • Niacin (pellagra)
 Inflammatory or autoimmune
  • Sarcoidosis
  • Dermatomyositis
  • Scleroderma
  • Rheumatoid arthritis
  • Systemic lupus erythematosis
  • Hypersensitivity myocarditis
  • Giant cell arteritis
  • Kawasaki disease

Endocrinologic disorders

  • Thyroid hormone excess or deficiency
  • Growth hormone excess or deficiency
  • Diabetes mellitus
  • Cushing syndrome
  • Pheochromocytoma or other catecholamine excess

Genetic with or without neuromuscular disease

  • Familial (and sporadic) genetic cardiomyopathies
  • Duchenne muscular dystrophy
  • Myotonic dystrophy
  • Friedreich ataxia
  • Arrhythmogenic right ventricular cardiomyopathy

Miscellaneous

  • Peripartum cardiomyopathy
  • Stress-induced cardiomyopathy
  • High-output state
  • Sleep apnea
