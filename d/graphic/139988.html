Spinal ependymoma in a patient with NF2-related schwannomatosis
Sagittal T2-weighted MRI of the cervical spine showing an intra-axial expansile cystic and solid T2-hyperintense mass at C2, consistent with a spinal ependymoma in a patient with NF2-related schwannomatosis.
NF2: NF2, moesin-ezrin-radixin like (MERLIN) tumor suppressor; MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
