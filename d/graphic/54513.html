Sustained ventricular tachycardia on ambulatory monitoring
The ambulatory monitor in a patient being evaluated for syncope, which persisted after pacemaker insertion, shows sustained monomorphic ventricular tachycardia (SMVT) which terminates spontaneously. A brief period of ventricular pacing follows ventricular tachycardia termination.
