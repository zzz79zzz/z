Classification of distal phalanx finger fractures
Distal phalanx fractures can be classified into the following categories according to the location of the fracture:
  • Tuft fractures - simple or comminuted
  • Shaft - transverse or longitudinal
  • Intra-articular fractures - palmar (volar) or dorsal
