Eccentric exercise for elbow epicondylitis
This photograph demonstrates an eccentric exercise for the wrist extensor muscles. Eccentric exercise involves the application of a load during the lengthening of a muscle and may assist with healing of a tendinopathy. Here the wrist extensors are contracting eccentrically against the resistance of an exercise band.
