Adrenal hyperplasia
Contrast-enhanced CT through the upper abdomen of a 72-year-old woman demonstrates uniform enlargement of the left adrenal gland (arrow), which is maintaining its shape; similar enlargement was present in the right adrenal gland (not shown). These findings are consistent with bilateral adrenal hyperplasia.
CT: computed tomography.
