Orbital zygomatic fracture
(A) Axial CT image demonstrates fractures of the posterior wall of the maxillary sinus (arrows), fracture of the zygomatic arch (dashed arrow), and gas in the malar soft tissues on the left (arrowhead). Notice the asymmetry between the zygomatic bones.
(B) Direct coronal CT image demonstrates fractures through the floor of the orbit (thick arrow) and lateral wall of the maxilla on the left (thin arrow).
CT: computed tomography.
