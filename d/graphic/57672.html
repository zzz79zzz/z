Spermatic cord course
This transverse section of the anterior abdominal wall demonstrates the structures that form the external and internal inguinal rings. The external inguinal ring is formed by the external oblique aponeurosis. The internal inguinal ring is located in the transversalis fascia and composed of the transversus abdominus and internal oblique muscles. The broad arrow represents the course of the spermatic cord.
Adapted from O'Rahilly. Abdominal walls. In: Basic Human Anatomy, W.B. Saunders Company, Philadelphia 1983. p.230.
