Painful arc sign for rotator cuff pathology
Pain with active abduction beyond 90 degrees (ie, painful arc sign) suggests rotator cuff tendinopathy. The test is most useful when combined with other rotator cuff tests, such as the Neer and Hawkins-Kennedy tests.
