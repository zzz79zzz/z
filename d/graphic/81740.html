Erosive osteoarthritis
Plain radiograph of the hand shows erosive osteoarthritis. There is normal bone mineralization with extensive erosive changes involving the distal interphalangeal joint spaces (arrows). These changes are associated with soft tissue swelling and with osteophyte formation. The proximal interphalangeal and metacarpophalangeal joints and the intercarpal joint spaces are relatively spared.
