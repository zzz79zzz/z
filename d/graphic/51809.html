Incomplete right bundle branch block
Delayed conduction through the right bundle branch does not affect the initial portion of the QRS complex (septal activation), but alters the terminal portion. There is an rsR' pattern in leads aVR and V1, and a qRs morphology in leads I and V6. The width of the QRS complex is only slightly prolonged, between 0.10 and 0.12 seconds.
