Temporary abdominal closure
Temporary closure of the abdomen involves (A) covering the bowel with a fenestrated drape, (B) laying drains laterally and covering the bowel with a sterile towel, and (C) covering the wound with a sterile occlusive dressing with the drains exiting the drape superiorly (as shown) or inferiorly.
