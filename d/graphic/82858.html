Location of the wrist anatomic snuffbox
The anatomic snuffbox is located proximal to the base of the thumb between the extensor pollicis longus tendon medially and extensor pollicis brevis and abductor pollicis longus tendons laterally. A fracture of the scaphoid waist often manifests as tenderness in this region.
