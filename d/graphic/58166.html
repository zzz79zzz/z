Digoxin reduces death and hospitalization due to worsening HF
Among 6800 patients with HF randomized to therapy with digoxin or placebo, patients treated with digoxin had a significant decrease in the combined endpoints of death from HF and hospitalization for worsening HF (p<0.001).
HF: heart failure.
Data from: The Digitalis Investigation Group, N Engl J Med 1997; 336:525.
