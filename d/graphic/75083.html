Longitudinal ultrasound images of the right kidney with grayscale (A) and color Doppler (B) showing a dilated renal pelvis but no calyceal dilation, consistent with an extrarenal pelvis. Contrast-enhanced computed tomography (C) of a different patient with bilateral extrarenal pelvises.
Long: longitudinal; RT: right.
