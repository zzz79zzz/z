Time interval between the occurrence of pathologic Doppler findings and birth in idiopathic FGR and FGR associated with preeclampsia
The columns indicate the time interval (median time in days) between occurrence of pathologic Doppler findings and birth in pregnancies with idiopathic FGR (black) and in pregnancies with FGR associated with preeclampsia (gray).
FGR: fetal growth restriction; MV: mitral valve; TV: tricuspid valve; TR: tricuspid regurgitation; AoA: aortic arch; RF: reversed flow; UV: umbilical vein; DPuls: double pulsation; DV: ductus venosus; AoI: aortic isthmus; PI: pulsatility index; UA: umbilical artery; MCA: middle cerebral artery; PSV: peak systolic velocity; HA: hepatic artery; IVC: inferior vena cava.
From: Mari G, Hanif F, Kruger M. Sequence of cardiovascular changes in IUGR in pregnancies with and without preeclampsia.
