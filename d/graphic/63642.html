Radiograph of dactylitis and periosteal reaction in child with psoriatic arthritis
Hand/wrist radiographs from a child with psoriatic juvenile idiopathic arthritis (psJIA). Note dactylitic swelling of the digit (arrows) and periosteal reaction of bone (arrowheads) in comparison with the contralateral hand.
