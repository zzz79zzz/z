Medial orbital rim fracture
A 43-year-old female softball coach is seen by you in the emergency department after sustaining an accidental blow to the left eye with a baseball bat from one of her players. She tells you that she cannot move her eye normally and it feels trapped. This coronal image from computed tomography of her orbits show a medial orbital rim fracture (arrow).
