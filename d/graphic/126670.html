Steps in goal-oriented care
GAS: goal attainment scaling; PROMs: patient-reported outcome measures.
​* Patient goals may be categorized as:
  • Disease specific versus overarching
  • Objective versus subjective
  • Maintenance versus improvement
  • Short-term versus long-term
¶ Reevaluation of goals may include:
  • Discarding goals
  • Modifying or recalibrating goals
  • Setting new goals
From: Reuben DB, Jennings LA. Putting goal-oriented patient care into practice. J Am Geriatr Soc 2019; 67(7):1342-1344. https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/jgs.15885.
