Unstable midshaft forearm fracture
Very unstable complete midshaft forearm fracture in a 14-year-old child. This fracture required open reduction and internal fixation in the operating room. Both bones are 100 percent displaced dorsally and 50 percent displaced laterally. There is no intact periosteum.
