Chest CT of COVID-19 pneumonia, early phase
Chest CT axial image (A) and coronal reformation (B) show foci of ground-glass opacities with "crazy paving pattern" forming a network of reticular opacities within the ground-glass opacities. These are classic findings of the early phase of organizing pneumonia in COVID-19.
CT: computed tomography.
