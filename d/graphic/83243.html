Bone marrow aspirate in T cell acute lymphoblastic leukemia
Bone marrow aspirate shows many lymphoblasts with different sizes, immature chromatin pattern, and inconspicuous nucleoli. A few lymphoblasts reveal vacuolated cytoplasm. Wright-Giemsa, 100x magnification.
