LH pulses stimulate progesterone release in mid-luteal phase
Serum concentrations of luteinizing hormone and progesterone during 24 hours of blood sampling at 10-minute intervals in a typical female studied during the mid-luteal phase. There is a significant correlation between LH pulses and increased serum progesterone concentrations. To convert serum progesterone values to nmol/L, multiply by 3.18.
LH: luteinizing hormone.
Data from: Filicori M, Butler JP, Crowley WF Jr. Neuroendocrine regulation of the corpus luteum in the human. Evidence for pulsatile progesterone secretion. J Clin Invest 1984; 73:1638.
