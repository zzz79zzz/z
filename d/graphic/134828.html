RT: radiation therapy.

* Neoadjuvant systemic chemotherapy has the potential to convert some patients with unresectable hepatic metastases to resectable disease, but the true frequency with which this occurs is probably low (5 to 30%).

¶ Subsequent management of the liver metastases is based on response to initial chemotherapy and RT of the primary tumor.
