Epinephrine lowers the plasma potassium
Plasma potassium concentration during an infusion of epinephrine (in physiologic doses) in six patients pretreated with a placebo or a thiazide diuretic for seven days. The plasma potassium concentration fell in both groups, but reached potentially dangerous levels in the diuretic-treated patients who had mild baseline hypokalemia.
Data from Struthers, AD, Whitesmith, R, Reid, JL, Lancet 1983; 1:1358.
