US hip gluteus medius insertion onto superior posterior facet in transverse view
This ultrasound scan shows the gluteus medius tendon insertion onto the greater trochanter in the transverse plane (short-axis view).
Arrows: subgluteus maximus bursa (trochanteric bursa); arrowheads: gluteus medius tendon; A: superior posterior facet of greater trochanter; B: musculotendinous transition of gluteus maximus muscle; C: gluteus maximus muscle.
Left side of image: posterior; right side of image: anterior.
