Ultrasound acute hamstring strain
This ultrasound image shows the area where the hamstring tendons insert onto the ischial tuberosity. In this image, a small, hyperechoic bone spur (arrow) and hypoechoic change representing edema (thick arrow) are seen, findings that suggest acute hamstring injury.
