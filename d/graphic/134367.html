Appearance of a nipple after breastfeeding
When your baby is latched on properly, breastfeeding should be comfortable and your nipple will look full and round after the feed, rather than creased, sloped, or compressed. Nipple compression can cause pain and injury to the nipple and makes it harder for your baby to get milk.
