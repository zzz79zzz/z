Wright-Giemsa staining from a malignant effusion of BIA-ALCL
(A) Wright-Giemsa staining from a malignant effusion demonstrating pleomorphic cells with horseshoe-shaped nuclei, nuclear folding, and abundant vacuolated cytoplasm consistent with BIA-ALCL (1000x magnification).
(B) Tissue section demonstrates sheets of large cells positive for CD30 staining on immunohistochemistry (440x magnification).

BIA-ALCL: breast implant-associated anaplastic large cell lymphoma.
