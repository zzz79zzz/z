Shave biopsy technique
The shave biopsy can be facilitated by raising the lesion with a wheal of injected anesthetic. When performing a superficial shave biopsy, a number 15 blade on a scalpel is held tangential to the skin surface. The lesion is then removed with a smooth sweeping stroke of the blade going just under and across the lesion.
