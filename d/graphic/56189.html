Sinus tarsi anatomy
The sinus tarsi is the space between the medial neck of the talus and the superior aspect of the anterior calcaneus. It runs an oblique course between the talus and calcaneus from lateral to medial coursing anterior to posterior. It is the lateral extension of the tarsal canal.
