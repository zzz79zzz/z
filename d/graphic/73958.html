Single-lead electrocardiogram (ECG) showing atrial fibrillation
Lead V1 showing coarse AF with moderate ventricular response. The two characteristic findings in AF are present: the very rapid atrial fibrillatory waves (f waves), which are variable in appearance; and the irregularly irregular ventricular response as the R-R interval between beats is unpredictable. Coarse AF may appear similar to atrial flutter. However, the variable height and duration of the f waves differentiate them from atrial flutter (F) waves, which are identical in appearance and occur at a constant rate of about 250 to 350 beats/min.
AF: atrial fibrillation.
