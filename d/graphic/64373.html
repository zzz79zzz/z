Passive neck rotation
Loss of rotation is the universal sign of neck pathology. The patient is asked to relax. The examiner places one hand on the shoulder and one hand on the chin. The neck is passively rotated to the affected side and compared with rotation to the opposite side. Rotation less than 90 degrees is abnormal.
