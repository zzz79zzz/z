Central perception of pain
The transmission of incoming nociceptive impulses is modulated by dorsal horn circuitry that receives input from peripheral touch receptors and from descending pathways that involve the limbic cortical systems (orbital frontal cortex, amygdala, and hypothalamus), periaqueductal endogenous analgesic center in the midbrain, pontine noradrenergic neurons, and the nucleus raphe magnus (NRM) in the medulla. Dashed lines indicate inhibition or modulation.
* Location of opioid receptors.
