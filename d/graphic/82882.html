External rotation testing of the shoulder
Isometric testing of the infraspinatus tendon is used to assess the strength and integrity of the tendon, as well as to elicit pain indicative of injury. This test can be performed by having the patient attempt to externally rotate their adducted arm while the clinician resists the movement with their hand
