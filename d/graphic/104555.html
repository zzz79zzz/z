US distal anterior thigh merging of quadriceps tendons
The ultrasound image above shows all three layers of the quadriceps tendon in the transverse plane (short-axis).
SL: superficial layer; ML: middle layer; DL: deep layer; QFP: quadriceps fat pad; PFFP: prefemoral fat pad.
Left side of image: lateral; right side of image: medial.
