Blood vessel within a colonic diverticulum
Endoscopy showing a blood vessel within a diverticulum. The blood vessel is separated from the bowel lumen only by mucosa. Over time, the vessel wall is exposed to injury along its luminal aspect, possibly leading to segmental weakness which predisposes to rupture into the lumen.
