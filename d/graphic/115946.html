Hyperostosis and osteitis of right humerus in SAPHO syndrome
Conventional radiograph (A) and coronal T1W MRI (B) image of the right humerus showing hyperostosis and osteitis with a mixed lytic and sclerotic appearance.
SAPHO: synovitis, acne, pustulosis, hyperostosis, and osteitis; T1W: T1-weighted; MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
