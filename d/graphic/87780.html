Pathological findings in PAM
Section of brain from a case of primary meningoencephalitis caused by Naegleria fowleri. A portion of a blood vessel (B) and the surrounding perivascular region are seen. Numerous trophic amoebae are present in the perivascular space. Hematoxylin-eosin stain. Bar measures 12 microm.
PAM: primary amebic meningoencephalitis.
