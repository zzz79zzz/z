Congenital syphilis: Interstitial keratitis
This photograph shows a stromal haze in both eyes of this child due to interstitial keratitis, a manifestation of late congenital syphilis. Interstitial keratitis is an inflammation of the connective tissue structure of the cornea. It usually is bilateral.
