Monoclonal plasma cells in immunoglobulin light chain (AL) amyloidosis
Immunoperoxidase stain of a bone marrow biopsy from a patient with immunoglobulin light chain (AL) amyloidosis. Evidence for a monoclonal plasma cell disease is provided by the intense staining for lambda light chains (left panel) with almost no staining for kappa light chains (right panel).
