Pseudomyxoma peritonei on CT scan
An axial CT scan (A) of an 85-year-old patient with pseudomyxoma peritonei shows large cystic accumulations of mucus (arrows) surrounded by calcified rims. A coronal reconstruction (B) shows that the cystic accumulations in the upper abdomen impinge on the diaphragm (arrowhead) and the liver (arrow).
CT: computed tomography.
