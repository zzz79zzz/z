Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) at term equivalent age in a preterm infant demonstrating multiple, bilateral punctate (grade 1) CBH.
(A) T2-weighted image demonstrating the punctate lesions with hypointense signal in both cerebellar hemispheres.
(B) Susceptibility-weighted image showing the extent of the lesions more clearly.
CBH: Cerebellar hemorrhage.
