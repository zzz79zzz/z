Thickened liquids
  • "Mildly thick" or "level 2" liquids – Nectar-thick, will pour easily off of a spoon or run through the tines of a fork. It will leave a thin coat on the spoon.
  • "Moderately thick" or "level 3" liquids – Honey-thick, will slowly drip through the tines of a fork or come off of a spoon in drips or dollops.
  • "Extremely thick" or "level 4" liquids – Pureed or pudding-thick, will sit on a fork or spoon and not pour off. It is hard to drink extremely thick liquids with a straw.
