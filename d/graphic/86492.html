Nasal foreign body removal with a balloon catheter
The catheter (5 to 8 French Foley or Fogarty balloon catheter) is lubricated with 2 percent lidocaine jelly and advanced past the object. The balloon is then inflated with 2 to 3 mL of air, and the catheter is withdrawn from the nose with the object.
