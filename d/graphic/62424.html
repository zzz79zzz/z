Chest radiograph of a newborn with a type 2 congenital pulmonary airway malformation (CPAM) in the right upper lobe. Large cysts are present inferiorly, and smaller more homogeneous cysts are present superiorly.
* CPAM was previously known as congenital cystic adenomatoid malformation (CCAM).
