Steady state after initiation of a thiazide diuretic
Sodium and potassium balance (intake minus excretion) after the administration of 100 mg of hydrochlorothiazide to three normal subjects. Negative balance persisted for only three days for sodium and six days for potassium before a steady state was reestablished, in which intake and excretion were roughly equal.
Data from Maronde RF, Milgrom M, Vlachakis ND, Chan L. Response of thiazide-induced hypokalemia to amiloride. JAMA 1983; 249:237.
