Interstitial lung disease in scleroderma
The CT scan through the lung bases in a patient with scleroderma shows bibasilar interstitial lung disease with ground glass changes in the right lower lobe. Note also the patulous esophagus (dashed arrow) and bilateral pleural effusions (black arrows). The heart is also enlarged.
CT: computed tomography.
