Continuous wave Doppler of mitral regurgitation
The transmitral continuous wave Doppler in severe mitral regurgitation shows an E wave dominant, restrictive inflow pattern; there is a high velocity E wave, which in this example is 1.7 m/sec. The A wave is small and the E/A ratio is therefore increased.
