Interstitial lung disease with honeycombing in scleroderma
High resolution CT in a patient with scleroderma and interstital lung disease showing a reticular pattern, honeycombing, and traction bronchiectasis in both lung fields. This pattern correlates with fibrotic changes on lung biopsy and is consistent with usual interstitial pneumonia.
CT: computed tomography.
