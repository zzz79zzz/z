Scarlatiniform rash
Scarlet fever rash on the volar surface of the forearm. The scarlet fever rash first appears as tiny red bumps on the chest and abdomen that may spread all over the body. Looking like a sunburn, it feels like a rough piece of sandpaper, and lasts about two to five days.
