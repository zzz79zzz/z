Beta blocker enhances kalemic response to a potassium load
Change in the plasma potassium concentration after a potassium chloride infusion in control subjects (dashed line) and those treated with a beta blocker (solid line). The rise in the plasma potassium concentration was signficantly greater in the presence of beta blockade.
Data from: Rosa RM, Silva P, Young JB, et al. N Engl J Med 1980; 302:431.
