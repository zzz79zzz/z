Dental/oral complications of immunodeficiency
IgA: immunoglobulin A; IgG: immunoglobulin G.
* The failure of up to 5 permanent teeth to develop (also called tooth agenesis).
¶ An abnormal tooth shape in which the roots are reduced in size and the body of the tooth is enlarged, most often affecting molars.
Δ Also bleeding and petechiae.
Adapted from: Peacock ME, Arce RM, Cutler CW. Periodontal and other oral manifestations of immunodeficiency diseases. Oral Dis 2016. DOI: 10.1111/odi.12584.
