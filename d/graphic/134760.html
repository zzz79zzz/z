Ratio of the brainstem diameter to the brainstem to occipital bone diameter
[A] and [B] are mid-sagittal views of the fetal brain demonstrating the measurements of brain stem diameter and brain stem to occipital bone (BSOB) diameter. Three lines are drawn, along the posterior border of the sphenoid bone (line A), along the middle of the line produced by the posterior border of the brain stem and the anterior border of the fourth ventricle (line B) and along the anterior border of the occipital bone (line C). The vertical distances between lines A and B and between B and C are the brain stem diameter and BSOB diameter, respectively.
From: Lachmann R, Chaoui R, Moratalla J, et al. Posterior brain in fetuses with open spina bifida at 11 to 13 weeks.
