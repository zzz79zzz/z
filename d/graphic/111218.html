FEV1 versus BMI percentile for adults with cystic fibrosis, 20 to 40 years, United States 2021
For adults, the goal for FEV1 percent predicted is ≥75. The goal for BMI is ≥22 kg/m2 for females and ≥23 kg/m2 for males.
FEV1: forced expiratory volume in 1 second; BMI: body mass index.
