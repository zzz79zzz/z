Oral lesions in Peutz-Jeghers syndrome
Photograph shows the characteristic circumoral pigmentation in a patient with the Peutz-Jeghers syndrome. The pigmentation may not be obvious as in this patient, and it should always be sought carefully in young patients presenting with unexplained gastrointestinal bleeding, particularly if there is a family history of such bleeding.
