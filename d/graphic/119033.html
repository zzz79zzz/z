Saphenopopliteal junction
An original photograph was enhanced with Photoshop CS-4 to highlight the relevant anatomy of the saphenopopliteal fossa. The small saphenous vein (SSV) actually curls under the tibial nerve (TN) to conjoin in the popliteal vein.
