Pattern of TOF stimulation (lower panel) and the evoked muscle response during partial (moderate) neuromuscular block (upper panel). Note there is no muscle response to the fourth TOF stimulus. For further information, refer to UpToDate content on monitoring neuromuscular blockade.
mA: milliamperes; T1: first twitch in sequence; T2: second twitch in sequence; T3: third twitch in sequence; T4: fourth twitch in sequence; TOF: train-of-four; TOFC: train-of-four count.
