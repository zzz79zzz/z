Peripheral artery disease risk loci and known causal risk factors
Peripheral artery disease risk loci identified in this GWAS analysis are depicted along with the plausible relationship to the underlying causal risk factor. Loci names are based on the nearest genes; however, the causal gene(s) remains unclear for some associated loci, and, as such, the resultant annotation may prove incorrect in some cases.
