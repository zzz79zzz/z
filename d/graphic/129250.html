Flap coverage options for pressure-induced trochanter injury
Selected flap coverage options for trochanteric pressure sores are shown:

(A) Posteriorly based thigh rotation advancement flap
(B) V-to-Y advancement flap with TFL muscle
(C) Posteriorly based TFL transposition flap
(D) Antero-lateral thigh flap with or without vastus lateralis
TFL: tensor fascia lata.
