The pathway of the biosynthesis of docosahexaenoic acid (DHA) and inhibitory effects that the preterm diet and steroids could have on the ability to internally synthesize adequate DHA.
LA: linolenic acid; ALA: alpha-linolenic acid; GLA: gamma-linolenic acid; SA: stearidonic acid; PGs: prostaglandins; DGLA: di-homo gamma-linolenic acid; ETA: eicosatetraenoic acid; AA: arachidonic acid; EPA: eicosapentaenoic acid; DPA: docosapentaenoic acid; DHA: docosahexaenoic acid.
