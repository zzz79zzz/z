US lateral knee extended field transverse view
This ultrasound image provides an extended field of view of the lateral knee structures in the transverse plane.
Small arrowheads: iliotibial band (short-axis); large arrowheads: musculotendinous junction of biceps femoris muscle (short-axis); dotted ellipse: peroneal nerve (short-axis).
Left side of image: posterior; right side of image: anterior.
