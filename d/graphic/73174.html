Riedel's thyroiditis: Microscopic section of thyroid parenchyma
Microscopic section (hematoxylin and eosin, magnification x 100) of the thyroid gland from a patient with Riedel's thyroiditis demonstrates replacement of the thyroid parenchyma by dense collagenous fibrous tissue mixed with lymphocytes, plasma cells, and eosinophilis.
