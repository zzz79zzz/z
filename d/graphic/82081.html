The characteristic electrolyte secretory concentration patterns observed in pancreatic fluid in response to secretin administration
Sodium and potassium are maintained at stable concentrations and isotonic to plasma. There is a gradual rise in bicarbonate concentration to a peak of approximately 120 mEq/L. Chloride exhibits a reciprocal decrease in concentration.
