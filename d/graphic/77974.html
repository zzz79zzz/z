AP view of midshaft femur fracture with intramedullary rod
According to many orthopedic sources, standard treatment of a femoral shaft fracture is an antegrade reamed intramedullary nail, shown in the radiograph above. This technique enables rigid fixation and is associated with a 98 to 99% union rate.
AP: anterior posterior.
