Efficacy of exercise training in HF
Plot of individual values for the ventilatory anaerobic threshold (VO2) before and after exercise training in 12 patients with chronic heart failure. All but one patient improved and the rise in mean VO2 (shown in bold lines with 95 percent confidence intervals) was highly significant (p = 0.01).
Data from: Sullivan MJ, Cobb FR, Chest 1992; 101:340S.
