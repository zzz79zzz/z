Doppler of ductus venosus in recipient twin
Doppler study of the fetal ductus venosus in this recipient twin at approximately 25 weeks of gestation. Fetal ascites can be noted in the top image. In the bottom image, the arrow points to the reversed "a" wave of the ductus venosus that occurs during diastole.
