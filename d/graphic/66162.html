Location of the Bartholin glands and ducts
Each Bartholin gland is approximately 0.5 centimeter (cm) in size and drains into a duct 2.5 cm long. The ducts emerge onto the vestibule, one at each side of the vaginal orifice, in the groove (superficial perineal pouch) between the hymenal ring and the labia minora.
