Similar outcome in myocarditis and IDC
Comparison of survival in 27 patients with biopsy-proved myocarditis (definite or borderline) versus 58 patients with idiopathic dilated cardiomyopathy (IDC) who had a negative endomyocardial biopsy. The survival rates were equivalent in the two disorders.
Data from Grogan, M, Redfield, MM, Bailey, KR, et al, J Am Coll Cardiol 1995; 26:80.
