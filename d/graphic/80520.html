Zipper fastener penile entrapment release with a screwdriver
To release the entrapped penile tissue, place the edge of a flathead screwdriver between the face plates of the zipper fastener on the side opposite of the entrapped tissue and rotate the tip towards the median bar of the fastener. These steps will widen the face plates and allow the tissue to be freed.
