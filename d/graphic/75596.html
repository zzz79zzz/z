Immunoglobulin genes
The mu (µ) and delta (δ) C genes are those nearest to the assembled VDJ sequence. Transcription of a complete immunoglobulin heavy chain gene starts at the V sequence and proceeds through the mu (µ) and delta (δ) sequences before terminating. The mature B cell expresses both IgM and IgD, which are the only isotypes produced by B lymphocytes prior to contact with antigen.
L: light chain gene; C: constant; V: variable sequence of heavy chain gene; D: diversity sequence; J: joining sequence of heavy chain gene; µ: mu; δ: delta; γ: gamma; α: alpha; ε: epsilon; IgM: immunoglobulin M; IgD: immunoglobulin D.
