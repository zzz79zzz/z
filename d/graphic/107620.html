Bicornuate uterus: Hysterosalpingogram and magnetic resonance images
Hysterosalpingogram (HSG) and magnetic resonance images (MRI) from a 30-year-old woman with infertility and bicornuate uterus (A and B). HSG (A) shows two uterine cavities (asterisks) originating from a single cervix. MRI fast spin echo T2-weighted image (B) shows a cleft (arrow) in the fundal myometrial contour (arrow).
