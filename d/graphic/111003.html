Malignant pleural mesothelioma overall survival by stage and histology TNM AJCC 8th edition
Overall survival according to "best" overall stage, AJCC 8th edition, for (A) cases with epithelioid histology, and (B) cases with non-epithelioid histology. Included cases: All classifiable cases. Best stage is defined as pathological stage where available, clinical stage otherwise. Statistical method: Kaplan-Meier.
MST: median survival time.
