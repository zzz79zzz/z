Transesophageal echocardiogram (TEE) showing a vegetation on the pulmonic valve
The transesophageal echocardiographic image, obtained with the longitudinal plane through the junction of the right ventricular outflow tract and the pulmonary artery, shows a vegetation of the pulmonic valve (arrow). This mass was not seen on transthoracic echocardiography.
LA: left atrium; LV: left ventricle; RV: right ventricle; PA: pulmonary artery.
