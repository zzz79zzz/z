Dermoscopic features of pigmentation induced by fluorouracil
The drug-induced, light brown pigmentation is accentuated on the surface ridges, resembling the parallel ridge pattern. This pigmentation can be differentiated from melanoma based upon the presence of multiple brown macules bilaterally on the palms and soles and history of drug intake.
