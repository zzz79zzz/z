Bone marrow aspirate from a patient with megaloblastic anemia secondary to vitamin B12 deficiency. This field shows a predominance of megaloblastic erythroid precursors (black arrows), two giant metamyelocytes (red arrows) and a mitotic figure (blue arrow). (Wright-Giemsa).
