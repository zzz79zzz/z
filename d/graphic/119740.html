Aortic aneurysms following repair of coarctation of the aorta
Panel A: Maximum-intensity-projection contrast-enhanced MR angiogram of a 34-year-old man with history of aortic coarctation operated on at age 14 years with prosthetic patch aortoplasty placement of a Dacron (DuPont) patch. There is a pseudoaneurysm (arrows) in the region of aortic isthmus where coarctation was repaired.
Panel B: Three-dimensional volume-rendered image in 58-year-old woman shows severe recurrent aortic coarctation (arrow), with abnormal aneurysmal enlargement of adjacent segments of thoracic aorta
MR: magnetic resonance.
