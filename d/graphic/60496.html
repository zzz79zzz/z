Dextrocardia
Characteristic changes of dextrocardia include a negative P wave and QRS complex in lead 1, since atrial and ventricular depolarization begin on the left and spread to the right. There is also reverse R wave progression across the precordium; the R wave is tallest in V1, and progressively decreases in amplitude in leads V2 to V6.
