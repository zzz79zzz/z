Abnormal lateral elbow
Lateral radiograph of the elbow of a 2-year-old girl. Both the anterior and posterior fat pads are elevated (small arrows). In addition, the anterior humeral line passes along the anterior edge of the capitellum rather than through its center. Mild buckling of the posterior cortex of the distal humerus can be seen (large arrow).
