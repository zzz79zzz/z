Dilated gastrojejunostomy
Dilation of the gastrojejunostomy is considered a technical failure of a gastric bypass. A limited UGI series may demonstrate the stomal diameter of the gastrojejunostomy. Endoscopy will show a dilated gastrojejunostomy as pictured here.
