Infectious colitis on ultrasound
Transabdominal ultrasonography in a patient with infectious colitis. Color Doppler imaging, reveals hypervascularity especially of the submucosa. The bowel wall is thickened (10 mm). Microbiological examination confirmed salmonella infection.
