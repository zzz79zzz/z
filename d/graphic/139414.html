Congenital cutaneous juvenile xanthogranuloma
Firm, pink-colored, solitary nodule with ulceration and raised, peripheral rim on right lateral thigh at 4 weeks of life. No evidence of systemic involvement.
From: Oza VS, Stringer T, Campbell C, et al. Congenital-type juvenile xanthogranuloma: A case series and literature review.
