Fingernail capillaroscopy in mixed connective tissue disease
Capillaroscopy showing widening and thickening of the nailfold capillary loops and patchy loss of capillary density (arrow) in a patient with MCTD. This pattern is also commonly seen in patients with SSc, but it is rare in those with classical SLE.
MCTD: mixed connective tissue disease; SSc: systemic sclerosis (scleroderma); SLE: systemic lupus erythematosus.
