Pediatric cardiac arrest algorithm for 2 or more rescuers for suspected or confirmed COVID-19 patients
The 2020 guidelines for basic life support of patients with breathing but no pulse recommend rescue breathing as follows: 1 breath every 2 to 3 seconds (20 to 30 breaths/minute) and addition of chest compressions if pulse remains <60/minute with poor perfusion. The new algorithm is available here.
BLS: basic life support; COVID-19: coronavirus disease 2019; PPE: personal protective equipment; AED: automated external defibrillator; CPR: cardiopulmonary resuscitation; ALS: advanced life support.
