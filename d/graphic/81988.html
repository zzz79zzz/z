Absence of pus in leukocyte-adhesion deficiency
The absence of pus formation at the sites of infection is a hallmark of leukocyte-adhesion deficiency syndromes. This elbow lesion shows the effect of a profound impairment of leukocyte mobilization into extravascular sites of inflammation.
