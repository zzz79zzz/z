Mobitz type I AV block and acute inferior MI
This rhythm strip shows sinus rhythm with Mobitz type I (Wenckebach) atrioventricular block with 4:3 and 3:2 conduction and progressive prolongation of the PR intervals of conducted beats. The marked ST segment elevation suggests acute inferior wall ischemia or infarction that is likely the substrate for the arrhythmia.
