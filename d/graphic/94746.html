Manual replacement of puerperal uterine inversion
The inverted uterus is replaced by placing a hand inside the vagina and pushing the fundus along the long axis of the vagina toward the umbilicus. If a constriction ring is palpable, pressure should be applied to the part of the fundus nearest the ring to ease it through from bottom to top.
