Mobilization of the splenic flexure
This figure depicts mobilizing the splenic flexure, one of the challenging components of a left colectomy. The dissection progresses from the midtransverse colon medially to distally, a technique particularly helpful in patients with obesity or in settings when the splenic flexure cannot be safely approached and mobilized laterally.
