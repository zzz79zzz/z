The EUTOS long-term survival score (ELTS)[4] www.leukemia-net.org/content/leukemias/cml/elts_score/ Low risk, intermediate risk, high risk
* These scoring systems were designed for patients with newly diagnosed chronic myeloid leukemia (CML) who have not yet received any treatment, including hydroxyurea. In addition, the EUTOS score was specifically designed to predict outcomes among patients undergoing initial treatment with imatinib.
