Posterior tibialis tendon tear ultrasound stump sign
This long-axis ultrasound image shows the retracted distal portion of the torn posterior tibialis tendon (arrow). The retracted tendon gives the appearance of a stump, and the ultrasound finding is referred to as the "stump sign."
M: medial malleolus.
