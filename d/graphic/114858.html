MRI grade three stress fracture of second metatarsal
The MRI images above show a grade three stress fracture. Image A is from an axial T1 MRI study and demonstrates low signal in the marrow of the second metatarsal (arrow). Image B is from an axial STIR MRI study and demonstrates marrow edema and surrounding soft-tissue edema (thick arrow).
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
