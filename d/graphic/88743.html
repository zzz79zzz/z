Chest radiograph showing lymphangitic carcinomatosis in a patient with ovarian cancer
Chest radiograph shows diffuse bilateral, fine linear and nodular opacities that correspond to thickened interlobular septa of the lung parenchyma in a patient with metastatic ovarian cancer. This appearance is consistent with lymphangitic carcinomatosis.
