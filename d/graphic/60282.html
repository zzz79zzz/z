Coronal CT of hamate fracture
This coronal CT of the wrist shows two larger comminuted fragments (arrows) and some smaller pieces, resulting from a fracture of the hamate (H). The base of the adjacent fifth metacarpal is also indicated (dashed arrow).
CT: computed tomography.
