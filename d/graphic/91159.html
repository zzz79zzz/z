Pleomorphic undifferentiated sarcoma on CT scan
A CT scan reformatted in the coronal plane following contrast administration (A) shows an enhancing heterogeneous mass in the vastus lateralis muscle of the right thigh. An axial image (B) shows the mass as well as the edema in the surrounding soft tissues (short arrow).
CT: computed tomography.
