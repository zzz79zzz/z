Integrative euvolemia/congestion evaluation at discharge
JVP: jugular venous pulsation; 6MWT: 6‐minute walk test; NP: natriuretic peptide; BNP: B‐type natriuretic peptide; NT‐proBNP: N‐terminal pro B‐type natriuretic peptide; HJR: hepatojugular reflux.
* The cut‐off for NT‐proBNP to exclude congestion as endorsed by the Heart Failure Association position paper on grading congestion is higher than the cut‐off endorsed by the European Society of Cardiology guidelines to exclude acute heart failure.
¶ Chest radiograph can be clear but presence of abnormalities suggests higher degree of congestion. Partially adapted from the Heart Failure Association position paper on assessing and grading congestion in acute heart failure.[1]
