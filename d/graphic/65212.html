String sign on MR angiography
Left internal carotid artery dissection in a 43-year-old man. A hemorrhagic crescent on axial T1 cervical MRI (A) and the string sign on follow-up neck MR angiography (B) are consistent with dissection.
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging; MR: magnetic resonance.
