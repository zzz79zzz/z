Approach to evaluation for Lynch syndrome in individuals with a known family mutation
* Lynch syndrome should be suspected in individuals with synchronous or metachronous colorectal cancer, colorectal cancer prior to 50 years of age, multiple Lynch-associated cancers (eg, colorectal and endometrial, ovarian, stomach, small intestine, or renal pelvis/ureter), and in cases of familial clustering of Lynch-associated cancers.
