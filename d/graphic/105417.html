US lateral patellar synovial recess
The ultrasound image above shows a transverse view of the lateral synovial recess (asterisks), and the overlying iliotibial band (arrowheads), which blends with the lateral retinaculum as it inserts into the patella.
Arrowheads: lateral patellar retinaculum; *: lateral recess of knee joint.
Left of the screen: lateral; right of the screen: medial.
