Musculoskeletal
(≥1 joint)

  • Range of motion

  • Pain control
  • Self-care (assessment, training and equipment needs)
  • N/A
  • Geriatric MD
  • Orthopedic MD
  • Rehabilitation MD

  • 1 to 6 weeks
  • Home health or
  • Outpatient
PT: physical therapy; OT: occupational therapy; SLP: speech-language pathology; RN: registered nurse; MD: medical doctor; MS: multiple sclerosis; TBI: traumatic brain injury.
