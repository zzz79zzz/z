Potential symptoms and signs of anaphylaxis during pregnancy
Potential symptoms and signs of anaphylaxis during pregnancy. Sudden onset of symptoms and signs in two or more body organ systems is characteristic. Most symptoms and signs are similar to those in nonpregnant patients with anaphylaxis. Itching in the vulvar/vaginal areas, low back pain, uterine cramps, fetal distress, and preterm labor can occur.
