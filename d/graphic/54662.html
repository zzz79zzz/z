Coronal computed tomograph of the calcaneus and its anatomic correlate
On the right, an anatomic correlation of anatomy (calcaneus with talus removed); and on the left, the coronal CT scan of the calcaneus. This CT view is perhaps the most important view to assess the clinical reconstructability of the subtalar joint in displaced intra-articular fractures of the calcaneus.
CT: computed tomography.
