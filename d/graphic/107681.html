Descending thoracic aortic replacement CTD
Repair of a Crawford extent II thoracoabdominal aortic aneurysm with individual anastomosis to each of the visceral vessels. This type of repair is often used in patients with connective tissue disorders to prevent visceral artery aneurysmal dilation in the future.
