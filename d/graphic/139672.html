Measurements of pyloric muscle in hypertrophic pyloric stenosis
Sagittal ultrasound image demonstrates thickening of the pyloric muscle on each side of the pyloric canal (two "+" pairs, each measuring 0.5 cm) and elongation of the pyloric canal (dashed line, measuring 1.8 cm), compatible with hypertrophic pyloric stenosis.
