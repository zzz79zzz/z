Placement of King LT laryngeal tube airway device
The King LT is an example of a laryngeal tube, which are commonly used for emergency airway management. Laryngeal tubes include two balloon cuffs, one pharyngeal and one esophageal, with ventilation fenestrations in between that align with the glottic opening.
