Proximal fifth metatarsal acute diaphysis fracture foot radiograph
This oblique view radiograph of the foot shows an acute fracture (arrow) of the diaphysis of the proximal fifth metatarsal. The narrow fracture line and normal-appearing thin cortex adjacent to the fracture are consistent with an acute injury.
