Larva currens
Larva currens ("running" larva): Erythematous, edematous urticarial lesions (shown on the back and the abdomen). Larva currens can progress approximately 1 cm in 5 minutes and 5 to 15 cm per hour. The finding is pathognomonic for strongyloidiasis.
From: Corte LD, da Silva MV, Souza PR. Simultaneous Larva Migrans and Larva Currens Caused by Strongyloides stercoralis: A Case Report. Case Rep Dermatol Med 2013; 2013:381583.
