Dermoscopic features of advanced melanoma of the sole
There is an ulcerated nodule on the right, surrounded by a blue-white veil (asterisk). The parallel ridge pattern (square) as well as the irregular fibrillar pattern (circle) are also detected. Irregular streaks can be recognized at the periphery of the central, black pigmentation.
