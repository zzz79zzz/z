Vein mapping for future dialysis access using angiography. The images show the lower forearm (A), elbow region (B), mid-upper arm (C), and shoulder region (D) and demonstrate the following veins:
  • 1 – Lower cephalic vein
  • 2 – Upper cephalic vein
  • 3 – Basilic vein
  • 4 – Axillary vein
  • 5 – Subclavian vein
