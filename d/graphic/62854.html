Duodenum in scleroderma
Section of duodenum from a patient with scleroderma (Masson trichrome stain). There is normal villus structure and epithelial cell morphology (right side), a mild inflammatory cell infiltrate in the lamina propria, collagen (seen in dark blue) encapsulating Brunner's glands, and fibrous replacement of the muscularis.
