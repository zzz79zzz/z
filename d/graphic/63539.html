Cubitus varus deformity
Varus deformity of the elbow, also called cubitus varus, results in prominence of the elbow and decreased carrying angle (the forearm pointing towards the body when the arm is anatomic position). This abnormality is a complication of supracondylar fractures and lateral condyle elbow fractures.
