Necrobiosis lipoidica
A close-up view of ulcerated necrobiosis lipoidica on the anterior shin. There is surrounding atrophy and faintly visible underlying blood vessels in the affected area. The ulcerations themselves are clinically nondescript and can be challenging to distinguish clinically from other causes of ulceration in the absence of surrounding skin changes classic for necrobiosis lipoidica.
