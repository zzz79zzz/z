Acromegaly
Plain film of the hand in a 53-year-old woman who presented with enlarged hands shows thickening of the soft tissues, exaggeration of the bony tufts of the distal phalanges, and prominence of the muscular attachments of the proximal phalanges. These are all features consistent with acromegaly.
