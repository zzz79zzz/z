Telephone triage for croup
When assessing patients with suspected croup by phone, the health care provider must distinguish children who need immediate medical attention or further evaluation from those who can be managed at home. This figure summarizes factors that indicate the child should be evaluated in person. Refer to UpToDate topic on management of croup for additional details.
