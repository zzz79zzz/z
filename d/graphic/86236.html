Continuous beta agonist nebulization set-up for face mask delivery in children
Dilute the calculated albuterol dose per hour with 0.9% normal saline for inhalation to a final volume of 20 mL. Set the pump to nebulize continuously at 20 mL per hour, with a driving oxygen flow rate set at 8 to 10 mL per minute. The pump should be labeled "for respiratory use only" to minimize the risk of inadvertent intravenous administraion.
