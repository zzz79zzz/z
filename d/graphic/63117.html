Cutting the zipper mechanism
The zipper mechanism consists of two faceplates connected with a median bar. The technique most often recommended for release of entrapment within the zipper mechanism involves cutting the median bar, which allows the zipper to fall apart, releasing the entrapped skin. The median bar may be cut with wire cutters, bone cutters, or a mini hacksaw.
