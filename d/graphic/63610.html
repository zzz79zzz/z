Acute liver failure
Contrast-enhanced CT scan of the liver in a 35-year-old female who took an overdose of acetaminophen demonstrates a heterogenous poorly enhancing liver with areas of lower attenuation due to acute fatty replacement. Note also the patent recanalized paraumbilical vein coursing through the ligamentum teres (arrow).
