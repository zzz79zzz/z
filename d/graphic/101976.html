Neurogenic bladder causing hydronephrosis
Ultrasound of the right (A) and left (B) kidneys shows moderate hydronephrosis (*). Cystogram (C) shows a small volume hypertrophied bladder with a diverticulum (arrow) and trabeculation of the wall (arrowhead) consistent with a neurogenic bladder.
