Two-dimensional endovaginal ultrasound image of posterior mid-sagittal view in a woman with bilateral levator ani avulsion. Note that the anteroposterior diameter (dotted line) of the genital hiatus does not change appreciably with muscle contraction (squeeze).
T: transducer; LP: levator plate; AR: anorectum; C: cephalad; EAS: external anal sphincter; P: posterior.
