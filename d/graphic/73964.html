Distal cholangiocarcinoma as seen on MRCP
This magnetic resonance cholangiopancreatography (MRCP) image, obtained without having to opacify the bile ducts, demonstrates a circumferential narrowing of the distal common bile duct (CBD, arrow) due to a focal distal cholangiocarcinoma. The obstructing tumor is causing dilation of the CBD.
