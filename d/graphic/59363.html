Well-differentiated thymic carcinoid: histologic appearance
Organoid nests composed of uniform polygonal cells with centrally located nuclei and finely granular nuclear chromatin, forming rosettes, are characteristic features of carcinoid tumors. Necrosis is frequently seen in thymic carcinoid. (Hematoxylin and eosin, magnification 100x).
