Lentigo maligna melanoma
Medium power light photomicrograph of a lentigo maligna melanoma. Within a background of epidermal atrophy, the neoplastic melanocytes are arrayed along the dermo-epidermal junction in a lentiginous pattern. Small dyshesive nests are also seen along the junction (black arrows).
