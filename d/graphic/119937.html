Chest radiograph of a 3-month-old patient with adenosine deaminase deficiency
Chest radiograph of a 3-month-old patient with adenosine deaminase deficiency. The image highlights the absence of the thymus shadow (the common position of a normal thymus is marked by arrowheads), as well as squaring and spurring of the inferolateral margin of the scapula (arrow).
