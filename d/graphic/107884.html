Epiglottis elevating bar of LMA Fastrach
The LMA Fastrach (pictured on the right) incorporates an epiglottic elevating bar that extends lengthwise over the aperture through which the endotracheal tube exits the LMA. The elevating bar prevents the epiglottis from interfering with passage of the endotracheal tube.
