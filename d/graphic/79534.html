Thumb spica splint
The thumb spica splint is used for scaphoid fractures and extra-articular fractures of the thumb metacarpal, ulnar collateral ligament injuries (ie, gamekeeper's thumb or skier's thumb), or after reduction of thumb dislocations. The splint is placed on the radial aspect of the forearm, wrapping the thumb up to the distal interphalangeal joint.
