Parotid gland enlargement in Sjögren's disease on magnetic resonance imaging
Sjögren's disease can lead to massive salivary gland enlargement, which is shown here on magnetic resonance imaging (MRI). MRI studies should be performed with gadolinium and with "fat subtraction" to permit evaluation of the ductal system.
