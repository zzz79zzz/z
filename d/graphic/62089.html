Obesity

For type I endometrial cancer: OR 1.5 for overweight (BMI 25.0 to <30 kg/m2), 2.5 for class 1 obesity (30.0 to <35 kg/m2), 4.5 for class 2 obesity (35.0 to 39.9 kg/m2), and 7.1 for class 3 obesity (≥40.0 kg/m2).

For type II: OR 1.2 for overweight (BMI 25.0 to <30 kg/m2), 1.7 for class 1 obesity (30.0 to <35 kg/m2), 2.2 for class 2 obesity (35.0 to 39.9 kg/m2), and 3.1 for class 3 obesity (≥40.0 kg/m2).
