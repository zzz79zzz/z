Ultrasound of shoulder dislocation
The ultrasound images above show normal glenohumeral joint positioning and a dislocation. In the reduced shoulder (A), the humeral head is seen articulating with the glenoid (arrows). In the dislocated shoulder (B), the humeral head is seen anterior to the glenoid with significant space between the two (arrowheads).
