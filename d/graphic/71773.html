Changes in mBODE after lung volume reduction surgery
The percentage of patients (x axis) experiencing changes in mBODE (modified Body mass index, airflow Obstruction, Dyspnea, and Exercise capacity) (y axis) six months after randomization to medical therapy or bilateral lung volume reduction surgery (LVRS). Patients who had died or were not able to provide data to calculate mBODE at each time point are also illustrated.
