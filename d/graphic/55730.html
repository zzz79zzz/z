Intracellular calcium pyrophosphate dihydrate crystal
A faintly yellow, positively birefringent, rhomboidal, intracellular crystal characteristic of calcium pyrophosphate dihydrate (CPP) is seen in the synovial fluid of a patient with acute CPP crystal arthritis (pseudogout) when viewed with compensated, polarized microscopy.
