Abdominal scintigram showing a peritoneal leak in a peritoneal dialysis patient
(A) Fifteen-minute activity within the peritoneal cavity. At two hours (B and C), leakage of dialysate occurs into the mid-lower anterior abdominal wall. Small leakage into the scrotum (D) at two hours is also demonstrable with high-intensity scintiphoto.
P: pubis; S: scrotum.
