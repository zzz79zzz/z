MRI appearance of dementia with Lewy bodies compared with Alzheimer disease
Thin section coronal T1-weighted images from a 61-year-old male with pathologically proven DLB (A) and a 69-year-old male with AD (B). There is relative preservation of the medial temporal lobes and hippocampal structures in the patient with DLB as compared with the patient with AD.
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging; DLB: dementia with Lewy bodies; AD: Alzheimer disease.
