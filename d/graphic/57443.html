Dose-related increase in LVEF with carvedilol in nonischemic cardiomyopathy
Change in left ventricular ejection fraction (LVEF) among patients with dilated cardiomyopathy after the administration of carvedilol. There is a dose-related increase in LVEF in those with nonischemic cardiomyopathy; no such dose relationship is seen in patients with ischemic cardiomyopathy.
Data from Bristow MR, Gilbert EM, Abraham WT, et al. Circulation 1996; 94:2807.
