Chronic inflammatory arthritis following chikungunya virus infection
Chronic inflammatory arthritis involving the MCP and PIP joints in a patient more than three months after initial infection with chikungunya virus.
MCP: metacarpophalangeal; PIP: proximal interphalangeal.
From: Miner JJ, Yeang HXA, Fox JM, et al. Chikungunya viral arthritis in the United States: A mimic of seronegative rheumatoid arthritis.
