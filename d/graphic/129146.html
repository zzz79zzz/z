Computed tomography (CT) scan of the abdomen showing neutropenic colitis
CT scan of the abdomen and pelvis demonstrating marked wall thickening and intramural edema of the ascending colon and cecum (arrows) in a patient with diarrhea and neutropenia following hematopoietic stem cell transplantation for multiple myeloma. The clinical picture is consistent with neutropenic colitis.
