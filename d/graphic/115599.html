Genetic classification of Hermansky-Pudlak syndrome
Type of HPS MIM # Gene Locus Encoding function Comments
HPS-1 203300 HPS1 10q23.1-10q23.3 Transmembrane protein in BLOC-3 within BLOC-4 and BLOC-5
  • Common in northwestern Puerto Rico due to founder effect
  • Progressive pulmonary fibrosis and colitis
HPS-2 608233 ADTB3A, AP3B1, HPS2 5q14.1 Lysosomal trafficking
  • Neutropenia and/or immune defects
  • Pulmonary fibrosis, conductive hearing loss, dysplastic hip
HPS-3 614072 HPS3 3q24 Vesicle-related protein
  • Found in central Puerto Rico
  • Mild systemic findings
  • Colitis
HPS-4 614073 HPS4 22q11.2-22q12.2 Involved in BLOC-3 and BLOC-4
  • Pulmonary fibrosis, colitis
HPS-5 614074 HPS5, RU2, KIAA1017 11p13-11p15 Organelle biogenesis
  • Hyperlipidemia, elevated creatinine clearance
HPS-6 614075 HPS6, RU 10q24.32 Organelle biogenesis
  • Mild bleeding
HPS-7 614076 DTNBP1, HPS7 6p22.3 Component of BLOC-1
  • Mild lung disease
HPS-8 614077 BLOC1S3, HPS8 19q13.32 Vesicular transport protein
  • Mild systemic findings
HPS-9 614171 BLOC1S6, PLDN, PA, HPS9 15q21.1 Intracellular vesicle trafficking
  • Few cases described
  • Immunodeficiency
  • Developmental delay in some
HPS-10 617050 AP3D1 19p13.3 Lysosomal biogenesis
  • Few cases described
  • Microcephaly, seizures, global delay
  • Immunodeficiency
  • Pulmonary issues in some
HPS-11 619172 BLOC1S5 6p24.3 Lysosomal biogenesis; cargo transport
  • Few cases described
  • Mild systemic findings
HPS: Hermansky-Pudlak syndrome; BLOC: biogenesis of lysosome-related organelles complex.
