Obstetric diameters of the pelvic inlet
The three anteroposterior diameters of the pelvic inlet are the true conjugate (average diameter 11.0 cm), obstetric conjugate (average diameter 10.5 cm), and diagonal conjugate (average diameter 12.5 cm). Also shown is the anteroposterior diameter of the mid-pelvis.
P: sacral promontory; Sym: symphysis pubis.
