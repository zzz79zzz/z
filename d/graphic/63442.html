Contrast CT of tumor in pancreatic neck
Contrast-enhanced CT axial images of a patient with a solid, ill-defined tumor in the neck of the pancreas (arrow in A). No vascular encasement is observed. However, multiple small hypodense lesions are seen throughout the liver parenchyma (arrows in B and C), which are highly suspicious for metastatic disease.
