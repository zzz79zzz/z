Example of SCIT prescription form in use
Maintenance concentration and subsequent dilutions reported as volume/volume (v/v) dilutions with maintenance concentration 1:1 v/v.
SCIT: subcutaneous immunotherapy; AU: allergy unit; BAU: bioequivalent allergy unit; w/v: weight per volume ratio; PNU: protein nitrogen unit; 50% G: 50% glycerinated; Aq: aqueous; Ly: lyophilized; AP: alum-precipitated; AcP: acetone-precipitated; HSA: normal saline with 0.03% human serum albumin.
* Volume to add = (maintenance concentration/concentration of manufacturer's extract) × total volume.
¶ Target maintenance dose volume is 0.5 mL per injection.
