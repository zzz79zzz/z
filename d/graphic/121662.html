Maximum intensity projection lower extremity CT angiogram
Maximum intensity projection of a CT angiogram of the aorta and bilateral lower extremities. Note the absence of flow noted in the popliteal artery (arrow). Also note the similarity of this vascular image to a conventional catheter-based DSA.
CT: computed tomographic; DSA: digital subtraction angiography.
