Buddy taping of toe fracture
This photograph shows proper splinting of a fractured third toe to the neighboring second toe with gauze and tape. Note the felt pad between the toes to prevent skin maceration and help maintain alignment.
From: Downey MS, Lawrence GA. Digital and sesamoid fractures.
