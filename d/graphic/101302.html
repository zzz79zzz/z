Galeazzi forearm fracture with DRUJ instability
These radiographs show a Galeazzi fracture in an adult. The radial shaft fracture is obvious on both views (arrowhead on lateral view), as is the dramatic displacement of the distal ulna (arrow on lateral view), demonstrating complete disruption of the distal radioulnar joint (DRUJ).
