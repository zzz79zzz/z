Maintenance of adequate ELF antitrypsin activity with monthly infusions
Effect of monthly infusions of 250 mg/kg of alpha-1 antitrypsin (AAT) on epithelial lining fluid (ELF) levels of AAT in three patients. The AAT levels are based upon a true laboratory standard. The dashed line represents the theoretical protective threshold. AAT levels were maintained above this threshold with monthly infusions.
Redrawn from Hubbard, RC, Sellers, S, Czerski, D, et al, JAMA 1988; 260:1259.
