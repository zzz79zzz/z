Mammographic image of a mass: Large cyst
A BB (a radiopaque marker seen as a hyperdense dot on [A] and [B]) was placed on a palpable mass. The mammogram that followed shows a solitary, ill-defined soft tissue mass underlying the BB and surrounded by a ring. A subsequent ultrasound confirmed the cystic nature of the mass.
