Hospitalization for neutropenia
Estimated hospitalization rates (per 10,000 days at risk) and 95 percent confidence intervals (vertical bars) for bacterial infection per absolute neutrophil count (ANC) stratum in 2047 HIV-infected patients. The values are based upon the earliest lowest ANC stratum for each patient. Hospitalization rates increased progressively when the ANC fell below 750/µL.
Data from Jacobson, MA, Liu, RC, Davies, D, Cohen, PT, Arch Intern Med 1997; 157:1825.
