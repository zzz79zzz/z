Treatment of severe alcohol withdrawal when IV diazepam is not available*

IV: intravenous; CIWA: Clinical Institute Withdrawal Assessment for Alcohol; RASS: Richmond Agitation Sedation Scale; AWS: alcohol withdrawal syndrome.

* Doses are for adults. Medications and doses may need adjustment based upon comorbities (eg, liver disease), patient age, or other factors (eg, coadministered medications).

¶ Lorazepam infusion is titrated to effect, and additional symptom-based bolus doses, 2 to 4 mg IV, are given when titrating the infusion up.
