Insulin pen parts
All insulin pens have these parts:
  • Large outer needle cap
  • Disposable needle with a small inner needle cap
  • Cartridge filled with insulin
  • Dial or knob to adjust the dose
  • Window or area to view the dose
  • Button to give the shot

Some insulin pens are disposable and are thrown away when the insulin cartridge is empty. Other insulin pens are refilled by placing a new cartridge of insulin in the pen. Some insulin pens also have a pen cap over the outer needle cap.
