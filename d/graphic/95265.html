Spongiform pustule of Kogoj in generalized pustular psoriasis
Examination under high power reveals neutrophils extending into the epidermis with spongiform pustule formation. Eosinophils are absent, helping to exclude a diagnosis of acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (hematoxylin and eosin original magnification 200x).
