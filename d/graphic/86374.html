Serum IgG basement membrane zone antibodies localizing to the dermal side of split skin substrate in epidermolysis bullosa acquisita by IIF microscopy
Linear IgG antibody staining on the dermal side of basement membrane zone-split skin substrate characteristic of epidermolysis bullosa acquisita. This pattern is also referred to as "staining the floor of the blister" and may be found in bullous lupus erythematosus and 2 subsets of pemphigoid: anti-laminin 332 and anti-laminin gamma-1 pemphigoid.
IgG: immunoglobulin G; IIF: indirect immunofluorescence.
