(A) B. coli trophozoite in a Mann's hematoxylin-stained smear, 500x magnification. Note the cytosome (black arrow) and the bean-shaped macronucleus.
(B) B. coli trophozoite in a wet mount, 1000x magnification. Note the visible cilia on the cell surface.
