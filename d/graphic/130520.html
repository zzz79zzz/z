Selection of a BCR-ABL1 TKI for initial treatment of chronic phase CML
Selection of a TKI must consider the toxicity profile, comorbid illnesses, availability, cost, and patient preference. Refer to related UpToDate topics for details of CML risk scoring, eligibility for TKI discontinuation to achieve TFR, outcomes, and adverse effects of individual BCR-ABL1 TKIs.
TKI: tyrosine kinase inhibitor; CP: chronic phase; CML: chronic myeloid leukemia; 2G: second generation; TFR: treatment-free remission.
