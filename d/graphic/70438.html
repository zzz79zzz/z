Telangiectasia, reticular veins and mild ankle edema
Reticular veins are dilated bluish subdermal veins, 1 to 3millimeters in diameter. The deeper blue reticular veins contrast the bright red, fine telangiectasias. Mild ankle edema in this patient is evident at the medial ankle below the malleolus.
