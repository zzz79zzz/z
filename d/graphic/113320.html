Superficial thrombophlebitis and ischemic ulcer
The picture shows the foot of a young male with thromboangiitis obliterans. There is a region of superficial thrombophlebitis on the dorsum of the foot (arrow) and an ischemic ulcer at the tip of the first toe. Dependent rubor of the forefoot is also apparent.
Original figure modified for this publication. From: Olin JW, Lie JT. Thromboangiitis obliterans (Buerger's disease).
