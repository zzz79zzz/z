Systemic diseases associated with acquired perforating dermatoses

Systemic diseases associated with acquired perforating dermatoses
  • Diabetes mellitus[1]
  • Chronic renal insufficiency[1]
  • Carcinoma
  • Liver[2], prostate[3], Hodgkin's disease[4], colon carcinoma[5], leukemia[6], nasopharyngeal carcinoma[7]
  • Endocrine disease[8,9]
  • Thyroid dysfunction
  • Hyperparathyroidism
  • Primary sclerosing cholangitis[10]
  • AIDS[11]
  • Pulmonary aspergillosis[12]
  • Autoimmune disease
  • Systemic lupus erythematosus[13]
  • Dermatomyositis[14]
  • Scabies[15-17]
  • Poland syndrome[18]
  • Hidradenitis suppurativa[19]
