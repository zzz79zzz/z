Management of oral leukoplakia
Oral leukoplakia is a benign and asymptomatic condition that carries a risk of malignant transformation to squamous cell carcinoma. Management depends on lesion size and type, presence of histologic dysplasia, and patient's assessed risk for oral cancer. Refer to UpToDate topics on leukoplakia for more information.
