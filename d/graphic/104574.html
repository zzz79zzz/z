US distal iliotibial band Gerdys tubercle coronal view
The ultrasound image above shows the distal iliotibial band (outlined by small arrowheads) in the coronal oblique plane (ie, long-axis) as it inserts onto Gerdy's tubercle at the proximal tibia.
Small arrowheads: iliotibial band; large arrowheads: lateral knee joint line; arrows: lateral meniscus.
Left side of image: caudal; right side of image: cranial.
