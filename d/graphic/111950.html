Clarus Video System right retromolar approach intubation
This video shows the Clarus Video System, introduced along the right side of the tongue, advanced toward the right molars, directed medially beneath the epiglottis and just beyond the vocal cords. The tracheal rings are clearly seen. The scope is stabilized and the endotracheal tube (ETT) is advanced into the trachea.
