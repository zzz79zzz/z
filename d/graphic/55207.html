Aortogram showing descending thoracic aortic dissection
This aortogram demonstrates dissection of the descending thoracic aorta, arising immediately distal to the origin of the left subclavian artery. An oblique lucency is noted within the lumen of the aorta, which is a diagnostic feature (arrows).
