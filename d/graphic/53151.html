Pleural effusion after lung transplantation
Radiograph taken on the 10th postoperative day in a patient who received a left, single-lung transplant. A left, predominantly subpulmonic pleural effusion of small to moderate size is present (arrow). The effusion resolved by the next week. Surveillance transbronchial biopsy revealed minimal acute rejection that did not require treatment.
