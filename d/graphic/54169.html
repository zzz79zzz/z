Progressive anatomic disruption of the gastroesophageal membrane
Endoscopic appearance, looking upward from the gastric cardia toward the hiatus. The normal view is shown, followed by progressively increasing laxity of the phrenoesophageal membrane, leading to gross incompetence (grade IV) and a type I hiatal hernia.
