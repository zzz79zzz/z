Regression lines are calculated using the Joinpoint Regression Program version 4.1.0, April 2014, National Cancer Institute. Joinpoint analyses for White and Black populations during the 1975 to 2012 period allow a maximum of five joinpoints. Analyses for other ethnic groups during the period 1992 to 2012 allow a maximum of three joinpoints.
AI/AN: American Indian/Alaska Native; API: Asian/Pacific Islander; CHSDA: Contract Health Service Delivery Area; NHIA: NAACCR Hispanic Identification Algorithm; SEER: Surveillance, Epidemiology, and End Results.
* Rates are age-adjusted to the 2000 United States standard population (19 age groups - census P25-1103).
¶ Rates for American Indian and Alaska Native are based on the CHSDA counties.
Δ Hispanic is not mutually exclusive from White populations, Black populations, Asian populations and Pacific Islanders, and American Indians and Alaska Natives. Incidence data for Hispanic people are based on NHIA and exclude cases from the Alaska Native Registry. Mortality data for Hispanic people exclude cases from New Hampshire and Oklahoma.
