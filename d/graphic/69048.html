90/90 rest position
The 90/90 rest position for patients with low back pain is achieved by lying on a flat surface and flexing both hips and knees to 90 degrees.
By permission from Sheon RP, Moskowitz RW, Goldberg VM. Soft Tissue Rheumatic Pain: Recognition, Management, Prevention, 3rd ed, Williams & Wilkins, Baltimore 1996.
