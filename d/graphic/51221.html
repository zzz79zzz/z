Mesorectum - Male pelvis lateral view
This figure depicts the lateral view of the male pelvis, illustrating the relationship of the mesorectum to the posterior rectum and presacral space. The mesorectum is not a true mesentery but rather is perirectal areolar tissue that is thicker posteriorly and contains terminal branches of the inferior mesenteric artery.
