Bilateral tonic pupils
The top panel shows the poor reaction to light in both eyes. The bottom panel shows the much better near reaction in both eyes, with the right pupil constricting a bit more than the left. These photos thus illustrate the characteristic light-near dissociation seen with tonic pupils.
