Serial MRI in opioid-associated amnestic syndrome
Gadolinium-enhanced MRI on day 7 shows bilateral gyriform hippocampal apparent diffusion coefficient hypointensity (A) with corresponding hyperintensity on diffusion-weighted imaging (B), indicating restricted hippocampal diffusivity (arrows). Hippocampal T2 hyperintensity (C; dashed arrows) along with subtle hippocampal gadolinium enhancement (D; thick arrows) is also seen. Follow-up MRI on day 12 shows resolution of hippocampal restricted diffusion (E) and resolution of T2 hyperintensity (F).
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
From: Taylor RG, Budhram A, Lee DH, Mirsattari SM. Opioid-associated amnestic syndrome observed with fentanyl patch use. Can Med Assoc J 2019; 191:E337. Copyright © 2019 Canadian Medical Association.
