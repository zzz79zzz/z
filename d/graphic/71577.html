Cecal volvulus
Plain abdominal radiograph of a 74-year-old man with severe abdominal pain and distension demonstrates a large air-filled cecum oriented transversely across the midabdomen (arrows) extending into the left upper quadrant. These features are consistent with a cecal volvulus. The subtle mural nodularity seen in the wall of the cecum suggests vascular compromise.
