Botulinum toxin injection sites for horizontal forehead rhytides
The five injection sites in the mid-forehead (red) represent the sites for the injection of botulinum toxin into the frontalis muscle for horizonal forehead rhytides. Treatment of the brow depressors is also required, and is represented by the injection sites in the procerus and lateral obicularis oculi muscles (white).
