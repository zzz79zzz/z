Nasal versus intramuscular calcitonin for bone pain
Effect of salmon calcitonin on spontaneous bone pain in patients with recent osteoporotic fractures treated with either intranasal (100 international units) or intramuscular (100 international units) calcitonin. The intranasal calcitonin led to more effective pain relief.
sCT: salmon calcitonin.
Data from: Gennari C, Agnusdei D, Camporeale A. Use of calcitonin in the treatment of bone pain associated with osteoporosis. Calcif Tissue Int 1991; 49 Suppl 2:S9.
