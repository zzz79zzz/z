Demand oxygen pulse delivery
The demand module is interposed between the pressurized oxygen source and the patient. As the patient inhales, inspiratory airflow is detected by pressure changes in the nasal cannula. A solenoid in the module is rapidly opened and closed, enabling a pulse of oxygen to be delivered at the beginning of inhalation.
Data from: Tiep BL. Electronic pulse oxygen. In: Portable Oxygen Therapy: Including Oxygen Conserving Methodology, Tiep BL (Ed), Futura Publishing, Mt Kisco, New York, 1991.
