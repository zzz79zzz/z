Hepatocellular adenoma
Hematoxylin and eosin slide of hepatocellular adenoma. Adenoma cells resemble normal hepatocytes but have small regular nuclei and some fat vacuoles and are arranged in thickened trabeculae. There is a characteristic absence of portal tracts.
