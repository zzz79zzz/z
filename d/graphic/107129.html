Stationary biking exercise
The stationary bike provides safe, low-impact exercise. The seat height should be adjusted so that the knees are slightly bent at the low point of the downstroke, and no more than 90 degrees flexed on the upstroke. Resistance is increased gradually. Leg cramps during sleep may be prevented by riding the stationary bicycle for a few minutes before retiring.
