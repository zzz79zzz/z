PET/CT imaging of hepatosplenic T cell lymphoma
Combined positron emission tomography/computed tomography (PET/CT) imaging of a 66-year-old female with hepatosplenic T cell lymphoma demonstrates dramatically increased uptake in the liver and spleen. There was also increased uptake in the pituitary and some lymph nodes.
