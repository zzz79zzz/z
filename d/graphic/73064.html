Bag-valve-mask to transtracheal catheter connector
The transtracheal catheter may be connected to a self-inflating bag-valve ventilation device by cutting standard intravenous tubing as pictured. The luer lock end is then connected to the catheter and the cut end is attached to an adapter that has been removed from a 2.5 mm internal diameter endotracheal tube.
