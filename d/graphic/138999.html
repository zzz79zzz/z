Score each function as follows:
  • 0 – normal;
  • 1 – 25% weak/mild impairment;
  • 2 – 50% weak/moderate impairment;
  • 3 – 75% weak/severe impairment;
  • 4 – paralyzed/unable to do.

In addition, record any conditions other than MG causing weakness in any of these muscles.

MG: myasthenia gravis.
