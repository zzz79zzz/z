Angiogram in a neonate with critical pulmonic stenosis that shows minimal contrast passing from the RV through the pulmonary valve into the main pulmonary artery. A wire is seen crossing through the pulmonary valve. Panel (A) is a frontal image, and panel (B) is a lateral image at higher magnification.
RV: right ventricle.
