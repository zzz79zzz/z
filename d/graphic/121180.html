Docking during robotic groin hernia repair
Positioning of the robotic system depends on room setup and the type of platform being utilized. Side docking in parallel or perpendicular is generally used (parallel docking depicted). However, the robot can also be docked in between the legs with the patient in lithotomy position if the room is more amenable to this setup. Docking from either the right or left side of the patient allows for bilateral access and should be chosen based on room setup rather than laterality of the hernia.
