Patterns of allergic crossreactivity between latex and food
The numeric risks cited here are based upon findings in limited series of patients and may not be generalizable to all populations. Foods that uncommonly cause reactions in latex-allergic patients include carrot, coconut, apricot, strawberry, loquat, spinach, pineapple, cherimoya, passion fruit, papaya, mango, and celery.
References:
  1. Rodriguez J, Crespo JF, Lopez-Rubio, et al. Clinical cross-reactivity among foods of the Rosaceae family. J Allergy Clin Immunol 2000; 106:183.
  2. Garcia Ortiz JC, Moyano JC, Alvarez M, et al. Latex allergy in fruit-allergic patients. Allergy 1998; 53:532.
