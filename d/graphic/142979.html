Hypnogram with examples of patterns for parasomnias or nocturnal seizures
Example of a hypnogram, showing typical timing and patterns for parasomnias or nocturnal seizures. This example is from a child with severe obstructive sleep apnea, which can contribute to arousals during REM sleep.
N1: stage N1 sleep; N2: stage N2 sleep; N3: stage N3 sleep; REM: rapid eye movement sleep; W: awake.
