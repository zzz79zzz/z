Airway exchange catheter set
This set includes an AEC and a guidewire. The guidewire can be placed through the working channel of a flexible intubating scope, and can be left in situ with the tip in the trachea. If reintubation is required, the tapered AEC is passed over the guidewire and the new endotracheal tube is then advanced over the AEC.
