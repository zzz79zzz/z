Metabolism of glucose
Simplified schedule demonstrates the aerobic metabolism of glucose, lactate and triglycerides in the mitochondrion, with a central role for OXPHOS (oxidative phosphorylation) system. When this system is blocked (dotted line), accumulation of lactate and triglycerides occurs, while formation of ketonic bodies increases.
