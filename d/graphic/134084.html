Ultrasounds at various transversus abdominis plane block locations
This graphic shows ultrasound images with the transducer placed as indicated on the graphic on the left, during TAP block. The panels show an initial midline scan, two images for subcostal TAP block, and two images for lateral TAP block.
RA: rectus abdominis; TA: transversus abdominis; IO: internal oblique muscle; EO: external oblique muscle; QL: quadratus lumborum; L. alba: linea alba; L. semilunaris: linea semilunaris.
