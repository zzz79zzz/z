Summary of fetal arrhythmias
Fetal heart rate (beats/minute) Fetal heart rate pattern Echocardiography findings Likely diagnosis(es)
<80
  • Fixed
  • AV dissociation
  • Atrial rate normal (110 to 160 beats/minute)
  • Complete heart block
100 to 160
  • Variable
  • 1:1 conduction, normal AV interval
  • Sinus rhythm
50 to 180
  • Irregular
  • Possibly abrupt drops in heart rate
  • 1:1 conduction with early atrial signals
  • Blocked atrial signals
  • Atrial or ventricular premature beats
  • Blocked atrial beats
  • Blocked atrial bigeminy
  • Possibly "low grade" ectopic atrial tachycardia
180 to 220
  • Essentially normal
  • Abrupt drops to normal
  • 1:1 conduction
  • Normal AV intervals
  • Persistent or permanent junctional reciprocating tachycardia (PJRT) type supraventricular tachycardia
180 to 200
  • Minimal variability
  • Smooth increases
  • 1:1 conduction
  • Normal AV intervals
  • Sinus tachycardia
200 to 220
  • Fixed
  • 2:1 atrial to ventricular conduction
  • Atrial flutter
>240
  • Fixed
  • Episodic break to 100 to 160 beats/minute
  • 1:1 conduction
  • Supraventricular tachycardia
AV: atrioventricular.
