Subglottic foreign body
A large subglottic foreign body (Montgomery thyroplasty implant) interfered with endotracheal intubation. Because the procedure was prolonged and the patient was overweight, the airway was initially managed by THRIVE followed by jet ventilation.
THRIVE: transnasal humidified rapid-insufflation ventilatory exchange.
