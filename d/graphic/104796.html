Systolic and diastolic murmurs in a child with mild valvar pulmonic stenosis
Phonocardiogram recorded at the left upper sternal border in a 3-year-old child with mild valvar pulmonary stenosis (peak Doppler systolic gradient 15 mmHg) and mild pulmonary regurgitation. There is a variable soft early ejection click, systolic murmur, and diastolic murmur.
PS: pulmonic stenosis; PR: pulmonic regurgitation; S1: first heart sound; EC: ejection click; A2: aortic valve closure sound; P2: pulmonic valve closure sound.
