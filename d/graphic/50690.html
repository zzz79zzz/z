Retinal whitening
Whitening, a change seen in areas of the retina in patients with cerebral malaria. This mosaic-like appearance is the result of capillary nonperfusion. In the image on the lower right, the pathological whitening is in the perimacular area, similar to the upper left image. The extramacular changes are reflections from flash on the fundus camera.
