Picture showing large joint involvement of patient with nephrogenic systemic fibrosis
Patients with nephrogenic systemic fibrosis can have involvement of large joints. The knees pictured here are in a permanently flexed position, making walking and standing impossible. In addition, there is brownish mottling of the skin.
