MRI insufficiency fracture
The T1 weighted magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) sequence of the pelvis (A, B) reveals bilateral broad dark bands suggesting edema (arrows). The T2 weighted images (C, D) confirm dark fracture lines (arrowheads). These findings are consistent with bilateral insufficiency fractures of the sacral ala.
