Plain radiograph of osteopenia of metacarpophalangeal joints in rheumatoid arthritis
The plain radiograph of the left hand in the AP projection shows a normal patient (A) and a patient with radiologically mild rheumatoid arthritis (B). The patient with rheumatoid arthritis demonstrates osteopenia around the metacarpophalangeal joints (arrows) and mild soft tissue swelling (arrowheads).
AP: anteroposterior.
