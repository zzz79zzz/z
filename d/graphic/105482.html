Laser treatment of hypertrophic burn scars
This five-year-old girl sustained an intermediate partial-thickness scald injury to her scalp and posterior neck. She underwent excision and allograft placement but developed severe hypertrophic scarring of her neck and posterior scalp with chronic folliculitis and minimal alopecia. She had complete resolution of her symptoms after four pulsed dye laser sessions and no steroid injections. Shown are preoperation and follow-up photos at 34 months.
