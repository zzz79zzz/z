Cheyne-Stokes respiration
This compressed figure shows four cycles of crescendo-decrescendo respiratory flow and effort, which defines Cheyne-Stokes respiration. Note that each cycle is associated with significant oxyhemoglobin desaturation, though this is not strictly required to diagnose Cheyne-Stokes respiration.
