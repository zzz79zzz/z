Upper trapezius muscle palpation
The upper portion of the trapezius muscle originates from the seven cervical spinous processes and the distal aspect of the acromial process. Palpate the superior trapezius muscle halfway between the acromion and spinous processes. Local muscular tenderness can result from trauma, but the majority of cases are due to reactive cervical muscular strain.
