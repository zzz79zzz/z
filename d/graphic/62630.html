Constrictive bronchiolitis
High power view of constrictive bronchiolitis in a patient with severe progressive airflow obstruction following toxic fume exposure. There is complete obliteration of the bronchiole by submucosal scarring. The surrounding alveolar spaces are spared. Pentachrome stain.
