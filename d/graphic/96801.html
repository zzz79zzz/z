Bunionette appearance
The foot pictured above belongs to a patient with a supinated gait that placed excess stress on fifth metatarsophalangeal (MTP) joint and led to the development of a bunionette - a lateral fifth toe MTP joint subluxation with varus deviation and rotation (arrow). Note how the small toe angles medially towards the other toes.
