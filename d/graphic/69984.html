Lateral neck radiograph of a child with pneumomediastinum
In this lateral neck radiograph of a young child with pneumomediastinum, characteristic findings include retropharyngeal lucency (arrowheads) and subcutaneous emphysema of the anterior and posterior neck and anterior chest wall (arrows).
