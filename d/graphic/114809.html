Fibrolamellar carcinoma: Imaging with fluorodeoxyglucose positron emission tomography (FDG-PET)
PET scan demonstrates multifocal fibrolamellar carcinoma in the liver with variable uptake of FDG. Some tumors are FDG avid (arrow), and others demonstrate less uptake (arrowhead).
