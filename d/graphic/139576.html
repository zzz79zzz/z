Tips for medication safety

Tips to use your medicine safely include:

  • (A) Read labels so you know how to take your medicines.

  • (B) Have a routine for taking your medicines.

  • (C) Make sure you know what foods, drinks, and other medicines are safe for you.

  • (D) Store medicines in a safe place.

  • (E) Work with your health care team and ask questions if you have them.
