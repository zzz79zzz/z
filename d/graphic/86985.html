Stage IIIA lung cancer
This figure depicts a stage IIIA cancer. In stage IIIA cancer, the tumor has spread to lymph nodes in the middle of the chest or it has spread into the structures in the middle of the chest like the main windpipe (called the trachea) or the swallowing tube (called the esophagus).
