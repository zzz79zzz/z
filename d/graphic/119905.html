Pure tone audiometry in a patient with conductive hearing loss
Results of pure tone audiometry testing in a child with conductive hearing loss in the right ear. The air-conducted pure tone thresholds are abnormal, while bone-conducted thresholds are normal. The difference in the air- and bone-conducted thresholds is termed the air-bone gap.
R: right; L: left; Hz: Hertz.
