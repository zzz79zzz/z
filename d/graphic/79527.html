Late gadolinium enhancement in myocardial infarction
Short-axis (upper panel) and long-axis (lower panel) late gadolinium enhancement (LGE) cardiovascular magnetic resonance (CMR) images in three patients with large myocardial infarctions (MIs). Hyperenhanced regions (arrows) were large and predominantly transmural. In comparison, small infarcts (not shown) were not transmural.
