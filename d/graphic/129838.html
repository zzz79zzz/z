Changes in asthma prevalence in the United States from 2001 to 2017
Bars represent total number of persons (millions) and line represents percentage of the population. About 25 million people, 8% of the population, had asthma in 2017, compared with 20 million, or 7.3% of the population, who had asthma in 2001.
Reproduced from: United States Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. Asthma Data, Statistics, and Surveillance. https://www.cdc.gov/asthma/asthmadata.htm (Accessed on September 15, 2020).
