Cylindrical bronchiectasis of right lower lobe
HRCT shows mild dilatation of the posterobasal and laterobasal segmental bronchi in the right lower lobe (arrow). Multiple ill-defined, peripheral right lower lobe nodules are visible and represent mucous plugs in dilated subsegmental bronchi (arrowhead).
HRCT: high resolution computed tomography.
