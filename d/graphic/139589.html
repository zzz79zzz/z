Colostomy and ileostomy

(A) A colostomy, in which the opening or "stoma" connects your large intestine (bowel) to the skin.

(B) An ileostomy, in which the opening or "stoma" connects your small intestine to the skin.

Bowel movements exit the body through the stoma. They go into a bag that is attached to the skin.
