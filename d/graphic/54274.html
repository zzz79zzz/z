Surgery to treat bladder cancer in women (cystectomy)
Some people with bladder cancer must have surgery to remove the bladder and nearby organs. In women, the surgeon removes the bladder, the uterus, the ovaries, and the tubes that connect the ovaries to the uterus (shown inside the dotted line).
