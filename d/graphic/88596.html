Nonobstructing left inguinal hernia on CT
The axial CT images of the lower pelvis (A, B) reveal a left-sided inguinal mass containing contrast-filled small bowel (arrow), which is consistent with an inguinal hernia. A coronal reconstruction (C) and sagittal reconstruction (D) demonstrate nondilated small bowel within the hernia sac (arrows).
CT: computed tomography.
