The effect of LV filling patterns on survival
Among patients with a first myocardial infarction, mortality was significantly higher in patients with impaired relaxation (p = 0.02), pseudonormal filling (p<0.00005) or restrictive filling (p<0.00005) than in patients with a normal filling pattern. With a Cox analysis, a pseudonormal and restrictive filling pattern were independent predictors of mortality.
LV: left ventricular.
Data from Moller JE, Sondergaard E, Poulsen SH, Egstrup K. J Am Coll Cardiol 2000; 36:1841.
