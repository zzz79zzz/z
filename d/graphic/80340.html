Acute fatty liver of pregnancy
Trichrome stain of a liver biopsy from a patient with acute fatty liver of pregnancy. Left panel: Low power view shows pallor of the hepatocytes surrounding the central vein (CV). Right panel: High power view reveals disruption of the plate pattern with "dropped out" hepatocytes, lobular disarray, and vacuolated, pale hepatocytes (microvesicular fat).
