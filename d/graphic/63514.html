Continuous kidney replacement therapy circuit represented pumps and sites of entry of dialysate and predilution and postdilution substitution fluid
Qs (pre): substitution fluid rate infused prefilter; Qb: blood flow rate; Qd: dialysate flow rate; Qef: effluent flow rate (equivalent to Quf in slow continuous ultrafiltration and continuous venovenous hemofiltration, Qd in continuous venovenous hemodialysis, and Quf + Qd in continuous venovenous hemodiafiltration); Quf: ultrafiltration rate; Qs (post): substitution fluid rate infused postfilter.
