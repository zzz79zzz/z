Gingival inclusion cysts (Bohn nodules) in a newborn infant
Epithelial inclusion cysts (also called Bohn nodules) appear as white or translucent, round papules along the gingival ridges. They are thought to arise from fusion abnormalities or remnants of minor mucous salivary glands.
