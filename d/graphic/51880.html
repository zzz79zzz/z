Transthecal block
The transthecal block (or flexor tendon sheath digital block) is performed by infusing the flexor tendon sheath with an anesthetic solution through a single injection. The needle is inserted at a 45 degree angle just distal to the distal palmar crease. The black ink lines on the skin outline the flexor tendon.
