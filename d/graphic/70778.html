Hidradenitis suppurativa and surgical unroofing
Surgical unroofing is the primary therapy for severe and extensive hidradenitis suppurativa. This picture shows the beveled edges of the wound following the unroofing of the inflamed nodule. Ferric chloride was used to control hemostasis.
