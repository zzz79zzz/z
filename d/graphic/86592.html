"T" exercise for scapular stabilizers with resistance
"T" exercises are performed keeping the elbows extended. Raise your arms straight to the side (combination of shoulder extension and abduction) until they are perpendicular to and in the same plane as the torso. Hold the position for one second, and then, slowly return the arms to the starting position.
