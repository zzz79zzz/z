Image from a 69-year-old male with non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) and bilateral lower extremity pain. Whole body bone of shows patchy uptake in the periosteal aspects of the long bones typical of hypertrophic pulmonary osteoarthropathy. No clear evidence of skeletal metastases.
