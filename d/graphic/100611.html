Pathologic features of histiocytic sarcoma
Histiocytic sarcoma. A) Note the multinucleated tumor cell. B) The cytoplasm is relatively abundant, but the neoplasm is difficult to distinguish from a large B cell lymphoma without a battery of immunohistochemical studies. C) Diffuse staining for the lysosomal marker CD68.
