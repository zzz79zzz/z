Atlantoaxial subluxation of the cervical spine in rheumatoid arthritis
Illustration demonstrating the forward subluxation of the atlas on the axis, pannus formation around the odontoid process, and osseous erosions. There is severe compression of the spinal cord between the pannus anteriorly and the arch of the atlas posteriorly.
