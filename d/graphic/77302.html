Transvaginal ultrasound examination of the ovary (A) and uterus (B) of a 39-year-old female with a gonadotroph adenoma and FSH hypersecretion

Scale: distance between open arrows = 10 mm.

(A) Multiple cysts (arrows) within the substance of the ovary.

(B) In a coronal view of the uterus (outlined by dashed arrows), a markedly thickened, echogenic endometrial stripe (thick arrows) is shown.
FSH: follicle-stimulating hormone.
