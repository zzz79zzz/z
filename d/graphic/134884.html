HRCT of organizing pneumonia in a young individual with a history of vaping. Axial CT scan (A) and coronal reformation (B) show basal-predominant consolidation and ground-glass opacification along bronchovascular structures with subpleural, lobular, and paramediastinal sparing of lung.
CT: computed tomography; HRCT: high-resolution computed tomography.
