Histologic features of fibrofolliculoma
Fibrofolliculoma has cystically-dilated follicular infundibula from which thin strands of keratinocytes radiate. Sebaceous glands can be found attached to the thin strands in some areas. A variably fibrotic and mucinous stroma is present (H&E 100x).
