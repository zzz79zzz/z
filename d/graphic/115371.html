MRI of an "early" T3 rectal cancer
Axial T2-weighted pelvic MRI image of an early T3 rectal cancer that has barely breached the muscularis propria. The dark line of the muscularis propria is breached (arrow), but no gross tumor is visible in the mesorectal fat. This finding was confirmed using endorectal ultrasound.
