Standard retinal photograph #8A
Standard photograph from the Early Treatment of Diabetic Retinopathy Study (ETDRS), which is used as the gold standard for grading severity in the clinical and research arena. Photograph #8A show intraretinal microvascular abnormalities (eg, dilated capillaries).
Reproduced with permission from: Early Treatment Diabetic Retinopathy Study Research Group. Grading diabetic retinopathy from stereoscopic colour fundus photographs--an extension of the modified Airlie House classification. ETDRS report number 10. Ophthalmology 1991; 98:786.
