Definition of sensitivity, specificity, and predictive values
TN (true negative): negative exercise ECG and negative angiogram; TP (true positive): positive exercise ECG and positive angiogram; FN (false negative): negative exercise ECG and positive angiogram; FP (false positive): positive exercise ECG and negative angiogram.
