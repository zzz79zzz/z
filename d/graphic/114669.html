Invasive disease-free survival with anthracyclines in early breast cancer
Forest plots of (panel A) invasive disease-free survival hazard ratio (HR) according to stratification variables, and (panel B) with test for interaction by combined hormone receptor and nodal status. TaxAC, doxorubicin, and cyclophosphamide regimens with a taxane; TC, docetaxel, and cyclophosphamide.
TC: docetaxel/cyclophosphamide; TaxAC: paclitaxel/doxorubicin/cyclophosphamide; HR: hazard ratio.
From: Blum JL, Flynn PJ, Yothers G, et al. Anthracyclines in early breast cancer: The ABC trials—USOR 06-090, NSABP B-46-I/USOR 07132, and NSABP B-49 (NRG Oncology). J Clin Oncol 2017; Vol. 35(23):2647-2655. Reprinted with permission © 2017 American Society of Clinical Oncology.
