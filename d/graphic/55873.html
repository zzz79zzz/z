Multiple cutaneous fusarial skin lesions
Multiple cutaneous fusarial skin lesions on the abdomen of an 8-year-old girl who experienced relapse of leukemia. The infection started as paranasal sinusitis, which was followed by pneumonia and skin lesions. Patient died 30 days later with persistent fusariosis and leukemia.
