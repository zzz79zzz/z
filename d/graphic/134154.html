Transmural inflammation
A classic example of transmural inflammation, with two concentric bands of inflammation, the thicker along the external elastic lamina and the thinner along the internal elastic lamina, with relative sparing of the interposed media ("concentric rings" appearance). Hematoxylin-Eosin, 20×.
