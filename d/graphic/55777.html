Increase in urinary aluminum excretion with administration of aluminum hydroxide, sodium bicarbonate, and citrate
Increase in urinary aluminum excretion in subjects on a control diet and those given AlOH alone or with sodium bicarbonate or sodium citrate. Aluminum excretion rose markedly in the last group due to increased intestinal absorption.
Al: aluminum; AlOH: aluminum hydroxide; NaHCO3: sodium bicarbonate; NaCitr: sodium citrate.
Data from: Walker JA, Sherman RA, Cody RF. The effect of oral bases on enteral aluminum absorption. Arch Intern Med 1990; 150:2037.
