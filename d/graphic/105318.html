Rigid bronchoscopy: Coring endobronchial tumor
Representative illustration of coring using a rigid bronchoscope. The barrel of the bronchoscope is shown in the trachea. A forceps has been placed through it to dissect visible obstructing tumor under direct visual guidance. The bronchoscope can also be used to push through the obstruction using a twisting or coring motion to open up the airway.
