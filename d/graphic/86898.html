Plain radiographs of lumbar spinous process and vertebral body fractures
A lateral radiograph of the lumbar spine (A, magnified in B) of a 54-year-old man who sustained a hyperflexion injury with distraction shows a transverse fracture through the T12 spinous process (arrow) and a compression fracture of the superior endplate of the vertebral body (arrowhead).
