Algorithm for the endoscopic management of central airway obstruction
This algorithm applies mostly to patients with central airway obstruction due to lung cancer, usually non-small cell lung cancer.
* Rigid bronchoscopy preferred.
¶ Careful airway evaluation and airway computed tomograpy scan, ± flexible bronchoscopy, ± endobronchial ultrasound, ± autofluorescence.
Δ Possibly with presurgical endoscopic intervention.
◊ Silicone/new hybrid stents generally preferred. Treatment may be followed by radiation if appropriate.
§ Can be used singly or in combination, may need stenting.
