Eosinophilic folliculitis histology
This low-power photomicrograph demonstrates spongiotic change with exocytosis extending from the sebaceous gland and its duct into the follicular epithelium of the infundibulum. For reasons which are not understood, the inflammatory process in HIV-associated eosinophilic folliculitis appears to be focused in these areas of the follicular apparatus.
