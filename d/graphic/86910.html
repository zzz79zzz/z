Histopathologic features of acute generalized exanthematous pustulosis (AGEP)
This higher power field shows epidermal spongiosis with exocytosis of neutrophils and eosinophils. Small microvesicles/microabscesses are developing. In other fields (not shown), there is subcorneal pustule formation.
