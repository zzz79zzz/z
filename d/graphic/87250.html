Radiograph of apparently nondisplaced talar neck fracture
A lateral (A) and magnified lateral (B) view of the foot reveal an apparently nondisplaced fracture (arrow) through the neck of the talus (Hawkins type I). To determine the full extent of the fracture, and whether it is truly nondisplaced, computed tomography is necessary.
