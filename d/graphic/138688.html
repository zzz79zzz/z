Ultrasound showing fluid within biceps tendon sheath and subacromial-subdeltoid bursa

This ultrasound image shows fluid within the biceps tendon sheath (a recess of the glenohumeral joint) surrounding tendon (star) and within the subacromial-subdeltoid bursa (arrow), findings suggesting a tear of the rotator cuff.

Left = medial; right = lateral; top = superficial; bottom = deep.
BT: biceps tendon.
