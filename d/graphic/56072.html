CT of a pulmonary arteriovenous malformation (AVM)
Pulmonary arteriovenous malformation presenting as a solitary pulmonary nodule. Chest CT shows a nodule in the periphery of the right middle lobe (arrow) with a proximal tail-like extension corresponding to the supplying artery and the draining vein.
CT: computed tomography; AVM: arteriovenous malformation.
