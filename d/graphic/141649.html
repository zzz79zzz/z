Procedural details of target lymph node biopsy
Marking of the TLN is always performed prior to NST, while localization can take place at one time point before, during, or after NST, depending on the marker and localization device. The method of choice for intraoperative identification of the TLN depends on the marker and localization device as well.
TLN: target lymph node; NST: neoadjuvant systemic therapy; RFID: radiofrequency identification device; US: ultrasound.
