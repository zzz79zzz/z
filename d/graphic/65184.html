Carvedilol improves left ventricular ejection fraction
After six months of therapy in patients with heart failure, carvedilol produced a dose-related increase in left ventricular ejection fraction (LVEF) which is significant with all doses used when compared with placebo.
* p <0.005 versus placebo.
• p <0.0001 versus placebo.
Data from Bristow MR, Gilbert EM, Abraham WT, et al. Circulation 1996; 94:2807.
