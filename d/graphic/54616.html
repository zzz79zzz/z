Intensity-modulated radiation therapy of prostate cancer
Segmental multileaf collimator (SMLC) shapes of three segments (A, B, C) of the right anterior oblique field from a beam's eye view, generated for the forward SMLC plan. The forward SMLC plan was generated using the UM-Plan software developed at the University of Michigan. The two dominant intraprostatic lesions (DILs) are depicted in lilac (white arrows), and the prostate is depicted in magenta.
Reproduced with permission from: Xia P, Pickett B, Vigneault E, et al. Forward or inversely planned segmental multileaf collimator IMRT and sequential tomotherapy to treat multiple dominant intraprostatic lesions of prostate cancer to 90 Gy.
