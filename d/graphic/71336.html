* The nutritional intake is adjusted based upon individual needs. This is particularly important for the carbohydrate, lipid, and mineral contents of the diet.
¶ GFR <70 mL/min/1.73 m2 with evidence for progression.
Δ Some recommend 0.56 to 0.75 g/kg/day, with 0.35 g/kg/day of high biological value protein. The protein intake is increased by 1 g/day of high biological value protein for each gram per day of urinary protein loss. This is performed under close supervision and dietary counseling.
◊ Phosphate binders often are also needed to maintain normal serum phosphorus levels.
§ 10 mg/day for males and nonmenstruating females, 18 mg/day for menstruating females.
