Obesity in children and adolescents, United States, 2016
In this chart, weight categories are defined as follows:
  • Overweight: BMI ≥85th to <95th percentile
  • Class I obesity: BMI ≥95th percentile to <120% of the 95th percentile and <35 kg/m2
  • Class II or III obesity: BMI ≥120% of the 95th percentile or ≥35 kg/m2
BMI: body mass index.
Data from: Skinner AC, Ravanbakht SN, Skelton JA, et al. Prevalence of obesity and severe obesity in US children, 1999-2016. Pediatrics 2018; 141:e20173459.
