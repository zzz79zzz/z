Thoracoscopic view of metastatic ovarian cancer
This video shows the circular motion used by thoracoscopists to comprehensively inspect the pleural space. Multiple metastatic deposits are seen as white patchy deposits on the parietal pleura. These are readily accessible to thoracoscopic biopsy under direct visualization but could be easily missed by closed needle biopsy.
