Inverted papilloma
A-B, At low power (A, 10x), anastomosing cords of normal appearing urothelium originating from the overlying mucosa are growing downward into the stroma. At higher power (B, 20x), the periphery of the cords displays palisading basal cells, while the central portion contains urothelial cells. The stromal component is mostly minimal and lacks inflammation.
