Types of vaginal bleeding as recorded by women from day 1 (administration of mifepristone) to day 15
The data are from 1506 women who did not undergo surgical termination of pregnancy and who recorded the types of bleeding they had from study day 1 to day 15 on menstrual diary cards. Bleeding was characterized as spotting, as similar to normal menstrual bleeding (normal), or as heavier than normal menstrual bleeding (heavy).
Data from Spitz IM, Bardin CW, Benton L, Robbins A. N Engl J Med 1998; 388:1241.
