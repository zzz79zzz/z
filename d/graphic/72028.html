Snowflake calcification in chronic hepatosplenic brucellosis
Noncontrast CT of the liver of patient at the time of admission to the hospital, showing large calcium density without surrounding hypodensity. Serological test results at this time were as follows: rose bengal, negative; agglutination, negative; and Coombs' test, 1/80. Diagnoses of liver abscess and brucellosis were delayed.
CT: computed tomography.
