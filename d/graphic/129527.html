Lateral acute MI ECG
The electrocardiogram above contains findings consistent with a lateral myocardial infarction. Note the ST segment elevations in leads I, aVL, V5, and V6. Q waves are present in multiple leads.
ECG: electrocardiogram; MI: myocardial infarction.
