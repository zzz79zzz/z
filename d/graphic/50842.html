Fourth metacarpal oblique shaft fracture
This lateral radiograph shows a long oblique fracture of 4th metacarpal shaft. Although minimally displaced, this fracture is at high risk for subsequent proximal displacement (ie, shortening) due to the length of the fracture (fracture length is greater than two times bone diameter).
