Type 3 breast lobule

Whole mount preparation of breast tissue of a 35-year-old parous woman showing type 3 breast lobule as viewed by light microscopy.

(Left panel) Tissue section stained with toluidine blue × 2.5. (Right panel) Histologic section of lobule type 3 stained with hematoxylin and eosin (H&E). Photographs at 10× (left panel) and 40× (right panel), respectively.
