Cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) manometry
Midsagittal section through lumbar spinal column showing positioning for measurement of CSF opening pressure. The manometer is attached to the spinal needle hub with a three-way stop-cock. CSF is permitted to enter the manometer; opening pressure is recorded at the highest level attained by the CSF in the manometer column. Pressure measurements should be taken with the patient recumbent.
Adapted from Dieckmann RA, Fiser DH, Selbst SM, (Eds). Illustrated Textbook of Pediatric Emergency and Critical Care Procedures. Mosby, St. Louis, 1997.
