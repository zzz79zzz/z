Healing pubic ramus fractures
This plain radiograph of the pelvis was obtained at the six-week follow-up visit of an elderly woman who sustained insufficiency fractures of the pubic rami. Note the callous formation (arrows) at the left superior pubic root and the inferior pubic ramus fracture sites.
