Serum TSH and free T4 in various forms of RTSH
Results shown are from a representative family having each of the 4 forms of RTSH. Note that by definition, affected family members have high TSH. Free T4 concentrations vary considerably according to the type of defect and within subjects with the same defect. For details, refer to the UpToDate topic on resistance to thyrotropin.
TSH: thyroid-stimulating hormone (thyrotropin); T4: thyroxine; RTSH: resistance to thyroid-stimulating hormone; Chr 15: chromosome 15.
