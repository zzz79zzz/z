Barrett's esophagus
Esophagectomy specimen in a patient found to have high-grade dysplasia during endoscopic surveillance. Salmon-colored Barrett's mucosa has replaced the squamous mucosa circumferentially. Scattered erosions are visible (arrows).
From: Lewin KJ, Appelman HD. Tumors of the esophagus and stomach. Atlas of tumor pathology (electronic fascicle), Third series, fascicle 18, 1996, Washington, DC. Armed Forces Institute of Pathology.
