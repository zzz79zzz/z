Light micrograph of a testicular choriocarcinoma. The central smaller cytotrophoblast cells with clear cytoplasm are surrounded by the much larger syncytiotrophoblastic giant cells (black arrow) that have huge, convoluted, pleomorphic nuclei and abundant eosinophilic cytoplasm.
