Cholecystokinin (CCK) regulation of enzyme release
The presence of fat and protein constituents in the duodenum and proximal jejunum causes the release of CCK from gut endocrine cells. CCK acts directly and through vagal mechanisms to stimulate the release of enzymes from pancreatic acinar cells.
