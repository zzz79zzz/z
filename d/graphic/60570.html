Collagenous colitis
High power view of a colonic biopsy from a patient with collagenous colitis. There is a thickened subepithelial collagenous band (arrow) associated with increased mononuclear cell infiltration and epithelial degeneration.

Normal colon
Low (left) and high (right) power views of a biopsy of a normal colon. Low power reveals straight crypts and mild lamina propria mononuclear cell infiltration. High power shows the surface enterocytes with interspersed goblet cells (arrows).
