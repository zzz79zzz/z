Gastrointestinal neuroendocrine (carcinoid) tumor high power histology
High power view of a gastrointestinal neuroendocrine (carcinoid) tumor reveals a trabecular or ribbon-like pattern of the tumor cells. This is the most common architectural pattern of a rectal carcinoid tumor. Immunohistochemical staining with synaptophysin confirms the diagnosis.
