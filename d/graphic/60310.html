Selected extra-abdominal causes of acute abdominal pain
Cardiac Hematologic
  • Myocardial ischemia and infarction
  • Myocarditis
  • Endocarditis
  • Heart failure
  • Sickle cell anemia
  • Hemolytic anemia
  • Henoch-Schönlein purpura
  • Acute leukemia
Thoracic Toxins
  • Pneumonitis
  • Pleurodynia
  • Pulmonary embolism and infarction
  • Pneumothorax
  • Empyema
  • Esophagitis
  • Esophageal spasm
  • Esophageal rupture (Boerhaave's syndrome)
  • Hypersensitivity reactions: insect bites, reptile venoms
  • Heavy metals and corrosives (eg, lead or iron)
Neurologic Infections
  • Radiculitis: spinal cord or peripheral nerve tumors, degenerative arthritis of spine
  • Abdominal epilepsy
  • Tabes dorsalis (tertiary syphilis)
  • Herpes zoster
  • Osteomyelitis
  • Typhoid fever
Metabolic Miscellaneous
  • Uremia
  • Diabetic ketoacidosis
  • Porphyria
  • Acute adrenal insufficiency
  • Hyperlipidemia
  • Hyperparathyroidism
  • Hypercalcemia
  • Muscular contusion, hematoma, or tumor
  • Opioid withdrawal
  • Familial Mediterranean fever
  • Psychiatric disorders
  • Heat stroke
