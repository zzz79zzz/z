Los Angeles grade A esophagitis
Los Angeles grade A esophagitis: One or more mucosal breaks no longer than 5 mm, not bridging the tops of mucosal folds. Mucosal breaks are defined as an area of slough or erythema with a discrete line of demarcation from the adjacent, more normal looking mucosa.
