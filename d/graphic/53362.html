Subcutaneous melanoma metastases
Axial CT image demonstrating a subcutaneous metastasis from melanoma involving the left anterior upper chest wall. These lesions are particularly conspicuous on CT scans because of the density of the metastatic nodules relative to the low attenuation of the surrounding subcutaneous fat.
CT: computed tomography.
