Turner-Warwick incision
Transverse skin incision. Subcutaneous tissue is dissected from the anterior sheath to a point at least 2 cm below the pubis. The sheath is incised 2 cm below the pubis and at least 4 cm in length. The incision is extended cephalad along the borders of the rectus muscles. Peritoneum is incised longitudinally.
