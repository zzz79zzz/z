Cryptosporidium cholangitis
Endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography in a patient with AIDS and a CD4 cell count of 30/mm3 shows sclerosing cholangitis, characterized by multiple irregular strictures throughout the medium size and large ducts, due to Cryptosporidium.
