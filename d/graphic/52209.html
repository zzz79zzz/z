Acute diaphyseal fracture of fifth metatarsal: Close up view
This fracture demonstrates typical extension of the fracture line towards the intermetatarsal joint. Note the characteristics of an acute fracture: narrow fracture line, normal thin cortex adjacent to the fracture, and normal intramedullary canal.
