Evaluation of unexplained macrocytosis (high MCV) or macrocytic anemia
Common causes of macrocytosis and macrocytic anemia include alcohol; liver disease; pernicious anemia, bariatric surgery or strict veganism without vitamin B12 supplementation. Refer to UpToDate content on hemolytic anemia, megaloblastic anemia, and other disorders for more information.
MCV: mean corpuscular volume; CBC: complete blood count; WBC: white blood cell; PLT: platelet; PMN: polymorphonuclear neutrophil; LDH: lactate dehydrogenase; HIV: human immunodeficiency virus; MMA: methylmalonic acid.
