Aortogram of a left lower lobe bronchopulmonary sequestration
Aortogram demonstrating the systemic (aortic) origin of the arterial blood supply of a left lower lobe introlobar pulmonary sequestration (ILS). Note that the feeding vessels arise from the subdiaphragmatic aorta (arrow).
