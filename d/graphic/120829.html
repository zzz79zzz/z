Feeding artery stenosis associated with hemodialysis AV access
The arteriograms show stenoses of the arteries feeding a hemodialysis AV access. Multiple stenoses (arrows) can be seen in the left radial artery (A). In addition, a severe focal stenosis is present in the left brachial artery (B; arrow).
AV: arteriovenous.
