Operative approach for a preperitoneal inguinal hernia repair
This figure depicts the cross-sectional anatomy of the right lower abdominal wall showing the preperitoneal space. This approach can be used for repair of a primary inguinal hernia or recurrent inguinal hernia previously repaired by an anterior mesh repair or a failed posterior approach.
