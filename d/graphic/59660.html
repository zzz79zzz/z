Nomogram for disease-specific survival (DSS) in patients with resected gastric cancer
Nomogram predicting 5- and 10- year overall survival after D2 gastrectomy for gastric cancer. The nomogram is used by adding up the points identified on the points scale for each variable. The total points projected on the bottom scales indicate the probability of 5- and 10-year survival.
LN: lymph nodes.
From: Han DS, Suh YS, Kong SH, et al. Nomogram predicting long-term survival after D2 gastrectomy for gastric cancer. J Clin Oncol 2012; 30:3834. Reprinted with permission. Copyright © 2012 American Society of Clinical Oncology. All rights reserved.
