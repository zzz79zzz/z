Leg movements on polysomnography that are too long to qualify as periodic limb movements of sleep (PLMS)
This figure shows a two-minute epoch of a PSG depicting leg movements lasting about 15 seconds, and therefore "too long" to be considered periodic leg movements of sleep (PLMS). The maximum duration of PLMS is 10 seconds.
PSG: polysomnography; PLMS: periodic limb movements of sleep; EOG: electrooculography; EEG: electroencephalography; EMG: electromyography; ECG: electrocardiography.
