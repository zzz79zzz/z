Pruzansky classification of mandibular abnormalities
Type I: Mild ramus hypoplasia with body minimally affected. Type II: Small condylar head and ramus, flat infratemporal surface, flat condyle, absent glenoid, and coronoid may be absent. Type III: Ramus thin lamina or absent. No TMJ.
TMJ: Temporomandibular joint.
