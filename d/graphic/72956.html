Collateral circulation right lower extremity
The collateral circulation of the right lower extremity is shown. It provides blood flow around regions of arterial obstruction due to atherosclerosis, which commonly affects the superficial femoral artery, thrombosed arterial aneurysm (eg, femoral, popliteal), or thromboembolism.
a: artery.
