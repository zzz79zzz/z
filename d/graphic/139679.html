Fixed finger flexion sparing pincer grasp
This is a depiction of fixed finger flexion with sparing of the pincer grasp. This posture is a common focal presentation of functional dystonia. Fixed postures are rare in nonfunctional dystonia.
Modified from: Espay AJ, Aybek S, Carson A, et al. Current concepts in diagnosis and treatment of functional neurological disorders. JAMA Neurol 2018; 75:1132.
