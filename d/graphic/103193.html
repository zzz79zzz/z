Transverse plane (short-axis view) ultrasound scan of the anterior ankle tendons. Left side of image: medial; right side of image: lateral.
Arrows: tibialis anterior tendon; arrowheads: extensor hallucis longus tendon; EHL: distal continuation of extensor hallucis longus muscle; dashed arrows: extensor digitorum longus tendon.
US: ultrasound.
