Bullae caused by Fusarium spp
Bullous skin lesions of the lower extremities caused by Fusarium spp in a 15-year-old girl with aplastic anemia. The patient presented with Fusarium sinusitis with subsequent development of cutaneous nodules on the face, extremities, and chest and bullous lesions on the lower extremities.
