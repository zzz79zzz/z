Anterior acetabular column fracture
(A) The AP view demonstrates a fracture from the iliac crest to the hip joint with disruption of the roof of the acetabulum. Fractues of the pelvic brim and the ischial ramus are also noted.
(B) The obturator oblique view demonstrates a single break in the iliopectineal line where the anterior column fracture crosses the pelvic brim.
(C) The iliac oblique view shows that the posterior border is intact.
