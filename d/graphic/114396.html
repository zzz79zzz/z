Three-dimensional EVUS right para-sagittal view of the anterior compartment showing a septated fluid-filled mass around the urethra, consistent with a urethral diverticulum.
EVUS: endovaginal ultrasound; A: anterior; C: cephalad; D: diverticulum; P: posterior; PS: pubic symphysis; R: right; T: transducer​.
