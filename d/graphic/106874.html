US hip gluteus medius tendon insertion in sagittal view
This ultrasound scan shows the gluteus medius tendon insertion onto the greater trochanter in the sagittal plane (long-axis view).

Arrows: gluteus medius tendon; arrowheads: iliotibial band; A: lateral facet of greater trochanter; B: gluteus medius muscle.

Left side of image: superior; right side of image: inferior.
