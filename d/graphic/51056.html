T2-weighted MRI, Canavan disease
Spongy degeneration of cerebral white matter (Canavan disease). Magnetic resonance imaging study. T2-weighted axial views show a marked increase in signal in the white matter that is most evident in the posterior portions of the hemispheres.
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
