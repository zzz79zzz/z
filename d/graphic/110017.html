Computed tomography of endotracheal tube-induced tracheal stenosis
A 54-year-old male patient with a history of a tracheostomy now with progressive difficulty breathing. Axial (A) and coronal (B) CT images of the chest demonstrate stenosis of the upper anterolateral tracheal wall (arrows).
CT: computed tomography.
