Radiograph of a hangman's (C2) fracture
Lateral radiograph of the cervical spine shows a fracture (arrow) through the C2 pedicle (so-called "hangman's fracture"). This unstable injury results when the cervicocranium (the skull, atlas, and axis functioning as a unit) is thrown into extreme hyperextension.
