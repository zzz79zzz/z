Congenital cutaneous mastocytosis in an infant
In this infant with cutaneous mastocytosis, brown-red plaques were spread over the whole body, including the face, but not affecting mucosal tissues. Episodic flushing and bronchoconstriction were triggered by heat and breastfeeding.
