Progression of acromioclavicular arthritis on MRI
This series of four MRI images depicts the changes seen with osteoarthritis of the acromioclavicular joint. Note the progression of joint space narrowing, subchondral cyst formation (thin arrow), and joint irregularity. The joint space in image A is indicated by the thick arrow.
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
