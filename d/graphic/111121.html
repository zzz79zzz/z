Frostbite injury of the hand - Grade 4
Frostbite injury of the hand at day 0: Initial lesion extending beyond the metacarpophalangeal joints (Grade 4). In the case pictured above, there is involvement of the entire hand, and injury extends just proximal to the wrist.
