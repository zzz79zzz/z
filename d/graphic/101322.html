World prevalence of childhood overweight, 2000s
Prevalence of overweight or obesity, defined as body mass index (BMI) ≥85th percentile for age and gender, using reference standards from the International Obesity Task Force (IOTF). Data for most of the countries shown are from 2005 or later*.
* Data from the late 1990s are used for Ethiopia, Iceland, Japan, Mali, Venezuela, and Zimbabwe.
