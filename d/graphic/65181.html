Subdural hematoma and epidural hematoma

A subdural hematoma is bleeding under the tough tissue that covers the brain, called the "dura."

An epidural hematoma is bleeding just under the skull on top of the dura. Many people with a head injury that causes an epidural hematoma also have a skull fracture.
