McGrath MAC
The McGrath MAC is a nonchannelled video laryngoscope, with an integral LCD monitor. It is shown in this graphic with a single use, Macintosh-style blade cover, which allows either direct or indirect laryngoscopy. For further details, refer to UpToDate content on video laryngoscopy.
