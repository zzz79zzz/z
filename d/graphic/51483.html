Tricuspid regurgitation predicts poorer outcome in HF
Among 117 patients with heart failure (HF), the presence of tricuspid regurgitation (TR) on echocardiography was associated with a lower one year event free survival (30 versus 68 percent in the absence of TR, p = 0.002).
Data from: Hung J, Koelling T, Semigran MJ, et al. Am J Cardiol 1998; 82:1301.
