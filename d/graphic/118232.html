Components of a medical emergency action plan for a sporting event
Emergency personnel
  • Team assembled with clear, assigned roles
    • First responder on scene
    • Scene safety officer
    • Person to activate and guide EMS
    • Person to retrieve equipment 
Emergency communication
  • Pre-event meeting with EMS
  • Clearly defined and visible (printed and/or electronic version) communication tree
  • Two forms of verbal communication (primary and secondary) available, such as cell phones and Walkie Talkies
Emergency equipment
  • Necessary and appropriate medical equipment prepared and readily available (eg, AED; bag and mask ventilation kit; collar and backboard; first aid kit)
Medical transport
  • Ambulance on site or on standby
  • Non-medical transport vehicles for stable patients with designated transport personnel
Venue directions with map
  • Clear, well-marked maps available that include access points, routes of entry and exit, and potential obstacles
  • Helicopter landing site noted, if available
EMS: emergency medical services; AED: automatic external defibrillator.
