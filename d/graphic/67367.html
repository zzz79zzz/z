Construction of a colostomy - Hartmann's pouch
This figure depicts the proximal limb of the colon brought onto the abdominal wall. The proximal bowel is opened with electrocautery. The distal limb will remain in the abdominal cavity and the suture line inverted with silk sutures, creating a Hartmann's pouch.
