Nonclassic mitral valve prolapse (with fibroelastic deficiency) and classic mitral valve prolapse (Barlow disease)
(A and B) Nonclassic MVP (fibroelastic deficiency)

  • (A) Parasternal long-axis view demonstrating presence of a flail segment of the posterior leaflet (arrow).

  • (B) 3D TEE of the same patient shows focal involvement of the P2 scallop with evidence of ruptured chords (arrow).

(C and D) Classic MVP (Barlow disease)

  • (C) Apical long-axis view shows diffusely thickened anterior and posterior mitral leaflets (arrows).

  • (D) 3D TEE of the same patient shows massive prolapse of the P2 to P3 scallops.

MVP: mitral valve prolapse; 3D: 3-dimensional; TEE: transesophageal echocardiography.
