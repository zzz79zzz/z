Aneurysm of SFA on x-ray
An x-ray of the right distal thigh (A) in the A-P projection shows atherosclerotic calcification of the distal SFA (arrow) as well as an aneurysm of the SFA at Hunter's canal (arrowhead). Image B is a magnified view and shows the calcified aneurysm (between arrows).
SFA: superficial femoral artery; A-P: anteroposterior.
