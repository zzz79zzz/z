Volume-controlled ventilator settings
Ventilator appearance during volume-controlled ventilation: The clinician sets the tidal volume, peak inspiratory flow rate, ventilation rate (mandatory breath rate), PEEP, and oxygen concentration. These settings determine the PIP, which will vary according to the patient's lung compliance and airway resistance.
SIMV: synchronous intermittent mandatory ventilation; PS: pressure support; PEEP: positive end-expiratory pressure; PIP: peak inspiratory pressure; FiO2: fraction of inspired oxygen.
