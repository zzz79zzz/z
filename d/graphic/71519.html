Chromosomal translocations
Translocations involve rearrangement of chromosomal material. With a balanced translocation, no chromosomal material has been lost or gained. With an unbalanced translocation, there may be loss or gain of chromosomal material in one of the daughter cells. Refer to UpToDate topics on chromosomal translocations and genetic variation for further information.
