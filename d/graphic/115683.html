MtF gender confirmation surgery: Creation of urethral meatus
The urethra is transected in the midline on the ventral side over the catheter until the level of the pubic bone. The urethral flap is trimmed and secured to the underlying fascia, leaving a mucosal bridge lining the area between the neourethral meatus and the neoclitoris.
