Apical 4 chamber echocardiogram with color Doppler showing tricuspid regurgitation
The four chamber view from a 2-D echocardiogram with color flow Doppler shows significant tricuspid regurgitation with a dilated right atrium. There is a prosthetic mitral valve suggesting that the etiology for tricuspid regurgitation is pulmonary hypertension resulting from previous mitral valve disease.
