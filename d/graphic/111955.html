Airtraq Avant intubation
This video shows the intubation of a larynx using the Airtraq Avant. The blade was rotated around the tongue base and introduced into the vallecula. The endotracheal tube (ETT) is aligned with the glottis by manipulating the laryngoscope. Note the excellent laryngeal exposure but lower quality image. It is perfectly adequate for intubation but not for diagnostic purposes.
