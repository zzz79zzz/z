CMR cine short-axis dilated RV with dyskinesis
Short-axis cine bright blood image through the basal right ventricle shows extensive regional dyskinesia of the RV inferior wall, which bulges downward toward the diaphragm and liver during systole. Systolic function is relatively preserved in the anterior aspect of the RV free wall with an additional dyskinetic segment in the RVOT region more superiorly.
CMR: cardiac magnetic resonance; RV: right ventricular; RVOT: right ventricular outflow tract.
