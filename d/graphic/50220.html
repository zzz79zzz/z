Posterior elbow dislocation
Radiographic findings.
(A) Anterior-posterior radiograph. The radial head is superimposed behind the distal humerus. There is increased cubitus valgus. The medial epicondyle has not been avulsed.
(B) Lateral radiograph showing that the proximal radius and ulna are both displaced posteriorly to the distal humerus.
