Digitate dermatosis (small plaque parapsoriasis)
Characteristic finger-shaped, erythematous patches with an atrophic, cigarette-paper surface are present on the flanks of this patient with digitate dermatosis, a variant of small plaque parapsoriasis. Note the distribution of the lesions along the skin cleavage lines.
