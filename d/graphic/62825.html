Circadian lung function in asthma
Circadian alterations in lung function (as assessed by peak expiratory flow rate or FEV1) in normal subjects and asthmatics. The nadir occurs at approximately 4 AM in both groups, but the amplitude of the peak-to-trough swings is much larger in asthma.
