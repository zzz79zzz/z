Duodenal transection during total gastrectomy
The duodenum is transected just distal to the pylorus using a GIA stapler device. Care is taken to preserve the structures of the portal triad. The duodenal stump is oversewn using a 3-0 Prolene. The omentum is removed with the specimen when treating gastric adenocarcinoma.
GIA: gastrointestinal anastomosis.
