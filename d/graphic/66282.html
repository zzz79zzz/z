Types of hepatic resection
The types of hepatic resection are based on the anatomic nomenclature. "Wedge resection" refers to any nonanatomic liver resection exclusive of biopsy. "Sectorectomy" refers to any one of the following: right anterior sectorectomy, right posterior sectorectomy, left medial sectorectomy, and left lateral sectorectomy.
