Sarcoidosis-associated pulmonary hypertension with relatively normal imaging findings
This graphic depicts relatively normal to minimal chest CT findings in a patient with SAPH. Pulmonary function revealed a disproportionately low DLCO relative to the FVC. Right heart catheter findings showed moderate PH.
6MWD: 6-minute walk distance; CT: computed tomography; DLCO: diffusing capacity for carbon monoxide; FVC: forced vital capacity; mPAP: mean PAP; PAP: pulmonary artery pressure; PCWP: pulmonary capillary wedge pressure; PH: pulmonary hypertension; PVR: pulmonary vascular resistance; SAPH: sarcoidosis-associated PH.
