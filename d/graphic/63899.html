Electroanatomic voltage map from a normal subject
The right ventricular (RV) voltage map in the right anterior oblique projection from a normal subject is obtained using electroanatomic mapping. The colors represent the amplitude of the local unipolar electrogram and blue and purple indicated normal unipolar voltage values (≥ 5 mV) throughout the RV.
LA: left atrium; RA: right atrium.
