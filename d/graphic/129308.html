Bilateral neonatal testicular torsion
Male infant presents with asynchronous testicular torsion.
(A) Demonstrated firm right testicle with overlying discolored, "bruised," hemiscrotum.
(B) Left testicle with overlying skin discoloration.
(C) Scrotal exploration confirms bilateral testicular torsion, with ischemic, necrotic right testicle. Left testicle pink and viable appearing after detorsion.
