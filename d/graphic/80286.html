Cecal bascule
A plain film of the abdomen (left panel) demonstrates marked dilatation of a stool-filled cecal bascule (arrows). Following the administration of barium (right panel), the site of torsion is evident as marked narrowing of the lumen in the midascending colon (arrow). These features are consistent with a cecal bascule.
