Computed tomography of chylous effusion and congestion in lymphangioleiomyomatosis
(A) Axial section of a high resolution computed tomographic (HRCT) scan of the chest showing a right-sided chylothorax in a patient with lymphangioleiomyomatosis (LAM). Notice the characteristic cysts in the pulmonary parenchyma.
(B) Axial HRCT scan in a patient with LAM showing a left-sided chylothorax and bilateral cysts characteristic of LAM. There is ground glass attenuation (arrow) in the right lower lobe as a result of chylous congestion and reflux.
