Pathological fracture in advanced blastic metastatic prostate carcinoma
An A-P radiograph (A) of the left humerus shows a pathological fracture through the middle of the shaft (arrow). Extensive blastic metastases are seen in entire visualized skeleton (arrowheads). Image B is a magnified view and shows the region of the transverse fracture (arrow), blastic metastases (arrowheads), and callus formation (dashed arrow), indicating healing.
A-P: anterioposterior.
