Plain radiograph of mixed cam-pincer morphology femoroacetabular impingement
Above is a close-up from an anteroposterior radiograph of the left hip showing acetabular retroversion (arrowhead). In addition, a cam morphology of the proximal femur (arrow) is present. Thus, femoroacetabular impingement of a mixed cam-pincer morphology is present.
