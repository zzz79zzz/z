Outcome is poor in patients with alcohol-induced cardiomyopathy who continue to drink
Survival curves of cardiac deaths show no difference in survival between patients with an idiopathic dilated cardiomyopathy and those with an alcohol-induced cardiomyopathy who abstain from further drinking. However, survival is significantly lower in those with an alcohol-induced cardiomyopathy who continue to drink (p = 0.002).
Data from: Fauchier L, Babuty D, Poret P, et al, Eur Heart J 2000; 21:306.
