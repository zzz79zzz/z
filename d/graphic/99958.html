Calcified splenic artery aneurysms on CT
An axial CT scan through the upper abdomen (left image) shows two splenic artery aneurysms with calcified walls (arrows). The image on the right from the same patient shows a third 4 cm aneurysm (asterisk) with a calcified wall (arrow). The aneurysms were an incidental finding.
