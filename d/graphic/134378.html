Neuroimaging features of Morton neuroma
Forty-three-year-old with foot pain and mass for 1 year. (A, B) Transverse axial T1-weighted, (C) coronal and (D) sagittal T2-weighted, and (E) sagittal fat-saturated, postcontrast T1-weighted MRIs demonstrate a faintly enhancing mass (arrows) in the third intermetatarsal space. Pathology revealed a Morton neuroma.
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
