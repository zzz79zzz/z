Pectoralis major muscle flap for chest wall reconstruction
The pectoralis major muscle flap can reliably reach the entire anterior chest wall except the lower sternum as a simple rotation flap when based on its pedicle. The muscle remains innervated and functional and allows for sternotomy.
Modified from: Althubaiti G, Butler CE. Abdominal wall and chest wall reconstruction. Plast Reconstr Surg 2014; 133:688e.
