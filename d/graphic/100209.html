Shoulder spica wrap
Patients recovering from rotator cuff strains or a glenohumeral dislocation may benefit from added support during activity. The wrap described here is one approach for providing such support.
(A) Encircle the arm in a posterior-to-anterior direction at the mid-biceps.
(B) Cross the anterior chest and bring the wrap under the opposite axilla, across the back, and over the acromion process.
(C) Continue to wrap through the axilla, around the arm, and again across the anterior chest.
(D) Reinforce with nonelastic tape.
