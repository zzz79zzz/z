Radiographic stent markers
Radiopaque markers are placed at the end of a stent to improve visualization of device. The markers (A; arrows) show the position of a stent located at the junction of the subclavian and brachiocephalic veins. The picture of a stent (B) shows the markers (dashed arrow) embedded within it.
