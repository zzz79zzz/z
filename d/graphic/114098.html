TEE video (midesophageal 4-chamber long-axis view) showing a small amount of residual air in the LV apex
This TEE video obtained in the midesophageal 4-chamber long-axis view after weaning from cardiopulmonary bypass demonstrates a small amount of residual air in the LV apex. Note that RV function has improved compared to the TEE video showing air in the LA and LV.
TEE: transesophageal echocardiography; ME: midesophageal; LAX: long-axis; LV: left ventricle; RV: right ventricle.
