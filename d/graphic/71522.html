Lateral skull of an elderly woman with Paget disease
Lateral skull of an older adult woman with Paget disease. Note the thickened table of the skull, the areas of lytic mixed with sclerotic bone, and the appearance of "cotton wool spots" or circular densities of sclerotic bone so characteristic of Paget disease.
