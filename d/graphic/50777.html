Saline infusion sonohysterogram of a submucous myoma
A posterior mid-segment submucous myoma measuring 1.6 x 1.9 cm is identified after infusion of saline. The distance from the back of the myoma to the serosal surface measures 1.2 cm (calipers). The endometrium surrounding the fluid is thin, compatible with early proliferative phase.
