Misplaced nasogastric tube
This adolescent had a nasogastric tube placed after a polydrug ingestion. Charcoal was administered, and the patient's respiratory status deteriorated. The chest radiograph shows the nasogastric tube in the left lower lung (arrow) and alveolar opacification (asterisk).
