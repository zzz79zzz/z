Ultrasound IVC distal to atrium sagittal view in trauma patient
Ultrasound examination of the IVC, when performed in addition to standard cardiac views, can provide insight into the fluid status of an adult trauma patient. This video clip shows the IVC just distal to the right atrium in the sagittal view.
IVC: inferior vena cava.
