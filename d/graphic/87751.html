Collagenous colitis high power histology
High power view of a random colon biopsy specimen in a patient with collagenous colitis reveals lymphocytic infiltration of the superficial epithelium and an excess of chronic inflammatory cells in the lamina propria. The pink stripe noticed on low power view is subepithelial collagen deposition (arrow), which has entrapped superficial blood vessels (circle).
