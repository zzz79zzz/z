Primary clinical sites of involvement among patients with melioidosis in the Northern Territory of Australia
This chart highlights the primary clinical presentations of 1148 individuals with culture-confirmed Burkholderia pseudomallei infection identified over a 30-year period in the Northern Territory of Australia (from 1989 to 2019). Prostatic abscesses comprised 74% of genitourinary infections. Neurologic diseases included meningoencephalitis, cerebral abscesses, myelitis, meningitis, and epidural abscess.
Data from: Currie BJ, Mayo M, Ward LM, et al. The Darwin Prospective Melioidosis Study: a 30-year prospective, observational investigation. Lancet Infect Dis 2021; 21:1737.
