Pulmonary paragonimiasis
(A, B) Chest radiograph and computed tomography (CT) on admission.
(A) Multiple mass-like opacities are shown in the right lung (arrow).
(B) Multiple nodules with adjacent areas of ground glass opacity, suggestive of hemorrhagic metastasis, are observed in both lungs (arrows).
(C) Fluorodeoxyglucose-positron emission tomography (FDG-PET) CT showing increased FDG uptake (max SUV: 3.1) (arrows).
