Light micrograph showing late-stage chronic kidney transplant rejection
Low-power light micrograph in chronic rejection reveals marked tubular dropout and interstitial fibrosis (in the area between the glomeruli). These findings represent the end stage of a process induced by ischemia from the associated vascular lesions and repeated episodes of interstitial inflammation (acute cellular rejection).
