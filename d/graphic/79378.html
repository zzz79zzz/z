Kidney allograft biopsy showing hyperacute rejection with marked vascular injury by light microscopy
Light micrograph in hyperacute rejection showing marked vascular injury with visible fibrin strands on the intimal surface (arrow). The vascular wall is largely replaced by fibrinoid necrosis; there is only minimal neutrophil infiltration despite the extensive vascular damage.
