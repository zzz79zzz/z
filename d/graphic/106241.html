Redirect spinal needle
If the needle hits bone during spinal needle placement, note the depth, withdraw the needle into the introducer if one is used, and redirect and reinsert in a slightly cephalad direction. If bone is contacted again, the depth compared with the initial insertion will help determine further maneuvers. For further details, refer to UpToDate topic on midline approach to spinal anesthesia technique.
