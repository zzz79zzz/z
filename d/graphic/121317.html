Algorithm for initial staging of rhabdomyosarcoma
CT: computed tomography; PAX: paired box protein; FOXO1: forkhead box protein O1.
* Unfavorable histology: alveolar histology or PAX-FOXO1 fusion.
¶ Full metastatic workup includes chest CT, bone scan, and bilateral bone marrow examination.

Histologic and clinical characteristics can guide staging evaluations for children and adolescents with rhabdomyosarcoma: a report from the Children's Oncology Group Soft Tissue sarcoma Committee.
