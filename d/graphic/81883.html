Prenatal ultrasound of a congenital pulmonary airway malformation (CPAM)*
On this axial view of the thorax, the cardiac silhouette is displaced to the right by the left-sided chest mass. The congenital pulmonary airway malformation (CPAM) is echobright and appears "solid" due to many microcysts.
* CPAM was previously known as congenital cystic adenomatoid malformation (CCAM).
