Right upper lobe atelectasis with juxtaphrenic peak
Right upper lobe atelectasis with veil of opacity in the right upper lung region. A juxtaphrenic peak is present (arrow); this finding indicates loss of volume in the upper lobe and can be a helpful sign of upper lobe atelectasis.
