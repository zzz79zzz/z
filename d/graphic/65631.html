Mitral stenosis
M-mode echocardiogram of severe mitral stenosis shows concordant motion of anterior and posterior mitral valve (MV) leaflets which are thickened. The E-F slope is flat, resulting from very elevated left atrial pressure throughout diastole, suggesting a marked gradient across the mitral valve.
