Sinus rhythm with sinus arrhythmia
Sinus rhythm with sinus arrhythmia. Lead V1 and lead II rhythm strips show an irregular rhythm with a ventricular rate that gradually increases, and with each QRS preceded by a sinus P wave, indicating sinus rhythm with sinus arrhythmia. This can mimic AF, especially if the P wave amplitude is low.
AF: atrial fibrillation.
