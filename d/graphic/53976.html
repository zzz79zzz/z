Abdominal paragonimiasis
Hepatic paragonimiasis in a two-year-old female with epigastric pain and remittent fever for about two months.
(A) Plain multidetector computed tomography (MDCT) scan shows slightly hypodense lesion with heterogeneous attenuation and obscure margin in left lateral lobe.
(B) Contrast-enhanced MDCT scan at portal venous phase shows multilocular lesion with enhanced septa. The margin and internal detail are clearly demonstrated. Some of the cysts in the lesion are mutually connected.
