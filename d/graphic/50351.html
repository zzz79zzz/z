Gram stain of gonococcus in urethral discharge
Gram stain of purulent exudate from the male urethra (x1000) shows polymorphonuclear leukocytes containing numerous intracellular gram-negative diplococci. As expected, Neisseria gonorrhoeae grew from this specimen.
