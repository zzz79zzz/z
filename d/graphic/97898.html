Endosalpingiosis: Pelvic cysts
Transvaginal ultrasound showed cystic formations in the areas of the right fallopian tube (A) and fundus uteri (B). The maximum diameter of the cysts was 7.5 cm. Laparoscopic presentation of the cystic mass (C); uterus (front) with right tube and right abdominal wall (background).
