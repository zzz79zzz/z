Histologic features of fixed drug eruption
(A) A small number of lymphocytes adhering to the epidermis seen in the resting lesional skin (H & E stain; ×66).
(B) Absence of lymphocytes in the epidermis of the adjacent uninvolved perilesional skin (H & E stain; x66).
