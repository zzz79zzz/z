Histopathology of allergic contact dermatitis
Epidermal spongiosis and spongiotic vesicles (arrows) are present in this biopsy of allergic contact dermatitis from poison ivy. Infiltrating lymphocytes are apparent in the epidermis, where they effect the cell-mediated delayed hypersensitivity reaction.
