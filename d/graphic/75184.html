Subpulmonic pleural effusion
Left panel: Right subpulmonic pleural effusion in patient after mediastinal irradiation. The right lung base is elevated as well as the right hilum. Right panel: After thoracentesis, a residual hydropneumothorax with a large right sided gas-liquid level ensues, thus proving that the elevation of the right lung base was not due to an elevated right hemidiaphragm but to a subpulmonic pleural effusion.
