Radiography and CT of a pulmonary hamartoma
Pulmonary hamartoma. A chest radiograph PA (A) and lateral (B) projections shows a 3 cm nodule with popcorn calcification (arrow) in the right lower lobe. Chest CT with contrast (C) performed two years later shows the nodule (arrow) unchanged in size and appearance.
CT: computed tomography; PA: posteroanterior.
