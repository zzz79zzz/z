ECG neonate with HCM and Pompe disease
A neonatal patient with Pompe disease. The ECG is performed with all leads at standard voltage calibration (10 mm = 1 mV). Note the biatrial enlargement and extreme biventricular hypertrophy with strain.
ECG: electrocardiogram; HCM: hypertrophic cardiomyopathy.
