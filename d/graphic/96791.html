Contrast-enhanced ultrasound of a malignant liver lesion
The arterial enhancement pattern is initially heterogenous displaying neoplastic vascularity (A). A few seconds later, the enhancement is homogenously hyperenhancing (B). Portal-venous and late phase enhancement provides important information regarding the character of a lesion, since malignant lesions are hypoenhancing (C).
