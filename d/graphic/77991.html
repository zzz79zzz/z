Histology section in congenital lobar emphysema
Histology section from a two-year-old girl with congenital lobar emphysema. The junction of the affected and unaffected lobules is shown. In the affected region (lower portion), airspaces and terminal bronchioles are enlarged; their walls are normal and resemble those in the adjacent uninvolved lobules.
