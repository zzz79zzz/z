Graded survival benefit of physical fitness
Cumulative age-adjusted mortality from cardiovascular causes over 16 years of follow-up, according to fitness quartile in healthy, middle-aged, Norwegian men. Mortality varied inversely with fitness (estimated from total work performed on a bicycle ergometer), being lowest in those who were most fit (Quartile 4).
Data from: Sandvik L, Erikssen J, Thaulow E, et al. N Engl J Med 1993; 328:533.
