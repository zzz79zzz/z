Prenatal ultrasound with ureteroceles
Prenatal ultrasound at 22 weeks of gestation that demonstrates biliateral ureteroceles resulting in significant bilateral hydronephrosis. Panels A and B demonstrate left hydronephrosis, and panels C and D right hydronephrosis. Panels E, F, and G: Sonogram of the bladder with bilateral ureteroceles.
