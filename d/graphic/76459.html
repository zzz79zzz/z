Botryoid rhabdomyosarcoma
Botryoid variant of embryonal rhabdomyosarcoma involving the nasal cavity with overlying intact respiratory mucosa. Subepithelial dense aggregates of rhabdomyoblasts (the so-called "cambrium" layer) are characteristic (H&E, 200x original magnification).
H&E: hematoxylin and eosin.
