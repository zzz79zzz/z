Plain radiograph of osteopenia of the wrist in rheumatoid arthritis
The normal radiograph of the left wrist in the AP projection (A) is compared with the left wrist of a patient with rheumatoid arthritis (B). The subtle diffuse osteopenia of the carpal bones is typified by an overall decrease in the density of the bones and by a relative paucity of trabecular markings. Less subtle is the prominent soft tissue nodule overlying the styloid process (arrow).
AP: anteroposterior.
