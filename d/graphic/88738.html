Carcinoid involvement of the tricuspid valve on CT scan
An axial CT image through the heart shows thickened leaflets of the tricuspid valve (arrowheads) and an enlarged right atrium with a bulging interatrial septum (arrow) reflecting elevated right atrial pressures. The patient was a 63-year-old woman with metastatic carcinoid disease with tricuspid valve involvement.
CT: computed tomography.
