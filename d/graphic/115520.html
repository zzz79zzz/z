Foveal hypoplasia
Fundus photograph of the right eye in a patient with OCA1A with two mutations in the TYR gene, showing foveal hypoplasia, failure of vessels to wreathe the intended fovea, and a mildly small optic nerve with peripapillary halo. The defocused choroidal vessels can be seen beneath the retinal vessels throughout the fundus.
OCA1A: oculocutaneous albinism type 1A.
