Examination of subtalar motion
This video clip shows a maneuver for assessing subtalar motion. The maneuver is performed by holding the patient's ankle firmly in dorsiflexion with one hand, and then passively inverting and everting the foot with the other hand. The patient examined in the video clip demonstrates normal subtalar motion.
