Color Doppler of donor twin with oligohydramnios
Stuck donor twin at 21 weeks of gestation. Arrow denotes the donor twin against the left upper quadrant of the uterine cavity. Note that the calipers indicate a maximum vertical amniotic pocket of only 1 cm. The cord can be seen with color Doppler adjacent to the abdomen of the donor twin.
