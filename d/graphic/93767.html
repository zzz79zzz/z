Multiple aortic aneurysms on aortography
An angiogram of the thoracic aorta (A) shows opacification of an aneurysmal ascending aorta (asterisk), and early opacification of the proximal descending aorta (arrow). Image B is a lateral projection of the abdominal aneurysm with thrombus and shows the calcified anterior wall of the aneurysm (arrow). Image C is an anteroposterior projection of the aortogram and shows a suprarenal aneurysm (asterisk) and the characteristic shape of a thrombus-filled infrarenal aneurysm (arrow).
