Relative COX-1 and COX-2 selectivity of NSAIDs
Concentrations of various NSAIDs required to inhibit the activity of COX-1 and COX-2 by 50% (IC50) in assays of whole blood. The diagonal line indicates equivalence. Drugs plotted below the diagonal line indicating equivalence are more potent inhibitors of COX-2 relative to COX-1 than drugs plotted on or above the line.
COX: cyclooxygenase; NSAIDs: nonsteroidal antiinflammatory drugs; 6-MNA: 6-methoxy-2-naphthylacetic acid.
Adapted from: FitzGerald GA, Patrono C. N Engl J Med 2001; 345:433.
