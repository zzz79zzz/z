Post-PVC aortic stenosis hemodynamics
Post-PVC hemodynamics in aortic stenosis (left) and HOCM (right) patients. In AS, post-PVC hemodynamics show aortic (Ao) pressure upstroke unchanged, larger pulse pressure and no change in aortic pressure wave form. These findings are different in the post-PVC HOCM tracings. Post-PVC of HOCM shows rapid aortic upstroke, narrow pulse pressure and spike and dome configuration of aortic pressure wave.
PVC: premature ventricular contraction; HOCM: hypertrophic obstructive cardiomyopathy; AS: aortic stenosis.
