Internal fixation distal radial fracture
The images show a distal radial fracture in a 24-year-old male in AP (A) and lateral (B) views. The patient was treated with a bridging plate because the fracture was located distal to the watershed line, which a typical volar plate could not fix.
AP: anteroposterior.
