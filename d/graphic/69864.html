Myofibroblast on electron microscopy
Myofibroblast viewed by electron microscopy. Myofibroblasts have an important role in the repair reaction. These cells, with features intermediate between those of smooth muscle cells and fibroblasts, are characterized by the presence of discrete bundles of myofilaments in the cytoplasm (arrows).
