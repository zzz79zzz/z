Chest radiographs of neonatal eventration of the diaphragm
Chest radiography of eventration of the diaphragm (arrows) in an 18-day-old infant demonstrated in anterior-posterior (AP) and lateral views. On the lateral view, the anterior and posterior diaphragmatic attachments are in the correct positions. With diaphragmatic paralysis, the elevated hemidiaphragm would be uniformly elevated.
