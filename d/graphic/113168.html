There is no consensus regarding the appropriate threshold for tuberculosis screening. Guidelines are developed for public health goals and therefore may not represent the best clinical choice for individual patients. Decisions regarding tuberculosis screening should be based on risk, and local or regional tuberculosis incidence data should be used to help define such risk.
