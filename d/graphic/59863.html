Right upper lobe atelectasis in an adolescent
Portable supine radiograph of a 12-year-old following intubation. There are focal opacities within the right upper lung and retrocardiac left lower lobe. The curvilinear inferior margin of the right-sided opacity represents a significantly elevated minor fissure, consistent with right upper lobe collapse; the left lower lobe opacity is less specific.
