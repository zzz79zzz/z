Mitral annular calcification
The apical four chamber view shows a marked degree of dense echoes at the mitral annulus, a result of mitral annular calcification (MAC). The mitral valve is not easily seen because it is obscured by the highly reflective MAC.
L: left ventricle; RV: right ventricle; LA: left atrium; RA: right atrium.
