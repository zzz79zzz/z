Normal postoperative CT scan after gastric bypass
This computed tomography (CT) scan shows the gastrojejunostomy and Roux limb (arrowhead) anterior to the gastric remnant (arrow), which has some fluid in it, and the small bowel anterior to the colon. In (B), the short arrow shows the contrast-filled Roux limb medial to the biliopancreatic limb (dashed arrow). This is a normal study on an antegastric antecolic gastric bypass.
