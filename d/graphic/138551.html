Management of hypertrophic scars
* Conservative therapies include:
  • Corticosteroid tape/plaster (preferred)
  • Intralesional corticosteroids
  • Topical corticosteroids
  • Compression therapy
  • Gel sheets
  • Scar fixation with tape

¶ Postoperative adjuvant therapies include:

  • Intralesional corticosteroids (preferred)
  • Intralesional fluorouracil or bleomycin
