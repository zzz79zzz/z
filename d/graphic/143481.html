Typical angiomyolipomas
Large, predominantly fat-containing mass on a contrast-enhanced CT (sporadic AML) displacing the right kidney with a classic "beak sign," indicating origin from the right kidney (arrows, A). Fat- and soft tissue-containing right kidney mass on a noncontrast CT, also a sporadic AML (arrowhead, B).
AML: angiomyolipoma; CT: computed tomography.
