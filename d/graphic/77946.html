Abnormal pancreaticobiliary junction
Endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography (ERCP) demonstrating an abnormal pancreaticobiliary junction in a child with recurrent abdominal pain and pancreatitis. Note the long, dilated common channel (thick arrow) containing a stone (thin arrow). The patient also has pancreas divisum.
