Development of the gastrointestinal vasculature
Embryo during the 6th week of development, showing blood supply to the segments of the gut and formation and rotation of the primary intestinal loop. The superior mesenteric artery forms the axis of this rotation and supplies the midgut. The celiac and inferior mesenteric arteries supply the foregut and hindgut, respectively.
