Ovarian vein thrombophlebitis
Axial contrast-enhanced computed tomography (CT) image of the abdomen in a 22-year-old woman who presented with postpartum fever shows an expansile thrombus in the right ovarian vein (arrow), with thickening and enhancement of the venous wall and perivenous inflammation.
