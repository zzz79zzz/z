Prognosis of gastric cancer according to molecular subgroups as defined by The Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA)
Patients in the MDACC cohort (A) and SMC cohort (B) were stratified by subtype; recurrence-free survival (RFS) and overall survival (OS) were plotted for each subtype.
EBV: Epstein-Barr virus; CIN: chromosomal instability; MSI: microsatellite instability; GS: genomically stable; MDACC: MD Anderson Cancer Center; SMC: Samsung Medical Center.
