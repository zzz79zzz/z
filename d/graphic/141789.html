Algorithm to determine degrees of diagnostic certainties for neonatal seizures
Algorithm to determine degrees of diagnostic certainties for neonatal seizures. This flow chart will help to determine the diagnostic certainty of neonatal seizures depending on the available diagnostic method (EEG, aEEG or observation by experienced personnel) and seizure type. Developed by the Brighton collaboration (adapted from[1]).
EEG: electroencephalogram; cEEG: conventional EEG; aEEG: amplitude-integrated EEG.
