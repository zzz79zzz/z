Infiltrating urothelial carcinoma, plasmacytoid subtype
The plasmacytoid subtype of urothelial carcinoma is characterized by cords or single malignant cells with eosinophilic cytoplasm and eccentrically placed, enlarged hyperchromatic nuclei.
