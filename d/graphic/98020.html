Ureteropelvic junction obstruction on imaging
Coronal CT (A) and MR (B) show right hydronephrosis without dilation of the right ureter (not shown), compatible with UPJ obstruction. A retrograde pyelogram in a different patient (C) also demonstrating right hydronephrosis with a transition point at the UPJ.
CT: computed tomography; MR: magnetic resonance; UPJ: ureteropelvic junction.
