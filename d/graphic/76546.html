Hydropic recipient twin
Sagittal view of the recipient twin at approximately 25 weeks of gestation. The fetal head (not in image) is to the right, and the fetal buttocks are to the left. Arrow points into area of ascites. A small pleural effusion is also present; thus, the fetus meets criteria for hydrops (Quintero Stage IV TTTS).
TTTS: twin-twin transfusion syndrome.
