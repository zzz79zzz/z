Erythema elevatum diutinum
Diffuse mixed dermal inflammation composed of lymphocytes, histiocytes, neutrophils with leukocytoclasis, and few eosinophils. Areas of vascular damage with fibrinogen necrosis, vascular wall infiltration with neutrophils, and red blood cell extravasation consistent with leukocytoclastic vasculitis are also noted.
