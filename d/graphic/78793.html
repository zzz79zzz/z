Aqueductal stenosis due to a tectal lesion
Sagittal T1-weighted magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) showing aqueductal stenosis due to a tectal lesion. There is dilation of the third ventricle (arrow), while the size of the fourth ventricle (thick arrow) remains relatively normal. The hydrocephalus was treated with a third ventriculostomy.
