Parinaud oculoglandular syndrome
Parinaud oculoglandular syndrome. Severe diffuse conjunctival inflammation along with a superotemporal conjunctival granuloma is present in this child with cat-scratch disease. Note the skin abrasions near the nose, which were presumably caused by a cat scratch.
