Associations for oligoarticular and RF-negative polyarticular JIA risk loci
The upper solid line indicates the threshold for genome-wide significance (p<5 × 10–8); loci reaching this threshold are labeled in bold font, and individual SNPs mapping to these loci are shown in red. The lower dashed line indicates the threshold for suggestive association (p<1 × 10–6 and p>5 × 10–8); loci reaching this threshold are labeled in non-bold font.
RF: rheumatoid factor; JIA: juvenile idiopathic arthritis; SNP: single nucleotide polymorphism.
