Equations to estimate energy requirements for children with chronic kidney disease at healthy weights
Age EER (kcal/day) = Total energy expenditure + Energy deposition
0 to 3 months EER = [89 × Weight (kg) – 100] + 175
4 to 6 months EER = [89 × Weight (kg) – 100] + 56
7 to 12 months EER = [89 × Weight (kg) – 100] + 22
13 to 35 months EER = [89 × Weight (kg) – 100] + 20
3 to 8 years Boys: EER = 88.5 – 61.9 × Age (years) + PA × [26.7 × Weight (kg) + 903 × Height (m)] + 20
Girls: EER = 135.3 – 30.8 × Age (years) + PA × [10 × Weight (kg) + 934 × Height (m)] + 20
9 to 18 years Boys: EER = 88.5 – 61.9 × Age (years) + PA × [26.7 × Weight (kg) + 903 × Height (m)] + 25
Girls: EER = 135.3 – 30.8 × Age (years) + PA × [10 × Weight (kg) + 934 × Height (m)] + 25
EER: estimated energy requirement; PA: physical activity coefficient.
