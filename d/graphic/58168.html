Hemangioma in the beard distribution
The "beard" distribution includes the preauricular skin, chin, anterior neck, and/or lower lip, bilaterally. Patients with segmental hemangiomas in this location are at increased risk of concomitant airway hemangioma and should also be evaluated for PHACE syndrome.
PHACE: posterior fossa anomalies, hemangioma, arterial anomalies, cardiac anomalies, and eye anomalies.
