Vein graft repair of brachial artery
Following a medial to lateral low velocity injury with blast effect, the brachial artery was controlled and explored and debrided back to healthy tissue on each end. A reversed great saphenous vein interposition graft was used for vascular reconstruction. A forearm fasciotomy was also performed (not shown).
