Transgluteal sciatic ultrasound transducer orientation
For an ultrasound-guided transgluteal sciatic block, place the transducer approximately midway between the greater trochanter and the posterior superior iliac spine, oriented perpendicular to the axis of the femur. For further details, refer to UpToDate content on sciatic nerve block.
