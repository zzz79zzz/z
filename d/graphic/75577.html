Using a spacer or a nebulizer

(A) This child is using a spacer with the inhaler. When the child presses down on the canister, a puff of medicine is released into the spacer and sits there until the child breathes it in.

(B) This child is using a nebulizer. This connects to a machine that turns the medicine into a fine mist. The child then breathes in the medicine through a mask.
