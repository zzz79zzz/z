Chest radiograph of patient with chronic mitral regurgitation
This plain chest radiograph from a female with known mitral regurgitation demonstrates cardiomegaly with left atrial (arrow) and left ventricular enlargement (arrowhead), as well as mild pulmonary venous redistribution, all features characteristic of mitral regurgitation.
