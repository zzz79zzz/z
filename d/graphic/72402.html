The proton gantry system at the MGH northeast proton therapy center (MGH-NPTC)
An isocentric gantry with a mobile proton beam source is used in conjunction with a robotic patient positioning treatment couch. The robotic unit has six degrees of freedom, three linear and three rotational. The patient is positioned to place the target lesion at the center of beam isocenter.
