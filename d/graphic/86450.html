Instruments and topical anesthetic for nasal foreign body removal
The headlamp frees both hands for instrumentation. When dosing the lidocaine and oxymetazoline mixture ensure no more than 1 mg/kg (0.1 mL per kg of the 2 percent lidocaine mixture) is administered. Instruments pictured from top to bottom: right angle Day hook, Lucae bayonet forceps, alligator forceps.
