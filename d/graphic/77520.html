The tubing from gastric banding causing abdominal pain
Abdominal pain after gastric banding can be caused by the tubing causing obstruction or pressure.
(A) This plain film shows the tubing being pulled down out of position, which caused abdominal pain.
(B) This computed tomography (CT) scan shows the tubing around the hilum of the spleen. At surgery, the tubing was wrapped around the hilum of the spleen and the colon.
