Crossed renal ectopia
Different forms of crossed renal ectopia.
  • Fused – Ectopic kidney moves across the midline and fuses to the lower pole of the normally positioned contralateral kidney.
  • Nonfused – Ectopic kidney moves across the midline without fusion and is positioned at the rim of the pelvis (pelvic kidney).
  • Bilateral – Both kidneys are ectopic and cross the midline with the ureters maintaining their normal bladder insertion.
