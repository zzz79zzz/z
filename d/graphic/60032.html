Mediastinal schwannoma
Spindle cell neoplasm demonstrates hypercellular areas (Antoni A on the left) and edematous, hypocellular areas (Antoni B on the right). Verocay bodies composed of two parallel rows of palisading nuclei are seen within the hypercellular areas. (Hematoxylin and eosin, magnification ×50).
