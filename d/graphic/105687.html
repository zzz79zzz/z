The long-acting antimuscarinic known as glycopyrrolate in the United States is called glycopyrronium in other countries. The dose of glycopyrrolate (glycopyrronium) can be expressed in any one of the four following ways depending upon country. This table provides the equivalence for doses expressed as:
  • Glycopyrrolate (glycopyrronium) base per inhalation capsule or per puff from MDI valve
  • Glycopyrrolate (glycopyrronium) bromide salt per inhalation capsule or per puff from MDI valve
  • Glycopyrrolate (glycopyrronium) base delivered from the mouthpiece
  • Glycopyrrolate (glycopyrronium) bromide salt delivered from the mouthpiece
