Wrist taping: Technique 2
Patients recovering from a wrist sprain may benefit from additional support, such as that described here:
(A) Place anchor strips around the wrist and metacarpal heads.
(B) Place three to four tape strips in an X pattern over the palmar aspect or dorsum of the hand.
(C-F) Apply a figure eight beginning on the radial aspect of the proximal anchor.
