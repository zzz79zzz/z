Sexually chimeric illustration of male (blue) and female (pink) tissues and organ systems shown to be responsive to estrogens. Examples of some clinical conditions, disorders, and diseases associated with females (pink boxes) or males (blue boxes) or conditions common to both sexes (gray boxes).
SLE: systemic lupus erythematosus; PCOS: polycystic ovary syndrome; BPH: benign prostatic hyperplasia.
