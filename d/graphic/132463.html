Sarcoidosis: Typical computed tomography (CT) findings
Axial image from high resolution CT in a 29-year-old man demonstrates nodular thickening of the peribronchial and perivascular interstitium (arrows), nodules along the pleura including interlobar fissures (dashed arrows), and nodules along the interlobular septa (thick arrow).
