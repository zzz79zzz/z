Alcohol-associated steatohepatitis with ballooning degeneration
Ballooning degeneration is a result of damage to the cytoskeleton of the hepatocyte resulting in swelling of the cell. The cell organelles, in particular the mitochondria, are susceptible to injury from toxins, in this case ethanol.
