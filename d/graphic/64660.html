Hemodynamic improvement after valvotomy in mitral stenosis
Left ventricular (LV) and left atrial (LA) pressures before and after balloon mitral valvotomy. After valvotomy, there is a fall in LA pressure and a substantial reduction in the transmitral pressure gradient (arrow). The cardiac output increased from 5.9 to 7.4 L/min.
