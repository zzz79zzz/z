Primary care management of hallux valgus (bunion)

This algorithm summarizes our suggested approach to management of painful hallux valgus (bunion).

This algorithm is intended for use in conjunction with additional UpToDate content. For additional details, refer to UpToDate topics on hallux valgus deformity (bunion) in adults.

* Obtain two weightbearing views, including a dorsoplantar (DP) and an axial view to assist with surgical evaluation.

¶ Both orthopedic and podiatric specialist foot surgeons, as well as nonspecialist surgeons, perform operations for hallux valgus deformity repair.

Δ These measures may be employed concurrently. If effective, conservative management is continued indefinitely.
