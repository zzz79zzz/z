Component separation technique skin flap dissection
The first surgical step for performing the component separation technique (CST) is to elevate the skin and subcutaneous tissues from the underlying fascia. The dissection extends from the costal margin cephalad to the pubis caudally and to the anterior axillary line and iliac crest laterally.
Modified from: Nguyen VT, Shestak KC. Separation of anatomic components method of abdominal wall reconstruction--clinical outcome analysis and an update of surgical modifications using the technique. Clin Plast Surg 2006; 33:247.
