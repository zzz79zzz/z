Reducing an indirect inguinal hernia
A) Anatomy of an indirect inguinal hernia. B) Gas and stool are first "milked out" of the bowel to reduce its size. C) Constant pressure is applied for up to 5 minutes (distal to proximal) to reduce the hernia.
