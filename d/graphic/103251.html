US tarsal tunnel tendons at sustentaculum tali short axis
This ultrasound image shows the tendons that traverse the tarsal tunnel (short-axis) at the level of the susentaculum tali in the transverse plane (short axis view). Note the anisotropy of the tibialis posterior.
FHL: flexor hallucis longus tendon; FDL: flexor digitorum longus tendon; TP: tibialis posterior tendon; ST: susentaculum tali.
Left side of image: cranial; Right side of image: caudal.
