Bronchiolitis radiographs
The above radiographs demonstrate the following findings that are consistent with bronchiolitis:
  • Arrows: Patchy atelectasis, in particular of the right middle lobe
  • Arrowhead: Bilateral peribronchial infiltrations with air bronchograms
  • Thick arrows: Hyper-inflation of the lungs with flattening of the diaphragms
