Deleterious effect of poor glycemic control on fetal outcome
Combined incidence of major malformation and spontaneous abortion according to the hemoglobin A1 (HbA1) value during the first trimester of pregnancy in 303 women with type 1 diabetes. The risk rose markedly at HbA1 values above 11% (approximately equivalent to an A1C value of 8.5%). Other studies have found an increase in risk at A1C values above 9.5%.
Data from: Greene MF, Hare JW, Cloherty JP, et al, Teratology 1989; 39:225.
