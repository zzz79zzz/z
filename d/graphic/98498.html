Solitary plasmacytoma of acetabulum on MRI
A T1-weighted coronal image of the pelvis shows a low intensity expansile lesion in the right acetabulum (asterisk). Image B is a coronal view of the pelvis using a STIR sequence and it shows a high-intensity mass in the right acetabulum (asterisk).
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging; STIR: short tau inversion recovery.
