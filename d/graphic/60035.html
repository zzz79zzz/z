Typical facial features in a patient with fragile X syndrome
A 4-year-old male with fragile X syndrome displays some of the typical facial features of the disorder including:
  • A long and narrow face with prominent forehead and chin (prognathism)
  • Large ears
  • Midface hypoplasia with sunken eyes
  • Strabismus
