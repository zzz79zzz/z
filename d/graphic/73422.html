IgA-dominant staphylococcus-associated glomerulonephritis on immunofluorescence microscopy
The glomerulus shown is from an older adult patient with IgA-dominant staphylococcus-associated glomerulonephritis. There is coarsely granular global mesangial and segmental glomerular capillary wall staining for IgA.
