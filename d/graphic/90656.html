Williams syndrome facial features
The facial appearance in patients with Williams syndrome is remarkably uniform. This child's eyebrows flare medially, the nasal bridge is depressed, the epicanthal folds are prominent, and the nares are anteverted. Note prominent lips and full cheeks.
