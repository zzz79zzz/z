How to wash your hands
Wet your hands with clean water, and apply a small amount of soap. Lather and rub hands together for at least 20 seconds. Clean your wrists, palms, backs of your hands, between your fingers, tips of your fingers, thumbs, and under and around your nails. Rinse well, and dry your hands using a clean towel.
