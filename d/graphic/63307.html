Fibrous tumor of the pleura
CT shows a large posterior thoracic mass enhancing heterogeneously after intravenous contrast material. The cardiomediastinal structures, including the descending aorta, are shifted to the right, and the left hilus is displaced anteriorly, indicating that this mass is intrapleural rather than intraparenchymal.
CT: computed tomography.
