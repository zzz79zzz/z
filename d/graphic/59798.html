Trapezoid edema with T2 axial MRI
This T2-weighted, axial magnetic resonance image of the wrist shows increased signal in the trapezoid compared to the marrow signal from the adjacent bones. This appearance of the trapezoid showing edema consistent with microtrabecular injury.
