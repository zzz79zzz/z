CO2 detector for endotracheal tube confirmation
(A) Capnography in the operating room.
(B) CO2 color-change indicator: purple color before connection to circuit.
(C) The indicator turns yellow when connected to the circuit, indicating correct ETT placement.
CO2: carbon dioxide; ETT: endotracheal tube.
