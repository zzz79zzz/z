Brain MRI showing diffuse meningeal enhancement with low cerebrospinal fluid pressure headache
Brain MRI of a patient with low cerebrospinal fluid pressure headache. Contrast (gadolinium) enhanced T1-weighted images in axial (A) and coronal (B) planes show diffuse pachymeningeal enhancement (arrows).
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
