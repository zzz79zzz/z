Trapezoid edema on T2 coronal MRI
This T2-weighted, coronal magnetic resonance image of the wrist shows increased signal in the trapezoid, when compared to the adjacent trapezium (T) and capitate (C). This appearance is consistent with edema due to microtrabecular injury to the trapezoid.
