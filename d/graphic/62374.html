Idiopathic pulmonary hemosiderosis
Film A shows the posteroanterior radiograph of a 32 year old woman with hemoptysis due to her first attack of idiopathic pulmonary hemosiderosis. There is diffuse, ground-glass opacification in both lungs. Film B taken one month later shows complete regression of the opacities following treatment with prednisolone.
