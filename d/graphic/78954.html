Kidney allograft biopsy showing hyperacute rejection by light microscopy
Light micrograph of a glomerulus in hyperacute rejection. There is a thrombus in the afferent arteriole (arrow), and there is extensive necrosis of the glomerular tuft (which has few cells) and the surrounding parenchyma.
