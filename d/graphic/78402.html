Mitral valve prolapse with mitral regurgitation
Four chamber view from a 2-D echocardiogram with color flow Doppler shows significant mitral regurgitation due to a prolapse of the posterior leaflet of the mitral valve. As a result, the mitral regurgitation "jet" is directed anteriorly, toward the interatrial septum and aortic root.
