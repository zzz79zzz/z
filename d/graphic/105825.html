Knee effusion
Knee effusions are usually not marked in OA. This person had a large effusion expanding the suprapatellar pouch, with positive balloon sign (fluctuance) and patellar tap, and OA with CPPD.
OA: osteoarthritis; CPPD: calcium pyrophosphate crystal deposition.
