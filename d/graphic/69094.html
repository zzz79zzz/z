Cross section of felon
The fingertip pulp contains compartments of eccrine sweat glands and fat globules separated by fibrous septae. An abscess within these compartments is a felon. The locations of the neurovascular bundles are shown; these areas should be avoided when incision and drainage are performed.
Adapted from Clark DC. Common acute hand infections. Am Fam Physician 2003; 68:2167.
