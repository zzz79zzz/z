Typical findings on contrast enhanced CT scan in patients with MPM
Initial CT findings Frequency, percent
Ascites 60 to 100
Omental involvement 66 to 91
Peritoneal changes 73 to 88
Diaphragm or liver surface involvement 77 to 82
Small bowel or mesentery involvement 60 to 82
Pleural changes* 23 to 80
Pleural effusion* 17 to 20
Abdominal adenopathy 12 to 27
Discrete masses 12 to 20

CT: computed tomography; MPM: malignant peritoneal mesothelioma.
* Some studies additionally used plan radiographs for interpretations.
