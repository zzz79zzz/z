This ultrasound image shows the flexor hallucis longus tendon in the transverse plane at the level of the concave groove of the talus.
FHL: flexor hallucis longus tendon; MT: medial tubercle of talus; LT: lateral tubercle of talus.
Left side of image: anterior; Right side of image: posterior.
