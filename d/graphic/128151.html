Component testing in grass and birch allergic patient
A representative result of immunoglobulin E (IgE) binding to individual allergen components in a patient with positive skin prick test results to grass and birch pollen extracts possibly related to sensitization to profilins (Phl p 12 and Bet v 2): t3-birch pollen extract, t6-timothy pollen extract, t215- recombinant (r) Bet v 1, t216 – rBet v 2, t220 – r Bet v 4, t225 – rBet v 6, g205 – rPhl p 1, g215 – rPhl p 5, g210 – rPhl p 7, and g212 – rPhl p 12.
CCD: Cross-reactive carbohydrate determinant; Ind: internal standard.
