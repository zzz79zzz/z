A pregnancy laboratory reference interval is an approximation of what can be expected in the overall healthy pregnant population. A value inside or outside of the interval does not necessarily indicate the presence or absence of a disorder in an individual patient.

* Unless otherwise specified, all normal reference values are from the seventeenth edition of Harrison's Principles of Internal Medicine[82].
