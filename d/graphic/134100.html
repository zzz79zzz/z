Kaplan-Meier melanoma-specific survival curves according to T category stage group for patients with stage I and II melanoma from the eighth edition International Melanoma Database
Patients with N0 melanoma were filtered, so that patients with T2+ melanoma were included only if they had negative sentinel lymph nodes, whereas those with T1N0 melanoma were included regardless of whether they underwent sentinel lymph node biopsy.
