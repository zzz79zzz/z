Calcified LV aneurysm on chest radiograph
A chest radiograph in the PA projection (A) shows a calcified left ventricular aneurysm with a rounded deformity of the LV (arrow). A magnified insert (B) shows the calcification (arrowhead). The lateral examination shows an abnormal bulge of the left ventricle (dashed arrows).
LV: left ventricular; PA: posteroanterior.
