Changes in pleural fluid composition after lung transplantation
Sequential changes in pleural fluid red cell and white cell counts (per mm3) and LDH concentration (IU/L) in six patients after lung transplantation. In addition to the progressive decline in LDH and cell counts, there were also reductions in the neutrophil proportions (89 down to 29 percent of total WBC) and total protein content (3.4 down to 2.1 g/dL) during this time period.
RBC: red blood cell count; WBC: white blood cell count; LDH: lactate dehydrogenase.
