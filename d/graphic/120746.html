Ocular, otic, gastrointestinal, and endocrine manifestations of lipoid proteinosis
Ocular manifestations
Trichiasis[1]
Madarosis[1]
Conjunctival nodules[1]
Punctate keratitis[1]
Epiphora[2]
Bilateral uveitis[3]
Corectopia (displacement of the pupils)[4]
Bilateral lens subluxation[5]
Prominent corneal nerves[1]
Keratoconus[6]
Glaucoma[7]
Cataract[7]
Esotropia[8]
Otic manifestations
Increase in hearing thresholds (deposition of hyaline material in the cochlea)[9]
Gastrointestinal manifestations
Esophageal aperistalsis[10]
Xerostomia[11]
Hyaline nodules within the gastrointestinal tract[12]
Endocrine manifestations
Insulin resistance diabetes[13]
Short stature[14]
Renal manifestations
Multiple simple renal cysts[15]
