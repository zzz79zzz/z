Home sleep apnea test tracing
Typical tracing from a four-channel cardiopulmonary recording performed at home. The parameters measured include pulse rate, chest wall impedance, airflow, and oxygen saturation. Obstructive apneas are denoted by the letter "A."
BPM: beats per minute; SaO2: arterial oxygen saturation.
