Radiograph and CT lateral process talus fracture
Radiograph and CT scan of the left ankle of an 85-year-old female following a fall. The radiograph is shown in the AP projection and reveals an obvious fracture of the distal tibia (A, arrow) and a more subtle fracture of the lateral process of the talus magnified in B (arrowhead). The CT is shown in a reconstructed coronal projection (C) and in axial projection (D). The images confirm the fracture of the distal tibia (arrows) and the more subtle fracture of the lateral process of the talus (arrowheads).
CT: computed tomography; AP: anterior posterior.
