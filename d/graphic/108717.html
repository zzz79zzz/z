Evaluating a child for hyponasal speech
Hyponasality occurs in nasal obstruction due to adenoidal hypertrophy or intrinsic intranasal obstruction. An affected child will have difficulty saying words or phrases containing the nasal consonants "m" and "n." A useful test phrase is "my name means money."
