US branches of tibial nerve
This ultrasound image shows the major branches of the tibial nerve in short-axis. Note that the location where the calcaneal, lateral plantar, and medial plantar nerve branches occur varies among individuals.
Small arrowheads: calcaneal branch of tibial nerve; large arrowheads: lateral plantar nerve; arrows: medial plantar nerve; A: tibial artery; V: tibial veins.
Left of screen: posterior; right of screen: anterior.
