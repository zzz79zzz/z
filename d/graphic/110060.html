Endotracheal tube-induced vocal cord granulomas
A 55-year old male patient with postextubation hoarseness due to prior tracheostomy tube. Sagittal (A) and coronal (B) CT images of the neck demonstrate a tracheal granuloma (arrows) in the left anterior wall of trachea resulting in luminal narrowing.
CT: computed tomography.
