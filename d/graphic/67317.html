Nodule in bronchioloalveolar cell carcinoma (focal mucinous adenocarcinoma)
Ill-defined nodule in the superior segment of the lingula with a so-called rabbit tail extending towards the anterior aspect of the chest wall and an air-bronchogram. The bronchus extends into the lesion, assuring a high yield for bronchoscopy.
