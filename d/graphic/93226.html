Hepatitis C virus infection with lymphoid aggregates
This photomicrograph shows a portal tract with a lymphoid aggregate (arrow). Lymphoid aggregates or lymphoid follicles are typically seen in chronic viral hepatitis (hepatitis C or hepatitis B) and rarely, autoimmune hepatitis. An arrow points to a focus of interface hepatitis.
