Ventilatory responses to hypoxia in patients with mild and severe hypothyroidism
The ventilatory responses are shown as the relationships between alveolar oxygen tension (PAO2) and VE (STPD).
PAO2: partial pressure of oxygen; STPD: standard temperature and pressure dry; VE: minute ventilation.
Data from: Zwillich AA, Pierson DJ, Hofeldt FD. Ventilatory control in myxedema and hypothyroidism. N Engl J Med 1975; 292:662.
