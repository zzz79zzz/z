Chikungunya - rash
Maculopapular rash during the acute infectious phase of chikungunya. The rash was distributed over the entire body, resolved after a few days, and was followed by desquamation.
From: Miner JJ, Yeang HXA, Fox JM, et al. Chikungunya viral arthritis in the United States: A mimic of seronegative rheumatoid arthritis.
