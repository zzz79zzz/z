Evaluation and management of chronic pain in patients with dementia who verbally report pain
This algorithm shows an overview of the approach to patients with dementia who are able to report pain verbally. In patients with dementia, complaints of pain may be related to sources of distress other than pain. For further information refer to UpToDate content on treatment of chronic pain in older adults.
* Basic comfort needs involve toileting, thirst, hunger, or visual or hearing impairment.
