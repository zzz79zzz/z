Securing the endotracheal tube
Securing the endotracheal tube. A) The tape is split into a Y and the base is applied to the cheek. B) One strip of tape is then applied to the skin above or below the lips while the other secures the tube. A second piece of tape may be applied from the other side in a similar manner for greater stability. Tincture of benzoin also may be used to increase adhesion.
