Chest radiographs of infants with transient tachypnea of the newborn
Figures A and B are radiographs of infants with transient tachypnea of the newborn (TTN). They demonstrate areas of diffuse infiltrates in the lung field and perihilar streaking indicative of retained lung fluid, consistent with the diagnosis of TTN.
