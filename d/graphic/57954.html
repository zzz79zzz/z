Plane xanthomas
Yellowish-reddish, slightly elevated plaques on the neck (arrows), noticeable mainly because of the accentuation of the skin texture in a normolipemic patient with lymphomas. Plane xanthomas occur most commonly on the upper trunk and neck and also occur in individuals with myeloma.
