Scanning electron micrograph showing abnormal RBC morphology in hereditary elliptocytosis
Scanning electron micrograph of the peripheral blood of a patient with HE and severe anemia. Note the abnormal RBC morphology with elliptocytes, poikilocytes, and fragmented RBCs. Original magnification ×10,000. Refer to UpToDate for our approach to the evaluation of individuals with suspected HE.
RBC: red blood cell; HE: hereditary elliptocytosis.
From: An X, Mohandas N. Disorders of red cell membrane. Br J Haematol 2008; 141:367. http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/wol1/doi/10.1111/j.1365-2141.2008.07091.x/abstract. Copyright © 2008.
