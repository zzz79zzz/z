Sporadic CCM with a DVA
Sporadic CCM with a very large DVA.
(A) Axial T2 SE of a 26-year-old female patient shows a CCM near the left lateral ventricle.
(B) Postgadolinium T1 shows a large DVA involving much of the left frontal lobe.
(C and D) SWI demonstrates very clearly the CCM and DVA without gadolinium administration.
CCM: cerebral cavernous malformation; DVA: developmental venous anomaly; SE: spin echo; SWI: susceptibility-weighted imaging.
