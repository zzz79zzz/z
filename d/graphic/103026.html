Subtle findings of a Lisfranc (tarsometatarsal) injury
Subtle findings of a Lisfranc injury are found in the plain radiographs above. The image on the left (A) is a non-weight-bearing anteroposterior radiograph showing minimal displacement between the first and second metatarsals that can easily be missed. The image on the right (B) is a weight-bearing film, which accentuates the diastasis and demonstrates a wide gap between the metatarsals.
From: Latterman C, Goldstein JL, Wukich DK, et al. Practical management of Lisfranc injuries in athletes. Clin J Sport Med 2007; 17:311. DOI: 10.1097/JSM.0b013e31811ed0ba.
