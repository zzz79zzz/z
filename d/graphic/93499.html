Calcified LV aneurysm with thrombus on CT scan
A CT scan through the heart (A) shows an apical LV aneurysm with calcified wall (arrow). There is thrombus in the aneurysm (arrowhead). The calcification indicates a chronic process. Image B is an axial CT scan through the heart and shows an apical LV aneurysm with a calcified wall (arrow). The thrombus in this case is calcified as well (arrowhead).
LV: left ventricular; CT: computed tomography.
