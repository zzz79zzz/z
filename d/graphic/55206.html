Osteoarthritis following ankle fracture
Ankle osteoarthritis developed in this 41-year-old male after an ankle injury with ligamentous damage and distal fibular fracture. Callus at the distal fibular fracture site, several centimeters above the joint line (upper arrow), and medial joint line narrowing with sclerotic changes (lower arrow), suggestive of osteoarthritis, can be seen.
