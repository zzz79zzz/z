Non-small cell lung cancer and multiple intramedullary spinal metastases
Sagittal T1-weighted MRI with fat saturation (A, B) demonstrates 3 enhancing intramedullary lesions (arrows) at C6, T4, and conus medullaris. Subsequent biopsy of the conus lesion confirmed carcinoma consistent with a lung cancer primary.
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
