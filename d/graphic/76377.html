Acute/evolving infero-postero-lateral myocardial infarction
Electrocardiogram showing the major features of an inferior (Q waves, ST elevations, and T wave inversions in II, III, and aVF), posterior (tall R waves in V1 and V2), and lateral (T wave inversions in V4 to V6) myocardial infarction.
