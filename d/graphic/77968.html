Survival in chronic myeloid leukemia after matched unrelated donor transplantation
This graph shows the probability of survival versus years after matched unrelated donor (MUD) hematopoietic cell transplantation for patients transplanted within the first year after diagnosis of chronic myelogenous leukemia (CML). Survival data is shown in red for those <21 years of age at the time of transplant, in blue for those 21 to 36, in green for those 37 to 50, and in red for those >50.
Data from Hansen, JA, et al. N Engl J Med 1998; 338:962.
