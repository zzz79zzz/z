Mild cylindrical bronchiectasis in a child
Chest CT scan in a child with mild cylindrical bronchiectasis in the right lower lobe showing airways with increased BAR and signet ring appearance. Pediatric guidelines define bronchiectasis as BAR >0.8[1].
CT: computed tomography; BAR: bronchoarterial ratio.
