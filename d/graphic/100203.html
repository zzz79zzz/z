Knee lateral collateral ligament taping
Patients recovering from an isolated lateral collateral ligament (LCL) strain or who need additional support of the lateral knee can be treated as described:
(A) Using elastic tape, apply distal anchor 2-3 in below tibial tuberosity; apply proximal anchor at midpoint of quadriceps.
(B) Apply lateral and medial strips in an X fashion.
(C) Reinforce with nonelastic tape.
(D) Close with strips moving from distal to proximal.
