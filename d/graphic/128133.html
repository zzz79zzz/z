Three-dimensional echocardiogram and computed tomography scan of membranous ventricular septal defect with muscular extension
Paired three-dimensional echocardiographic still frame (A) and CT image (B) of membranous VSD with extension into the anterior muscular septum. Both images display the right ventricular aspect of the septum. Arrow in still frame (A) denotes aortic valve seen through defect.
RV: right ventricle; CT: computed tomography; VSD: ventricular septal defect (border outlined with yellow arrows on CT scan); RVOT: right ventricular outflow tract.
