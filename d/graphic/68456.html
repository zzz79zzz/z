Precipitin curve: Influence of antigen to antibody ratio
The Heidelberger precipitin curve demonstrates the influence of antigen:antibody ratios on the size of immune complexes and amount of precipitated immune complexes. The antigens and antibodies circled with dashed lines are in precipitates. Other antigens and antibodies remain in solution.
