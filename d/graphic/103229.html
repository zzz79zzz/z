US fibularis tendons transverse view behind malleolus
This ultrasound image shows the fibular (or peroneal) tendons in a transverse view (short-axis) behind (or posterior to) the lateral malleolus.
Flt: fibularis longus tendon; Fbt: fibularis brevis tendon; LM: lateral malleolus; *: fibrocartilaginous plate; arrowheads: superior fibular retinaculum.
Left side of image: posterior; Right side of image: anterior.
