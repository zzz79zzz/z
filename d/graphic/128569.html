3D ultrasound of T-shaped uterus
A diagnosis of a T-shaped uterus can be made if all of the following are present on 3D ultrasound[1]:
(A) Lateral indentation angle ≤130°
(B) Lateral internal indentation depth ≥7 mm
(C) T-angle ≤40°
Reference: 
  1. Ludwin A, Coelho Neto MA, Ludwin I, et al. Congenital Uterine Malformation by Experts (CUME): diagnostic criteria for T-shaped uterus. Ultrasound Obstet Gynecol 2020; 55:815.
