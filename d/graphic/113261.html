Measurement of intermalleolar distance in the evaluation of knock-knees
Intermalleolar distance is measured with the patellas facing straight ahead and the femoral condyles touching. Intermalleolar distance >8 cm (approximately 3 inches) is a clue to a pathologic cause of knock-knees.
