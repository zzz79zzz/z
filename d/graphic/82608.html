Sick sinus syndrome and atrial fibrillation
Rhythm strip showing sick sinus syndrome. The initial part of the tracing reveals coarse fibrillation with a slow ventricular response in the absence of drugs that impair AV nodal conduction. The atrial fibrillation breaks and is followed by a sinus escape beat with a long escape inverval of nearly four seconds and then another escape beat at a similar interval (not shown).
