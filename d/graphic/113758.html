Off-label indications of photodynamic therapy

Off-label indications of photodynamic therapy
  • Cutaneous T cell lymphoma, localized patch-plaque[1]
  • Disseminated superficial actinic porokeratosis (DSAP)[2]
  • Extramammary Paget's disease[3]
  • Granuloma annulare[4]
  • Hidradenitis suppurativa[5]
  • Hypertrophic/keloid scars[6]
  • Leishmaniasis, cutaneous[7]
  • Lichen sclerosus[8,9]
  • Linear porokeratosis[10]
  • Molluscum contagiosum in human immunodeficiency virus[11]
  • Nonmelanoma skin cancer prevention in solid organ transplant recipients[12-15]
  • Nonmelanoma skin cancer treatment in solid organ transplant recipients[16]
  • Necrobiosis lipoidica[17]
  • Psoriasis[18]
  • Rosacea[19]
  • Sebaceous hyperplasia[20]
  • Verrucae (hand/foot and genital)[21,22]
