MRI scan

MRI is a test used to create pictures of the inside of the body. This is done with a special machine that uses a strong magnet. MRI scanners come in different sizes and shapes, including standard (A), wide-bore (B), and open (C).

For an MRI scan, you lie still inside the machine while it takes pictures. The technician will not be in the room with you, but they will be nearby and able to see you and talk to you.
