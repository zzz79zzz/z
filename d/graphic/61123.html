Cautery of epistaxis with a silver nitrate stick
The silver nitrate applicator tip should be applied to a small area surrounding the bleeding site, starting proximally. The bleeding point itself cannot be cauterized until hemostasis is achieved through proximal cautery, vasoconstrictive agents, or direct pressure.
