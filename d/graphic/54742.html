Nonoperative healing of a Jones fracture in an adolescent
This 13-year-old girl sustained a "Jones" fracture playing basketball and was treated nonoperatively. A) Initial x-ray shows minimal displacement. B) At 3 months, there is residual lucency, but the patient is asymptomatic. C) At 6 months, consolidation is still delayed but patient remains asymptomatic.
