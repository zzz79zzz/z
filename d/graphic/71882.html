Lung fibrosis due to carmustine
Postmortem slide of cut surface of lungs in a case of late-onset carmustine lung fibrosis from a woman who died 13 years after receiving carmustine for astrocytoma. There is extensive subpleural fibrosis, especially at the lung apices.
