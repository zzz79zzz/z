Histopathology of Darier disease
Histologic examination of lesional skin in Darier disease shows a suprabasal cleft containing acantholytic cells, eosinophilic dyskeratotic cells (corps ronds) in the epidermis and flattened parakeratotic cells (grains) in the stratum corneum.
