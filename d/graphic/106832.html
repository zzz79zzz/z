This ultrasound scan shows the tensor fascia latae muscle and tendon in the sagittal plane (long-axis view) as it inserts onto the ASIS.

TFL: tensor fasciae latae muscle; arrowheads: tensor fasciae latae tendon; ASIS: anterior superior iliac spine; GMI: gluteus minimus muscle.
