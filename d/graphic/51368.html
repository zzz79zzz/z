Tryptophan and serotonin metabolism
Pathways of tryptophan and serotonin metabolism in the carcinoid tumor cell. Patients with the carcinoid syndrome often have increased levels of 5-hydroxyindoleacetic acid (5-HIAA) excretion in the urine and serotonin in the blood; urinary serotonin excretion is either normal or slightly increased.
