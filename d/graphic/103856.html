Sagittal view of posterior cruciate ligament tear on magnetic resonance imaging
Above is a side-by-side comparison of two sagittal view MRI images. Image A shows a normal ligament within the red oval that appears curvilinear and dark on T1 or T2 sequences, while image B shows a torn PCL within the red oval associated with a loss of normal contours and density.
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging; PCL: posterior cruciate ligament injury.
