Techniques for transbronchial penetration: cough method
Schematic diagram shows the cough method used for tracheobronchial wall penetration in transbronchoscopic needle aspiration. The fiberoptic bronchoscope and needle assembly are held stationary and the patient is asked to cough, which forces the tracheobronchial wall over the needle.
Reproduced with permission from Minai, OA, Dasgupta, A, Mehta, AC. Transbronchial needle aspiration of central and peripheral lesions. In: Bolliger, CT, Mathur, PN (Eds), Progress in Respiratory Research - Interventional Bronchoscopy. Karger, Basel, Switzerland, 1999.
