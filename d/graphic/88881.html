Polypoid form of colorectal B cell lymphoma on CT scan
Computed tomography (CT) scan of the abdomen at the level of the iliac crests in a patient with colorectal B cell lymphoma. There is a polypoid enhancing mass of the cecum (arrowhead) as well as enhancement and thickening of the ileum (arrow) suggesting involvement with lymphoma.
