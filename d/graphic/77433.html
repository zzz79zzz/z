Solitary enchondroma of the hand
Postero-anterior radiograph of the hand. Observe the large geographic area of radiolucency within the mid-diaphysis of the fifth metacarpal. Significant expansion, endosteal scalloping, bone deformity, and pathologic fracture are noted (arrows). Matrix calcification is evident.
