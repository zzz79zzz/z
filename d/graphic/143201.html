Prenatal ultrasound and MRI of a large fetal hybrid lesion at 22 weeks of gestation

(A) Axial US demonstrates a vessel (arrow) originating from the thoracic aorta coursing into a heterogeneous mass (arrowhead).

(B) Sagittal ultrasound demonstrates the heterogeneous mass in the right thorax (arrow) with eventration of the diaphragm (arrowhead).

(C [sagittal], D [coronal], E [axial]) T2-weighted MRI demonstrates the large high signal mass in the right lung (arrows). The mass is deviating the heart (arrowhead) to the left. The CVR was 3.5 (high risk for hydrops developing).
CVR: congenital pulmonary airway malformation volume ratio; MRI: magnetic resonance imaging; US: ultrasound.
