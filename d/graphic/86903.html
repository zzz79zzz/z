Healing deep partial-thickness burn
These photographs show a deep partial-thickness burn of the proximal leg at different stages of healing. The top image was taken at one week, the middle image at four weeks, and bottom image at 10 months after the initial injury was sustained. As this burn crosses the knee joint, referral to a burn center for care is appropriate.
