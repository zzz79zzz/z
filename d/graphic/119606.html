Avascular necrosis (AVN) of the humeral head in a patient with sickle cell disease
Plain radiographs of the right shoulder showing AVN of the humoral head in a pediatric patient with SCD and a normal control for comparison.
  • (A) Shoulder of a patient with SCD. There is enlargement of the humeral head with mild flattening (vertical arrows) and ill-defined areas of sclerosis (arrowheads).
  • (B) Normal shoulder (unaffected individual). Note the rounded articular surface and uniform lucency of the humeral head.
AVN: Avascular necrosis; SCD: sickle cell disease.
