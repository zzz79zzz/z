Axial CT and endoscopic nasal view of a juvenile angiofibroma
This juvenile angiofibroma was a cause of repeated epistaxis in this teenage boy. Axial computed tomography with contrast shows a vascular mass arising from the pterygopalatine fossa and filling the nasopharynx (arrow).
CT: computed tomography.
