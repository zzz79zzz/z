US subacromial-subdeltoid bursal fluid
Dynamic ultrasound (US) examination revealing fluid within the subacromial-subdeltoid bursa mimicking fluid within the bicipital tendon sheath. The fluid displaces medially and laterally, superficially and away from the bicipital tendon with compression indicating that the fluid is not contained within the bicipital tendon sheath.
D: deltoid muscle; MED: medial; SA: fluid within the subacromial-subdeltoid bursa; BT: long head of the biceps brachii tendon; H: humerus bone.
Left: medial; Right: lateral; Bottom of screen: deep; Top of screen: superficial.
