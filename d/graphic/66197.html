Captopril lowers cardiovascular mortality post-MI
Life table for death from cardiovascular disease in 2231 patients in the SAVE trial with left ventricular dysfunction but without overt heart failure randomized to captopril or placebo 3 to 16 days after an acute myocardial infarction. Captopril significantly lowered the cardiovascular mortality.
Data from Pfeffer MA, Braunwald E, Moye LA, et al. N Engl J Med 1992, 327:669.
