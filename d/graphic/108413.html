Cuboid avulsion fracture AP radiograph
The AP plain radiograph above shows a small avulsion fracture of the cuboid (arrow). Inversion-type ankle sprains involving external rotation of the tibia can cause such a fracture. A spiral fracture involving the distal fifth metatarsal is also seen (arrowhead).
AP: anterior-posterior.
From: Labovitz J, Chan F, Nouvong A, Christman RA. Classification of fractures and dislocations. In: Foot and Ankle Radiology, 2nd ed, Christman R (Ed), Wolters Kluwer, Philadelphia 2014.
