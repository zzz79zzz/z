Various presentations of congenital cutaneous candidiasis
The rash associated with congenital cutaneous candidiasis may have a variable presentation, including diffuse maculopapular rash (A); dry, flaky rash (B); or dry, cracking, scaly rash (C). Yellow-white plaques (arrows) may be seen on the umbilical cord (D).
