Idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis
Chest radiograph shows diffuse, coarse reticular opacities involving all compartments of both lungs. Dilatation of both central pulmonary arteries indicates pulmonary arterial hypertension and cor pulmonale. Traction bronchiectasis contributes to the cystic and tubular lucencies. The lung volumes are preserved.
