Using a neti pot
A neti pot is a small pot with a long spout. This woman is using a neti pot to rinse out her nose with salt water.
Reproduced from: U.S. Food and Drug Administration. Is Rinsing Your Sinuses With Neti Pots Safe? Available at: https://www.fda.gov/consumers/consumer-updates/rinsing-your-sinuses-neti-pots-safe (Accessed May 21, 2019).
