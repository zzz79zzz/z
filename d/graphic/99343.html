US pathologic subacromial subdeltoid bursa
Ultrasound (US) image of subacromial-subdeltoid bursopathy (arrows). Note the thickened, hypoechoic appearance of the bursal tissue. There is also an associated partial thickness tear of the supraspinatus (SS).
Deltoid: deltoid muscle; GT: greater tuberosity; Humerus: humerus bone.
Left: medial; Right: lateral; Bottom of screen: deep; Top of screen: superficial.
