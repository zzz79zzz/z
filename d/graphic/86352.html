Papoose restraint for ear or nose foreign body removal
Prior to removal of the foreign body from the external auditory canal or nose, the child is restrained in the supine position using either a papoose board (pictured) or a sheet wrap. The head is then held by an assistant. For removal of ear foreign bodies, the affected ear is turned up as shown.
