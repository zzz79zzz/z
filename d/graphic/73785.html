Laryngoscopic images showing a normal infant larynx from above (panel A) and below (panel B) the level of the epiglottis. Note the healthy appearance of the normal structures: epiglottis (E), glottis (G), vocal folds (vf), arytenoids (A), and aryepiglottic folds (ae).
