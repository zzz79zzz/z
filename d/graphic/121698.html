Whole-body MRI of chronic nonbacterial osteomyelitis (CNO)/chronic recurrent multifocal osteomyelitis (CRMO)
Whole-body MRI includes coronal STIR of the whole body, sagittal STIR of the entire spine, axial STIR of the pelvis and knees, and sagittal STIR of both ankles. There is abnormal signal within the metaphysis of the left distal tibia.
MRI: magnetic resonance imaging; STIR: short tau inversion recovery.
