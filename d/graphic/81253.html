Pneumatosis intestinalis of the colon on plain film
A plain film of the abdomen demonstrates air within the wall of the ascending colon (arrow). This can be seen in ischemic bowel but may also occur after colonoscopy or surgical anastomoses and in patients taking corticosteroids or with HIV infection.
HIV: human immunodeficiency virus.
