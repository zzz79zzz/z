Combined right middle and right lower lobe atelectasis
Upright chest radiograph showing atelectasis in the right middle and lower lobes after cholecystectomy. The relevant findings include elevation of the right lung base (arrow) with obscuration of the right hilar structures, basal pulmonary vessels, and right heart border. This radiograph was initially interpreted as a subpulmonic effusion.
