Commonly available products in the United States are shown; most products are available by both prescription and over-the-counter (except as noted above). Enteric-coated and extended-release formulations are also available for some products, but we generally do not advise use of these preparations because they are poorly absorbed. Refer to UpToDate and to Lexicomp drug monographs (included with UpToDate) for details of dosing, treatment of iron deficiency anemia, and management of acute iron poisoning.
