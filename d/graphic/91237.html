Histologic features of cheilitis granulomatosa
Cheilitis granulomatosa is characterized by non-necrotizing granulomas in the lamina propria (arrows). Granulomas may be focal, subtle, or poorly formed. A perivascular infiltrate of lymphocytes and plasma cells is commonly present.
