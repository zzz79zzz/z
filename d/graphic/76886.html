The fatigue and anemia subscales of the Functional Assessment of Cancer Therapy (FACT) questionnaire
Fatigue component for both subscales
1. I feel weary
2. I always feel weak
3. I feel exhausted
4. I feel tired
5. I'm too tired to eat
6. I need to sleep during the day
7. I need help to carry out daily activities
8. I feel frustrated because I'm tired and I can't do the things I want to
9. I have to limit my social activities because I feel tired
10. I have difficulty in starting to do something
11. I have difficulty in finishing something I have started
12. I feel I don't have any energy
13. I am unable to carry out normal activities
Nonfatigue component for anemia subscale
1. I have trouble walking
2. I am dizzy
3. I have headaches
4. I am short of breath
5. I have chest pain
6. I lack motivation
7. I have no interest in sex
