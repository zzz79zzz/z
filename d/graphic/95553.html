Early IUP with double decidual sac sign
Transvaginal ultrasound shows an early intrauterine pregnancy with double decidual sac sign. Image B is a color overlay of the structures that contribute to the appearance, including the GS, surrounded by the decidua capsularis (light pink), partially surrounded by the decidua parietalis (dark pink). In between these two layers is a small amount of fluid (yellow).
IUP: intrauterine pregnancy; GS: gestational sac.
