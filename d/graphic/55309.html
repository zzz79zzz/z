Timing of pacemaker implantation according to age at diagnosis of congenital CHB
In a study of 102 cases of congenital complete heart block (CHB), most patients ultimately had a pacemaker inserted, regardless of the time of onset of the syndrome. By 20 years of age, only 11 percent of neonatal and 12 percent of childhood cases had not required pacemaker implantation.
Data from Jaeggi ET, Hamilton RM, Silverman ED, et al, J Am Coll Cardiol 2002; 39:130.
