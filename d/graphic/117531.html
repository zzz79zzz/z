3D TEE mitral valve short axis
Zoomed 3D transesophageal echocardiographic view of the mitral valve in patients with degenerative disease demonstrating:
(A) A patient with fibroelastic deficiency with P2 flail segment and ruptured chord.
(B) A patient with Barlow's disease with multi-segmental, bileaflet billowing.
(C) A patient with predominantly anterior leaflet multisegmental prolapse.
(D) A patient with posteromedial commissural prolapse.
3D: three-dimensional; TEE: transesophageal echocardiography.
Original figure modified for this publication. From: Lang RM, Tsang W, Weinert L, et al. Valvular heart disease: Value of three-dimensional echocardiography. J Am Coll Cardiol 2011; 58:1993. Illustration used with the permission of Elsevier Inc. All rights reserved.
