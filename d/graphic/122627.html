Variation in the umbilical artery waveform in type 3 sFGR
Type 3 sFGR is characterized by a pathognomonic umbilical artery waveform that has a variable flow pattern that cycles between forward, absent, and reversed flow over a short interval, which is termed intermittent absent/reversed end-diastolic flow.
sFGR: selective fetal growth restriction.
