Hydrosalpinx
Transvaginal ultrasound image of the adnexa showing a
hydrosalpinx with three-dimensional rendering. A cystic structure
with a septation (arrow) is identified in the adnexa. The rendered image (on right side of illustration) demonstrates a tubular fluid collection with incomplete septations indicating a serpiginously dilated fallopian tube.
