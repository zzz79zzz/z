TEP hernia reduction for direct inguinal hernia repair
This figure shows a small direct hernia viewed from the extraperitoneal space. Gentle traction on the peritoneum with one hand and pressure on the abdominal wall with the other facilitates reduction of the direct hernia sac.
TEP: totally extraperitoneal.
