Velamentous cord insertion
Diamnionic, monochorionic twin placenta with the intervening amnion rolled in the center. Notice the haphazard vascularization (large arrow). The velamentous cord insertion (small arrow) of the smaller twin of this discordant pair is not an unusual finding in monochorionic twins.
