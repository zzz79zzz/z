Opening the parametrium during a radical hysterectomy
The uterus is placed under tension and the round ligament divided as far laterally as possible. The pelvic peritoneum is then incised lateral to the external iliac artery and vein, and the incision carried cephalad to the corresponding paracolic gutter.
