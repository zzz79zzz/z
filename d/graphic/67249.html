Sternum, pericardium, and related anatomy
This dissection exposes the pericardial sac posterior to the body of the sternum from just superior to the sternal angle to the level of the xiphisternal joint. The pericardial sac lies approximately one-third to the right of the midsternal line and two-thirds to the left.
