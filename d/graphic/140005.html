Hydroxychloroquine-induced myopathy

Frozen muscle sections from a patient with hydroxychloroquine-induced myopathy.

(A) H&E stain showing muscle fibers harboring vacuoles of various sizes, some of which contain granular material.

(B) Acid phosphatase: increased reactivity (red, large, darker color spots) in several muscle fibers. Some vacuoles (arrow) are filled with acid phosphatase positive material.
H&E: hematoxylin and eosin.
