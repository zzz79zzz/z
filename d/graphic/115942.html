MRI and MDCT of the thoracic spine in SAPHO syndrome
Sagittal T1W MRI (A) and MDCT (B) of the thoracic spine showing contiguous anterior vertebral corner lesions with adjacent vertebral body sclerosis. Note the erosive process extending along the anterior body cortices with mild prevertebral soft tissue swelling.
SAPHO: synovitis, acne, pustulosis, hyperostosis, and osteitis; MRI: magnetic resonance imaging; MDCT: multidetector computed tomography; T1W: T1-weighted.
