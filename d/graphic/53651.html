H. pylori and GERD
The effect of Helicobacter pylori (H. pylori) on gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD) is dependent on the distribution of the gastritis and the predisposition for GERD (esophagogastric junction (EGJ) competence, hiatus hernia, esophageal acid clearance).
