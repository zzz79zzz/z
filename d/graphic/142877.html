Arteriovenous malformation on magnetic resonance imaging
Coronal MRI following contrast administration in the arterial (A) and venous (B) phases shows a vascular lesion in the left kidney with enhancement similar to the aorta. An MRI maximum intensity projection reconstruction (C) depicts the location and size of the AVM.
AVM: arteriovenous malformation; MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
