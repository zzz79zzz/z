Presacral venous plexus - Fascial relationship
This figure shows the location of the presacral venous plexus, the presacral facia, and the posterior rectum in the lateral view. The wise surgeon will not violate the presacral fascia, so as to avoid the risk of life-threatening hemorrhage.
