Neuropathic disease of foot
Plain radiograph of the foot demonstrating features typical of neuropathic joint disease involving the tarsometatarsal joint spaces (arrows); there is destruction of the joints and adjacent bones, infralateral subluxation of the metatarsal bones with respect to the tarsal bones, and proliferative new bone formation, including extensive intertarsal degenerative change.
