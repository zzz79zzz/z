Histopathologic features of hand-foot syndrome (toxic erythema of chemotherapy): Vacuolar interface dermatitis
Basovacuolar alteration with scattered dyskeratosis of the dermal-epidermal junction. There is an overlying, acute, basket-weave stratum corneum.
