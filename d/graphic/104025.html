Square steps exercise with resistance band
The exercise shown is used in exercise programs for a range of athletes. With an elastic band encircling both knees, stand in a half-squat position with toes pointing forward and a neutral spine. Take steps to the left, forward, right, and backward, making a square, while maintaining continuous tension in the band.
