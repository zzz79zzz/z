Erythrodontia in CEP
Photograph taken with the aid of long wavelength ultraviolet light in a patient with congenital erythropoietic porphyria (CEP). Bright fluorescence of the teeth is demonstrated, due to the presence of high concentrations of uroporphyrin deposited in the teeth in this disorder.
Photograph provided by Shigeru Sassa, MD, PhD.
