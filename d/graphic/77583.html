Panretinal photocoagulation for severe proliferative diabetic retinopathy: Appearance on fundus photograph
Color fundus photograph of the posterior portion of laser panretinal photocoagulation (PRP) for severe proliferative diabetic retinopathy with areas of neovascularization at the disc and elsewhere (NVD and NVE) as well as white ischemic retinal vessels, and displaying numerous laser burns outside the macula; the treatment would further continue in all quadrants to the midperiphery.
