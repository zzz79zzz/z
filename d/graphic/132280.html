ICH features on head CT associated with the risk of hematoma expansion
Noncontrast head CT showing acute ICH with signs associated with the risk of subsequent hematoma expansion: irregular shape (A), island sign (B; arrow), hypodensity (C; dashed arrow), heterogeneity (D), black hole sign (E; thick arrow), swirl sign (F), and blend sign (G; arrowhead).
ICH: intracerebral hemorrhage; CT: computed tomography.
