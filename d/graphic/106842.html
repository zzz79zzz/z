This ultrasound scan shows the direct head of the rectus femoris tendon in the transverse plane (short-axis view) at its origin on the AIIS.
Arrows: direct head of rectus femoris tendon; AIIS: anterior inferior iliac spine; SA: sartorius muscle.
Left side of image: lateral; right side of image: medial.
