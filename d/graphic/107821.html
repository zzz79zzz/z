Ultrasound transducer manipulation techniques
As shown in this figure, the ultrasound transducer can be manipulated using a variety of motions to change the direction and orientation of the ultrasound beam. Note that the sliding maneuver (figure A) can be performed in the long axis (longitudinal or sagittal plane), as shown above, or in the short axis (transverse plane).
