Effects of testosterone versus placebo treatment on sexual function in TTrials participants
The primary outcome of the Sexual Function Trial was the change from baseline in the score for sexual activity (question 4) on the PDQ; range, 0 to 12, with higher scores indicating more activity. The p value was calculated with the use of a linear random-effects model for sexual activity, and the error bars represent 95% confidence intervals.
TTrials: Testosterone Trials; PDQ-Q4: Psychosexual Daily Questionnaire - Question 4.
Data from: Snyder PJ, Bhasin S, Cunningham GR, et al. Effects of testosterone treatment in older men. N Engl J Med 2016; 374:611.
