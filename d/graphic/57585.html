Ultrafast CT scanner
Diagram of ultrafast CT scanner, also called electron beam CT or fifth generation CT. An electron gun produces high energy electrons which are deflected and focused on four tungsten target rings by magnetic coils. The produced x-rays penetrate the patient and are intercepted by two arrays of detectors. No moving parts are present.
Adapted from McCollough, CH, Morin, RL, Radiol Clin North Am 1994; 32:521
