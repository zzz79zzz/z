Microscopic features of lymphangioleiomyomatosis
High magnification photomicrograph showing the abnormal spindle cells that characterize lymphangioleiomyomatosis. The proliferating spindle cells have many characteristics of modified smooth muscles cells and frequently stain for smooth muscle-associated intermediate filaments using immunohistochemistry.
