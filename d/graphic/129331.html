(A,B) Increased FDG uptake in the shoulders and trochanteric areas (arrows), consistent with shoulder and hip bursitis.
(C,D) Increased FDG uptake in the cervical and lumbar interspinous areas and trochanteric area, consistent with bursitis (dashed arrows).
PET: positron emission tomography; PMR: polymyalgia rheumatica; FDG: fluorodeoxyglucose.
