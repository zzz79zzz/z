Histopathologic features of kaposiform hemangioendothelioma

(A) Spindle cell lesion involving multiple planes of tissue.

(B) Coalescing lobules.

(C) Extravasated red blood cells, hemosiderin, and platelet microthrombus (insert).

(D) Immunoreactive to PROX1 (lymphatic endothelial marker).
