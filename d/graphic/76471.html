Course of osmotic demyelination in hyponatremia
Clinical course in 7 previously healthy women show developed postoperative hyponatremia and seizures presumably due to cerebral edema. Rapid correction of the plasma sodium concentration was associated with late deterioration into coma or a permanent vegetative state that may have been due to osmotic demyelination.
