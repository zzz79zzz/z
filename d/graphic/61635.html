Radial pulse delay in subclavian steal syndrome
Simultaneous right and left radial pulse volumes in a patient with dizziness induced by left upper extremity exercise shows late arrival of the left subclavian artery pulsation, which can be appreciated by simultaneous palpation of the radial arteries. Note the delay in the left radial pulse at the brown bar.
