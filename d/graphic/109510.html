Fascia iliaca block ultrasound
For ultrasound-guided fascia iliaca block, the fascia lata, iliacus muscle, and fascia iliaca are identified. The needle is inserted in-plane, with the tip placed under the fascia iliaca for injection of local anesthetic. The arrow shows the needle trajectory.
For further details, refer to UpToDate content on fascia iliaca block.
m.: muscle; n.: nerve; a.: artery.
