Moraxella catarrhalis on Gram stain of a sputum specimen
This sputum sample shows several intact neutrophils and gram-negative diplococci with flattened internal edges resembling Moraxella species or Neisseria species. By culture, the organism was identified as Moraxella catarrhalis.
