Bone scan of Paget affecting both femurs
Bone scan in a patient with Paget disease affecting both femurs. Note the intense uptake, beginning in subchondral bone and moving in one direction through that bone. There is a suggestion by this bone scan of Paget disease in the R tibia as well.
