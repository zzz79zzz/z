Unequal placental sharing resulting in sFGR
In sFGR, the placental territory is distributed unequally between twins: one twin benefits from having the majority of the placental share while the growth-restricted twin is starved because of access to only a small portion of the placenta.
sFGR: selective fetal growth restriction.
