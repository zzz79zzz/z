Bulky foot compression dressing
The bulky foot compression dressing is used to treat calcaneal fractures and foot injuries. Sheet wadding in several layers is applied after cotton fluffs are placed between the toes, and elastic bandage is used to wrap the foot. The elastic wrap should be looser around the leg than the ankle, providing a pressure gradient to facilitate drainage for edema.
