Healing transverse fracture of little toe
AP view (panel A) and oblique view (panel B). Note the easily seen calcific density adjacent to both sides of the fracture site (arrow), indicating the presence of callus. A more subtle sign of callus formation is seen in panel B: diffuse increased bony density overlying the fracture site (arrowhead).
AP: anterior posterior.
