Complete atelectasis of the left lung
Bedside chest radiograph of a intubated patient with complete left lung atelectasis. The endotracheal tube was advanced too far into the airway, obstructing the left mainstem bronchus. The left hemithorax is completely opaque. The cardiac silhouette is totally obscured due to its deviation into the left hemithorax.
