US hamstring muscles extended field transverse view
This ultrasound scan provides an extended field of view of the hamstring muscles in the transverse plane (short-axis view) at mid-thigh.
ST: semitendinosus muscle; SM: semimembranosus muscle; BFL: long head of biceps femoris muscle; BFS: short head of biceps femoris muscle; G: gracilis muscle; AD: adductor muscles; dotted ellipse: sciatic nerve.
Left side of image: lateral; right side of image: medial.
