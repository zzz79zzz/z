Computed tomography demonstrating retropharyngeal and parapharyngeal abscesses
This contrast-enhanced computed tomography of the neck shows parapharyngeal (A) and retropharyngeal (B) abscesses as indicated by a low-density core, soft-tissue swelling, obliterated fat planes, mass effect, and rim enhancement.
