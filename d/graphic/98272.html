Rehabilitation scheme for isolated low-grade LCL injuries
Time frame Grade 1 sprain Grade 2 sprain
Week 1
  • Relative rest, ice, compression
  • Crutches as needed and hinged brace protection
  • ROM exercise
  • Relative rest, ice, compression
  • Crutches as needed; brief immobilization (2 to 3 weeks)
Week 2
  • ROM and stretching exercises
  • Begin light quadriceps and hamstring strength exercises while wearing hinged brace
  • Hinged brace worn during all weightbearing activities; knee motion restricted to between 20 and 90 degrees
  • NWB ROM exercises
  • Light strength and aerobic exercises
Weeks 3-5
  • Gradual progression of strength exercises
  • Light aerobic exercise while wearing hinged brace
  • Gradual progression to sport-specific exercises and return to sport
  • Use hinged brace that permits full ROM
  • Progress to full weightbearing
  • Gradual progression of ROM exercises
  • Gradual progression of strength and aerobic exercises
  • Balance training
Weeks 6-10
  • N/A
  • Continue strength exercises
  • Progress to light jogging; gradually introduce cutting movements
  • Balance training
Weeks 11-16
  • N/A
  • Progressive plyometric training
  • Gradual progression to sport-specific exercises and return to sport
N/A: not applicable; NWB: non-weightbearing; ROM: range of motion.
