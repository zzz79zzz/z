Confirmation of the diagnosis of anaphylaxis
COPD: chronic obstructive pulmonary disease.
* Common entities in the differential diagnosis include acute hives, acute asthma, anxiety/panic attack, syncope/faint, and choking on/aspiration of a foreign body. There are many less common entities, including histamine syndromes, restaurant syndromes, flush syndromes, and other forms of shock.
