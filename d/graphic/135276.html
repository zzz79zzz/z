Sample approach to third trimester management of vasa previa in singleton pregnancies
In pregnancies with vasa previa, the goal is to deliver the fetus before rupture of membranes or onset of labor while minimizing the sequelae accruing from iatrogenic preterm birth. This decision is based on patient-specific factors and clinical judgment.
NST: nonstress test.
