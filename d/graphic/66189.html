Posterior tibiofibular ligament and inferior transverse ligament
Posterior view of the key ligamentous attachments between the distal tibia and fibula. The posterior tibiofibular ligament (PTiFL) and the inferior transverse ligament (ITL) attach the posterolateral tibila tubercle to the posterior aspect of the fibula.
