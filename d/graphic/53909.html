Benign nephrosclerosis
Light micrograph in benign nephrosclerosis showing a completely sclerotic glomerulus (upper left) adjacent to 2 shrunken glomeruli that are still intact. There is also prominent tubular atrophy and dilatation with intratubular hyaline casts (arrows). These changes are induced by ischemia resulting from arterial and arteriolar thickening (not shown).
