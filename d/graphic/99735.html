FDG PET/CT pulmonary neuroendocrine tumor
Images of the chest from CT (A) and fusion PET/CT (B) show a nodule in lung apex measuring 12 mm and with an SUV of 1.7 (arrow). CT-guided needle biopsy (C) revealed neuroendocrine tumor from a gastric primary malignancy.
FDG: fluorodeoxyglucose (18F); PET: positron emission tomography; CT: computed tomography; SUV: standardized uptake value.
