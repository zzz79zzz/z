AL amyloidosis affecting facial skin and eyelids
In this patient with AL amyloidosis, amyloid deposits are seen below the nose, around the lips, and on the eyelids. These lesions are waxy and raised, and they show characteristic hemorrhage. This patient also had severe amyloid arthritis and eventually developed a malignant plasma cell dyscrasia.
