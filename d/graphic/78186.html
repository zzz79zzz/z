Computed tomography (CT) scan demonstrates a soft tissue mass containing coarse central calcifications (arrowhead) in the right lower quadrant. This neuroendocrine tumor is producing a characteristic desmoplastic response with spiculation of the adjacent mesenteric fat (arrow).
