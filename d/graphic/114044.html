Arm swing during running gait
Arm swing provides counterbalance to the lower extremities during running gait. Ideally, this swing is symmetric and well synchronized. The runner in this video clip demonstrates mild asymmetries, which can be appreciated by comparing the excursion of each arm relative to the white lines drawn on the image.
