Ultrasonography left hip effusion
The anechoic fluid collection at the lateral aspect of the left hip adjacent to the femoral neck is a joint effusion (Panel A). The effusion causes lateral bowing of the gluteus minimus muscle, which can sometimes be seen on plain film as displacement of the gluteal fat pad. Ultrasound of the right hip shows no such collection (Panel B).
