Heliotrope eruption in dermatomyositis
A reddish-purple eruption on the upper eyelid (the heliotrope eruption), accompanied by swelling of the eyelid in a patient with dermatomyositis (DM). This is the most specific cutaneous eruption in DM, although it is only present in a minority of patients.
