Computed tomography type 1 congenital pulmonary airway malformation (CPAM)*
Computed tomography of the chest of a one-year-old patient with type 1 congenital pulmonary airway malformation (CPAM). A single, large (5 x 5 x 4 cm), air-filled cyst is demonstrated in the right upper lobe.
* CPAM was previously known as congenital cystic adenomatoid malformation (CCAM).
