Infraclavicular approach to the subclavian vein
The midpoint approach to cannulation of the subclavian vein is most common. Insert the needle 2 to 3 cm inferior to the midpoint of the clavicle. Advance the needle aiming just deep to the suprasternal notch, keeping the needle parallel to the ground.
