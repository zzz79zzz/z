Aneurysm and pseudoaneurysm of the left ventricular apex
The apical 4-chamber view in panel A shows an aneurysm (AN) of the LV apex; the LV does not narrow at the opening to the aneurysm. Panel B shows a pseudoaneurysm (PAN) identified by its connection to the LV across a narrow neck (n).
AN: aneurysm; LV: left ventricle; LA: left atrium; PAN: pseudoaneurysm; n: neck.
