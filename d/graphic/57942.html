Schematic depiction of the innervation of the kidney
Sympathetic, parasympathetic, and sensory enervation supplying the kidney. The solid line represents the sympathetic component. The dotted line reflects the parasympathetic nerve supply. Afferent (sensory) fibers are represented by the dashed line and travel with the autonomic nerves.
