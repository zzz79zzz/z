Initiation and adjustment of insulin regimens in type 2 diabetes mellitus
Insulin regimens should be designed taking lifestyle and meal schedule into account. The algorithm can only provide basic guidelines for initiation and adjustment of insulin. Many different approaches are possible.

A1C: glycated hemoglobin.

¶ The A1C goal should be individualized in accordance with patient age, comorbidities, and life expectancy.
