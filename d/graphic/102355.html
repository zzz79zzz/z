Approach to initial management of locoregional paragangliomas
Prior to initiating treatment, patients with head and neck paragangliomas should undergo testing for hypersecretion of catecholamines, appropriate radiographic imaging, and a complete examination of the head and neck region as well as swallowing and voice evaluation to assess for lower cranial nerve function. Refer to UpToDate content on treatment of paragangliomas.
