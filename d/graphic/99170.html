US short axis view of subscapularis tendon
Short-axis view of the subscapularis tendon (outlined by the black line) over its insertion on the lesser tuberosity of the humerus (Lesser Tuberosity). Note the heterogeneous appearance of the tendon due to the multiple hyperechoic tendon slips (arrows) interspersed with hypoechoic muscle.
Deltoid: deltoid muscle.
Left: cephalad; Right: caudad; Bottom of screen: deep; Top of screen: superficial.
