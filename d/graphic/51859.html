Echogenic fetal intracardiac focus
The arrow is pointing to two adjacent echogenic intracardiac foci. The sonographic criterion for an echogenic intracardiac focus is brightness equivalent to that of bone. Echogenic intracardiac foci usually occur as a single focus in the left ventricle, but multiple foci, biventricular foci, or a right ventricular location can also occur.
