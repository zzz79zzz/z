Cardiovascular magnetic resonance imaging of isolated right ventricular (RV) infarction
(A) Steady-state free precession cine images in the four-chamber orientation, demonstrating focal basal RV free wall akinesis consistent with isolated RV infarct.
(B) Inversion-recovery images in the four-chamber orientation, demonstrating focal transmural late gadolinium enhancement of the basal RV free wall consistent with isolated RV infarct.
