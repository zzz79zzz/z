Fumarate hydratase-deficient leiomyoma

Fumarate hydratase-deficient leiomyoma
(A) Leiomyomas with fumarate hydratase deficiency have distinctive eosinophilic cytoplasmic inclusions (arrows) and nuclear features including prominent nucleoli with perinucleolar clearing, nuclear enlargement, and binucleation (thick arrow).
(B) Hemangiopericytic vasculature is also a common finding.
