Incidence of stroke in individuals with sickle cell disease according to patient age
The graph illustrates the rates of ischemic and hemorrhagic stroke in a cohort of 255 hospitalized patients with sickle cell disease and stroke. Refer to UpToDate content on stroke in sickle cell disease for further details.
SCD: Sickle cell disease.
From: Strouse JJ, Jordan LC, Lanzkron S, et al. The excess burden of stroke in hospitalized adults with sickle cell disease.
