Reactive skin decontamination lotion (RSDL)
RSDL lotion is packaged on a foam applicator inside a single use pouch. The lotion is applied within three minutes of contamination, and the non-toxic residue is washed away at a later time. Application of RSDL to wounds or the eyes is considered off label use.
