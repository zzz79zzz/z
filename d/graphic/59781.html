Echocardiography of hypoplastic left heart syndrome
Apical view of the heart in an infant with hypoplastic left heart syndrome. The RV is enlarged with a smaller, hypoplastic LV. There is a large atrial septal defect (arrow) connecting the RA and LA.
LA: left atrium; RA: right atrium; TV: tricuspid valve; LV: left ventricle; RV: right ventricle.
