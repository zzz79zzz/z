Criteria for the Kidney Disease Improving Global Outcomes (KDIGO) acute kidney injury for children
Stage Serum creatinine (SCr) Urine output
1 Increase to 1.5 to 1.9 times baseline, OR increase of ≥0.3 mg/dL (≥26.5 mcmol/L) <0.5 mL/kg per hour for 6 to 12 hours
2 Increase to 2 to 2.9 times baseline <0.5 mL/kg per hour for ≥12 hours
3

Increase greater than 3 times baseline, OR

SCr ≥4 mg/dL (≥353.6 mcmol/L), OR

Initiation of renal replacement therapy, OR

eGFR <35 mL/min per 1.73 m2 (<18 years)

<0.3 mL/kg per hour for ≥24 hours, OR

Anuria for ≥12 hours
The time frames for the increases in serum creatinine are:
  • Increase of SCr ≥0.3 mg/dL (≥26.5 mcmol/L) within 48 hours
  • Increase in SCr >1.5 times the baseline within the prior seven days
eGFR: estimated glomerular filtration rate.
