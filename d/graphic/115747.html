Emergency cesarean delivery is indicated for women with placenta previa and active labor, severe and persistent vaginal bleeding such that the maternal hemodynamic stability cannot be achieved or maintained, or vaginal bleeding after 34 weeks of gestation.
* The diagnosis of placenta previa is based on identification of placental tissue extending over the internal cervical os. When the placental edge is <20 mm from, but not over, the internal os, the placenta is labeled "low lying."
