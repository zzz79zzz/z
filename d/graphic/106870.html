US lateral hip gluteal tendon insertion on greater trochanter in transverse view
This ultrasound image shows the gluteal tendons at their insertion onto the greater trochanter in the transverse plane (short-axis view).
*: apex of greater trochanter; 1: anterior facet; 2: lateral facet; arrows: gluteus minimus tendon; arrowheads: gluteus medius tendon; +: iliotibial band; open arrowheads: musculotendinous transition of gluteus maximus muscle; a: gluteus medius muscle.
Left side of image: posterior; right side of image: anterior.
