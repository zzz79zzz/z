Spontaneous echo contrast seen in the left atrium on transesophageal echocardiogram
Transesophageal echocardiography showing spontaneous echo contrast (or "smoke") in an enlarged left atrium (LA). This finding is a marker for stasis and is associated with a higher risk of thrombus formation and thromboembolism.
