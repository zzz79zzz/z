Histopathology of critical illness myopathy
A myosin ATPase (pH 9.4)-reacted frozen muscle section reveals atrophic, type 2 (dark) fibers, patchy regions of reduced reactivity (arrows) in some type 1 (light) and type 2 fibers, and absent staining (asterisks) in other fibers. Normal muscle is seen in the inset.
ATPase: adenosine triphosphatase.
