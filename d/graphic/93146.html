Ovarian cancer with hypercoagulable state and complications on CT scan
(A) A CT scan through the pelvis. The image shows a thrombus in the left femoral vein (arrow).
(B) A CT scan of the posterior fossa of the same patient shows a wedge-shaped infarction in the right cerebellar hemisphere (arrow) likely as a result of the hypercoagulable state.
(C) The image shows bilateral pulmonary emboli (arrows) in a second patient.
(D) An image of the same patient shows a wedge-shaped pulmonary infarction in the lateral basal segment of the right lower lobe (arrow).
CT: computed tomography.
