Diagnosing patients who present with altered voluntary motor or sensory function*

* Diagnosing functional neurological symptom disorder rests upon identifying the typical, positive clinical findings that establish the diagnosis. Full details of diagnosing functional neurological symptom disorder are discussed in the diagnosis topic.

¶ Among patients with functional neurological symptom disorder, comorbidities can include psychiatric disorders, such as illness anxiety disorder, somatic symptom disorder, and anxiety and depressive disorders.
