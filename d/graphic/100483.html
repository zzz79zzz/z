Diagnostic evaluation of nonimmune hydrops fetalis[1-3]
This algorithm assumes that immune causes of hydrops have already been excluded by negative results on red blood cell antibody screening. Refer to UpToDate content for information on the evaluation and treatment of specific etiologies of hydrops.

CMV: cytomegalovirus; IgG: immunoglobulin G; IgM: immunoglobulin M; MCA: middle cerebral artery; MCV: mean corpuscular volume; MoM: multiples of the median; PSV: peak systolic velocity; RBC: red blood cell.

* MCA Doppler should be performed when cardiac changes, such as cardiac hypertrophy, are observed that might be a result of anemia.
