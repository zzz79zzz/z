Wilson disease

Liver biopsy from a 4-year-old female patient whose 10-year-old sister presented with liver failure and cirrhosis secondary to previously unrecognized Wilson disease. Liver biopsy was performed because serum ceruloplasmin was low (4.0 mg/dL) and serum aminotransferases were repeatedly two to three times normal.

(A) Low power shows portal fibrosis, mild portal inflammation, and fatty infiltration (Masson trichrome).

(B) High power view shows fatty infiltration of hepatocytes and two glycogenated nuclei (Masson trichrome).
