Transpedicle approach to CT-guided needle biopsy of bone
CT scan image with bone windows, demonstrating a transpedicle approach to CT-guided needle biopsy of a bone tumor involving the spine. This technique allows access to approximately two-thirds of the vertebral body, avoids the spinal nerve roots, and is relatively painless.
