Nomogram of hour-specific serum or plasma total bilirubin (TB) concentration in healthy term and near-term newborns
Risk zones are designated according to percentile: high (TB ≥95th), high intermediate (95th >TB ≥75th), low intermediate (75th >TB ≥40th), and low (TB <40th). Infants with values in the high risk zone are at increased risk for the development of clinically significant hyperbilirubinemia requiring intervention.
