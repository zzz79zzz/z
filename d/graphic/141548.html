Hematoxylin and eosin (H&E) stain. Resected lesion from a patient with chronic cavitary pulmonary aspergillosis. The interface between the fungal ball (arrow) and the cavity wall (dashed arrow). The cavity wall is chronically inflamed, and the epithelium is denuded.
