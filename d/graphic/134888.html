Three-dimensional spectral CT of the chest in COVID-19 pneumonia
This graphic shows three-dimensional spectral computed tomography of the chest in COVID-19 pneumonia with dual- energy and iodine map display of the lungs. Frontal and lateral rotating maximal intensity projection shows the normally perfused lung (in orange) and the bilateral subpleural foci of pneumonia (in light blue), indicating decreased perfusion of the involved lung parenchyma.
CT: computed tomography.
