Anatomical high-risk regions of the face
The dark pink-shaded regions of the face delineate the so-called H zone. Tissue-sparing surgery techniques, such as Mohs micrographic surgery and staged excision, are indicated for removal of skin tumors in this area. The light pink-shaded area, entire scalp, and neck are called the M area.
