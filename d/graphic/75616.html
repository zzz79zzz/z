Hepatic toxocariasis
A 43-year-old man with hepatic toxocariasis. Contrast-enhanced arterial (A), portal phases (B), and equilibrium phase (C). Computed tomography images display two small triangular and oval nodules in the periphery of the liver. The nodules are clearly seen on portal venous phase image but not clearly seen on arterial and equilibrium phase images.
