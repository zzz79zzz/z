Fetal echocardiography of tricuspid valve atresia with a ventricular septal defect
Fetal echocardiography showing a typical 4-chamber view in a fetus with TV atresia, demonstrating muscular atresia with a hypoplastic RV and a VSD. The interarterial communication is through the patent foramen ovale (*).
TV: tricuspid valve; RV: right ventricle; LV: left ventricle; VSD: ventricular septal defect.
