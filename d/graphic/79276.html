Poorly differentiated thymic carcinoma
Low power photomicrograph shows cystic changes within a thymic carcinoma. The cysts are filled with pale eosinophilic secretions and are reminiscent of an acquired multilocular thymic cyst. (Hematoxylin and eosin, magnification 2.5x).
