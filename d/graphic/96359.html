Dermoscopic features of hemangioma
(A) Hemangioma of the lip in an 80-year-old man presenting as a blue-colored papule.
(B) On dermoscopy, the lesion shows patterns of clods and lines with bluish, red, and whitish colors, corresponding to well-demarcated, blue-red lacunae separated by white septa.
