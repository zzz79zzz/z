Anterior-posterior (A) and oblique (B) views of the right ankle. Salter Harris type I fracture of the distal fibula. Notice the lateral malleolar soft tissue swelling (arrowheads) with the widening of the fibular physis (arrow) and a slight medial displacement of the epiphysis (dashed arrow).
