Solitary plasmacytoma of clavicle on radiograph and MRI
A radiograph of the left clavicle in A-P projection shows a bubbly lesion within an expanded distal clavicle (A, arrow). Image B is a T2-weighted MRI showing a hyperintense lesion (arrow).
A-P: anteroposterior; MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
