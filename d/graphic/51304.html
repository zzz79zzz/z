Lumbosacral spinal lipoma
Sagittal magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) study showing a lumbosacral spinal lipoma (asterisk) on fluid attenuated inversion recovery (FLAIR) (A) and T2 (B) sequences. The latter view shows that the lipoma extends into the subcutaneous fat (arrowhead), and hence is palpable on physical exam in this patient.
