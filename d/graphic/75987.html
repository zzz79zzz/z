Episodic LH secretion during the follicular phase
Patterns of episodic LH secretion during the EFP, MFP, and LFP of the menstrual cycle. Day 0 is the day of the midcycle LH surge. There is a unique suppression of LH secretion during sleep in the EFP.
EFP: early follicular phase; LFP: late follicular phase; LH: luteinizing hormone; MFP: middle follicular phase.
Data from: Filicori M, Santoro N, Merriam GR, Crowley WF Jr. Characterization of the physiological pattern of episodic gonadotropin secretion throughout the human menstrual cycle. J Clin Endocrinol Metab 1986; 62:1136.
