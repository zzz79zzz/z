Neonatal femur fracture
Panel A) This infant had a birth-related left femoral fracture. Panel B) An anteroposterior splint was used but ended at the fracture site, only increasing the angulation. Panel C) A Pavlik harness reduced the fracture by flexing the distal fragment.
