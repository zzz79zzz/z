Locking the magnetic sphincter device by aligning the two black lines into one
The sutures attached to the terminal beads are grasped in sequence by a grasper to stabilize the unit; simultaneously, another grasper is used to squeeze the two terminal beads together and lock the device into place. This should result in alignment of the two black lines (A) into a single black bar (B).
