Endovaginal ultrasound image of levator ani muscle detachment​
(A) 3D endovaginal ultrasound image of right mid-sagittal view of right LAM detachment. The arrows show the gap between the PS and torn ends of LAM.
(B) 3D endovaginal ultrasound image of the same woman viewed axially and demonstrating bilateral LAM detachment. The arrows show the gap between the PS and torn ends of LAM.
3D: three-dimensional; A: anterior; PS: pubic symphysis; B: bladder; T: transducer; AR: anorectum; LAM: levator ani muscle; R: right; L: left; P: posterior.
