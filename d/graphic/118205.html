Retrohepatic IVC injury
For retrohepatic IVC injury, balloon occlusion may permit repair by providing temporary hemostasis in this challenging anatomic location. Patch repair is ideal for this IVC injury since primary repair of a large IVC tear is likely to narrow the lumen, which increases the risk for thrombosis.
IVC: inferior vena cava.
