Balance error scoring system
Clinical test battery:
  • Perform a total of six 20 second trials using three different stances (double, single, tandem), each one used with two different surfaces (firm, foam)
Recorded errors:
  • Hands lifted off iliac crests
  • Opening eyes
  • Step, stumble, or fall
  • Moving into >30 degrees hip flexion or abduction
  • Remaining out of testing position for >5 seconds
