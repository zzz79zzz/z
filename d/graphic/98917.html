Avulsion fracture of the fifth metatarsal close-up view
An avulsion fracture of the proximal fifth metatarsal (arrow) is a relatively common cause of acute lateral foot pain following injury. Other fractures of the proximal fifth metatarsal are prone to complications so clinicians must be certain of the diagnosis.
