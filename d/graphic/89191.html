Contemporary estimates of labor duration by dilation at admission
The 95th percentiles of cumulative duration of labor from admission among singleton term nulliparous women with spontaneous onset of labor, vaginal delivery, and normal neonatal outcomes. Colors represent cervical dilation when women were admitted to the labor unit: green (5 cm), yellow (4 cm), blue (3 cm), red (2 cm).
Data from: Zhang J, Landy HJ, Branch DW, et al. Contemporary patterns of spontaneous labor with normal neonatal outcomes. Obstet Gynecol 2010; 116:1281.
