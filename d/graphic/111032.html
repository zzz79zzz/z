Survival of cholangiocarcinoma of the distal bile duct based on AJCC 8th edition tumor (T) categories
Survival based on T stage category. Results from 147 United States patients who underwent resection of distal bile duct carcinoma confirm earlier study results from 222 Korean patients regarding the use of depth of tumor invasion to predict prognosis and stratify T category.
