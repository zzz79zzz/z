Open basket weave ankle taping
Patients in need of additional ankle support, including athletes returning to sport following an ankle sprain, may benefit from taping, as described here:
(A) Apply proximal and distal anchors.
(B) Apply a stirrup strip.
(C) Apply a horseshoe strip.
(D) Continue to alternate stirrups and horseshoes, leaving an opening on the anterior aspect, and close the plantar foot with strips of tape.
