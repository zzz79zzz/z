Calcaneus anterior process fracture on sagittal CT
This sagittal CT image shows a large avulsion fracture of the anterior process of the calcaneus (arrow). As demonstrated here, CT is superior to plain radiograph for determining the size, displacement, and extent of such fractures.
CT: computed tomography.
