US FDL overlying FHL (Knot of Henry)
This ultrasound image shows a short axis view of the Knot of Henry where the flexor digitorum longus tendon crosses over and becomes superficial to the flexor hallucis longus tendon.
Arrowheads: flexor digitorum longus tendon; *: flexor hallucis longus tendon; dotted ellipse: medial plantar nerve.
