Ultrasound of the third extensor compartment of the wrist
This ultrasound image shows the third extensor compartment of the wrist. The image was obtained with the ultrasound probe placed at the site of the red mark in the photograph.
ECRB: extensor carpi radialis brevis tendon; LT: Listers tubercle; EPL: extensor pollicis longus tendon; IV: fourth extensor compartment.
