Nasal CPAP interfaces
Standard nasal interfaces used for noninvasive positive pressure ventilatory assistance/support with passive ventilator circuits for nasal/oronasal bilevel support or with the portals sealed by tape to use with active circuits if nonvented nasal interfaces are not readily available. Nasal prongs and nasal pillows are also options.
CPAP: continuous positive airway pressure.
