Recurrence rate of insulinoma
Recurrence rate of insulinoma after initial curative surgery in 196 patients in the Mayo Clinic cohort (1927 to 1986). The likelihood of recurrence was greater in those patients with MEN1 (21 versus 7% at 20 years).
MEN1: multiple endocrine neoplasia type 1.
