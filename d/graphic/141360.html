Global distribution of the Duffy-null phenotype

(A) Heat map showing the global prevalence of the Duffy-null phenotype, characterized by lack of Duffy antigens on RBCs, also called Fy(a–b–).

(B) Corresponding uncertainty map (interval between the 25% and 75% quartiles of the posterior distribution [IQR]).
RBCs: red blood cells; IQR: interquartile range.
