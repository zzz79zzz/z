Cross-section of Lisfranc joint
The so-called “Lisfranc ligament” consists of three distinct ligaments: the dorsal ligament, interosseus ligament, and plantar ligament. The image above shows these ligaments in a coronal cross-section view.
Modified from: Rettedal DD, Graves NC, Marshall JJ, et al. Reliability of ultrasound imaging in the assessment of the dorsal Lisfranc ligament. J Foot Ankle Res 2013; 6:7.
