Diagram of functional reach test to assess balance in elderly persons
Subject stands with fist extended alongside a wall. Subject leans forward as far as possible, moving fist along wall without taking a step or losing stability. Length of fist movement is measured. Distances of less than 6 in (15 cm) indicate an increased risk of falling.
