Body mass index, or "BMI," is a measure of weight in relation to height. Doctors use this calculation to help understand a person's health risks. Based on the number, a person falls into 1 of 4 categories: underweight, healthy weight, overweight, or obesity.

To calculate your BMI, find your height (in inches) on the left. Then, look at that row to find your weight (in pounds). The number at the top of that column is your BMI.
