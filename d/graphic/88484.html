Acute pneumonitis on chest radiography and computerized tomography imaging in systemic lupus erythematosus
The chest x-ray performed on this 41-year-old female shows a diffuse interstitial pattern, and the CT scan through the chest shows multicentric ground glass changes centered around the bronchovascular bundle compatible with acute lupus pneumonitis.
