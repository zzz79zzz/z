Erdheim-Chester disease lesion biopsy
The figure demonstrates a representative hematoxylin/eosin-stained ECD lesion biopsy sample from a retroperitoneal, perinephric infiltrate (400x magnification). It shows the bland histiocytic infiltrate with foamy (xanthomatous) cytoplasm and interspersed inflammatory cells.
