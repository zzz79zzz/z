Simple scheme of categorizing pediatric epilepsy by using interictal electroencephalogram (EEG)
Genetics, clinical outcome, and epilepsy syndrome examples of each EEG pattern group.
AD: autosomal dominant; GTC: generalized tonic-clonic seizure; EFMR: epilepsy and mental retardation restricted to females; EME: early myoclonic encephalopathy; EIEE: early infantile epileptic encephalopathy.
From: Kim SH, Korff CM, Kim AJ, Nordli DR Jr. A practical, simple, and useful method of categorizing interictal EEG features in children. Neurology 2015; 85:471. DOI: 10.1212/WNL.0000000000001805.
