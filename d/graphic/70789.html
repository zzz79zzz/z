Lunotriquetral shear test for instability of the lunotriquetral ligament
The lunotriquetral shear test is performed by stabilizing the lunate and moving the pisotriquetral mass back and forth past the lunate in the anterior and posterior direction. A positive test is increased movement and pain compared to the other side.
