MAZE III procedure
Modification of the posterior incisions to the vena cava and placement of the septal incision posterior to the orifice of the superior vena cava (SVC) are noted.
CS: coronary sinus; FO: foramen ovale; IVC: inferior vena cava; LAA: left atrial appendage; MV: mitral valve; RAA: right atrial appendage; SAN: sinoatrial node; TV: tricuspid valve.
