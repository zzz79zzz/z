US doppler femoral nerve artery vein in transverse oblique view
The images above are transverse oblique plane ultrasound scans of the femoral nerve, artery, and vein in short-axis. Both grey-scale (left side image) and color Doppler (right side image) are shown. Note the diminished resolution with color Doppler imaging.
Left image: greyscale image; right image: color Doppler image; A: femoral artery; V: femoral vein; red: femoral artery; blue: femoral vein; dotted circle: femoral nerve; arrows: iliopsoas tendon; IP: iliopectineal eminence.
Left side of image: lateral; right side of image: medial.
