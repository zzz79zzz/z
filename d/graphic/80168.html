Reported cases of Lyme disease – United States, 2019
One dot placed randomly within county of residence for each confirmed case. Each dot represents one case of Lyme disease and is placed randomly in the patient's county of residence. The presence of a dot in a state does not necessarily mean that Lyme disease was acquired in that state.
Reproduced from: Lyme Disease: Lyme Disease Maps: Most Recent Year. United States Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. Available at: https://www.cdc.gov/lyme/datasurveillance/maps-recent.html (Accessed July 20, 2021).
