  • Marfan syndrome

  • Cirrhosis
  • Alcohol
  • Heparin
  • Glucocorticoids
  • Thyroxine suppressive therapy
  • Antiseizure medications
  • Gonadotropin-releasing hormone analogs
  • Cyclosporine
  • Chemotherapy
  • HIV medications (eg, tenofovir)

  • Tobacco
