Cryptogenic organizing pneumonitis
Sixty-two-year-old man with a one-month history of dyspnea with exertion, fatigue, and weight loss due to cryptogenic organizing pneumonitis. Posteroanterior roentgenogram reveals bilateral patchy alveolar opacities. There was complete resolution following glucocorticoid therapy. Unfortunately, the disease recurred several times following withdrawal of glucocorticoid therapy.
