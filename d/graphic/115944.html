Sacroiliac joint fusion with hyperostosis and osteitis in SAPHO syndrome
Axial MDCT (A) and T1W MRI (B) images of the sacroiliac joints showing unilateral right sacroiliac joint fusion with extensive hyperostosis and osteitis of the ilium typical of SAPHO.
SAPHO: synovitis, acne, pustulosis, hyperostosis, and osteitis; MDCT: multidetector computed tomography; T1W: T1-weighted; MRI: magnetic resonance imaging; SAPHO: synovitis, acne, pustulosis, hyperostosis, osteitis.
