Respiratory bronchiolitis-associated interstitial lung disease
CT scan (5 mm thick section) in a 35-year-old woman with heavy smoking history and progressive dyspnea with exertion shows extensive ground-glass opacities. The plain chest film was normal. The diagnosis was confirmed by open lung biopsy. Symptoms improved following smoking cessation.
CT: computed tomography.
