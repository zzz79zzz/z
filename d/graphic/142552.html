Salivary gland ductal carcinoma
Salivary gland ductal carcinoma is a tumor characterized by high grade cytology, abundant eosinophilic cytoplasm, and a nested growth pattern. Comedonecrosis, brisk mitotic activity, and abundant desmoplastic stroma are typical findings.
