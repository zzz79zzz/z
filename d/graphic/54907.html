Prenatal ultrasound images of physiologic midgut herniation
Three-dimensional ultrasound of a fetus at 11 weeks and 4 days of gestation with PMH. Arrows point to the PMH.
(A) Sagittal view of fetus with PMH.
(B) Transverse section at the level of the PMH.
(C) Coronal section.
(D) Three-dimensional rendering demonstrating the PMH.
Follow-up ultrasound in 1 week showed the bowel back in the abdomen, confirming the diagnosis of PMH.
PMH: physiologic midgut herniation.
