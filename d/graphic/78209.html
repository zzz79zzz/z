Orthotopic neobladder (also called Studer neobladder or pouch)
This figure depicts one option for people who have a cystectomy (removal of bladder) due to bladder cancer. A new bladder may be created from a segment of bowel. The new bladder is connected to the urethra, allowing the person to urinate normally. This is called an orthotopic neobladder (commonly referred to as a Studer neobladder or pouch).
