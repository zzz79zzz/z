Klebsiella pneumoniae liver abscess
Computed tomography (CT) of a primary liver abscess due to Klebsiella pneumoniae. The multiloculated appearance can make radiographic distinction from a hepatic tumor difficult. In such cases, diffusion-weighted magnetic resonance imaging may be helpful to better differentiate between abscess and tumor.
