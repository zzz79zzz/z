Age-specific kidney stone incidence rates
Age-specific incidence rates for the first kidney stone event were calculated using data from 3 large cohort studies: Health Professionals Follow-Up Study (HPFS)[1], Nurses' Health Study I (NHS I)[2], and Nurses' Health Study II (NHS II)[3]. A total of 8262 cases were confirmed among over 250,000 participants followed for up to 30 years.
References:
  1. Health Professionals Follow-Up Study (HPFS). Available at: https://sites.sph.harvard.edu/hpfs/ (Accessed on October 13, 2020).
  2. Nurses' Health Study I (NHS I). Available at: https://www.nurseshealthstudy.org/ (Accessed on October 13, 2020).
  3. Nurses' Health Study II (NHS II). Available at: https://www.nurseshealthstudy.org/ (Accessed on October 13, 2020).
