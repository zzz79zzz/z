Type I endoleak after EVAR on CT scan
A CT scan through the mid-abdomen (panel A) shows a large endovascular leak (arrowhead) originating from the posterior aspect of the endograft. Panel B is a coronal reconstruction of the CT scan and shows the leak originating between the covered stent (arrowhead) and fenestrated stent at the region of overlap (arrow).
EVAR: endovascular aneurysm repair; CT: computed tomography.
