Mirizzi syndrome with compression of the common hepatic duct by an impacted stone
Schematic representation of Mirizzi syndrome.
(Panel A) An impacted stone in the cystic duct or Hartmann's pouch of the gallbladder obstructs the common hepatic duct both by extrinsic compression and associated inflammation.
(Panel B) Treatment consists of cholecystectomy.
