Deep veins of the lower extremity
Deep veins of the right lower extremity. The paired tibial veins (anterior tibial, peroneal, and posterior tibial) are shown with their adjacent arteries. The bridging veins between the paired veins are also demonstrated. The popliteal and femoral veins are also sometimes duplicated (omitted from the diagram) with one of the duplicated segments frequently larger in caliber than the other.
