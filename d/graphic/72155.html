Identification of poison ivy, oak, and sumac
Characteristic features useful for identifying poison ivy, poison oak, and poison sumac. Poison ivy and poison oak are often identified by 3 leaflets with flowering branches arising from axillary positions on a single stem. Poison sumac more often forms leaflets of 5, 7, or more that angle upward toward the top of the stem.
