Examination for malrotation of metacarpals
The MCP and PIP joints are flexed to 90 degrees in this photograph of two normal hands. Axial lines drawn through the middle and distal phalanges of the second through fifth digits are nearly parallel and converge near the wrist.
MCP: metacarpophalangeal; PIP: proximal interphalangeal.
