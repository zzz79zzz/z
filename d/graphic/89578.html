Radial nerve
Important compartment syndrome findings:
Pain is the most important finding. Any pain with passive stretch is worrisome.
Palpate for tender and tense compartments
Late findings can include motor deficits involving the muscles of the involved compartment and sensory deficits involving the nerves in the involved compartment
