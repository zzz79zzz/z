Rates of reported cases of gonorrhea by state and territory, United States, 2020
In 2020, rates of reported gonorrhea ranged by state from 22 cases per 100,000 population in Vermont to 463 cases per 100,000 population in Mississippi. The rate of reported gonorrhea in the District of Columbia was 550 per 100,000 population.
VT: Vermont; NH: New Hampshire; MA: Massachusetts; RI: Rhode Island; CT: Connecticut; NJ: New Jersey; DE: Delaware; MD: Maryland; DC: Washington, DC.
