Magnetic resonance cholangiopancreatography in a patient with primary sclerosing cholangitis
A thick slab coronal magnetic resonance image of the liver in a patient with primary sclerosing cholangitis. The image sequence shows all bile ducts with a bright white signal. Note the beaded duct in the left lobe (arrow) as well as the many dilated intrahepatic ducts.
GB: gallbladder.
