Acquired cystic disease of the kidney on ultrasound and MRI
Longitudinal ultrasound of the left kidney (A) demonstrates a relatively small (9.2 cm) kidney with echogenic cortex and numerous round, anechoic lesions consistent with cysts. Coronal T2-weighted MRI of the same patient (B) showing numerous bilateral bright, round lesions consistent with cysts.
Long: longitudinal view; lt: left; MRI: magnetic resonance imaging.
