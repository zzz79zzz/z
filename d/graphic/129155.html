Example of START* adult triage pathway for mass casualty events
START: Simple Triage and Rapid Treatment.
* START includes rapidly assessing (in less than 1 minute) every patient, determining which of 4 clinical categories the patient belongs, and visibly identifying the category for rescuers who will treat the patient.
Reference:
  1. Benson M, Koenig KL, Schultz CH. Disaster triage: START, then SAVE-a new method of dynamic triage for victims of a catastrophic earthquake. Prehospital Disaster Med 1996; 11:117.
Reproduced from: START Adult Triage Algorithm. US Department of Health & Human Services. Available at: https://chemm.hhs.gov/startadult.htm (Accessed on December 30, 2021).
