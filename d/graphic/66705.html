Peripheral blood smears from two patients with sideroblastic anemia.
(A) Mild degree of changes, consisting mainly of a small number of hypochromic red cells.
(B) Severe changes, including hypochromia, microcytosis, and many misshapen red cells. (Wright's stain.)
