Distribution of major RVVs among mesocarnivores in the United States and Puerto Rico. The areas indicated by black diagonal stripes represent the distribution of Arizona gray fox RVV. The solid-colored areas represent RVV distributions for 2014 through 2018; dashed borders represent the previous 5-year distributions for 2013 through 2017.
RVV: rabies virus variant.
