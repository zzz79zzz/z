US fibularis tendons transverse view above malleolus
This ultrasound image shows the fibular (or peroneal) tendons in a transverse view (short-axis) above the lateral malleolus.
Fl: fibularis longus tendon; Fbt: fibularis brevis tendon; Fbm: fibularis brevis muscle. Left side of image: posterior; Right side of image: anterior.
