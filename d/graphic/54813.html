Cervical esophagostomy
This figure depicts the suturing of the proximal portion of the cervical esophagus to the anterior chest wall. This procedure can be performed as a temporizing procedure for an esophageal perforation when a primary repair cannot be performed due to severe contamination of the mediastinum, large friable defect, or unstable condition of the patient.
