Rapidly involuting congenital hemangiomas
This healthy 13-month-old boy had a firm bluish-purple plaque on his left leg at birth. The lesion flattened and lightened in color within six months and continued to regress without therapy, leaving a slightly atrophic 5 cm pale purple plaque with overlying telangiectasias.
