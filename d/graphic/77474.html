Meningococcal disease incidence by year – United States, 1970 to 2019
The figure shows incidence rates (per 100,000 persons) of meningococcal disease in the United States by year from 1970 to 2019. The incidence rate began declining in 1995 and has remained low in 2019.
Reproduced from: Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. Meningococcal Disease: Surveillance. Available at: https://www.cdc.gov/meningococcal/surveillance/index.html. (Accessed on November 3, 2021.)
