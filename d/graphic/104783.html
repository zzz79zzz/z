Systolic murmur and fixed split S2 in a child with an atrial septal defect
Phonocardiogram recorded at the left upper sternal border in a 5-year-old patient with a 12 mm secundum atrial septal defect. There is a fixed split S2 (0.075 seconds). There is a systolic ejection murmur created by the increased right ventricular stroke volume.
S1: first heart sound; A2: aortic valve closure sound; SM: systolic murmur; P2: pulmonic valve closure sound.
