Aspergillus bronchiolitis and bronchopneumonia
High-resolution computed tomography images (1.0 mm collimation) at the level of the right middle lobe bronchus (A) and liver dome (B) show patchy peribronchial consolidation, centrilobular nodules, and branching linear structures (arrows). Cavity formation is seen in both lower lobes.
