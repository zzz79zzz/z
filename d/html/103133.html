Padua score interpretation


0 to 3 points: Lower risk; 0.3% risk of symptomatic VTE*
4 to 20 points: Higher risk; 11% risk of symptomatic VTE*


Notes
  • XRT: x-ray (radiation) therapy; MI: myocardial infarction; VTE: venous thromboembolism.
  • * 90-day risk of symptomatic VTE in patients who did not receive anticoagulation during hospitalization.
