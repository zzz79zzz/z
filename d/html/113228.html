Notes
  • Vasoactive drug dose criteria based on administration for at least 1 hour.
  • The SOFA score helps predict morbidity rather than mortality, though individuals with a score of 15 or more had a mortality rate of 90%. 1
  • Refer to the UpToDate topic review on predictive scoring systems in the intensive care unit for information on interpreting the SOFA score.
  • PaO 2 : arterial partial pressure of oxygen (in mmHg); FiO 2 : fractional inspired percentage of oxygen (as a decimal fraction).
