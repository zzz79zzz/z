Notes
  • The serum anion gap is calculated from the difference between the major measured serum cation (Na) and measured anions (Cl + HCO 3 ).
  • The normal value varies among laboratories and must be known for proper assessment.
  • Clinical use of the anion gap calculation is discussed in appropriate UpToDate topics.


Equations used

Serum anion gap = Na - (Cl + HCO 3 )


Only digits 0 to 9 and a single decimal point (".") are acceptable as numeric inputs. Attempted input of other characters into a numeric field may lead to an incorrect result.
