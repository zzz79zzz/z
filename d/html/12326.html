Warning
  • urn:lims:b498:s4522206 • No This drug may raise the chance of infection, including severe infections. Sometimes severe infections have led to death. Most people who had these infections were taking other drugs to lower the immune system like methotrexate or steroid drugs. If you have any infection, are taking antibiotics now or in the recent past, or have had many infections, talk with your doctor.
  • urn:lims:b498:s7300711 • No TB (tuberculosis) has been seen in patients started on this drug. These patients were exposed to TB in the past, but never got the infection. You may be tested to see if you have been exposed to TB before starting this drug. You will also be watched while you take this drug for signs of TB. Tell your doctor right away if you feel sick or weak, or if you have fever, night sweats, or abnormal weight loss.
