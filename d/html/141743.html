Notes
  • *To screen out unreliable diagnoses and very mild COPD , COPD for this calculator was defined as a diagnosis of COPD with FEV1 <75%, COPD causing functional disability, or COPD previously leading to a hospitalization.
  • This tool has been further validated with similar model performance (C statistic: 0.860 and 0.855, respectively) and excellent predictive performance. [2]
  • COPD : chronic obstructive pulmonary disease; FEV1 : forced expiratory volume in 1 second; ASA : American Society of Anesthesiologists; ENT : ear, nose, and throat.


Equations used

Terms = -2.8977 + (0.0144 * Age) + COPD* + Functional status + ASA physical status + Sepsis + Smoking within last year + Type of procedure
Postoperative pneumonia risk = 100 * e (Terms) / (1 + e (Terms) )




Equation parameters, such as COPD* , have 2 or more discrete values that may be used in the calculation. The numbers in the parentheses, eg, (-0.4553), represent the values that will be used.
