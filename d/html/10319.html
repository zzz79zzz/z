Dosing: Adult
Hemorrhoids, anorectal pain/itch and anorectal cleansing

Hemorrhoids, anorectal pain/itch and anorectal cleansing:

Note: General dosing guidelines provided; refer to specific product labeling for dosing instructions.

Topical (external) (OTC labeling): Apply to anal area, as needed, up to 6 times daily or after each bowel movement.

Skin irritation

Skin irritation: Topical: Apply to affected area, as needed.

Vaginal irritation

Vaginal irritation: Topical: Cleanse the area by gently wiping, patting, or blotting; may repeat, as needed. May be used as a moist compress by placing wipe in contact with tissue and leave in place for up to 15 minutes; repeat, as needed.

Dosing: Kidney Impairment: Adult

There are no dosage adjustments provided in the manufacturer’s labeling.

There are no dosage adjustments provided in the manufacturer’s labeling.

Dosing: Older Adult

Refer to adult dosing.

Dosing: Pediatric

Children ≥12 years: Refer to adult dosing.

Adverse Reactions

There are no adverse reactions listed in the manufacturer’s labeling.

Warnings/Precautions

Other warnings/precautions:

• Self-medication (OTC use): For external use only. When used for self-medication, notify health care provider and discontinue use if condition worsens, does not improve within 7 days, or if bleeding occurs. Do not put rectal products into the rectum using fingers or any mechanical device or applicator; do not exceed recommended dose unless directed by a health care provider.

Administration: Adult

Topical: For external use only; do not use in or around eyes. When practical, cleanse the affected area with mild soap and warm water and rinse thoroughly.

Rectal products: Apply rectal wipes by gently wiping, patting, and blotting affected area. Do not put product into the rectum using fingers or any mechanical device or applicator.

Administration: Pediatric

Topical: For external use only; avoid contact with eyes.

Use: Labeled Indications

Hemorrhoids, anorectal pain/itch and anorectal cleansing: Relief from local itching and discomfort associated with hemorrhoids; relief of anorectal irritation and burning; protection of anorectal areas.

Skin irritation: Relief from minor skin irritations, including insect bites, minor cuts, and minor scrapes.

Vaginal irritation: Relief from vaginal irritation.

Metabolism/Transport Effects

None known.

Drug Interactions

There are no known significant interactions.
