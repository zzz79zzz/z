Total criteria point count:
 

 

 Severity of post-thrombotic syndrome

 
0 to 4 points: Not present
5 to 9 points: Mild
10 to 14 points: Moderate
15 to 48 points: Severe

 
Notes
  • * The presence of venous ulceration, while not usually the only clinical finding, is indicative of severe post-thrombotic syndrome.
  • If severe post-thrombotic syndrome is based solely on venous ulceration, the Villalta score should be considered 15.
