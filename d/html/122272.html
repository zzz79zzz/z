 PUCAI interpretation

 
0 to 9 points: Remission
10 to 34 points: Mild disease
35 to 64 points: Moderate disease
65 to 85 points: Severe disease

 
Notes
  • Answers should reflect a daily average of the last 2 days.
  • For rectal bleeding, "large amount" describes blood present in most stools.
  • For number of stools, several small stools clustered over a short period of time should be considered as 1 stool, since this may be due to incomplete evacuation or tenesmus.
  • PUCAI: pediatric ulcerative colitis activity index.
