Reported Use

Postpartum: To produce uterine contractions during the third stage of labor to promote separation of the placenta and to control postpartum bleeding or hemorrhage

Dosage Range

Labor stimulation: IM: Ergometrine maleate 0.5 mg and oxytocin 5 units after delivery of anterior shoulder or immediately after full delivery

Postpartum hemorrhage:

Product available in various countries; not currently available in the US
