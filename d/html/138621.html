Index Terms
  • Sulpirid;

Pharmacologic Category
  • Antipsychotic Agent, Benzamide
Reported Use

Treatment of psychotic disorders, including schizophrenia; short-term treatment of anxiety

Dosage Range

Adults: Oral: 400 mg to 2.4 g/day in 2 to 4 divided doses (psychotic disorders) or 50 to 150 mg/day (anxiety).

Product Availability

Product available in various countries; not currently available in the United States

Dosage Forms

Capsule, Oral: 50 mg

Solution, Oral: 40 mg/mL

Tablet, Oral: 200 mg, 400 mg
