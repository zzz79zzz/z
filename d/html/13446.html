Pulmonary embolism risk score interpretation


Score >6: High probability

Notes
  • A score of <2.0 and a negative D-dimer results in a PE rate of 1.5% (95% CI 0.4-3.7). Refer to the original article below for all details.
  • DVT : deep vein thrombosis; PE : pulmonary embolism.
