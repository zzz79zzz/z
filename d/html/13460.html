Important: Inputs must be complete to perform calculation.



SVR dynes-sec-cm -5





Notes
  • In most cases, Systemic flow is equivalent to the cardiac output.
  • Normal SVR is approximately 1170 plus or minus 270 dynes-sec-cm -5 .
  • In most cases, the Mean aortic pressure is equivalent to the mean arterial pressure, which can be obtained from most hemodynamic monitors.
  • For information on the use of the SVR, please see UpToDate topic reviews on the use of vasopressors and inotropes and on intraoperative transesophageal echocardiography.
  • SVR : systemic vascular resistance.


Equations used

SVR = 80 * ( Mean_aortic_pressure - Right_atrial_pressure) / Systemic_flow
