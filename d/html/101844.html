Use: Labeled Indications

Skin protectant: Protects and conditions sore or cracked nipples due to breastfeeding.

Metabolism/Transport Effects

None known.

Drug Interactions

There are no known significant interactions.

Breastfeeding Considerations

Considered safe for infant; removal prior to breastfeeding is not necessary.
