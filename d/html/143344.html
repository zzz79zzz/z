Notes
  • Previous emboli: An embolism event occurred before the initiation of antibiotics.
  • Vegetation length: Maximum length measured by echocardiography.

 
Equations used
 
Hazard ratio = e((Age*0.01315524)+Diabetes+Previous emboli+Atrial fibrillation+Vegetation length_Factor+Staphylococcus aureus identified as cause)

 

 
Calculation details and variables
  • VegetationLengthFactor is assigned values by this ordered logic:
    • If Vegetation length ≥10 mm, VegetationLengthFactor = 1.49626773
    • Else VegetationLengthFactor = 0.23113278.
  • Embolic risk at specific time intervals is calculated by the following steps:
    • Day-specific Accumulated risk is calculated by iterating through the day-specific risk coefficients in the table below.
    • The Hazard ratio (of all risk factors combined, calculated above) is multiplied by the day-specific risk coefficient, and these day-specific products are added to Accumulated risk
    • Then, day-specific Embolic risk percent = 100 * (1 - e-Accumulated risk [that day])
