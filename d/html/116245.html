Equations used

Terms = (0.056 * Height) + Obstetric history - Arrest disorder at previous cesarean - (0.023 * Maternal age) - (0.024 * Pre-pregnancy weight) - On antihypertensive drug therapy - 5.952
Probability of successful vaginal delivery = 100 * e (Terms) / (1 + e (Terms) )




Equation parameters, such as Obstetric history , have 2 or more discrete values that may be used in the calculation. The numbers in the parentheses, eg, (0), represent the values that will be used.
