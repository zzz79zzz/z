Warning
  • urn:lims:b498:s4750745 • Female No Do not take this drug if you are pregnant. This drug may cause harm to the unborn baby if you take it while you are pregnant. A pregnancy test will be done before starting this drug to show that you are NOT pregnant. Pregnancy tests will also be done every month while you take this drug and for 1 month after you stop taking it. Use birth control while taking this drug and for 1 month after your last dose. Talk with your doctor about what type of birth control to use. If you get pregnant or plan on getting pregnant while taking this drug or within 1 month after your last dose, call your doctor right away.
