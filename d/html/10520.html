Reported Use

Treatment of respiratory tract disorders associated with viscid mucus; relief of sore throat symptoms (lozenge only)

Dosage Range

Oral:

Children 2 to <6 years: 7.5 to 15 mg 3 times daily.

Children 6 to 12 years: 15 to 30 mg 2 to 3 times daily.

Children >12 years, Adolescents, and Adults: 60 to 120 mg/day in 2 to 3 divided doses (immediate release) or 75 mg once daily (extended release capsules; adults only).
