 Calculator: Plasma sodium concentration correction for hyperglycemia in adults (conventional and SI units)

 
Input
 
Measured plasma or serum sodium concentration  
Serum glucose  

 
Result
 
Important: Inputs must be complete to perform calculation.

 
 
Estimated (corrected) plasma sodium*  
 
Decimal precision  

 
 

 
*The estimated (corrected) plasma sodium is a rough estimate of how much the serum sodium concentration will rise as hyperglycemia is corrected.

 

 
Equations used
 
Estimated (corrected) plasma sodium* = Measured plasma or serum sodium concentration + (2 * (Serum_glucose - 100) / 100)

 

 
References
