Notes
  • This calculator uses the equations from Hankinson 1999 (NHANES III) where available and equations from Knudson 1983 where there are deficits in the data (patients who are not 8 to 17 years of age and patients age 8 to 17 years who are not White , Black or African American , or of Mexican American ethnicity).
  • PEF : peak expiratory flow.


Calculation details and variables
  • In the following cases the Hankinson equation is used: PEF predicted[L/min] = 60 * (b0 + (b1 * Age[yr]) + (b2 * Age 2 [yr]) + (b3 * Height[cm] 2 )) is used with the specified coefficients:
    • For a White male 8 to 17.99 years old: b0 = -0.5962; b1 = -0.12357; b2 =  0.013135; b3 = 0.00024962.
    • For a Black or African American male, 8 to 17.99 years old: b0 = -0.2684; b1 = -0.28016; b2 = 0.018202; b3 = 0.00027333.
    • For a Mexican American male, 8 to 17.99 years old: b0 = -0.9537; b1 = -0.19602; b2 = 0.014497; b3 = 0.00030243.
    • In all males under age 8 and in males of other race/ethnicities: PEF predicted[L/min] = ((Height[cm] - 100) * 5) + 100
