 RAPID score interpretation

 
0 to 2 points: Low risk; 1.5% 3-month mortality
3 to 4 points: Medium risk; 17.8% 3-month mortality
5 to 7 points: High risk; 47.8% 3-month mortality

 
Notes
  • RAPID: Renal, Age, Purulence, Infection source, and Dietary factors.
