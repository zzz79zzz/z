Reported Use

Treatment of pain and inflammation including low back pain, trauma, dental pain, gynecological pain, scapulohumeral periarthritis, and extra-articular rheumatism. Note: Approved indication may vary per country/region (consult local product labeling).

Dosage Range

Adults: Oral:

Immediate release: 100 mg twice daily

Sustained release: 200 mg once daily

Product Availability

Product available in various countries; not currently available in the US

Tablet Sustained Release, Oral: 200 mg
