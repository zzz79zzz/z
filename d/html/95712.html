Notes
  • SI units: Système Internationale d'Unités, or International System of Units.
  • US units: Gravimetric, imperial, or traditional English units.
  • ANCA: antineutrophil cytoplasmic antibodies; GBM: glomerular basement membrane; IgA: immunoglobulin A; IgE: immunoglobulin E; IgG: immunoglobulin G; IgM: immunoglobulin M.
  • To make immunology unit conversions, first choose from the top pull-down selector.
  • Next, select the starting unit and enter a value in the FROM input.
  • Select a TO unit from the lower right selector and the converted value will appear immediately in the TO box.
  • The conversion factor for any selected analyte can be found by entering 1.0 in the "FROM" input box for that analyte and viewing the conversion factor in the "TO" output box. Conversion is calculated by multiplying a value in the FROM units by the conversion factor.
  • In this calculator, mcg is the abbreviation for micrograms.
