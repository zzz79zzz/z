Index Terms
  • Mebeverine Hydrochloride;
  • Mebeverine Pamoate
Pharmacologic Category
  • Antispasmodic Agent, Gastrointestinal
Reported Use

Treatment of symptoms associated with irritable bowel syndrome and/or spasm in organic GI diseases.

Dosage Range

Adults: Oral: 300 to 450 mg/day in 3 to 4 divided doses (immediate release products) or 400 mg/day in 2 divided doses (modified release capsules).

Product Availability

Product available in various countries; not currently available in the US

Dosage Forms

Capsule Modified Release, Oral, as hydrochloride: 200 mg

Suspension, Oral, as pamoate [strength expressed as hydrochloride equivalent]: 50 mg/mL

Tablet, Oral, as hydrochloride: 100 mg, 135 mg
