SDAI interpretation


SDAI ≤3.3: Remission
SDAI >3.3 and ≤11: Low disease activity
SDAI >11 and ≤26: Moderate disease activity
SDAI >26: High disease activity




Notes
  • In the hands, the joints that are evaluated include the 1 st through 5 th MCP, the IP of the thumb, and the 2 nd through 5 th PIP.
  • The PGA estimate represents the patient's self-assessment of disease activity on a 0 to 10 scale where 10 means maximal activity.
  • The EGA estimate represents the evaluator's assessment of disease activity on a 0 to 10 scale where 10 means maximal activity.
  • An SDAI reduction by 7 represents a moderate improvement. A reduction by more than 17 represents a major improvement.
  • CRP : C-reactive protein; PGA : Patient Global disease Activity; EGA : Evaluator Global disease Activity; TJC : tender joint count; SJC : swollen joint count; IP : interphalangeal joint; MCP : metacarpophalangeal joint; PIP : proximal interphalangeal joint.
  • In this calculator, mcg is the abbreviation for micrograms.


Equations used

SDAI = SJC + TJC +PGA + EGA + CRP
