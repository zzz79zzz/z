Calculator: Modified Geneva score for estimation of the clinical probability of pulmonary embolism in adults


Risk factors

Age >65 years old (1 point)

Previous DVT or PE (3 points)

Surgery or lower limb fracture within 1 month (2 points)

Active malignant disease * (2 points)
Symptoms

Unilateral lower limb pain (3 points)

Hemoptysis (2 points)
Signs

Heart rate <75 beats per minute (0 points)

Heart rate 75 to 94 beats per minute (3 points)

Heart rate ≥95 beats per minute (5 points)

Pain (tenderness) on palpation of lower limb deep venous structure and unilateral edema (4 points)

Modified Geneva score interpretation


Modified Geneva score (points) Clinical probability of PE
0 to 3 Low
4 to 10 Intermediate
11 to 22 High


Notes
  • DVT : deep venous thrombosis; PE : pulmonary embolism.
  • *Active malignant disease, defined as a solid or hematologic malignant condition, currently active or considered cured less than 1 year ago.


References
