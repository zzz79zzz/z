Notes
  • * Varicose veins must be ≥3 mm in diameter (while standing) to count in this score.
  • ¶ Venous edema must be a regular finding, not occasional. It is typically brawny rather than pitting and it is altered by leg elevation or standing.
  • Δ Pigment changes presume venous origin and must be relatively diffuse, not just focal over varicose veins.
  • The venous clinical severity score is a tool to help to objectively follow patient response to clinical management.
