Notes
  • This calculator is not appropriate for use in females with any of the following: a personal history of breast cancer (including ductal carcinoma in situ [DCIS]), breast augmentation, or mastectomy.
  • First-degree relatives comprise female birth parent, female siblings, and female offspring.
  • The Native American group includes Native Alaskans. The Asian group includes Native Hawaiians and Pacific Islanders.
  • For females of mixed/other/unknown race/ethnicity, calculation is done with data for non-Hispanic White women.
  • BCSC: Breast Cancer Surveillance Consortium.

 
Calculation details and variables
  • The 5- and 10-year average breast cancer risks are read directly from tables 2 and 3 below.
  • Density, Rels, and Biopsy adjustment factors are based on risk combination (RC) index numbers. These RC numbers are used to look up the adjustment factors in the DensityAdj, RelsAdj, and BiopsyAdj columns of table 4 below:
    • RRE (Recoded RE) = RE except for Native Americans for which RRE = 5, and Hispanics for which RRE = 8.
    • RC_DensityAdj = (1000 * RRE) + (10 * Age) + Den
      DensityAdj = DensityAdj column[RC_DensityAdj]
      If not in table, DensityAdj = 1
    • RC_RelsAdj = (1000 * RRE) + (10 * Age) + Rels
      RelsAdj = RelsAdj column[RC_RelsAdj]
      If not in table, RelsAdj = 1
    • RC_BiopsyAdj = (1000 * RRE) + (10 * Age) + Biopsy
      BiopsyAdj = BiopsyAdj column[RC_BiopsyAdj]
      If not in table, BiopsyAdj = 1
  • Absolute risk is calculated by looping through the next steps with index "i" spanning 1 to 10. This creates an array "inc" containing the individual risk per year in inc[1] to inc[10]. The "C1" to "C14" variables are read from table 1 below. The variable "notatrisk" starts at 0, and the variable "atrisk" starts at 1:
    • MAge = Age + i - 1
    • inc[i] = DensityAdj * RelsAdj * BiopsyAdj * ((MAge3 * C1[RE]) + (MAge2 * C2[RE]) - (MAge * C3[RE]) + C4[RE])
    • while i >1 do the following 4 steps for each i:
    • MAge = MAge - 1
    • notatrisk = notatrisk + atrisk * (C5[RE] * e(C6[RE] * MAge)) / 100 + atrisk * DensityAdj * RelsAdj * BiopsyAdj * ((MAge3 * C7[RE]) + (MAge2 * C8[RE]) - (MAge * C9[RE]) + C10[RE]) / 100 + atrisk * DensityAdj * RelsAdj * BiopsyAdj * ((MAge3 * C11[RE]) + (MAge2 * C12[RE]) - (MAge * C13[RE]) + C14[RE]) / 100
    • atrisk = 1 - notatrisk;
    • inc[i] = inc[i] * atrisk
  • Finally, the 5- and 10-year risks are calculated by simply summing inc[1-5] and inc[1-10] respectively.
