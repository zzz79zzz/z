ANSWER — Incorrect.

Several tests are recommended for the evaluation of the patient suspected of having diabetic gastroparesis, including radionuclide gastric emptying studies, radiolabeled breath tests, and wireless motility capsules. The availability and use of these procedures vary from one institution to another, although a consensus recommendation has been published [ 1 ]. While studies using optimal gastric emptying test methods demonstrate significant associations between abnormal results and symptoms, studies are insufficient to provide recommendations for patients with diabetes [ 2 ].
