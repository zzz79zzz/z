MIPI score prognostic interpretation


MIPI ≤5.7: Low risk
MIPI >5.7 and ≤6.2: Intermediate risk
MIPI >6.2: High risk




Notes
  • ECOG: Eastern Cooperative Oncology Group; PS: performance status.
  • LDH is the patient's baseline serum lactate dehydrogenase level.
  • ULN LDH is the upper limit of the reference range for serum LDH in the laboratory where the patient's LDH was measured.
  • WBC is total white blood cell count (eg, 7500/microL [7.5 x 10^9/L]).


Equations used

MIPI = (0.03535 * Age) + ECOG_performance_status + (1.367 * log(LDH / ULN_LDH)) + (0.9393 * log(WBC))




Equation parameters, such as ECOG performance status , have 2 or more discrete values that may be used in the calculation. The numbers in the parentheses, eg, (0), represent the values that will be used.



References
