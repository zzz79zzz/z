Adverse Reactions

The following adverse drug reactions and incidences are derived from product labeling unless otherwise specified.

Frequency not defined: Local: Local irritation

Warnings/Precautions

Other warnings/precautions:

• Appropriate use: For external use only; avoid contact with eyes. Do not use on animal bites, severe burns, deep or puncture wounds, or lacerations.

• Self-medication (OTC use): Stop use and consult healthcare provider if condition worsens, symptoms last >7 days, or if symptoms resolve and then recur within a few days.

Dosage Forms: US

Excipient information presented when available (limited, particularly for generics); consult specific product labeling.

Ointment, topical:

A+D Original: (42.5 g); (120 g); (454 g) [in lanolin-petrolatum base]

A+D Prevent: (113 g [DSC]) [in lanolin-petrolatum base]

Curad Vitamin A & D: (56.6 g) [in petrolatum base]

Generic: (5 g, 28.4 g, 60 g, 113 g, 120 g, 425g, 454 g)

Generic Equivalent Available: US

Yes: Ointment

Administration: Adult

For external use only; avoid contact with eyes. Cleanse affected area with mild soap and water; allow to dry thoroughly prior to application. Apply directly on affected area or by spreading on sterile gauze or clean cloth then to the affected area. Do not use on animal bites, severe burns, deep or puncture wounds, or lacerations. Bandage loosely if bandages are needed.

Administration: Pediatric

Topical: For external use only; avoid contact with eyes. Cleanse affected area with mild soap and water; allow to dry thoroughly prior to application. Apply directly on affected area or by spreading on sterile gauze or clean cloth then to the affected area, bandage loosely, if needed. Do not use on animal bites, severe burns, deep or puncture wounds, or lacerations.

Use: Labeled Indications

Diaper rash: Prevention and treatment of diaper rash; protects chafed skin and helps seal out wetness

Skin protectant: Temporary protection of skin due to minor cuts, minor scrapes, or minor burns; protection and relief of chapped, chafed, or cracked skin and lips

Metabolism/Transport Effects

None known.

Drug Interactions

There are no known significant interactions.
