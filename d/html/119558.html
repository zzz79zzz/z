ABCD 2 score interpretation


0 to 3 points: Low 2-day stroke risk (1%)
4 to 5 points: Moderate 2-day stroke risk (4%)
6 to 7 points: High 2-day stroke risk (8%)


Notes
  • ABCD 2 : age, blood pressure, clinical features, duration, diabetes; TIA: transient ischemic attack.
