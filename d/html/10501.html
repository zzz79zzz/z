Index Terms
  • Ramosetron Hydrochloride
Pharmacologic Category
  • Antiemetic;
  • Selective 5-HT3 Receptor Antagonist
Reported Use

Prevention of chemotherapy-induced or postoperative nausea and vomiting; treatment of irritable bowel syndrome with diarrhea

Dosage Range

Adults:

Irritable bowel syndrome with diarrhea: Oral (tablet):

Females: 2.5 to 5 mcg once daily (maximum: 5 mcg/day).

Males: 2.5 to 10 mcg once daily (maximum: 10 mcg/day).

Prevention of chemotherapy-induced nausea and vomiting:

Oral (orally disintegrating tablet): 100 mcg once daily administered 1 hour prior to chemotherapy.

IV: 300 to 600 mcg once daily (maximum: 600 mcg/day).

Prevention of postoperative nausea and/or vomiting: IV: 300 to 600 mcg once daily (maximum: 600 mcg/day).

Product Availability

Product available in various countries; not currently available in the US

Dosage Forms

Solution, Intravenous: 300 mcg/2 mL (2 mL)

Tablet, Oral: 2.5 mcg, 5 mcg

Tablet Dispersable, Oral: 100 mcg
