 ARISCAT risk index interpretation

 
0 to 25 points: Low risk: 1.6% pulmonary complication rate
26 to 44 points: Intermediate risk: 13.3% pulmonary complication rate
45 to 123 points: High risk: 42.1% pulmonary complication rate
