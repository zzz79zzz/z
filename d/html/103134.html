 Geneva risk score interpretation

 
0 to 2 points: Lower risk; 0.8% 30-day risk of symptomatic VTE or VTE-related mortality*
3 to 30 points: Higher risk; 3.5% 30-day risk of symptomatic VTE or VTE-related mortality*

 
Notes
  • Acute infectious disease includes sepsis.
  • Immobilization: Complete bed rest or inability to walk >30 minutes/day.
  • Dehydration: Subjectively assessed by the clinician.
  • * 30-day risk of symptomatic VTE or VTE-related mortality in patients who did not receive adequate anticoagulation during hospitalization.
  • BMI: body mass index; VTE: venous thromboembolism (includes deep venous thrombosis and/or pulmonary embolism).
