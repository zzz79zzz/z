 CMML prognosis score interpretation

 
0 points: Low risk; 32 months median survival
1 point: Intermediate risk; 18.5 months median survival
2 to 4 points: High risk; 10 months median survival

 
 

 
Notes
  • Absolute monocyte counts >10,000/microL [>10 x 109/L] contribute one point to the CMML prognosis score.

 
Equations used
 
Absolute_monocyte_count = White_blood_cell_count * Percent_monocytes / 100
Score = (1 if the following expression is true, 0 if false)(Absolute_monocyte_count > 10000) + Circulating_immature_cells + Decreased_hemoglobin + Decreased_platelets

 

 
Equation parameters, such as Circulating immature cells, have 2 or more discrete values that may be used in the calculation. The numbers in the parentheses, eg, (0), represent the values that will be used.
