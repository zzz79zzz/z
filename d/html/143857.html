Therapeutic Category
  • Antiviral Agent, Topical
Adverse Reactions

The following adverse drug reactions and incidences are derived from product labeling unless otherwise specified. Reported adverse reactions are for children, adolescents, and adults.

>10%: Local: Application-site pain (≤12%)

1% to 10%:

Local: Application-site dermatitis (≤3%), application-site edema (≤2%), application-site erythema (5% to 6%), application-site pruritus (2% to 4%), application-site vesicles (≤1%), local skin discoloration (≤1%; application site), local skin exfoliation (≤3%; application site)

Miscellaneous: Fever (2%)

<1%:

Dermatologic: Skin erosion (application site)

Local: Application-site irritation

Contraindications

There are no contraindications listed in the manufacturer's labeling.

Product Availability

Zelsuvmi: FDA approved January 2024; availability anticipated in 2024.

Use

Treatment of molluscum contagiosum (FDA approved in patients ≥1 year of age and adults).

Metabolism/Transport Effects

None known.

Drug Interactions

There are no known significant interactions.
