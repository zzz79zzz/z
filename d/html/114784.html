Disaster management: Links to UpToDate resources and society guidelines

Introduction — This topic includes links to guidelines related to health concerns following a disaster. We will update these guideline links periodically; newer versions of some guidelines may be available on each society's website.

The recommendations in the guidelines in this section may vary from those that appear in UpToDate topic reviews. Relevant UpToDate topic reviews, society guideline links topics, and patient education topics can be found below or by using the UpToDate search box. (See 'Related UpToDate topics' below.)
