Pharmacologic Category
  • Antibiotic, Macrolide
Reported Use

For treatment of susceptible bacterial infections including mycetoma, upper and lower respiratory tract infections, pneumonia, genital/venereal infections (except N. gonorrhea), otitis media, sinusitis, skin and soft tissue infections, periodontal infection; for prevention of N. meningitides transmission to contacts

Dosage Range

Oral: Doses should be taken before meals:

Children: 5 to 8 mg/kg/day in 2 divided; maximum dose: 150 mg twice daily

Adults: 300 mg/day in a single dose or 2 divided doses

Product Availability

Product available in various countries; not currently available in the US

Dosage Forms

Tablet, Oral: 150 mg, 300 mg

Tablet Dispersible, Oral: 50 mg
