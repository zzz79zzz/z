ANSWER — Correct.

A fibric acid derivative such as fenofibrate is an acceptable form of treatment for patients with diabetes and mild to moderate hypertriglyceridemia. It may also reduce the low-density lipoprotein (LDL) cholesterol level and raise the high-density lipoprotein (HDL) cholesterol level.
