Notes
  • Post-test probability of positive result is the probability that the disease is present when the test result is positive .
  • Post-test probability of negative result is the probability that the disease is present when the test result is negative .
  • Pre-test probability is usually equivalent to the prevalence of a disease or finding in a particular population.


Equations used

Post_Odds_Pos = (Pre-test probability * Sensitivity) / ((1 - Pre-test probability) * (1-Specificity))
Post-test probability of positive result = Post_Odds_Pos / (1 + Post_Odds_Pos)
Post_Odds_Neg = (Pre-test probability * (1-Sensitivity)) / ((1-Pre-test probability) * Specificity)
Post-test probability of negative result = Post_Odds_Neg / (1 + Post_Odds_Neg)
