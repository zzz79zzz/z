What are some things I need to know or do while I take this drug?

  • urn:lims:b498:s6434278 • No If you have high blood sugar (diabetes) and take drugs to lower blood sugar, talk with your doctor.   • urn:lims:b498:s6524099 • No If you are being treated for high blood pressure, talk with your doctor. Call your doctor right away if you have signs of low blood pressure like very bad dizziness or passing out.
  • urn:lims:b498:s4816442 • No This drug may cause an acid blood problem (metabolic acidosis). Over time, metabolic acidosis can cause kidney stones, bone problems, or growth problems in children.
  • urn:lims:b498:s2701043 • No If you are allergic to tartrazine (FD&C Yellow No. 5), talk with your doctor. Some products have tartrazine.
