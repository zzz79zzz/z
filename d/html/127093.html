 Interpretation

 
Score ≤1.5680: Low risk
Score >1.5680 and ≤2.2185: Intermediate risk
Score >2.2185: High risk

 
 

 
Notes
  • All inputs are the values present at the time of diagnosis.
  • For information on the ELTS score, please see UpToDate topics that discuss the prognosis of chronic myelogenous leukemia.
  • EUTOS: European Treatment Outcome Study.

 
Equations used
 
ELTS_score = 0.0025 * (Age/10)3 + (0.0615 * (Round the following expression to the nearest integer)(Spleen_size_below_costal_margin)) + (0.1052 * (Round the following expression to the nearest integer)(Blasts_in_peripheral_blood)) + (0.4104 * (Platelet_count/1000)-0.5)
