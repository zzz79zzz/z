Stool osmolal gap ( SOG ) interpretation


SOG <50: Compatible with secretory diarrhea
SOG ≥50 and ≤125: Indeterminate
SOG >125: Compatible with osmotic diarrhea




Notes
  • Please see appropriate UpToDate topic reviews for further information.
  • Stool Na : stool sodium; Stool K : stool potassium.


Equations used

SOG = Stool_Osm - (2 * (Stool_Na + Stool_K))




References
