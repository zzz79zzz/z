Notes
  • U1 is the BUN just after the first dialysis of the week.
  • U2 is the BUN just prior to the second dialysis of the week.
  • ID : interdialytic; BUN : blood urea nitrogen; RRF : residual renal function.


Equations used

Mean_BUN = (U1 + U2) / 2
RRF = ID_urine_volume * Urine_urea_concentration / ID_period / Mean_BUN




References
  1. Daugirdas JT, Ing TS. Handbook of Dialysis, 2nd ed, Little, Brown 1994.
