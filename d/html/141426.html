Index Terms
  • Vildagliptin
Pharmacologic Category
  • Antidiabetic Agent, Dipeptidyl Peptidase IV (DPP-IV) Inhibitor
Reported Use

Management of type 2 diabetes mellitus as an adjunct to diet and exercise as monotherapy or in combination with other antidiabetic agents.

Dosage Range

Adults: Oral: 50 mg once or twice daily.

Product Availability

Product available in various countries; not currently available in the United States.

Dosage Forms

Tablet, Oral: 50 mg
