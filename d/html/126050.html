Notes
  • PEF : peak expiratory flow; PEF LLN : PEF lower limit of normal.


Equations used

PEF = 60 * (b0 + (b1 * Age) + (b2 * sq(Age)) + (b3 * sq(Height)))
PEF_LLN = 60 * (b0 + (b1 * Age) + (b2 * sq(Age)) + (b4 * sq(Height)))




Calculation details and variables
  • For a White female ≥18 years old: b0 = 0.9267; b1 = 0.06929; b2 = -0.001031; b3 = 0.00018623; b4 = 0.00012148.
  • For a Black or African American female, 18 years old or older: b0 = 1.3597; b1 = 0.03458; b2 = -0.000847; b3 = 0.00019746; b4 = 0.00012160.
  • For a Mexican American female, 18 years old or older: b0 = 0.2401; b1 = 0.06174; b2 = -0.001023; b3 = 0.00022203; b4 = 0.00014611.
