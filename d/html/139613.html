Decimal precision  

 

 

 Interpretation

 
PaO2/FiO2 ratio (mmHg) ARDS category
>300 None
>200 and ≤300 Mild
>100 and ≤200 Moderate
≤100 Severe

 
 

 
Notes
  • The PaO2/FiO2 ratio is discussed in UpToDate topic reviews on ARDS, sepsis, multiorgan failure, mechanical ventilation, and predictive scoring systems.
  • PaO2: partial pressure (arterial) of oxygen; FiO2: fraction of inspired oxygen (entered as a decimal fraction, eg, 0.6 for 60% inspired oxygen); ARDS: acute respiratory distress syndrome.

 
Equations used
 
PaO2/FiO2 ratio = PaO2 / FiO2
