Interpretation


PaO 2 /FiO 2 ratio (mmHg) ARDS category
>300 None
>200 and ≤300 Mild
>100 and ≤200 Moderate
≤100 Severe




Notes
  • The PaO 2 /FiO 2 ratio is discussed in UpToDate topic reviews on ARDS, sepsis, multiorgan failure, mechanical ventilation, and predictive scoring systems.
  • PaO 2 : partial pressure (arterial) of oxygen; FiO 2 : fraction of inspired oxygen (entered as a decimal fraction, eg, 0.6 for 60% inspired oxygen); ARDS : acute respiratory distress syndrome.


Equations used

PaO 2 /FiO 2 ratio = PaO 2 / FiO 2
