 ALBI interpretation

 
ALBI score ALBI grade Median survival in months (newly diagnosed HCC patients)* Median survival in months (treated with sorafenib)**
≤-2.6 A1 85.6 12.7
>-2.6 and ≤-1.39 A2 46.5 7.2
>-1.39 A3 15.5 3.6

 
 

 
Notes
  • * Median survival for all HCC patients including those who later had potentially curative surgery; results from initial Japanese training set from which the ALBI model was derived.
  • ** Median survival for HCC patients with unresectable or relapsed incurable disease; based on data from clinical trials of sorafenib in HCC.
  • HCC: Hepatocellular carcinoma.
  • Please refer to UpToDate topic reviews for additional information on the use of the ALBI grade estimate of prognosis for adult patients treated for hepatocellular carcinoma.

 
Equations used
 
ALBI_score = (log(Bilirubin[mcmol/L]) * 0.66) - (Albumin[g/L] * 0.085)
