Index Terms
  • Lomefloxacin Hydrochloride
Pharmacologic Category
  • Antibiotic, Quinolone
Reported Use

Treatment of bacterial infections caused by susceptible organisms including bronchitis, cystitis/urinary tract infections (uncomplicated and complicated), skin or skin structure infections, prostatitis, Salmonella enteric fever or gastroenteritis, shigellosis, or surgical prophylaxis (transurethral procedures)

Dosage Range

Adults: Oral: 400 mg once daily

Product Availability

Product available in various countries; not currently available in the US

Dosage Forms

Tablet, Oral: 100 mg, 200 mg, 400 mg
