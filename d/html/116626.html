 Calculator: PLASMIC score for estimating the likelihood of severe ADAMTS13 deficiency in adults with suspected TTP

 

 Platelet count <30,000/microL [<30 x 109/L]

 One or more indicators of hemolysis:
      Reticulocyte count (percentage) >2.5%; or
      Haptoglobin undetectable; or
      Indirect bilirubin >2.0 mg/dL [>34 mcmol/L]

 No active cancer in the preceding year

 No history of solid organ or hematopoietic stem cell transplant

 Mean corpuscular volume (MCV) <90 femtoliters

 International normalized ratio (INR) <1.5

 Creatinine <2.0 mg/dL [<177 mcmol/L]

 Interpretation

 
PLASMIC score (points) Risk of severe ADAMTS13 deficiency
0 to 4 Low risk
5 Intermediate risk
6 to 7 High risk

 
Notes
  • The PLASMIC score is only intended for use in individuals suspected to have a thrombotic microangiopathy based on the finding of schistocytes on the blood smear.
  • Each criterion, if present, contributes one point to the total criteria point count. One point is added for one or more of the "Indicator of hemolysis" subcriteria, if present.
  • TTP: thrombotic thrombocytopenic purpura.
  • ADAMTS13: a disintegrin and metalloproteinase with a thrombospondin type 1 motif, member 13.

 
References
