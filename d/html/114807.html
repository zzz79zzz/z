Notes
  • *Lymphoma-specific survival at 5 years.
  • LDH: lactate dehydrogenase; LAD: lymphadenopathy.

 
Equations used
 
Prognostic_index = (0.2 * Hemoglobin) + (0.006 * Platelets) - LDH_score - LAD_score

 

 
Equation parameters, such as LDH score, have 2 or more discrete values that may be used in the calculation. The numbers in the parentheses, eg, (0), represent the values that will be used.
