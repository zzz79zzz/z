Notes
  • This model [1,2] uses the 1997 American Joint Committee on Cancer (AJCC) Tumor (T), Node (N), and Metastatic (M) staging system. [3] Localized disease is defined as any T and N0 and M0. Metastatic disease is defined as any T and N≥1 and M≥0. For more information regarding contemporary staging systems, please refer to UpToDate discussion of staging of renal cell carcinoma.
  • This prognostic model has been validated through observational studies; however, it has not been validated in large groups of patients treated with immune checkpoint inhibitors and other targeted agents.
  • ECOG : Eastern Cooperative Oncology Group; PS : performance status; Intermed : Intermediate.
