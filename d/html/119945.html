Notes
  • The fractional excretion of phosphate (FePO 4 ) can be calculated by entering the values from simultaneously obtained urine and blood samples.
  • For this calculation, urine and serum input values may be entered as mg/dL (default) or in SI units.
  • In this calculator, mcmol is the abbreviation for micromol.


Equations used

FePO 4 = (Urine phosphate / Serum phosphate) / (Urine creatinine / Serum creatinine) * 100
