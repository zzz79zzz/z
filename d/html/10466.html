Index Terms
  • Itopride Hydrochloride
Pharmacologic Category
  • Gastrointestinal Agent, Prokinetic
Reported Use

Treatment of gastrointestinal symptoms (feeling of enlarged abdomen, upper abdominal pain, anorexia, heartburn, nausea, and vomiting) caused by reduced gastrointestinal motility.

Dosage Range

Adults: Oral: 50 mg 3 times/day

Product Availability

Product available in various countries; not currently available in the US

Dosage Forms

Tablet, Oral: 50 mg
