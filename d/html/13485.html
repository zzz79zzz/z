 Prognostic score interpretation

 
0 points: Low (median survival of 5.7 yrs)
0.5 to 1 point: Intermediate 1 (median survival of 3.5 yrs.)
1.5 to 2.0 points: Intermediate 2 (median survival of 1.2 yrs.)
2.5 to 3.5 points: High (median survival of 0.4 yrs.)
